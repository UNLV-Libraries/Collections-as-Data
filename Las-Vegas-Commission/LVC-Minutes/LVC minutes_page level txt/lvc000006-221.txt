NOTICE OF PUBLIC HEARING UPON PURPOSED AMENDMENTS TO THE
MASTER PLAN OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS

NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN:	That the Planning Commission of the City of

Las Vegas has certified to the Board of Commissioners of said City
an attested copy of amendments, extensions of, and additions to the
Master Plan of said City adopted gy said Planning Commission, and
that on the 4th day of February, 1948, at the hour of 7:30 P.M., of
said day, the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Clark
County, Nevada, will meet in the Commissioners' Room at the City Hall
in the War Memorial Building, at Stewart Avenue and Fifth Street, in
said City, and hold a public hearing upon the proposed amendments,
extentions, of, and additions to the Master Plan aforesaid before
adopting the same, and all persons interested will be given an
opportunity to be heard thereon at said time and place.
