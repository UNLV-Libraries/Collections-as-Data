City Manager Kennedy presented two applications for package liquor licenses
by Allan M. Kelley at 404 West Bonanza and Henry Martin Inc. at 4l8 West Bonanza.

The Commission instructed Mr. Kennedy to notify these applicants there were no
package liquor licenses available, and authorized the refund of the fees de-
posited with these applications.

Supervisor of Building and Safety Art Trelease presented the application of
Wheeler Trucking Co. for permission to move a 20' x 20' frame building from
the Consolidated Construction Yard at H and Mesquite to 1701 Western Avenue
and recommended this permit be approved.

Commissioner Fountain moved that a house moving permit be granted to Wheeler
Trucking Co. to move a building from H & Mesquite to 1701 Western Avenue,
subject to compliance with City ordinance.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye;
noes, none.

City Manager Kennedy reminded the Commission that the annual spring meeting
of the Nevada Municipal Association will be held in Wells, Nevada on May 10th
through 13th, 1957, and requested that those wishing to attend notify Robert W.
Notti, Assistant to the Mayor as soon as possible.

Commissioner Wendell Bunker, President of the Nevada Municipal Association
extended an invitation to all members and any department heads who would like to
attend this meeting. Mr. Bunker reported that arrangements had been made for
excellent speakers at the convention, and a very good program outlined.

Commissioner Sharp and Mayor Baker indicated that they would be in attendance
at this meeting.

Commissioner Whipple moved that Commissioner Wendell Bunker be commended for
his outstanding service as President of the Nevada Municipal Association, and
that an appropriate resolution be drawn by the City Attorney to be presented to
the Board at the next meeting for adoption and entrance in the minutes of this
Board; and that a copy be forwarded to the Executive Secretary of the Nevada
Municipal Association acknowledged at the meeting in Wells, Nevada.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Not voting:	Commissioner Bunker.

Commissioner Sharp asked for a report from the City Manager on the status of
the improvement of Hyde Park Circle.

Mr, Kennedy advised the Commission that blow sand was being hauled in and sprinkled
down at the present time. The City Manager stated that the installation of the
sprinkling system would commence within eight to ten days.

Commissioner Sharp, member of the committee to investigate and make a recommenda-
tion on the request of Vaughn and Gerald Leavitt And tenants in the Leavitt Building
at 1502 South 5th Street for vacation or correction of the parking problem on the
triangle in front of their building, made a report at this time. Commissioner
Sharp stated that it was the recommendation of the Committee that parking meters
be installed in this area which will park between 12 and 13 cars.

Commissioner Sharp moved this recommendation be accepted and that one-hour parking
meters be installed in this area.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the Following vote: Com-
missioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
