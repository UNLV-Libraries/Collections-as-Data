CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO-- Viola Burns, City Clerk
Las Vegas, Nevada.

A Special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada is hereby called
to be held in the Board of Commissioners Room in the City Clerk's Office, Wednesday the 15th day
of February, 1941 at the hour of ten o'clock A.M. on said day to consider and act upon the following:

1. To allow warrants Nos. 468 to 472 inclusive being pay rolls of the City of Las Vegas for
the first half of the month of February, 1941.

John L. Russell

Mayor of the City of Las Vegas.

NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO— John L. Russell Mayor and to Pat Clark

Wm. Gore, R.J Kaltenborn and A.F.Smith Sr.

Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

YOU AND EACH OF YOU WILL PLEASE TAKE NOTICE: That pursuant to a call this day issued by
the Mayor of the City of Las Vegas, a Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City
of Las Vegas, Nevada will be held in the Board of Commissioners Room in the City Clerk's Office
Wednesday the 19th day of February, 1941 at the hour of ten o'clock A.M. on said day to consider
and act upon the following:

1. To allow Warrants Nos. 468 to 472 inclusive being pay rolls of the City of Las Vegas
for the first half of February, 1941

Viola Burns
City Clerk

ACCEPTANCE OF SERVICE

WE, the undersigned Mayor and Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas hereby admit due ser-
vice of the foregoing notice of Special Meeting.

OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK CITY OF
LAS VEGAS, CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

February 19th, 1941

Minutes of a Special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,

Nevada held on the 19th day of February, 1941 at the hour of ten o'clock A.M.

Present Mayor Russell, Commissioners Gore, Clark, Kaltenborn and Smith and City Clerk

Absent City Attorney Austin.

In motion of Commissioner Smith seconded by Commissioner Clark it was moved and carried
that Warrants Nos. 468 to 472 be allowed and that the Mayor and Clerk sign the same.

Vote Commissioners Clark, Gore Kaltenborn and Smith and His Honor the Mayor voting aye.

Noes, none.

The business for which said meeting was called being concluded the meeting thereupon
adjourned.

Approved:

Mayor.

Attest:

City Clerk

OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK CITY OF
LAS VEGAS CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA,

FEBRUARY 21st, 1941.

 Minutes of a recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las
V gas Nevada held on the 21st day of February, 1941 at the hour of seven thirty P.M.

Present Mayor Russell Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti together with
the City Attorney and Clerk.

Also Present Messrs Gore and Smith. Absent Messrs. Kaltenborn and Clark.

Minutes of recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners held on the 17th day of
February and a Special Meeting called by the Mayor with Messrs Clark, Gore, Kaltenborn and
Smith read.

On motion of Commissioner Corradetti seconded by Commissioner Gilbert it was moved and car-
ried that the minutes of meeting of February 19th be stricken from the records.

On motion of Mr. Smith seconded by Mr. Gore it was moved that the minutes be approved as
read.

No vote was called on either of the above motion and no minutes were approved as read,
and no minutes corrected or amended.
