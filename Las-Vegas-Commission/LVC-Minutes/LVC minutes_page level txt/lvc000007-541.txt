LAS VEGAS, NEVADA
February 1, 1952

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is
hereby called to be held in the Mayor's Office at the City Hall, on the 1st day of
February, 1952, at the hour of 9:00 A. M. to consider the following matters:

1. Payroll Warrants Nos, 1269 to 1272 inclusive in the
net amount of $31.738.31.

2. Service & Material Warrants Nos. 2232 in total amount
of $706.69.

s/ C. D. Baker
C. D. BAKER, Mayor

WHIPPLE, Commissioners, and C. W. SHELLEY, City Manager, HOWARD W. CANNON, City-

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada

Mayor	C. D. Baker

PAYROLL WARRANTS	Commissioner Whipple moved that Payroll Warrants Nos. 1269 to 1272 inclusive in the

net amount of $31,738.31 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign
same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote: Commis-

sioners Bunker, Jarrett, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:
Commissioner Peccole.

SERVICE & MATERIAL	Commissioner Jarrett moved that Service and Material Warrant No. 2232 in the total
