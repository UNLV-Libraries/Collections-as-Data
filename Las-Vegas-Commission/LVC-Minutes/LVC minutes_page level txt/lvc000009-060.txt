Las Vegas, Nevada
April 1, 1954

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada
held this 1st day of April, 1954, was called to order at the hour of 4:30 P.M. by
His Honor Mayor Baker with the following members present:

Mayor	C. D. Baker

Commissioner	Rex A. Jarrett

Commissioner	Harris P. Sharp



City Manager	George Rounthwaite

Asst. City

Attorney	Ralston 0. Hawkins

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Absent:	Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

Commissioner Sharp moved the following Resolution be adopted:

BE IT RESOLVED, that the City Clerk be directed to issue a permit to the
Las Vegas New Car Dealers Association upon payment of $125.00 license fee, to
conduct an auction at Cashman Fields; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the requirements dealing with the hours of
conducting such sale, the posting of a bond, and investigation, be waived; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the requirement prohibiting gifts be waived,
so that a car may be given away.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Jarrett, Sharp, and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:
Commissioners Bunker and Whipple.

There being no further business to come before the Board at this time, the
meeting adjourned.
