Las Vegas, Nevada

March 8, 1956

(CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING)

A Special, meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,

Nevada held this 8th day of March, 1956 was called to order at the hour
of 4.30 P.M. by His Honor Mayor Baker with with the following members
present:

Mayor	C.D.Baker

Commissioner	E.W. Fountain

Commissioner	Harris P. Sharp

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

City Manager	A.H. Kennedy

City Engineer	George E.Wade

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Absent:	Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

Asst.City Mgr.	Robert W. Notti

Also Present:

Municipal Judge	S. George Gilson

Chief of Police	George W.Allen Jr.

Architect	Harry H. Whitely

At this time the Commission inspected the preliminary plans for the
expansion of the City Jail and Municipal Court with the Architect,

Harry H. Whitely.

Chief of Police George W. Allen and Municipal Judge S. George S. Gilson both
indicated their approval of the preliminary plans for this expansion.

Commissioner Sharp moved the preliminary plans for the expansion of
the City Jail and Municipal Court as submitted by the architect be
approved and the architect be notified in writing to proceed with the
final working drawings and specifications to be presented not later
than May 2, 1956 and the Director of Public Works be instructed to
set the date for advertising and opening of bids for this construction
on April 18, 1956.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Fountain, Sharp,Whipple and His Honor voting aye;

noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Bunker.

There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting
adjourned.

APPROVED:

ATTEST:		Mayor
