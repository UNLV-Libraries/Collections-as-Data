Captain Larsen of the Fire Department was present and stated that there is a 
problem on the recommendation of the City Manager as it pertains to the Fire 
Department. He indicated they definitely did not go along with this recommendation.

He advised that there were various meetings with the City Manager and the 
compromise has been explained to the Commission this evening. He further stated that
they felt they have given their position, and are still below the Police 
Department and do not see how they can be expected to back down and accept the City
Manager's recommendation.

At the hour of 9:35 P.M. Commissioner Sharp moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:	

Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:
Commissioner Whipple.
