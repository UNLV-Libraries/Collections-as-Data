DESERT SNACK BAR Restaurant Beverage License	At this time the investigation of the Police Department on Fred and Georgana Broadbent, applicants for a Restaurant Beverage license at the Desert Snack Bar, 222 North Main Street was presented to the Board for consideration. This report revealed that Mr. and Mrs. Broad- bent are not registered Voters in the State of Nevada.  Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved the Clerk notify Mr. and Mrs. Broadbent that since they do not meet the requirements for a liquor license, that they discontinue the sale of beer immediately.  Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote;Commissioners Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent Commissioner Bunker.
