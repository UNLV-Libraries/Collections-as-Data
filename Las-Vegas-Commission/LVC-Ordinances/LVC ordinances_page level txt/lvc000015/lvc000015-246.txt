ORDINANCE NO. 567

AN ORDINANCE CREATING A CITY PLANNING COMMISSION FOR THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA; PROVIDING FOR THE APPOINT-
MENT OF THE MEMBERS OF SAID COMMISSION; PRESCRIBING THEIR DUTIES; PROVIDING FOR OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED
THERETO; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

SECTION 1. There is hereby created the City Planning Commission of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada,
pursuant to Section 5063.02, N.C.L. 1943-1949 Supplemtn, to consist of nine members. Six members of said
Commission shall be appointed by the Mayor and Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas. The City
Engineer of the City of Las Vegas, one member of the Board of City Commissioners and City Manager shall be
ex-officio members of said Commission. All members of said Commission shall serve as such without compensation
excepting reasonable travel expenses made necessary in the fulfillment of their duties. All appointed members
of said Commission shall hold no other public office, except that one appointive member may be a member of the
zoning board of adjustment.

SECTION 2.	The term of	each appointed member	shall be six years or until his successor takes office

except	that the respective terms of five of the members	first appointed shall be one, two, three, four, and

five years. Members may be removed after public hearing, by a majority vote of the Board of City Commissioners:
for inefficiency, neglect of duty, or malfeasance in office. Vacancies occurring otherwise than through the
expiration of a term, shall be filled for the unexpired term.

SECTION 3.	It shall be	the function and duty	of the City Planning Commission to prepare and adopt

a comprehensive long	term general	plan for the physical	development of the City of Las Vegas. Such plan shall

be known as the Master Plan, and shall be so prepared that all or portions thereof may be adopted by the legis-
lative body as the basis for the development of the City, for such reasonable period of time next ensuing
after the adoption thereof as may be practically covered thereby. The Master Plan, with the accompanying map,
diagrams, charts, descriptive matter, and reports shall include the subject matter contained in Section
5063.07, N.C.L. 1943—1949 Supplement, as is appropriate to the City of Las Vegas, provided, however, that any
existing comprehensive long term general plan for the physical development of the City of Las Vegas may be
incorporated into the future plans of the City Planning Commission.

SECTION 4. The City Planning Commission of the City of Las Vegas shall elect annually a chairman
from its appointed members. It shall have power and authority to employ technicians, clerks, a secretary,
and to pay for their services and for such other expenses as may be necessary and proper. The City Manager
may appoint a Director of Planning or a Planning Engineer who shall be a department head and all employees
of said department shall be governed by any appropriate civil service laws pertaining to the City of Las Vegas,

SECTION 5. Any matters pertaining to Planning and Zoning which have been heretofore approved or
acted upon by the Regional Planning Commission of Clark County, Nevada, up to and including the effective date
of this ordinance are hereby ratified, confirmed and approved insofar as said actions pertain to property
within the City of Las Vegas.

SECTION 6. Upon the effective date of this ordinance, the City of Las Vegas shall withdraw its
membership in the Regional Planning Commission of Clark County, Nevada, and all of the provisions of Ordinance
No. 463 as pertain to the membership of the City of Las Vegas in said organization are hereby repealed.

SECTION 7. All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

SECTION 8. This ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its adoption and publication as in
the next section provided.

SECTION 9. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas shall
cause this Ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following its first
reading and adoption, in the Las Vegas Review Journal, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
