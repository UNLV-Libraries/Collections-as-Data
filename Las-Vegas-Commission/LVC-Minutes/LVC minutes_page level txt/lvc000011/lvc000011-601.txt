CLARK COUNTY SCHOOL	Acting City Manager Heath made a report to the Commission regarding various

DISTRICT PROPERTIES	properties owned by the Clark County School District which the City might

consider acquiring. Mr. Heath stated the only parcel of interest was that in
Held in Abeyance	Twin Lakes Subdivision No. 6, all of Block 1; however, in view of the fact Mr.

George Harris of the School District recently notified him that they are not
interested in completing the improvements that the City Manager's Office
recommended, it was respectfully suggested that the School District be informed
of the City's disinterest in such and all of the parcels indicated.

Commissioner Fountain moved this matter be held in abeyance for further study.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Elwell, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor Mayor Gragson
voting aye; noes, none.
