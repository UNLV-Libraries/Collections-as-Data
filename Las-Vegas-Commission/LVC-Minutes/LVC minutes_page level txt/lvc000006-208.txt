TAVERN LICENSE	Commissioner Baskin moved that the application of Andrew Finaly for

Turf Saloon	a retail tavern license at the Turf Saloon, 203 N. 1st Street be held

	in abeyance until further investigation can be made. Motion seconded

by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye. Noes, none.

RETAIL RESTAURANT	Commissioner Clark moved that the application of Nevada Pray for a

BEVERAGE APPLICATION retail restaurant beverage license at Cutright's Cafe, 1519 S. Main

	Street be approved. Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried

by the following vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple

and His Honor voting aye. Noes, none.

PINBALL MACHINES	Commissioner Whipple moved that the matter of the operation of pinball

machines in the City of Las Vegas be referred to the City Manager for
	handling. Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the

	following vote; Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His

Honor voting aye. Noes, none.
