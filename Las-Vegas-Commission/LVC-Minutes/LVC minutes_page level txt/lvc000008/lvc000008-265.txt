LEGISLATIVE MATTERS  Bill #37 Senate	Howard Cannon, City Attorney reported to the Board on Senate Bill No. 37 that is now pending before the legislature. Mr. Cannon explained that if this bill passes the City will be required to publish quarterly a statement of the finances of the City.  Commissioner Peccole moved the City Attorney be instructed to notify the Clark County Delegation that the Board of Commissioners are opposed to this Senate Bill No. 37.  Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes, none, Absent: Mayor Baker,
Assessmbly Bill Nos. 17 & 31  t	Discussion was held on Assembly Bill No. 17 which provided payment of a fee of $5.00 to witnesses subpoenaed for criminal cases, and Assembly Bill No. 31 providing for the transfer of funds from other funds upon the declaration of an emergency by the County Commissioners.  Commissioner Peccole moved the City Attorney be instructed to contact the Clark County delegation to the state legislature and make exception to Assembly Bill Nos. 17 and 31.  Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Mayor Baker.
