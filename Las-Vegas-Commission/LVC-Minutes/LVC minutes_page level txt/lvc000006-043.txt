Meeting called to order at the hour of 10 A.M. with the following
members present:	Commissioners Clark, Corradetti; Mayor E. W. Cragin;

City Manager, T. E. Fennessy; City Attorney C. Norman Cornwall; and
City Clerk Helen Scott Reed. Absent:	Commissioners Baskin and Bates.

RECREATION DEPARTMENT	Commissioner Clark moved that the City purchase a 1940 Ford two door

Car Purchase	sedan from the Westside USO in the sum of $315.00 and the Mayor and

City Clerk authorized to sign said warrant.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Corradetti and carried by the follow-
ing vote:	Commissioners Clark, Corradetti and His Honor voting aye;

noes, none. Absent:	Commissioners Baskin and Bates.

HARVEY DICKERSON CLAIM Commissioner Corradetti moved that the sum of $500.00 for legal work
Jondahl Case	done on the Jondahl Case be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk

authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Clark, Corradetti and His Honor voting aye;

noes, none. Absent:	Commissioners Baskin and Bates.

There being no further business to come before the meeting at this
time, this meeting adjourned.

March 22, 1947

At a regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners held this
22nd day of March, 1947. Meeting called to order by His Honor Mayor
E. W. Cragin at the hour of 10 A.M. with the following members
present Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Corradetti; City Manager T. E.
Fennessy; City Attorney C. Norman Cornwall; and City Clerk Helen
Scott Reed. Absent: Commissioner Bates.

MOTION RE:		Commissioner Clark moved that the Minutes of February 21, March 7

ASPHALT TILE BID	and 14, be approved with the exception of the motion relative to

	the letting of new bids for asphalt tile floor covering for the two

fire stations made by Commissioner Bates, seconded by Commissioner
Clark and unanimously carried at the March 7, 1947 meeting, which
motion is hereby rescinded.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the following
vote: Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Corradetti and His Honor voting
aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Bates.

PAYROLL CLAIMS	Commissioner Clark moved that Payroll claims Nos. 1098 to 1105

inclusive and 1107 in the net sum of $22,388.60 be approved and
the Mayor and City clerk be authorized to sign same.
