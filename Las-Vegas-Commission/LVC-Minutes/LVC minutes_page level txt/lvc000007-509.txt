At this time Commissioners Peccole and Whipple made a report to the Board on their in-
vestigation of the Planning and Zoning violations of John H. Stohr, at the location of
1516 Hastings Avenue.

Commissioner Peccole moved that the City Attorney be instructed to immediately take
  legal proceedings necessary to force the removal as a public nuisance of that part of
the building located at 1516 Hastings Avenue, and constructed in violation of the Plan-
ning and Zoning Ordinance, so it will conform to Planning and Zoning requirements.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote; Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioners Peccole and Whipple made their report and recommendation on the Planning
and Zoning violations of Richard Toleno at 2317 Santa Clara Drive and 2321 Santa Clara
Drive.

Thereafter Commissioner Peccole moved the City Attorney be instructed to immediately
take legal proceedings necessary to force the removal as a public nuisance of that
part of the building constructed in violation of the Plaining and Zoning Ordinance on
the properties located at 2317 and 2321 Santa Clara Drive, so that they will conform
  to Planning and Zoning requirements.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Peccole moved that inasmuch as Mr. Richard Toleno has been notified by letters
of May, 1951, June, 1951 and several letters in August, 1951 of his violation of Zoning
and Planning Ordinances insofar as his property located at 343 North 10th Street is con-
cerned, we wish to make the following recommendation:

That the City Attorney be authorized to notify Mr. Toleno that he is
requested to do one of two things—secure a Use Permit within a sixty
day period and if he cannot do that he be compelled to reconvert this
building into a duplex within the next sixty days, making a total of
one hundred twenty days.

Further that Mr. Toleno be advised that if he does not comply with the above request of
the Board the City Attorney shall take action to abate the same as a public nuisance.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Peccole moved that in the event Mr. Toleno does not follow the request of
the Board of Commissioners regarding the property at 343 North 10th Street he will be
required to show cause why his Contracting License should not be revoked.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
