LAS VEGAS, NEVADA
NOVEMBER 19, 1947

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO:	HELEN SCOTT REED, CITY CLERK

LAS VEGAS, NEVADA

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is hereby called to
be held in the Office of the Mayor at the War Memorial Building on Wednesday, November 19th,

1947 at the hour of 10:00 A.M., to act upon the following:

1.	Act on Resolution to County Commissioners re:	gasolene tax

2.	Consider request of Clark County Building Trades.

3.	Meeting with Public Housing Authorities.

4.	Police, Slark, Mohr.

/s/ E. W. Cragin______________

Mayor

NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING

TOP E. W. CRAGIN, Mayor and to C. R. CLARK, R. T. BASKIN, R. T. MOORE, REED WHIPPLE, Commissioners,
and J. M. MURPHY, City Manager of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

You, and each of you, will please take notice that pursuant to a call this day issued by Mayor
Cragin, a Special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada will
be held in the Office of the Mayor, at the War Memorial Building, on Wednesday, November 19,

1947 at the hour of 10:00 A.M. to act upon the following:

1.	Act on Resolution to County Commissioners re:	Gasoline Tax

2.	Consider request of Clark County Building Trades.

3.	Meeting with Public Housing Authorities.

4.	Police, Slark, Mohr.

KELSO TURNER	One of the purposes of the call of this Special Meeting was for

DISPOSITION	discussion of disposition and/or occupancy of Kelso-Turner Terrace

	and Addition in the City of Las Vegas. Representatives of the Army

including General McDaniel of Mather Field; and Mr. Redmond, Re-
presenting the Federal Housing Authority; also Mr. Berkeley L. Bunker,
