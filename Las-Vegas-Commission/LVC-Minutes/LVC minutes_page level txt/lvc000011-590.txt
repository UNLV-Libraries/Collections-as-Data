(CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING)

A Special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, held
this 15th day of October, 1959; was called to order at the hour of 1:30 P.M. with the
following members present:

Mayor	Oran K. Gragson	City Manager	A. H. Kennedy

Commissioner	William H. Elwell	Adm. Assistant	Robert D. Heath

Commissioner	E. W. Fountain

Absent:	Commissioner Sharp	Absent:	City Attorney Calvin Cory

Commissioner Whipple	(Attending Court on City

Case)

LIONS CLUB PARK	At this time members of the Service League and the Lions Clubs met to discuss with the

DEVELOPMENT	Commission the proposed development by the Service League to be located on a designated

PROPOSAL	part of the Lions Club Park and to be known as the "Service League Park." Plans for

the Lions Club Building for the Blind and the Art League's Gallery and Museum were also
Referred to Engr.	discussed. Attorney George Dickerson presented a proposed resolution from these groups

Dept.	outlining these proposals.

Joe Mast of the Engineering Department presented a drawing of the Lions Club Park area
which had been prepared in accordance with the proposed plans of these various agencies.

Commissioner Elwell moved the proposed resolution presented on behalf of the Lions Club,
the Service League and the Art League be received and referred to the Engineering 
Department with the instructions that final drawings covering the uses described in said
resolution be presented to the Board of City Commissioners at their regular meeting to be
held November 4, 1959.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:

Commissioners Elwell, Fountain and His Honor Mayor Gragson voting aye; noes, none.

Absent:	Commissioners Sharp and Whipple. Commissioner Fountain added that copies of

this final drawing should be sent to all the agencies concerned.
