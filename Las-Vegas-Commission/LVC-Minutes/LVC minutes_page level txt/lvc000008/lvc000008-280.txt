SUNRISE PARK TRACT Mr. Shanks was present and wished to compliment the Engineering Department for their help and
NO. 1, Blk 4 co-operation in this project. He requested the Engineering Department to make an estimate on
Sewer Mains this project and if the Board would be willing to put this on an assessment basis.

	Discussion was held on this matter.

Thereafter Commissioner Bunker moved the matter of the Sewer Mains in Sunrise Park Tract #1,
Blk. 4, be referred to the Engineering Department to make a survey and report to be turned in
as soon as possible.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote; Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner
Peccole.
