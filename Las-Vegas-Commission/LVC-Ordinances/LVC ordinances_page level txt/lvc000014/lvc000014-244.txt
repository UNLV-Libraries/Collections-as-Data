was published and posted in the manner and for the time required by law; and,

WHEREAS, at said general election duly held on the 8th day of May, 1945, pursuant

to said proclamation, there was submitted to the qualified electors, both property owners and

non-property owners, the following question:

Shall the Board of City Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas be empowered
to incur an obligation on behalf of the City of Las Vegas and be empowered
to issue in the name of the City of Las Vegas, One Hundred Forty Thousand
($140,000.00) Dollars of general obligation bonds for the construction of a
police station and facilities in the City of Las Vegas; said bonds to be in
denominations of One Thousand ($1,000.00) Dollars each and to bear interest
at the rate of not more than six per cent per annum from date of issuance
until date of redemption; and to be redeemed in twenty equal annual install-
ments, with the first installment to be redeemed one year from date of
issuance of said bonds.

AND, WHEREAS, thereafter on May 17, 1945, the Board of Commissioners of the City of

Las Vegas met for the purpose of canvassing the votes upon said question cast at the municipal

election held May 8, 1945, and did find and declare that the said question of issuing said

bonds for the purpose and in the amount aforesaid was carried by the approving vote of more

than a majority of the qualified voters, both property owners and non-property owners;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT ORDAINED BY THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS:

Section 1. That for the purpose of constructing a police station and necessary

facilities for said police station for the use of the City of Las Vegas pursuant to the powers

vested in the said city and its Board of Commissioners under the charter of said city and the

general laws of the State of Nevada, there be issued bonds of said city of Las Vegas designated

Las Vegas, Nevada, Police Station bonds in the aggregate principal amount of One Hundred Forty

Thousand Dollars ($140,000.00) consisting of 140 bonds in the denomination of $1,000.00 each,

bearing interest at the rate of not more than six per centum (6%) per annum, both principal

and interest being payable at the office of the City Treasurer in any coin or currency of the

United States of America, which on the respective dates of payment is legal tender for the

payment of public and private debts, all dated as of January 1, 1946, numbered consecutively



from 1 to 140, both numbers inclusive, and maturing serially in twenty equal annual install-
ments of Seven Thousand Dollars ($7,000.00) each, commencing January 1, 1947, to and including
January 1, 1966. Interest on said bonds shall be payable semi-annually commencing July 1,1946,
and on the first days of January and July in each year thereafter until the principal of said
bonds has been fully paid and discharged. Said bonds shall be signed by the Mayor of the City
of Las Vegas or Mayor Pro Tem of the City of Las Vegas, countersigned by the City Treasurer with
the seal of the City of Las Vegas affixed thereto, attested by the City Clerk or Deputy City
Clerk. Interest accruing on said bonds shall be evidenced by the proper number of semi-annual
interest coupons thereto attached bearing the signature or facsimile signature of the City
Treasurer, and when so executed, said bonds and appurtenant coupons shall be the binding
obligations of the City of Las Vegas according to their import.
