Las Vegas, Nevada
March 1, 1954

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas held this 1st
day of March, 1954 was called to order at the hour of 4:45 P.M. by His Honor Mayor Baker
with the following members present:

Mayor	C.D. Baker

Commissioner	Rex A. Jarrett

Commissioner	Harris P. Sharp

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

Acting City Mgr. George Rounthwaite
City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

Comptroller	Harold A. Laird

City Engineer	George Wade

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Absent:	Commissioner Wendell Bunker

ORDINANCE NO. 588 An ordinance entitled:	AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND ORDINANCE NO. 564 OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS

Condemned Buildings	ENTITLED: "AN ORDINANCE TO PROVIDE FOR THE REFUSAL TO ISSUE BUSINESS LICENSES OR FOR

	THE REVOCATION OF BUSINESS LICENSES IN CERTAIN INSTANCES; PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY

RELATED THERETO; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH,"
AND PROVIDING FOR EXTENSION OF TIME IN CERTAIN CASES, having been reported on favorably
by Commissioner Whipple, was read in full to the Board.

Commissioner Whipple moved the above entitled Ordinance No. 588 be adopted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Commissioner Jarrett passed his
vote:	Absent:	Commissioner Bunker
