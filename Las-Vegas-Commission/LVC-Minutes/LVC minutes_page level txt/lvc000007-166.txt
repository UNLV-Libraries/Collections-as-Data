Las Vegas, Nevada
September 7, 1950

A regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners held this 7th day
of September, 1950 was called to order at the hour of 3:25 P.M. by
His Honor Mayor E.W. Cragin with the following members present:
Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple; City Manager C.W.
Shelley, City Attorney Howard W. Cannon and City Clerk Shirley
Ballinger.

PAYROLL WARRANTS Commissioner Moore moved that Payroll Warrants Nos. 1147 to 1149
inclusive in the net sum of $24,634.60 be approved and the Mayor
	and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the fol-
lowing vote: Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and
His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

SERVICE & MATERIAL      Commissioner Whipple moved that Service & Material Warrants Nos.
WARRANTS	1994 to 2001 inclusive in the total amount of $34>903.27 be

	approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the fol-
lowing vote: Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and

His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

You and each of you will please take notice that pursuant to a call
this day issued by Mayor Cragin, a Special Meeting of the Board of
Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, will be held in the
Council Chambers at the City Hall, 5th & Stewart, on the 31st day of
August 1950, at the hour at 4:00 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Set Public Hearing Date on Rezoning - Govt. Lot 2 Sec 2, T 21 S., R 61 E.

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners held this 31st day of
August, 1950 was called to order at the hour of 4:10 P.M. by His
Honor Mayor R.W. Cragin with the following members present:

Commissioners Bunker,. Moore, Peccole, Whipple; City Attorney Howard
W. Cannon, City Manager C.W. Shelley and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger.

REZONING - Public	His Honor read the report of the Regional Planning Commission under date

Hearing	of August 23, 1950 concerning the petition of amendment to the Land

Date Set	Use Plan changing the zone in Government Lot 2, Section 2, T. 21 S.,

R 61 E., from R-l to R-4, and the said petition submitted to the Planning
Commission by the owner of the property, Mr. D.G. Lorenzi.

His Honor also read a Notice of Appeal of the denial of the foregoing
petition filed with the City Clerk August 30, 1950 by D.G. Lorenzi and
O.H. Nelson.

Thereafter Commissioner Moore moved that the public hearing on this
matter be set for September 19, 1950 at the hour of 8:00 P.M.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor

voting aye; noes, none.

There being no further business to come before this special meeting, the
meeting adjourned.
