Section 17. If any part, provision or section of this Ordinance or the application
thereof to any person or circumstances shall be held to be invalid by any court of compe-
tent jurisdiction, the remainder of this ordinance or the application of such part, pro-
vision or section thereof to any other person or circumstances shall not be affected
thereby.

Section 18. This Ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its publication
as in the next section provided, and final passage.

Section 19. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas shall cause this Ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks
immediately following its first reading and adoption, in the Las Vegas Evening Review
Journal, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
