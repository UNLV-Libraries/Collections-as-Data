A RESOLUTION COVERING ANNEXATION PROCEDURE.

WHEREAS, the Charter of the City of Las Vegas provides for annexation of lands
under certain conditions; and

WHEREAS, it is the desire of the Board of Commissioners to adopt a uniform pro-
cedure in relation thereto; and

WHEREAS, no fees therefor have been established and it is desirable to fix fees,
to cover the expenses of the City incurred in annexation proceedings;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas, that the filing fees for the Petition for Annexation shall be as
follows:

a.	A filing fee of twenty-five ($25.00) dollars shall be charged
for each petition containing lands of forty (40) acres or less.

b.	An additional fifteen ($15.00) dollars shall be paid by the applicant
for each additional forty (40) acres or fraction thereof.

c.	Provided that no fee shall be charged for the annexation of any pro-
perty in which the City of Las Vegas shall have been the petitioner.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the following procedural steps shall govern:

a.	The Petition for Annexation shall be filed with the City Clerk
and shall be accompanied by a plat of the proposed land to be annexed, on linen,
signed by a registered engineer.

b.	The City Clerk shall notify the City Manager and department heads
by memorandum that an application is on file and refer the application and plat to
the Planning Department.

c.	The Planning Department shall check the land use and zoning, secure
additional prints, if needed, from the Engineering Department, and compile informa-
tion and recommendation from the Department of Public Works, covering utilities,
drainage, topography, etc., and shall refer the application to other city departments
for appropriate recommendations. The Planning Department thereupon shall coordinate
the recommendations of the various departments and present the matter to the Planning
Commission for approval or disapproval. Upon approval by the Planning Commission,
the petition shall be referred to the City Attorney for legal study and comments,
and upon completion thereof the City Clerk shall transmit the file and recommenda-
tions to the City Manager.

d.	The City Manager shall place the matter on the Agenda for the Board
of City Commissioners for their consideration. Upon tentative approval by the
Board of City Commissioners, the City Attorney shall prepare an annexation ordinance
and present to the Board of City Commissioners for their action.

e.	Upon adoption of annexing ordinance by the Board of City Commissioners,
a plat of the land to be annexed shall be filed with the County Recorder of Clark
County by the City Engineer who shall notify the Director of Finance so that license
fees, utility charges, etc. can be established.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that this Resolution shall take effect immediately on adoption
by the Board of Commissioners.
