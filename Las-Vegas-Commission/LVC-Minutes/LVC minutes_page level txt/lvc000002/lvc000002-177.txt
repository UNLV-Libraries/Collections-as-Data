Thereupon, it appearing to the Board that J.F. Hesses has received a majority of all votes
cast for the office of Mayor of the City of Las Vegas, be it THEREFORE RESOLVED, on motion of
Commissioner Anderson, seconded by Commissioner Elwell, that the said J.F. Hesse be and he is
hereby declared to be the duly elected Mayor of the City of Las Vegas for the ensuing two
years, and be it further RESOLVED that the city clerk be and she is hereby directed to issue
a certificate of election to the said J.F. Hesse. Voting on said Resolution, Commissioners
Anderson, Elwell, and Matzdorf, and His Honor the Mayor, Aye. Noes, none.

It appearing to the Board that W.B. Mundy has received the majority of all votes
cast for the office of Commissioner of the First Class of the City of Las Vegas, be it
THEREFORE RESOLVED on motion of Commissioner Elwell, seconded by Commissioner Matzdorf, that
the said W.B. Mundy be and Re is hereby declared to be the duly elected Commissioner of the
First Class of the City of Las Vegas for the ensuing two years, and be it further RESOLVED
that the city clerk be and she is hereby directed to issue a certificate of election to the
said W.B. Mundy. Voting on said Resolution; Commissioners Anderson, Elwell, Matzdorf and His
Honor the Mayor, Aye. Noes, none.
