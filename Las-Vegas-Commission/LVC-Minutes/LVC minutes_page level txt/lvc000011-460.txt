GOLFRIDGE TERRACE #1	At the regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners held May 6, 195, the

Tentative Map and	tentative map for Golfridge Terrace #1 submitted by Louis Miranti, was approved 

Agreement	subject to the conditions that Mr. Miranti submit a bond sufficient in amount to assure

an adequate water supply for a period of at least 10 years, together with letters
Approved	from the State Engineer and the Public Service Commission stating authority and

permission to operate a private water company.

At this time City Attorney Calvin Cory stated that this type of bond could not be
procured and presented an agreement signed by Louis Miranti, President, American
Homes, Inc., guaranteeing adequate water supply to the residents of Las Vegas
Square and Golfridge Terrace #1 Subdivisions as this water system serves both of
these subdivisions. He added that under this agreement the City will hold title
of the water system as security for furnishing water to these residents, and in
the event sufficient water was not being furnished the City wculd be empowered to
transfer the ownership to the Las Vegas Valley Water District.

Commissioner Sharp moved that the tentative map for Golfridge Terrace #1 be 
approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign this agreement.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:	

Commissioners Fountain, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes,
none. Absent:	Mayor C. D. Baker.
