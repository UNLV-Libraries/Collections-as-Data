installments shall bear interest not to exceed seven per cent (7%) per annum, payable semi-annually, in the man-
ner hereinafter directed, on the whole amount due and unpaid. Such installments and the interest thereon shall
be and remain a lien on the said lots and parcels of land until paid in full, and shall be collected and paid
in the following manner:

on and against the several owners and premises, and the County Assessor of Clark County, acting Ex-Officio As-
sessor of the City of Las Vegas, is hereby authorized and directed to enter the same on the said tax roll of
Clark County and to extend the same in a special column for special assessments on the said tax roll of Clark
