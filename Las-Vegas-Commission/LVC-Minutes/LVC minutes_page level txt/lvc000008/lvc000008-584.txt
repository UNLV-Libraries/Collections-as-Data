ORDINANCE NO. 582  Annexation  	At this time Commissioner Bunker member of the committee for recommendation on the proposed annexation ordinance reported favorably.  Thereafter an Ordinance entitled: AN ORDINANCE ANNEXING TO, AND MAKING A PART OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, CERTAIN SPECIFICALLY DESCRIBED TERRITORY ADJOINING AND CONTIGUOUS TO THE CORPORATE LIMITS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; DECLARING SAID TERRITORY AND INHABITANTS TO BE ANNEXED THERETO AND SUBJECT TO ALL LAWS AND ORDINANCES; ORDERING A PLAT SHOWING SAID TERRITORY TO BE RECORDED IN THE OFFICE OF THE COUNTY RECORDER OF THE COUNTY OF CLARK, STATE OF NEVADA; AND PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO, was read in full to the Board.  Commissioner Bunker moved the above entitled Ordinance No. 582 be adopted with the condition that Parcel 6 of the above entitled Ordinance include THE NE 1/4 SEC. 25, T 20 S, R 60 E.  Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried: Absent: Commissioner Sharp.  Commissioner Sharp returned to the meeting at this time.
ORDINANCE NO. 583 Parking Meter Zones	At this time Commissioners Bunker and Jarrett members of the committee for recommendation on the proposed ordinance for parking meter zones, reported favorably.  Thereafter an ordinance entitled: AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND CHAPTER 36, SECTION 116, CODE OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA AS AMENDED BY ORDINANCE NO. 396, AND ORDINANCE NO. 429 BY EXTENDING PARKING METER ZONES WITHIN THE CITY; PROVIDING FOR OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO; AND RE- PEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH, was read in full to the Board.  Commissioner Jarrett moved the above entitled Ordinance No. 583 be adopted.  Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote: Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Commissioner Sharp passed his vote.
