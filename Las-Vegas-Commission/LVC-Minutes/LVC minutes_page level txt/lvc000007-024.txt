Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved that the resignation of Chief Malburg be accepted,
effective immediately. Further that any accrued vacation time be allowed.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following votes Commissioners
Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

There being no further business to come before this special meeting, the meeting adjourned.
