WILLIAM PECCOLE, Commissioner

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
held this 29th day of October, 1951 was called to order at the hour of 4:30
P.M. by His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple with the following members present:

Mayor Pro Tem	Reed Whipple

Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

Commissioner	William Peccole

City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

City Manager	C. W. Shelley

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Comptroller	Harold A. Laird

Absent:	Mayor	C. D. Baker

Commissioner	Rex A. Jarrett

PAYROLL WARRANT	Commissioner Bunker moved that Payroll Warrant No. 1241 in the net amount of

$1,157.74 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting
aye; noes, none. Absent: Mayor Baker and Commissioner Jarrett.

SERVICE & MATERIAL WARRANTS Commissioner Bunker moved that Service and Material Warrant No. 2193 in
the total amount of $1,214.83 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk
	authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting
aye; noes, none. Absent: Mayor Baker and Commissioner Jarrett.

CLAIM - Ben Quintana	At this time the claim of Ben Quintana in the amount of $21.00 was pre-

	sented to the Board for their consideration.

Commissioner Peccole moved action on this claim be held in abeyance until
the next regular meeting of the Board on November 7, 1951.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting
aye; noes, none. Absent: Mayor Baker and Commissioner Jarrett.

There being no further business to come before the meeting at this time,
Commissioner Peccole moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following tote:
Commissioners Bunker, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting
aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker and Commissioner Jarrett.
