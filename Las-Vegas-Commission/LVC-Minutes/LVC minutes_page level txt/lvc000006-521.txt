GAMING APPLICATIONS Commissioner Bunker moved the application of Robert J. Kost, Kost's Mobile Service Station

at Main & Stewart Streets for one slot machines license for the 4th quarter of 1949 be granted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Whipple.

Commissioner Peccole moved that the application of Robert Cardinal and J. Emmett Sullivan,
Knipp's Service Station, 10th & Fremont for one additional slot machine license for the
4th quarter of 1949 be granted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Whipple.
