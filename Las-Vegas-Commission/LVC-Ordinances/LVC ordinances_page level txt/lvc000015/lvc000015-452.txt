ORDINANCE NO. 697

AN ORDINANCE AMENDING THE UNIFORM BUILDING CODE AND OTHER SPECIALIZED CODES DEALING WITH THE CONSTRUCTION
INDUSTRY; PROVIDING FOR AN ADDITIONAL FEE IN THE EVENT OF SECOND INSPECTIONS REQUIRED; AND OTHER MATTERS
PROPERLY RELATED THERETO.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

SECTION 1. In the event construction work should receive a stop work order placed thereon by
the Building Department for a violation of the provisions of the Uniform Building Code or any other
specialized code dealing with the construction industry, or a failure to comply with the plans and speci-
fications on file, the person or persons to whom the permit for such construction or installation was is-
sued shall pay an additional fee to the City of Las Vegas as follows:

(a)	10% of the amount of the initial payment fee; provided however, a maximum fee of $50.00
per stop work order and a minimum fee of $10.00 per stop work order.

SECTION 2. If the stop work order is issued by the City and it develops that such stop work
order was improperly issued or that there was no violation of the Uniform Building Code or other specialized
codes, fees provided for in Section 1 shall not be paid, or if paid, shall be refunded.

SECTION 3. All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith sure hereby expressly re-
pealed.

SECTION 4. This ordinance shall be in full force and effect as in the next section provided,
and final passage.

SECTION 5. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
shall cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following its
first reading and adoption in the Las Vegas Review Journal, a daily newspaper published in the City of
Las Vegas.

APPROVED:

/s/ C.D. BAKER

ATTEST:	C.D. BAKER, Mayor

/s/ SHIRLEY BALLINGER

SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk
