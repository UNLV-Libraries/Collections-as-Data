BE IT RESOLVED, by the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada,
in a regular meeting on the 7th day of March, 1956, having considered the ap-
plication of the Southern Nevada Power Company for a use permit to install an
electric power substation on Lots 11 through 13 of Block 5, Clark's Las Vegas
Townsite; and

The Board having considered the recommendation of the Board of Adjustment
approving this application subject to compliance with the architectural
sketches submitted and all known devices to reduce the safety hazards;

NOW, THEREFORE, it is the decision of this Board that the application of the
Southern Nevada Power Company for a use permit to install an electric power
substation on Lots 11 through 13 of Block 5, Clark's Las Vegas Townside be
and the same hereby is approved subject to the conditions set forth by the
Board of Adjustment

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes,
none. Commissioner Sharp stated that the Southern Nevada Power Company
is a client of his, and passed his vote.

The Commission considered the application of the First Lutheran Church of
the Good Shepherd for variance to construct an addition to an existing church ,
with the structure designed in a manner which required a variance from the
open space requirements, on property described as Irregular Lots 1 through
10, Block 9; Pioneer Heights and Irregula Lots 1 through 5, Block 4, Ladd's
Addition, known as 301 Maryland Parkway. The Board of Adjustment approved
this application subject to an eight foot setback on the side yard and the
installation of all offSite improvements.

His Honor asked if there were any objections. None were voiced.

Commissioner Bunker moved the recommendation of the Board of Adjustment
be accepted and the following resolution adopted:
