The City Manager recommended that Bid No. 57.24 for the furnishing of three (3)
motorcycles be awarded to Knerr's Las Vegas Harley Davidson Sales in an amount
not to exceed $5,180.96, inasmuch as there was only one bid, and they are the
only firm able to furnish American made motorcycles. Mr. Kennedy explained, how-
ever, the bid called for 1958 model motorcycles, and the bidder advises there
will be a price increase on the 1958 model of not more than 7½% over the present
price of the 1957 model which would be $4,819.50;the bidder further advised that
inasmuch as the 1958 models are not yet available, his bid is submitted at the
price not to exceed $5,180.96.

Commissioner Sharp moved that inasmuch as there was only one bid on Bid No. 57.24,
and they are the only bidder able to furnish American made motorcycles, that the
bid of Knerr's Las Vegas Harley Davidson Sales for three (3) motorcycles, 1958 models,
not to exceed $5,180.96, be accepted and the award made.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:	Commis-
sioners Bunker, Fountain and Sharp voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker and

Commissioner Whipple.
