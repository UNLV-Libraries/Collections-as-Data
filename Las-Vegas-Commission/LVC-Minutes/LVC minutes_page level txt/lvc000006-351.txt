MINUTES	Commissioner Baskin moved that the minutes of November 5, 1948 be corrected as follows:

	The committee on proposed ordinance No. 373 relative to the Solicitation of Funds in

the City of Las Vegas, composed of Commissioners Baskin and Clark reported that the
ordinance in its present form was not acceptable, and recommended that interested
parties meet with the City Attorney to see if an ordinance could be prepare to
meet with the approval of all concerned.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioners Moore and Whipple.

Thereafter Commissioner Baskin moved that the minutes of November 5, 1948 as corrected and
the minutes of November 10, 1948 be approved by reference and the Mayor and City Clerk author-
ized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners Baskin,

Clark and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioners Moore and Whipple.
