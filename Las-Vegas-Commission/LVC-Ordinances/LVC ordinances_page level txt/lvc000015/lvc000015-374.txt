ORDINANCE NO. 646

AN ORDINANCE ANNEXING TO, AND MAKING A PART OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, CERTAIN SPECIFICALLY DESCRIBED
TERRITORY ADJOINING AND CONTIGUOUS TO THE CORPORATE LIMITS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; DECLARING SAID
TERRITORY AND INHABITANTS TO BE ANNEXED THERETO AND SUBJECT TO ALL LAWS AND ORDINANCES; ORDERING A
PLAT SHOWING SAID TERRITORY TO BE RECORDED IN THE OFFICE OF THE COUNTY RECORDER OF THE COUNTY OF CLARK,
STATE OF NEVADA; AND PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

SECTION 1. Pursuant to Petitions duly presented to the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las
Vegas, signed by a majority of the freeholders in the area containing each of the hereinafter described
parcels of real property requesting the Board of City Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas to annex
to and make a part of the City of Las Vegas each of the hereinafter described parcels of real property,
the exterior boundaries of the City of Las Vegas are hereby extended to annex to, and include therein,
each of the following described parcels of real property, to-wit:

PARCEL NO. 1

SE¼ of Section 6, Township 21 South, Range 61 East, M,D,B, & M, Clark County, Nevada.

PARCEL NO. 2

That portion of the Southeast Quarter (SE¼) of Section 1, Township 21 South, Range 6l East, M.D.B. & M.,
described as follows:	Commencing at the Northwest corner of the Southeast Quarter (SE¼) of said Section

1; thence South 0°28,25” East along the West line thereof a distance of 59.64 feet to a point; thence
South 42°27' East a distance of 1573.11 feet to the most Southerly corner of that certain parcel of land
conveyed by Murray Wollman et al to Orra F. Johnson and Wilma L. Buecker by deed recorded January 18,

1951 as Document No. 361250, Clark County, Nevada records, the true point of beginning; thence continu-
ing South 42°27' East a distance of 700.00 feet to a point; thence North 47°33' East a distance of 300.00
feet to a point on the Southwesterly right of way line of U.S. Highway Nos. 93-95-466 (200 feet wide);
thence North 42°27' West a distance of 700.00 feet to the most Easterly comer of the said conveyed
parcel; thence South 47°33’ West a distance of 300.00 feet to the true point of beginning.

TOGETHER with the tenements, property and inhabitants within each of said parcels of real property
and each of said parcels of real property is hereby declared to be apart of the City of Las Vegas.

SECTION 2. Each of said parcels of real property, together with the tenements and inhabitants
thereof, shall be subject to all of the laws applicable to the City of Las Vegas.

SECTION 3. The City Engineer of the City of Las Vegas is hereby instructed to prepare a plat of
each of said territories described to Section 1 of this Ordinance and to file the same for record in the
office of the County Recorder of Clark County, Nevada.

SECTION 4. This ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its final passage and publication
as in the next section provided.

SECTION 5. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas shall
cause this Ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following its its
first reading and adoption in the Las Vegas Sun, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.

/s/	C.D. BAKER

ATTEST:	Mayor

/s/ ShIRLEY BALLINGER
City Clerk
