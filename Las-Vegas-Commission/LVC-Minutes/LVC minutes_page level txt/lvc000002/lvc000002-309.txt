OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS,
CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA. January 17, 1928.

At an adjourned regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas, Clark Clark County, Nevada, held on the 17th day of January, 1928, at the hour of three
o'clock P. M. Present: Hon. J. F. Hesse, Mayor, Commissioners W. B. Mundy, W. C. German,

O. J. Smith, together with the City Attorney and Clerk. Absent: Commissioner Roy Neagle.

Meeting called to order by the Mayor.

At this time the Board was engaged in a discussion of the question of altering a
certain section of the sewer system of the City of Las Vegas.

The Board also discussed the question of cleaning the said sewer system.

Mr. J. P. King, City Street Superintendent, was present and discussed the above
mentioned questions with the Board.

No further business appearing it was ordered upon motion duly made that this meeting
adjourn until Wednesday the 18th day of January, 1928, at the hour of three o'clock P. M.
