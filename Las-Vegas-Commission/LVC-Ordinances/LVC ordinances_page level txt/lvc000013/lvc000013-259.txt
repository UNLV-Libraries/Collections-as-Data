THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS is hereby authorized and
empowered to issue in the name of said City Forty (40) bonds of the denomination of One
Thousand Dollars ($1,000.00) each, and Ten (10) bonds of the denomination of Nine
Hundred Fifty-nine, and 51/100 Dollars ($959.51), Four (4) of said bonds of the denomination
 of One Thousand Dollars ($1,000.00) and One (l) bond of the denomination of

Nine Hundred Fifty-nine and 51/100 Dollars ($959.51) each, payable at annual periods
of from one to ten years from January 1, 1930, each bearing interest at the rate of not
to exceed seven per cent, per annum; said interest to be payable annually on the first
day of January of each year; which said bonds shall be sold to the bidder making the

best bid, but shall not be sold for less than par value and accrued interest.

Section 2. The Board of Commissioners of said City shall cause said bonds to
be prepared and they shall be signed by the mayor or Mayor Pro Tem, and the City Clerk,
and countersigned by the City Treasurer and authenticated by the seal of the City of
Las Vegas. Coupons representing the several installments of interest to fall due thereon
shall be attached to each bond so that they may be removed, without injury to the same,
numbered consecutively, and bear the signature or facsimile signature of the Mayor or
Mayor Pro Tem and countersignature of the City Treasurer. Said bonds shall be dated
January 1, A.D. 1930, and shall be sold at not less than their face or par value and
accrued interest, and the proceed thereof shall be placed in the General Fund of the
City of Las Vegas, to replace the money heretofore paid out of said General Fund for
making said improvement, by reason of the non-payment of certain Special assessments
by certain of the property owners at the time required by law. The said bonds shall be
known as and called "Las Vegas Special 1930 Pavement Improvement Bonds, Series C."

Section 5. The amount remaining unpaid of said Special Assessments for which
these bonds are to be issued, as shown by the assessment Roll on file in the office
of the City Clerk of the City of Las Vegas appears to be Forty-Nine Thousand Five Hundred
 ninety-five and 51/100 Dollars ($49,595.51), and said bonds and the said annual
installments due and to become due thereon for Ten (10) annual periods commencing
January 1, 1931, are and shall be a lien upon the lots and parcels of land hereinafter
particularly described, for the amounts of the assessments due thereon, together with
interest at the rate not to exceed seven (7%) per cent per annum from the first day of
January, 1930, until said bonds are paid, the exact amount of interest on said bonds
to be fixed and determined at the rate offered by the successful bidder on said bonds,
