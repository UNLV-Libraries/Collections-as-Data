September 18, 1953

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO: SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk
Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada is
hereby called to be held in the Mayor's office of the City Hall, on the 18th day of
September, 1953 at the hour of 9:00 A.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Payroll Warrants Nos. 1490 thru 11.95 inclusive in the total amount of $39,360.59.

2.	Service & Material Warrants Nos. 50136 to 50139 inclusive in the total amount of
$3,692.11.

s/ C.D. BAKER
Mayor
