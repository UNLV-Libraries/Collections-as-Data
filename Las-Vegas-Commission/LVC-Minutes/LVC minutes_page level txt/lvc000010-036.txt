At this time the Board considered the application of Martin J. Cunningham for a Restaurant
Beverage license at Cunningham's Chili Spot, 225 North 1st Street.

Mr. Cunningham was advised that his Building was in violation of the Building
Code and could not meet the requirements for a liquor license at this time.

Commissioner Fountain moved that Martin J. Cunningham's application for a Restaurant
Beverage liquor license at Cunningham's Chili Spot, 225 North 1st Street, be held
in abeyance until his building is in compliance with the Building Code.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Com-

missioners Bunker, Fountain, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Absent:	Commissioner Sharp.
