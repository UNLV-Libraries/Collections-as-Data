Las Vegas, Nevada
October 17, 1952

A recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada
this 17th day of October, 1952 was called to order at the hour of 3:00 P.M. by His Honor Mayor
C.D. Baker with the following members present:

Mayor C.D. Baker
Commissioner Wendell Bunker
Commissioner William Peccole

City Clerk Shirley Ballinger
Absent Commissioner Rex A. Jarrett
Commissioner Reed Whipple
City Attorney Howard W. Cannon
Asst. City Mgr. Dora Lord
City Manager Chet Shelley

Commissioner Peccole moved that Payroll Warrants Nos. 1353 to 1358 inclusive in the net amount
of $35,133.06 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker,
Peccole and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent Commissioner Jarrett and Whipple.

Commissioner Peccole moved that Service and Material Warrant No. 2332 in the total amount of
$6668.31 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker,
Peccole and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent Commissioners Jarrett and Whipple.

There being no further business to come before the Board at this time Commissioner Bunker,
moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker,
Peccole and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioners Jarrett & Whipple.

er City Clerk

APPROVED:

D.D. BAKER, MAYOR
