ACCEPTANCE OF SERVICE

We, the undersigned Mayor and Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada
hereby admit due service of the foregoing notice of Special Meeting.

HOWELL C. GARRISON , Mayor	A. P. Rubidoux________Commissioner

M. C. Tinch Commissioner	A. F. Smith, Sr.______Commissioner

Pat Clark_____Commissioner

Commissioner Smith moved that Commissioner Clark act as Mayor of this Special
Meeting, due to the absence of Mayor Garrison. Motion seconded by Commissioner Rubidoux and
carried by the following vote. Commissioners Smith, Tinch, and Rubidoux, voting aye. Noes,
none.

At the hour of 11:00 A. M. the meeting was called to order by Mayor pro tem,

Clark with the following members present. Commissioners Smith and Rubidoux and Tinch.

City Clerk, Helen Scott.

Commissioner Rubidoux moved the approval of the plat for Builtmore Annex No. 1
subject to the approval of the city engineer. The Mayor and city clerk to sign same
when said approval is secured. Motion seconded by Commissioner Smith and carried by
the following vote. Commissioners Smith, Tinch, Rubidoux and His Honor the Mayor
Pro Tem voting aye. Noes, none.

 The city engineer made the following recommendation. "that the plat of Huntridge
Subdivision Tract No. 2 be approved subject to the presentation of a bond to be released
upon the satisfactory construction of the required monuments, and that this bond be
extended to cover Tract I, of this subdivision since in the construction of the sewer
lines many of the monuments in this tract were destroyed and will have to be replaced".

Commissioner Smith moved that the recommendation of the city engineer be approved.
Motion seconded by Commissioner Rubidoux and carried by the following vote. Commissioners
Smith, Tinch, Rubidoux and Mayor Pro Tem Clark voting aye. Noes, none.

 Commissioner Rubidoux moved that the plat of Huntridge Subdivision Tract No. 2 be
APPROVED and the Mayor and city clerk authorized to sign same. Motion seconded by
Commissioner Tinch and carried by the following vote. Commissioners Smith, Tinch,

Rubidoux and the Mayor Pro tem voting aye. Noes, none.

Commissioner Rubidoux moved that the plat of the Scotch Eighty Tract be approved.
Motion seconded by Commissioner Smith and carried by the following vote. Commissioners
Smith, Tinch, Rubidoux and the Mayor Pro Tem voting aye. Noes, none.

Commissioner Smith moved that this meeting adjourn. Motion seconded by Commissioner
Tinch and carried by the following vote. Commissioners Smith, Tinch, Rubidoux and the
Mayor pro tem voting aye. Noes, none.
