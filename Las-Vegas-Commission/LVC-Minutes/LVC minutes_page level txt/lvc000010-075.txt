CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

Date: December 5, 1955

To:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board, of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, is hereby called to be
held in the Mayor's Office of the City Hall, on the 5th day of December, 1955; at the hour of 4.30 P.M. to
consider the following matters:

1.	USE PERMIT (U-37-55) - Application of Las Vegas Bowl to conduct a restaurant and tavern
in connection with a bowling alley at 1825 South 5th Street.

2.	Liquor and Gaming Applications for Las Vegas Bowl, Inc.

/s/ REED WHIPPLE__________

Mayor Pro Tem
