Las Vegas, Nevada
February 9, 1951

A recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners was called to order at the
hour of 3:30 P.M. by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin with the following members present:
Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple; City Manager C. W. Shelley and Chief
Deputy City Clerk Betty Funston. Also present was Acting Mayor Dewey Jones of
Troop No. 66 of the Boy Scouts. Absent: City Attorney Howard W. Cannon and City

Clerk Shirley Ballinger.

	pany in the amount of $l,544.95.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote: Commis-

sioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
