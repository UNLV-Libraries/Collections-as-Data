Thereupon, the following Resolution was adopted by the Board upon motion of Commissioner O.J.
Smith, seconded by Commissioner W. B. Mundy:

BE IT RESOLVED that the said Budget be signed in behalf of this Board by the Mayor and attested
by the City Clerk and the sane be filed with the City Clerk and a copy thereof published in a newspaper
published in the City of Las Vegas, for two publications, one week apart. The vote on said Resolution
was as follows: Commissioners Mundy, Smith, German, Neagle and His Honor, the Mayor, Aye. Noes; None.

The Board then proceeded to take up the matter of the levy of taxes for the City of Las Vegas for
