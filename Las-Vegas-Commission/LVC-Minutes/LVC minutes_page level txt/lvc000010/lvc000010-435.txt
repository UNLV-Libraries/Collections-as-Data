PROCLAMATION

WHEREAS, it appears that the Charter of the City of Las Vegas provides that a
General Election be held on the first Tuesday after the first Monday in May, 1957, in the
City of Las Vegas, for the election at large by the qualified voters of the City of two
Commissioners of the second and fourth class, respectively, a City Attorney, and a Judge
of the Municipal Court,

NOW, THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED by the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas, that such General Election be held in the City of Las Vegas on May 7, 1957; at
which election there shall be elected at large by the qualified voters of said City two
Commissioners, one of the second class and one of the fourth class, who shall be elected
and hold office for a period of four years and until their successors are elected and
qualified; a City Attorney, who shall be elected and hold office for. a period of four years
and until his successor shall be elected and qualified; and a Judge of the Municipal Court,
who shall be elected and hold office for a period of four years and until his successor
shall be elected and qualified; and a Judge of the Municipal Court, who shall be elected
and hold office for a period of four years and until his successor shall be elected and
qualified, and

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED, that the places in said City of holding said election are
