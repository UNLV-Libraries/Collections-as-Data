Las Vegas, Nevada
October 15, 1952

A regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas held this 15th day
of October, 1952, called to order at the hour of 7:30 P.M. by His Honor Mayor C.D. Baker
with the following members present:

Mayor C.D. Baker
Commissioner Wendell Bunker
Commissioner Rex A. Jarrett
Commissioner William Peccole
Commissioner Reed Whipple

City Attorney Howard Cannon
City Manager Chet Shelley
Asst. City Mgr. Dora Lord
City Engineer C.C. Boyer

Commissioner Whipple moved the Minutes of October 1 and 6, 1952 be approved by reference
and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote; Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Bunker moved that Service & Material Warrants Nos. 2327 to 2331 in the total
amount of $29,574.84 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote; Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

This being the time set for the Public Hearing on the vacation of a portion of Westwood
Drive extending south from Park Circle to Waldman Drive, His Honor asked if there were
any protestants present. No objections were voiced or filed.

Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved the recommendation of the Regional Planning Commission
that this street be vacated be approved and the following order of vacation adopted;

ORDER

A Petition, dated the 28th day of August, 1952, signed by three (3)
freeholders residing in the area affected, having been filed with the Clerk of
this Board, petitioning for the vacation of certain streets and parts of streets
in Westwood Drive hereinafter described, and said Petition having been by Order
of this Board referred to the Planning Commission of the City of Las Vegas for
its recommendation in the premises, and said Planning Commission having filed its
report, dated August 28, 1952, with this Board approving and recommending said
vacation;

And this Board, by an order made at its regular meeting held on the 3rd day of
September, 1952, set the 15th day of October, 1952, at the hour of 7:30 o'clock
P.M. at the Commissioners' Room of the Board of Commissioners, in the War Memorial
Building, located at the Northwest Corner of Fifth and Stewart Streets, in the City
of Las Vegas, Clark County, Nevada, as the time and place for the Public Hearing on
said Petition and recommendation, and Ordered the City Clerk to cause the aforesaid
streets and parts of streets proposed to be vacated to be posted with a Notice setting
forth the time and place of the Public Hearing and the extent of the proposed vacation;
