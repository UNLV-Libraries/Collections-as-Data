RADIO EQUIPMENT	Commissioner Moore moved the City Manager be instructed to purchase

	necessary radio equipment for the Police Department.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Moore, peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes,
none.

SALARY CHANGE	Commissioner Whipple moved the recommendation of the City Manger on salary

adjustments be approved with the exception of William Hartman, whose salary
	is to remain sis it is, these changes to be effective May 1, 1950.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting "aye";
noes, none.

HELLDORADO BEAUTY Commissioner Peccole moved that $50.00 be appropriated for the City's entry
PARADE	in the Helldorado Beauty Parade.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes,
none

There being no further business to come before the meeting at this time,
Commissioner Moore moved this meeting adjourn until May 5, 1950 at 3:00 P.M.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting "aye"; noes,
none.
