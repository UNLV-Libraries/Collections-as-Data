PROCLAMATION

WHEREAS, the Board of City Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas did on
January 6, 1945 proclaim the need for increased recreational facilities for the
citizens of the community, particularly the children and

WHEREAS, it was deemed necessary at that time to construct two (2) swimming
pools for the City of Las Vegas, to meet in part the need for these recreation
facilities, and

WHEREAS, there has been authorized by ordinance duly enacted by the Board
of City Commissioners, the issuance of $160,000 of general obligation bonds for the
construction of said swimming pools in the City of Las Vegas, and

WHEREAS, the increase in construction costs resulted in the committal or
expenditure of the entire amount of said bond issue of $160,000 for the swimming
pools, leaving no provision for financing the cost of constructing the bathhouse
facilities required to serve the users of the swimming pools, and

WHEREAS, adequate bathhouse and sanitary facilities are necessary to the	

proper operation of the swimming pools.

NOW THEREFORE, by reason of the authority vested in the Board of Commissioners
of the City of Las Vegas by Sub-Section 6 of Section 31 of the Las Vegas City Charter,
approved March 16, 1911, as amended, the Board of City Commissioners do proclaim as
follows:

1.	That the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, construct the necessary bathhouse
and sanitation facilities required for the proper operation of the two (2) swimming
pools now under construction in said City of Las Vegas, Nevada. That the construc-
tion of said additional facilities necessary to place said pools in a complete operat-
ing condition, shall not cost in excess of $100,000.00 in total. That said $100,000.00
is the estimated cost of said construction as per report of the City Manager of the
City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

2.	That the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, shall issue bonds for the full sum

of $100,000.00 to cover the cost of said construction; said bonds to be payable within
twenty (20) years from the date of issuing the same, and to carry interest rate not
in excess of 3% per annum,and to be payable from that fund to be known as the Swimming
Pool Bond Fund, that all of said bonds outstanding after five (5) years from date of issue
shall be callable on any interest payment date upon the discretion of the Board of
Commissioners.

The Board of Commissioners will pass an ordinance providing for the construc-
tion of said bathhouse and facilities necessary to complete the swimming pools and place	

them in an operating condition, and for the assurance of bonds for said construction,

at a regular meeting of said Board of Commissioners to be held__________7th day of

______March____________1947, at 2:00 P.M. of said date, in the City Hall, City of Las

Vegas, Nevada, County of Clark, State of Nevada, at which time any parties having
objection to said action of the Board of City Commissioners shall be present and make
said reason known, if any, why said action should not be taken by the Board of City
Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada.
