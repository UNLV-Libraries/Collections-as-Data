BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada at
a regular meeting held on the 4th day of December, 1957; having considered an ap-
plication of Jo Ann Pulliam Fike for a use permit to authorize the establishment
and operation of a "Treatment Center" by the Nevada Society for Crippled Children,
Inc., at 818 South 7th Street, legally described as Lots 24 and 25; Block 31; South
Addition; and

The Board having considered the recommendation of the Board of Adjustment approving
this application subject to 1-moving of a chain link fence at rear of property next
to alley to provide off-street parking add 2-changing of front and rear doors to
meet requirements of Building Code for this type occupancy;

It is the decision of this Board that the application of Jo Ann Pulliam Fike for a
use permit to authorize the establishment and operation of a "Treatment Center" by
the Nevada Society for Crippled Children, Inc. at 818 South 7th Street be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioners Bunker and carried by the following vote:	

Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. 

Absent:	Commissioner Sharp.

VACATION PETITION	The Commission considered the petition for the vacation of a 40 foot strip on the

East Charleston	north and south sides of Charleston Boulevard from 15th Street to Fremont Street.

Public Hearing Set

Mr. Bills reported the Planning Commission recommended approval of this petition,
subject to the following conditions:

1.	That provision be made for dedication and improvement of
alleys at the rear of all properties fronting on Charleston.

2.	That provision be made for removal of all utilities from the land
to be vacated.

Commissioner Whipple moved the public hearing on this petition for vacation be
held January, 8, 1958 at 7:30 P.M.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:	

Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
