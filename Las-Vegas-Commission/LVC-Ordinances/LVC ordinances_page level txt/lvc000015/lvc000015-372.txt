EMERGENCY ORDINANCE NO. 644

AN EMERGENCY ORDINANCE CONCERNING SPECIAL ASSESSMENT DISTRICT NO. 100-25; CORRECTING IN CERTAIN PARTICU-
LARS THE CONFIRMED AMENDED ASSESSMENT ROLL FOR SAID DISTRICT; RATIFYING, APPROVING, AND CONFIRMING ACTION
TAKEN HERETOFORE IN THE PROCEEDING FOR ASSESSMENT DISTRICT NO. 100-25; PRESCRIBING DETAILS IN CONNECTION
THEREWITH AND OTHER MATTERS RELATIVE THERETO; REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH; AND DECLAR-
ING AN EMERGENCY.

WHEREAS, Emergency Ordinance No. 601, as amended by Emergency Ordinance No. 629 provided that the
entire cost and expense of making said improvements were deemed to be $104,161.07; and

WHEREAS, it appears that an erroneous assessment was made on Page 57 of the amended Assessment Roll
for Special Assessment District No. 100-25 on Lot A, owned by B.P.O.E. #1468, in the amount of $1,494.79,
as total cost; and

WHEREAS, the actual cost chargeable against this said above described property, as appears on Page
57 of the amended Assessment Roll should be $1,101.00; and

WHEREAS, other corrections made in said amended Assessment Roll reduce the total cost to the City
for said improvements to the sum of $103,070.27; and

WHEREAS# Emergency Ordinance No. 631 authorized the issuance of bonds in the total amount of $62,533.27
which is the amount of assessments levied against private property, less cash payments received; and

WHEREAS, the total amount chargeable against public corporations, for which an assessment cannot be
levied, was the sum of $11,494.34; and

WHEREAS, the deduction of cash payments received and the cost chargeable against public property
leaves the sum of $62,533.27 to be attained by the sale of bonds,

NOW, THEREFORE, THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, DO ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

SECTION 1. That that certain assessment against Lot A owned by the B.P.O.E. #1468, as appears on
Page 57 of the confirmed amended Assessment Roll for Special Assessment District No. 100-25, is hereby
amended to charge the proper cost against said property and shall read as follows:

Lot. No.	Ownership	Realty	Total	Final Cost

A	Lodge: B.P.O.E.	$2202.00	$2292.00	$1101.00

#1468, 128 S. 3rd
Street, City

SECTION 2. That a certified copy of this ordinance shall be posted in the amended Assessment Roll
for Special Assessment District No. 100-25 the same bearing No. 33-1955 and shall constitute conclusive
authority for the charge so made.

SECTION 3. That all action, proceedings, minutes and things inconsistent with the provisions of this
ordinance heretofore taken, had and done by the City of Las Vegas and the officers of said City concern-
ing Street Improvement Assessment District No. 100-25, be and the same is hereby ratified, approved and
confirmed.

SECTION 4. That all ordinances or Resolutions, or parts thereof, in conflict with the .provisions
of this ordinance, are hereby repealed.

SECTION 5. That by reason of the fact that the streets of the City of Las Vegas are inadequate to
meet the present and future needs of the City and its inhabitants and that it is necessary immediately
to raise funds to improve said streets, therefore, it is hereby declared that an emergency exists, and
that this ordinance is necessary for the immediate preservation of the public peace, health and safety.

SECTION 6. That the City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
shall cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following
its final reading and adoption, in the Las Vegas Sun, a daily newspaper published in said City, and this
ordinance shall became effective immediately following the second publication hereof.

PASSED, ADOPTED AND APPROVED this 11th day of May, 1955.
