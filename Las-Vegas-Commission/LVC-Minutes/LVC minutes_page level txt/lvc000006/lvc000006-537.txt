RESOLUTION

WHEREAS, the proposals received for the construction of two fire stations
(Central Fire station and Sub Fire station) at Las Vegas, Nevada, F.W.A. Project
26-130-N, have been duly opened, read and compared, and

WHEREAS, it appears that Ira Goldring, doing business as the Pioneer Con-
struction company, is the low bidder in the amount of $56,916.00 based upon his low base
bid of $58,616.00 less alternates 1 and 2 as set forth on the construction bid of said pro-
ject in the sum of $1,700.00;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the contract for the construction of said
work be and the same is hereby awarded to Ira Goldring, doing business as the Pioneer
Construction Company, for the Items and Amounts above stated.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that E. W. Cragin and Helen Scott Reed, Mayor and City
Clerk respectively, of the City of Las Vegas, be and they are hereby authorized to execute
such contract and any other documents, agreements or instruments in connection therewith
for and on behalf of said City of Las Vegas.

This award is tentative and may be cancelled or rescinded at any time prior to
notification by Helen Scott Reed to the contemplated awardee that the award is final.

CITY OF IAS VEGAS

ATTEST:	Sign:	E. W. Cragin, Mayor

signed: Helen Scott Reed, City Clerk
