The cost and expense of making the improvements of said district described in
the foregoing paragraph shall be paid by special assessment to be made according to the
benefits against the owners and upon the taxable lots and premises embraced within the
special assessment district as created by Ordinance No. 398. Diagrams and estimates of
cost are on file in the office of the City Clerk.

The Board has set Tuesday, the 10th day of January, 1950, at the hour of 8:00
P.M., as the time for the hearing of suggestions and/or objections that may be made by
parties in interest to the proposed improvements, said hearing to be held in the Council
Chambers in the City Hall at the corner of Stewart and Fifth Streets, in the City of
Las Vegas, Nevada.

Dated this 27 day of December, 1949.

His Honor asked if there were any present who wished to object to this proposed improve-
ment.

Mr. Herbert Gerson said that he felt that curbs and gutters should be installed at the
same time the street paving was done in this area.

After considerable discussion was held it was decided that the property owners should be
contacted to see if they wanted curbs and gutters or other improvements included in this
Assessment District.

There being no further business to come before this meeting Commissioner Bunker moved
this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Moore and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
