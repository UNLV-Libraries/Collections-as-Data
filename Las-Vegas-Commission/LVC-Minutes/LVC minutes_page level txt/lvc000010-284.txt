(CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING)

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada held
this 31st day of May, 1956 was called to order at the hour of 7.40 P.M. by Mayor Baker
with the following members present:

Mayor	C. D. Baker

Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

Commissioner	E. W. Fountain

Commissioner	Harris P. Sharp

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

City Manager	A. H. Kennedy

Administrative

Assistant	Robert Boos

City Engineer	George E. Wade

City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Commissioner Whipple moved that Service & Material Warrant No. 6886 in the total
amount of $675.00 be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

At this time the Commission considered the plans for the proposed sewer plant as prepared
by Headman, Ferguson and Carolla. Discussion followed.

Mayor Baker advised the Commission that Wally White, Nevada State Sanitary Engineer had
complimented the City Manager, City Engineer and Consulting Engineer on these plans, and
requested that this comment be made a part of the record.

Commissioner moved the proposed final plans for the new sewage disposal plant be approved
as presented.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioners Whipple moved that bids for the construction of the proposed new sewage dis-
posal plant be opened on July 10, 1956, and authorization be granted to advertise for said
bids.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
