A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas held this 11 th day of a

the following members present:

Commissioner Bunker moved that Bid No. 52.11 for the installation of Charleston Boulevard inter-
section improvements be awarded to the low bidder J.M. Murphy Construction Co. in the total amount
of $4,199.64.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote Commissioners Bunker,
Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes, none. Absent:Mayor Baker.

There being no further business to come before this special meeting the meeting adjourned.
