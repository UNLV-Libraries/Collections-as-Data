Las Vegas, Nevada
August 27, 1957

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING



TO:	SHIRLEY LODWICK, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas, Nevada is hereby called to be held in the Council Chambers
of the City Hall, on the 27th day of August, 1957, at the hour of
4:00 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	City Jail Addition

2.	Letter to Palm Mortuary

3.	Color for City Hall

4.	Publication on Huntridge Fire Station

5.	Letter from Franklin Bills

6.	Discussion - Headman, Ferguson & Carollo payment.

_______C. D. Baker____________

Mayor
