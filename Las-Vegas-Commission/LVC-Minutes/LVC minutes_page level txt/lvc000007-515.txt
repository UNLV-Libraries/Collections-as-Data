A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas called this
21st day of December, 1951 was called to order at the hour of 10:00 A. M. by His
Honor Mayor C. D. Baker with the following members present:

Mayor	C. D. Baker

Commissioner 	Wendell Bunker
Commissioner 	Rex A. Jarrett
Commissioner 	William Peccole
Commissioner 	Reed Whipple

City Attorney 	Howard W. Cannon
City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Absent: City Manager C. W. Shelley

DEFENSE MOBILIZATION 	His Honor presented a letter from Manly Fleischmann, Defense Production Administra-
MEETING	tion stating that there would be a meeting in Los Angeles, January 11, 1952 for

	Mayors of the cities in the eleven Western States, concerning Defense Mobilization.

Commissioner Bunker moved that Mayor Baker be authorized to attend the aforementioned
meeting.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote: Commis-

sioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

CHIEF OF POLICE	Discussion was held by the Board on the administration of the police Department by

	Chief L. B. Maxwell.

The Cleric was requested to call Chief Maxwell and ask him to attend this meeting.
Thereafter Chief Maxwell arrived, and further discussion was held.

There being no further business to come before the meeting at this time Commis-
sioner Bunker moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote: Commis-

sioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
