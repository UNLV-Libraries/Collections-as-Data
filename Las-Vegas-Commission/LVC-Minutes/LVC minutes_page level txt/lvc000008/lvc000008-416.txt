GALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

May 29, 1953

TO: SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is
hereby called to be held in the Council Chambers at the City Hall, on the 29th day
of May, 1953, at the hour of 7:30 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Public Hearings:

a)	REZONINGS

1. FROM R-3 to R-4 - Lots 1 to 3, Block 3, South 5th Street Tract
No. 1, Generally located at the southwest comer of St. Louis
Street and Paradise Valley Road.

2.	FROM R-3 to R-4 — Lot 14, Block 9 of the Boulder Addition; gener-
ally located on South 2nd Street between Colorado and Imperial
Avenues.

3.	FROM R-3 to R-4 - Portions of Blks 2, 4 5, 6, 10, 11, Meadows
Addition and all of Blocks 7, 8, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16 and unnumber-
ed lots and blocks lying north

b)	VARIANCE APPLICATIONS (Denied by the Planning Commission and said

denial appealed by the applicant.)

1.	Application of Howard Burgess to construct and maintain a dormi-
tory for men located at 213 North 2nd.

2.	Application of Howard Burgess to construct and maintain a trailer
park located north of Bonanza Road and south of Marble Manor and
west of H Street.

2.	Mr. R.B. Griffith will be present regarding a building permit in the

Griffith Tract.	

3.	Final map on McNeil Tract No. 2 and Hyde Park No. 3.

4.	Mr. William Coulthard will be present concerning the request of P.O.
Silvagni for aerial passage way over the alley between the Apache Hotel
and annex on North 2nd Street.

5.	Proposed Ordinance on City Clerk's position.

6.	Application of Irving F. Quale and Wallace E. Gogert for transfer of the
license of the Square Deal Loan, 118 North 1st Street. Said pawnshop
license is currently issued to Glen Tondevold.

C.D. Baker
Mayor
