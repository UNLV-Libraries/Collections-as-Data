ORDINANCE #339	 Commissioner Baskin and Moore having reported favorably on proposed

ANNEXATION MALONE	Ordinance #339 said ordinance entitled "AN ORDINANCE DECLARING CERTAIN

PROPERTY FIRST READING TERRITORY ADJOINING AND CONTIGUOUS TO THE CORPORATE LIMITS TO THE CITY

OF LAS VEGAS, TO BE ANNEXED TO THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; ORDERING A PLAT
	SHOWING SAID TERRITORY TO BE RECORDED IN THE OFFICE OF THE COUNTY RE-

CORDER, IN THE COUNTY OF CLARK, STATE OF NEVADA", and other matters
properly relating thereto was read to the Board for the first time,
thereafter Commissioner Baskin moved that the foregoing ordinance be
adopted on its first reading.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Moore and carried by the following
vote Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor
voting aye; noes, none.
