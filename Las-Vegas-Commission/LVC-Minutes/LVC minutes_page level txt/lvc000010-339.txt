Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

At this time Assistant Director of Planning Shawnee Stewart reported to the Commission
on the proposed tentative street numbering system for the City of Las Vegas.

Mr. Stewart explained that this study had been made by the Planning Commission in con-
junction with the public utilities companies, the Better Business Bureau and City Depart-
ments, and that the plan was briefly as follows:

1)	To adopt a uniform system for numbering.

2)	To correct those instances where there are streets with several names

3)	To correct those instances where there are several streets with duplicate
names.

4) To correct those streets with the same names that run in several directions.

The Commission indicated approval of this study and requested that the study be continued.

Commissioner Fountain moved that Bid No. 56.18 for the installation of a traffic signal
system awarded to Galland Electric be rejected on the basis that the financial status
of the Company does not meet the requirements of the City.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Whipple moved that on all contracts for $5,000.00 or more that the financial

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
