CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

	TO: SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is hereby c ailed
to be held in the Council Chambers at the City Hall, on the 3rd day of July, 1953, at the hour
of 4:00 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1. Payroll Warrant Nos. 1460 to 1465 inc. in the total sum of $39,509.11

2.	Gaming and Liquor Applications on file - 3rd Quarter, 1953.

3.	Recreation Director.

4. County Officials will be present regarding sewer.

5.	Sale of City Property.

6.	Sewer Plant - negotiation with Government

7.	Bids on the Bonds for Assessment District Nos. 100-11 and 100-18

s/ C.D. BAKER
Mayor
