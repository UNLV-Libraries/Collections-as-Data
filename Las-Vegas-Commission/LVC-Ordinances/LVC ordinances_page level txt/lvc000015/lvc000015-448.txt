ORDINANCE NO. 695

AN ORDINANCE AUTHORIZING CITY OFFICERS, INSPECTORS AND EMPLOYEES CHARGED WITH THE ENFORCEMENT OF CITY
ORDINANCES TO PREPARE AND SERVE SUMMONS ON PERSONS CHARGED WITH VIOLATION OF CITY ORDINANCES, SUMMONS-
ING THEM TO APPEAR IN COURT AT A TIME SPECIFIED TO ANSWER THE CHARGE AGAINST THEM; PROVIDING A PENALTY
FOR FAILURE TO APPEAR AS SUMMONSED; AND PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

SECTION 1. Except when otherwise expressly directed by law or ordinance, upon any violation
of any ordinance of the City of Las Vegas, the Fire Chief of the City of Las Vegas, his deputies and
assistants; The Supervisor of Building and Safety, his deputies and assistants; The Health Department
Director, his deputies and assistants; the deputy poundkeeps and assistant employed by the Clark County
Humane Society; The Garbage Contractor and its officers and agents; and all other officers, inspectors
and employees charged with the enforcement of city ordinances in lieu of arresting the person com-
mitting the violation, may prepare, sign, and serve on the accused person a written summons to appear in
the Municipal Court to answer such charge. Such summons shall be personally served on the person ac-
cused.

SECTION 2. The summons provided for in the preceding section shall be in substantially
the following form:

In the Municipal Court of the City of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada

SUMMONS

City of Las Vegas, Nevada
Plaintiff



vs

Defendant

The State of Nevada to The Above Named Defendant

You are hereby summoned to appear before the judge of the above entitled Court at the

Courtroom thereof at 400 North 1st Street, Las Vegas, Nevada on ............... the

......day of....................., 195..., at the hour of.............o'clock ...M to

answer a charge of ............................. in violation of Ordinance No.......

or Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, Chapter........Section No.........

Date of Offense_______________

City of Las Vegas
by__________________

(office or position of person issuing and
serving summons)

SECTION 3. The time specified in such summons for the accused to appear must be not less

five (5) days after service, unless an earlier hearing is demanded by the accused.



SECTION 4. Any person willfully failing to appear in Court as required by such summons is
guilty of a misdemeanor, regardless of the disposition of the charge of which he is accused.

SECTION 5. All Ordinances or parts of Ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

SECTION 6. This Ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its final reading and
adoption, and final publication as in the next section provided.

SECTION 7. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
shall cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks Immediately following
its first reading and adoption in the Las Vegas Review Journal, a daily newspaper published in the
City of Las Vegas.

APPROVED:

/s/ C.D. BAKER
