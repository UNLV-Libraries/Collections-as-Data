ORDINANCE NO. 352

AN ORDINANCE DEFINING AND PROHIBITING PETIT LARCENY; FIXING A PENALTY FOR THE VIOLATION OF
ANY OF THE PROVISIONS OF THIS ORDINANCE; AND REPEALING ORDINANCE NO. 11 AND ALL ORDINANCES
OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

Section 1. Every person who shall steal, take, and carry, lead, or drive away, the
personal goods or property of another, under the value of two hundred fifty dollars ($250.00),
shall be deemed guilty of petit larceny, and upon conviction thereof shall be punished by
imprisonment in the City Jail not more than six (6) months, or by fine not exceeding five
hundred dollars, ($500.00), or by both such fine and imprisonment.

Section 2. All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby
repealed.

Section 3. This ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its publication,
as in the next section provided, and final passage.

Section 4. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las
Vegas shall cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks im-
mediately following its first reading and adoption, in the Las Vegas Evening Review Journal,
a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
