OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, CITY OF LAS VEGAS,
CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

JUNE 4th, 1932.

Minutes of a regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
held on the 4th day of June 1932 at the hour of three o'clock P.M.

Roll call showed the following present, W.C.German, Mayor Pro Tem, Commissioners
Mundy and Thomas, City Attorney and Clerk.

Absent Mayor Cragin, Commissioner Hansell.

All claims allowed as per claims and warrant register on file in the office of the

Clerk.

$3,000.00 Transferred from the General Fund to the Street Fund.

Ryland Taylor appeared before the Board at this time and ask the City to give the
sum of $75.00 from the Publicity Fund for the purchase of street decorations to be used dur-
ing the American Legion Convention, and at the close of the convention the decorations
should become the property of the City of Las Vegas.

On motion of Commissioner Thomas seconded by Commissioner Mundy it was moved that
the sum of $75.00 be taken from the Publicity Fund to purchase street decorations, same to
be the property of the City of Las Vegas.

Vote on said motion was as follows: Commissioners Mundy and Thomas and Mayor
Pro Tem German voting aye. noes none.

A vacancy occurring on the Board of Examiners of the Electricians of the City of
Las Vegas the name of L.E.Beedy, a journeyman electrician , was recommended to the Board to
fill said vacancy.

On motion of Commissioner Mundy seconded by Commissioner Thomas L.E.Beedy was
appointed a member of the Board of Examiners for Electricians, in the City of Las Vegas.

Vote on the motion was as follows: Commissioners Mundy and Thomas and Mayor Pro
tem German voting Aye. Noes none.

No further business appearing before the Board at this time the meeting recessed
until Wednesday the 8th day of June, 1932 at the hour of three o'clock P.M.

OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, CITY OF LAS VEGAS,
CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

JUNE 8th, 1932.

Minutes of a recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas held on the 8th day of June 1932 at the hour of three o'clock P.M.

Roll call showed the following present: W.C.German, Mayor Pro Tem, Commissioners
Mundy and Hansell, City Attorney and Clerk.

Absent Mayor Cragin, Commissioner Thomas.

Mr. Owen appeared before the Board at this time and ask that the Board of Com-
missioners consider the changing of the names of the following streets in Grand View Addition
of the City of Las Vegas to conform with the established streets in the City as follows:

State Street to Ninth Street.

Clark Street to Tenth Street
La Salle Street to Eleventh Street
Hill Street to Twelfth Street.

No action was taken at this time.
