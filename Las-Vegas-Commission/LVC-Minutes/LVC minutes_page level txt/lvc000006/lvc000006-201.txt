RECREATION BOARD	Commissioner Whipple moved that the recommendation of the City Recreation

Recommendation	Board dated December 16, 1947 requesting	the	City to amend Ordinance

Re:	Curfew	#21 to	conform with the County Ordinance	in	regard to curfew time for

juveniles be approved. As the city expects to revise all of the
Ordinances next year, it was referred to the City Attorney. Motion
seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye.
Noes, none.

CLAIM - George	Commissioner Clark moved that the claim of George Thompson covering

Thompson 	expenses incurred on special investigation in narcotic case in the

carried by the following vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore,

Whipple and His Honor voting aye. Noes, none.
