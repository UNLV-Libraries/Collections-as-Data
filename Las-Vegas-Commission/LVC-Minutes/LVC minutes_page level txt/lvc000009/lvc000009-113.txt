This being the time set for the opening of bids on Bid No. 54.9 the 2½

Ton Truck with Hydro Aerial Tower for the Electrical Department, the
following bids were received:

A. C. GRANT	$12,186.49	$11,652.60

SELLMAN MOTORS	11,428.84

HOWARD MOTORS	11,563.81

CASHMAN GMC CO.	12,485.69

INLAND SERVICE	13,154.00

CLARK COUNTY WHLSE	11,283.88	10,975.58

Thereafter Commissioner Bunker moved the foregoing bids be referred to
the City Manager and Comptroller for computation and recommendation.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem
Whipple voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

This being the time set for the award of the bid on the Cash Registers,

Bid No. 54.10, the matter was considered by the Board.

Thereafter Commissioner Sharp moved the bid of the National Cash Register
Company in the amount of $7,025.00 be accepted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

At this time the Board considered the awarding of the bid on Bid No. 54.11
the Compressor for the Cemetery, and it was decided to hold this matter
in abeyance until May 10, 1954.

The Mayor Pro Tem announced that this was the continuance of the time and
place designated for the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
Nevada to meet and review the assessments and hear any objections to said
assessments which may be made on the proposed improvements in Assessment
District No. 100-23, as shown on Assessment Roll No. 29-1954.

The Mayor then asked if any written objections concerning said assessment
roll had been filed in the office of the City Clerk. The City Clerk
answered there had been no objections filed in her office prior to said
meeting.

The Mayor Pro Tem then publicly asked if anyone desired to make any further
objections, suggestions or comments, or raise any questions concerning
said special assessment roll. There were no objections, suggestions or
comments voiced.

Thereafter Commissioner Sharp moved the following resolution confirming
the Assessment Roll on District No. 100-23, as amended, be adopted:

WHEREAS, the City of Las Vegas in the County of Clark and State of
Nevada, and the officers thereof, have taken action to improve certain
streets and parts of streets therein; and

WHEREAS, said City has created Street Improvement Assessment District
No. 100-23 for the purpose of making said improvements; and

WHEREAS, said City assessed the entire cost and expense of making said
improvements, including all incidental expenses which may be legally in-
cluded in the sums assessed against the owners and the assessable lots,
premises and property specially benefited by such improvements and included
within said District; provided however, no assessments were made against
public property, but which proportionate amounts are to be paid in cash
by said property owners or from the City's general fund; and

WHEREAS, the said assessment roll was filed with the City Clerk on
the 21st day of April, 1954; and

WHEREAS, notice of said filing and the public hearing on said assess-
ment roll were duly published and as required by the Charter of the City
of Las Vegas; and

WHEREAS, on May 3, 1954 a public protest hearing was held on said
assessment roll; and

WHEREAS, the assessor was directed to correct or revise said roll be-
fore confirmation to correct typographical errors or clerical errors and
to modify all assessments in accordance with the construction price and
costs of the improvements; and

WHEREAS, said revised roll has been filed; and

WHEREAS, the time has now arrived to confirm the said assessment roll
as revised.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Commissioners of the
City of Las Vegas at a recessed regular meeting thereof, held on the 5th
day of May, 1954, that revised Assessment Roll No. 29-1954 be and the same
hereby is confirmed.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the City Clerk shall endorse said confirma-
tion on the said roll and forward the same to the County Assessor, Ex-Officio
City Assessor for the placing of said assessments on the official rolls of
the County of Clark, State of Nevada.
