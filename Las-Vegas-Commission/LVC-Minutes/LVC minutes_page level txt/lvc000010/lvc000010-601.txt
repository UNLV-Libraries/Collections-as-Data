DATED:	October 2, 1957.

Commissioner Bunker moved the foregoing resolution be adopted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye;
noes, none.

Commissioner Bunker moved the City Manager be authorized to attend the
City Manager Convention in Washington, D. C. October 20th through 26th,
1957, with expenses of $500.00 allowed.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye;
noes, none.

Commissioner Bunker moved that the Board of Commissioners go on record as
being in favor of the extension of service of Bonanza Airlines permitting
stops at St. George and Kanab, Utah.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye;
noes, none.

At this time Mayor Baker left the meeting.

Mr. Lou Maynard appeared before the Commission at this time speaking on
behalf of the property owners in the Biltmore Addition #1 and #2 and re-
quested the installation of sidewalks on North 2nd Street from Bonanza
to Mesquite.

Commissioner Bunker moved this matter be referred to the City Manager for
recommendation.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

An ordinance entitled:	AN ORDINANCE TO REAPL ORDINANCE NO. 731; REGULATING

AND CONTROLLING THE POSTING OF RATES OF HOTELS, INNS, MOTELS, MOTOR COURTS
AND BOARDING AND LODGING HOUSES WITHIN THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; TO REGULATE
AND CONTROL THE POSTING AND MAINTAINING OF OUTSIDE ADVERTISING SIGNS OF
MOTELS, MOTOR COURTS, HOTELS, AND LIKE ESTABLISHMENTS; PROVIDING PENALTIES
FOR THE VIOLATION THEREOF; AND PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS RELATED THERETO,
was read by title and referred to Commissioner Sharp for recommendation.

There being no further business to come before the meeting, at the hour
of 10:00 P.M. Commissioner Sharp moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.
