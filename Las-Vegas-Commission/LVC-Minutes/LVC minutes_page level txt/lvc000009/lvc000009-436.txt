Thorough knowledge of the methods, tools, supplies, and general practices required
to construct and maintain greens, fairways, and other parts of a golf course;
knowledge of grass culture, grass diseases, and the chemicals and other means used
in the control and eradication of grass diseases, rodents and other pests; ability to
train and supervise the work of others; ability to get along well with others.

It is recommended that the above Ordinance #257 he changed to bring personnel,
financial responsibilities and other authorities under the City Manager and remove the
responsibilities for the operational character of the course from going directly to
the City Commissioners with these problems."
