CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

April 29, 1953

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, is
hereby called to be held in the Council Chambers at the City Hall, on the 29th
day of April, 1953, at the hour of 8:00 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Award of Construction Bids for Assessment District Nos. 100-11, 100-18
and 100-19.

2.	Agreement with State Highway Department re:	acquiring rights-of-way

for North 5th Street improvement.

3.	Letter to Houston Mills, State Highway Engineer re: 10 foot set-back
on Charleston Blvd, from 10th to 15th Streets.

s/ REED WHIPPLE_________

Mayor Pro Tem

NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO:	C.D. BAKER, Mayor and to WENDELL BUNKER, REX A. JARRETT, WILLIAM PECCOLE,

REED WHIPPLE, Commissioners and C.W. SHELLEY, City Manager, DORA LORD, Assistant
City Manager, HOWARD W. CANNON, City Attorney of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

You and each of you will please take notice that pursuant to a call this day
issued by Mayor Baker, a special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City
of Las Vegas, Nevada will be held in the Council Chambers, at the City Hall, 5th
and Stewart, on the 29th day of April, 1953, at the hour of 8:00 P.M., to consider
the following matters:

1.	Award of Construction Bids for Assessment District Nos. 100-11, 100-18,
and 100-19.

2.	Agreement with State Highway Department re: acquiring rights-of-way for
North 5th Street improvement.

3. Letter to Houston Mills, State Highway Engineer re: 10 foot set-back
on Charleston Blvd. from 10th to 15th Streets.
