BID NO. 59.12	Director of Public Works Sauer advised the Street Department was in need of

(PART)	gravel on the east side of the railroad tracks and added that sometime ago

Type 2 Aggregate	H. W. Polk was awarded a part of Bid No. 59.12 for the furnishing of 5,000

cu. yds. of Type 2 Aggregate, to be delivered east of the railroad tracks at
Wells Cargo, Inc.	$1.29 per cu. yd.; subsequently, bid was withdrawn from this contractor and no

gravel obtained under this contract.

Award

Mr. Sauer advised Wells Cargo, Inc., the company awarded the other part of Bid
No. 59.12 for the furnishing of 10,000 cu. yds. of Type 2 Aggregate, to be
delivered west of the railroad tracks, at $1.20 per cu. yd., would take over
H. W. Polk's portion of this contract for delivery east of the railroad tracks
at $1.29 per cu. yd., furthermore, Mr. Sauer stated he had checked with the
City Attorney's Office and had been advised this would be legal.

His Honor Mayor Gragson asked if other companies had the opportunity to rebid
this, and Mr. Sauer advised that, of all the original bids submitted, these
were the two low quotations.

Commissioner Whipple moved that part of Bid No. 59.12 for 5,000 cu. yds. of
Type 2 Aggregate for delivery east of the railroad tracks, originally given to
H. W. Polk, be awarded to Wells Cargo, Inc. in the amount of $1.29 per cu. yds.,
on the basis of the City Attorney's recommendation.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Elwell, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor Mayor Gragson
voting aye; noes, none.
