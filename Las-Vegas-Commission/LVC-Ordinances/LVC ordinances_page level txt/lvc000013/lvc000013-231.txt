ORDINANCE NUMBER 144.

AN ORDINANCE OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS REGULATING THE ERECTION, CONSTRUCTION, 
ENLARGEMENT, ALTERATION, REPAIR, MOVING, REMOVAL, DEMOLITION, CONVERSION, OCCUPANCY
EQUIPMENT, USE, HEIGHT, AREA, AND MAINTENANCE, OF ALL BUILDINGS AND/OR STRUCTURES IN
THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; PROVIDING FOR THE ISSUANCE OF PERMITS, AND COLLECTIONS OF FEES
THEREFOR; PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR THE VIOLATION THEREOF; DECLARING AND ESTABLISHING
FIRE ZONES; REPEALING ORDINANCES NOS. TWENTY-SEVEN (27), FIFTY-NINE (59), SIXTY-ONE (61)
and SEVENTY-TWO (72) OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS AND ALL OTHER ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF

ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH.		

THE Board OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS DO ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

	Section 1. That, a certain document, three (3) copies of which are on file in the

office of the City Clerk of the City of Las Vegas, being marked, and designated "Uniform

Building Code, 1027 Edition, published, January 1928, by Pacific Coast Building Officials
Conference", be and the same is hereby adopted as the building code of the City of Las
Vegas for the regulation of the erection, construction, enlargement, alteration repair,
moving, removal, demolition, conversion, occupancy, equipment, use, height, area, and
maintenance of all buildings and/or structures in the City of Las Vegas providing for
the issuance of permits and the collection of fees therefor; providing penalties penalties
 for the violation of such building code; declaring, and establishing fire zones;
and each and all of the regulations, provisions, penalties, conditions, and terms of such
"Uniform Building Code, 1927 Edition, published January 1928, by Pacific Coast Building
Officials Conference" on file in the office of the City Clerk are hereby referred to,
adopted, and made a part thereof as if fully set out this ordinance. 

Section 2. That, Ordinance No. 27 of the City of Las Vegas, entitled, "An Ordinance
Fixing the Fire Limits of the City of Las Vegas, regulating building construction
and keeping, and storage of combustibles, and explosives" passed, and adopted March 6th,

1912, and an Ordinance No. 59 of the City of Las Vegas, entitled an Ordinance to Amend
Section Eight (8) of Ordinance No. 27 of the City of Las Vegas, entitled "An Ordinance
Fixing the Fire Limits of the City of Las Vegas, regulating Building Construction, and
the keeping and storage of combustibles, and explosives," passed, and adopted March 3rd
1915, and Ordinance No. 61 of the City of Las Vegas, entitled "An Ordinance to Amend
Section 6 of Ordinance No. 27 (27) entitled "An Ordinance Fixing the Fire Limits of the
City of Las Vegas, regulating the building, and construction and the keeping, and storage
of combustibles, and explosives,'' passed and adopted August 4, 1915 and Ordinance No 72
of the City of Las Vegas, entitled "An Ordinance amending Sections 2, 3, 6 and & 7 of Ordinance
 No. 27 of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, entitled "An Ordinance fixing the fire limits
of the City of Las Vegas, regulating building construction, and the keeping of combustibles
and explosives, passed and adopted June 28th, 1918, and all other Ordinances and	

parts of Ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby appealed.			

Section 3. That, the entire incorporated area of the City of Las Vegas is hereby
declared to be and is hereby established as a fire district and said Fire District shall
be known and designated as Fire Zone 2 and 3, and each such zone shall include such territory
 or portions of said City as illustrated, outlined, and designated on a certain map
on file in the office of the City Clerk of the said City of Las Vegas being marked and
designated as "Fire Zones of the City of Las Vegas", which is hereby adopted as the fire
zoning map of the City of Las Vegas for the application of the regulations included in the

Uniform Building Code 1927 Edition, published January 1928, by the Pacific Coast Building
Officials Conference".

Section 4. There is hereby created the office of Building Inspector, who shall have
such powers and exercise such duties as are prescribed in said Uniform Building Code and
as may be prescribed from time to time by Ordinance. He shall be appointed by and hold office
 at the pleasure of the Board of City Commissioners, and his compensation shall be
fixed and determined from time to time by Resolution of the said Board of City Commission.

Section 5. This Ordinance shall take effect, and be inforce from and after its
passage and publication for one week (six issues) in the Las Vegas Evening Review and
Journal, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.	

Passed, and adopted this 1st day of August 1929, by the following vote, Commissioners
 Thomas, and Smith and His Honor the Mayor J. F. Hesse voting Aye, Noes None.

First reading July 19th 1929.	.

Second reading August 1st, 1929.

Approved this 1st day of August 1929.

J. F. Hesse

		Mayor of the City of Las Vegas.
