PETITION	At this time the following petition signed by business men in

	that locality was presented to the Board.

PETITION

We, the undersigned retail merchants and professional men, doing business in
the 500 to 700 block on East Fremont Street, Las Vegas, Nevada, do hereby
request that a maximum one hour parking limit be established And enforced
between the 500 to 700 block on East Fremont St. This request is made in view
of the fact of the number of business and offices in this block depending on
drive-in trade has increased and that parking facilities are limited.

Thereafter, the foregoing petition was referred to the Chief
of Police for handling.

ORDINANCE NO. 331	An Ordinance entitled "AN ORDINANCE ESTABLISHING A PUBLIC POUND:

First Reading	PRESCRIBING THE POWERS AND DUTIES OF THE POUND MASTER: FIXING THE

	FEES AND CHARGES TO BE COLLECTED BY HIM: AND DIRECTING THE

DISPOSITION THEREOF: PROVIDING FOR THE REGISTRATION OF DOGS AND
DOG KENNELS AND FIXING THE FEES THEREFOR:	PROVIDING FOR THE

VACCINATION OF DOGS AND CATS:	PROVIDING FOR THE REDEMPTION SALE

OR OTHER DISPOSITION OF DOGS OR OTHER ANIMALS IMPOUNDED:

PROHIBITING CRUELTY TO ANIMALS: PROVIDING A PENALTY FOR VIOLATION
OF ANY OF THE PROVISIONS THEREOF: REPEALING ORDINANCES Nos. 5, 12, 19,
51, 58, 63, and 101 AND ALL ORDINANCES PARTS OF ORDINANCES OF THE
CITY OF LAS VEGAS IN CONFLICT THEREWITH: OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY REL-
ATING THERETO," was reported favorably to the Board by the
Committee composed of Commissioners Baskin and Clark and read for
a first time.

Thereafter Commissioner Clark moved that the foregoing ordinance
be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Corradetti and His Honor voting

aye; noes, none.
