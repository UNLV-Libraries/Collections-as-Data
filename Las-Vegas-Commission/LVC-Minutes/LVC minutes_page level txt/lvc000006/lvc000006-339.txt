A regular meeting of the Board	of Commissioners	held	this	5th day	of November, 1948 was 	called

Solicitation	of	relative to the	solicitation of funds in the City of Las Vegas that this ordinance will not be

Funds	accepted in its	present form, but will be referred to the City Attorney to hold a meeting with
