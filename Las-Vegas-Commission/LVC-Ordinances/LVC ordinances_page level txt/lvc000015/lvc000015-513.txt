ORDINANCE NO. 732

AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND CHAPTER 7, CODE OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA, 1949, AS AMENDED BY ORDINANCE NO. 728, BY
PROHIBITING THE PLACING OF WRITTEN ADVERTISEMENTS UPON STRUCTURES ON THE PUBLIC RIGHT OF WAY; PROVIDING
CERTAIN EXCEPTIONS THERETO; PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR THE VIOLATION OF THIS ORDINANCE; PROVIDING OTHER
MATTERS RELATED THERETO; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

SECTION 1. Section 1, Chapter 7, the Code of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, 1949, as
amended by Ordinance No. 728, is hereby amended to read as follows:

It shall be unlawful for any person to paste, print or in any manner whatsoever place or attach
to any building, fence, gate, bridge, rock, tree, board, sidewalk, curb, telephone pole, electric light
pole or other utility pole, or any other type of structure whatsoever within the right-of-way of any
highway, street or alley, any written, printed, painted or other outdoor advertisement, bill, notice,
sign, picture, card or poster; provided, however, the prohibitions hereinabove set forth shall not apply
to the placement of advertising matter on benches maintained within the public right-of-way at bus stops
throughout the City pursuant to permit issued by the Board of City Commissioners.

SECTION 2. All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

SECTION 3. Any person violating the provisions of this ordinance shall be guilty of a misdemeanor,
add on conviction thereof, shall be punished by a fine of not more than $500.00, and/or six (6) months
in jail, or by a combination of both such fine and imprisonment.

SECTION 4. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas shall
cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks Immediately following its first
reading and adoption in a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
