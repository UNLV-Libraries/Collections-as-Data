The matter of the street enroachment of the Wilshire Service Station at
3rd & Wyoming was presented to the Board at this time. Mr. J. A. Brewer,
lessee of this property was present.

Commissioner Bunker moved this matter be deferred until September 19, 1951.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Mayor Baker

Commissioner Peccole moved the City Manager be authorized to proceed with
the plans for the construction of the Bonanza Road Fire Station, and to
enter into an agreement with Architects Zick and Sharp.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Mayor Baker

Commissioner Jarrett moved the application of O. W. Glenn, Sal Sagev Hotel,
Main and Fremont for license for one slot machine for the third quarter of
1951 be granted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent; Mayor Baker

Commissioner Bunker moved the application of Uvalde Caperton, Cotton Club,

500 Jackson for license for one additional "21" game for the third quarter
of 1951 be granted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Mayor Baker

Commissioner Jarrett moved the application of Dave Stearns, Santa Anita
Turf & Bar, 113 E. Fremont for one additional "21" table for the third
quarter of 1951 be denied.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes,
none. Commissioner Peccole passed his vote. Absent: Mayor Baker

Commissioner Bunker moved the application of Scherer-Schellang-Shaprio,

Las Vegas Club, 18 Fremont for gaming license for 15 additional slot machines

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Mayor Baker

Commissioner Peccole moved the application of W. Gwilliam, Hertz U-Drive

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
Absent: Mayor Baker

At this time a letter under date of August 28, 1951 received from Mrs. Robert
Carey, 310 North 16th concerning smoking in theaters was presented and read
to the Board.

Commissioner Peccole moved this matter be referred to the Fire Chief for
investigation, and that the ordinances which prohibit smoking except in
allocated sections of the theater, be inforced.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent! Mayor Baker

At this time the request of Mr. Stanley S. Kuthowsky of Los Angeles, Calif-
ornia, dated September 4, 1951, for a reduction of the license required
Itinerant Merchants, was presented to the Board for their consideration.

Commissioner Peccole moved the above mentioned request for reduction of
license be denied.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Mayor Baker

An application for license by H. J. Mulling, 610 North F Street, was pre-
sented at this time. Mr. Mulling repairs sewing machines, and has been
classified as an Itinerant Merchant by the City Clerk because he has no
established place of business. Discussion was held and thereafter it was
decided that Mr. Mulling should be requested to appear before the Board
at a regular meeting.
