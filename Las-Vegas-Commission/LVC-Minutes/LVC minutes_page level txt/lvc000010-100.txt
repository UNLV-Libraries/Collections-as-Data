Las Vegas, Nevada
December 29, 1955

A recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City
of Las Vegas, Nevada held this 29th day of December, 1955 was called
to order at the hour of 7.45 P.M. by His Honor Mayor Baker with the
following members present:

Mayor	C.D. Baker

Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

Commissioner	Harris P. Sharp

Commissioner	E.W. Fountain

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

City Manager	A.H. Kennedy

City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

City Engineer	George E. Wade

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Absent:	Asst. City Manager Robert W. Notti

The Commission considered the application of J.A. Ewing, W.G. Steele,

Lila R. Lee for gaming license for 1 Craps, 2 "21", 1 Keno, and 2
Poker at the Cotton Club, 500 Jackson and the application of J.A. Ewing
and Lila R. Lee for the tavern liquor license for this business. These
licenses were formerly issued to Uvalde Caperton.

The Commission discussed this matter with Messrs. Ewing and Steele and
their attorneys Madison Graves. The Commission advised these applicants
that they would prefer that Miss Lee not be connected with this operation.

Thereafter Commissioner Fountain moved a gaming license for 1 Craps, 2 "21",

1 Keno and 2 Poker for the Cotton Club, 500 Jackson Street for the first
quarter of 1956 be granted to J. .Ewing and W.G. Steele and the tavern liquor
license be granted to J.A. Ewing providing Miss Lee does not have any active
management of the business.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker,Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye;
noes, none.

Commissioner Bunker moved the following applications for renewal, of gam-
