RESOLUTION
Conference Committee
Juvenile Court

ORDINANCE NO. 606
Reduction of Building
Area - Meadows Add'n.

PROCLAMATION
CONSERVATION OF WATER

Commissioner Jarrett moved the above entitled Ordinance No. 607 be adopted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

At this time the Board considered a request from the Clark County Juvenile
Board for the appointment of the following citizens to act as a Conference
Committee in conjunction with the Juvenile Court:

1.	Father Don Carmody

2.	Mrs. Jake Kozloff

3.	Mrs. Victor Giasson
4. Mr. Jack Pursel

3. Mr. Vernon Welbourne

6.	Mrs. Deborah Moore

7.	Dr. J. M. George

Thereafter Commissioner Bunker moved this request be approved and the
following resolution adopted:

BE IT RESOLVED by the Governing Body of the City of Las Vegas
of Las Vegas, Nevada, that a Committee be appointed to act in conjunction
with the Clark County Probation Department with a Probation Officer as
Chairman, to investigate and make recommendation to the Clark County Pro-
bation Department in matters where violations of laws are alleged to have
been made in which juveniles are involved.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Honorable Judges of the Clark
County Courts be requested to approve the appointment of such a Committee
and that when and if such approval is given that the Mayor with the consent
of the Governing Body will appoint such Committee for the purposes stated
herein.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor Mayor pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

At this time Commissioners Jarrett and Sharp members of the committee for
recommendation of the proposed ordinance for reduction of building area
in Meadows Addition reported favorably.

Thereafter an ordinance entitled:	AN ORDINANCE TO PROVIDE FOR THE REDUCTION

OF THE BUILDING SITE AREA REQUIREMENTS IN THE MEADOWS ADDITION TO THE CITY
OF LAS VEGAS: PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS RELATED THERETO: AND REPEALING ALL
ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH, was read in full
to the Board.

Commissioner Sharp moved the above entitled Ordinance No. 606 be adopted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

Discussion was held on the request of the Las Vegas Water District for
conservation of water.

Thereafter it was determined a proclamation should be issued alternating
the watering days starting May 1, 1954, and ending September 15, 1954.

There being no further business to come before the meeting at this time,
Commissioner Sharp moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.
