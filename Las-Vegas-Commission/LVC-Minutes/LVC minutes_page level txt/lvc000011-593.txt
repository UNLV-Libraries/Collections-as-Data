MINUTES	Las Vegas, Nevada

October 23, 1959

A regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada,
held this 23rd day of October, 1959, was called to order at the hour of 7:30 P.M.
with the following members present: (The regular meeting to be held October 21,

1959, was changed to this date by resolution adopted at a Special Meeting of
the Board of City Commissioners held October 15, 1959.)

CONNIE MORMON	His Honor Mayor Gragson opened the meeting stating he would entertain a motion

Child Nursery	to cite Connie Mormon to appear at the hour of 8:00 P.M. at the regular meeting

Operator	of the Board of City Commissioners to be held November 18, 1959, in the Council

Chambers of the City Hall, to show cause, if any she has, why her licenses to
Cited to Show	operate child nurseries should not be revoked. His Honor further stated this

Cause	action was being taken because of information from the operator that children with

a contagious or communicable disease were being kept on the premises, and for
non-cooperation with officials of the City, insufficient adult supervision and
improper treatment of children.

Commissioner Whipple moved Connie Mormon be cited to appear at the hour of
8:00 P.M. at the regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners to be held
November 18, 1959, in the Council Chambers of the City Hall, to show cause, if
any she has, why her licenses to operate child nurseries should not be revoked,
and that the City Attorney transmit written charges to Connie Mormon as instructed
by the Commission.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Elwell, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor Mayor Gragson
voting aye; noes, none.
