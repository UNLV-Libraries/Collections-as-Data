ORDINANCE NO. 619

AN ORDINANCE REGULATING AND RESTRICTING THE HEIGHT OF STRUCTURES AND OBJECTS OF NATURAL GROWTH AND OTHERWISE
REGULATING THE USE OF PROPERTY WITHIN THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA, NEAR THE VICINITY OF NELLIS AIR FORCE
BASE, CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA, BY CREATING HEIGHT LIMITING ZONES AND ESTABLISHING THE BOUNDARIES THEREOF;

PROVIDING FOR CHANGES IN THE RESTRICTIONS AND BOUNDARIES OF SUCH ZONES; DEFINING CERTAIN TERMS USED HEREIN;
PROVIDING FOR ENFORCEMENT; ESTABLISHING METHODS AND PROCEDURES; IMPOSING PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS; AND
PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO.

In pursuance of the authority conferred by Chapter 110, Statutes 1941, State of Nevada, and Chapter 205
Statues, 1947 State of Nevada, and for the purpose of promoting the health, safety, and the general welfare
of the inhabitants of Las Vegas, Nevada, in cooperation with other political subdivisions by preventing the cre-
ation or establishment of flight hazards, thereby protecting the lives and property of users of Nellis Air
Force Base, and of occupants of land in the vicinity and preventing destruction or impairment of the utility
of the airfields and the public investment therein;

THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, COUNTY OF CLARK, STATE OF NEVADA, DO ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

SECTION 1. Short Title. This ordinance shall be known and may be cited as the "Airfield Zoning Ordinance of
the City of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada."

SECTION 2. Definitions. As used in this ordinance, unless the text otherwise requires:

(1)	Airfield means Nellis Air Force Base.

(2)	Flight hazard means any structure or tree or use of land which obstructs the airspace required
for the flight of aircraft in landing or taking-off, at any airport, or is otherwise hazardous to such
landing or taking off of aircraft.
