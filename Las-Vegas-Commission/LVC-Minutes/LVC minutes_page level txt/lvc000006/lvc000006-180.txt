LIQUOR APPLICATION Commissioner Moore moved that the application of Cornet Stores
CORNET STORES	for a package liquor license for the 1st quarter of 1949, at the

	new proposed store be denied on the grounds that there are no

licenses available under the provisions of the existing Resolution,
further, that applicant Mr. Cornet is not a legal resident of the
city of Las Vegas.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye;
noes, none.
