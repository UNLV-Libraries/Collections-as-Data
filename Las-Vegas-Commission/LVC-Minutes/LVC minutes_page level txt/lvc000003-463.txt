Pioneer Title Insurance and Trust Company,

Las Vegas, Nevada.

Gentlemen;

With regard to the instructions contained in our letter to you of June 15th, re-
garding the purchase of certain property owned by the Golden West Oil and Refining Company,
a corporation, you are hereby directed to disregard the instructions contained in that
letter and in lieu of those instructions you will be guilded by the following.

When you issue the City of Las Vegas title insurance in the sum of Two Thousand Five Hundred
($2,500.00) Dollars, showing a good and clear title to the City of Las Vegas, free and clear
of all encumbrances, save and except patent reservations, in and to Lots, 6,7,8 in Block 2,
and Lots 3.4.5.6.7. and 8 in Block 33 of Original Plat, Las Vegas Townsite, a shown by may
thereof in the office of the County Recorder of Clark County, Nevada, in Book 1, of Plats
page 17, you will then deliver to Golden West Oil and Refining Company of the money you now hold
for the City the cum of Two Thousand Five Hundred ($2,500.00) Dollars, which is payment in full
for the said lots.

The remaining Five Hundred ($500.00) Dollars you will pay to the said Golden West Oil and Re-
fining Company if and when you can execute to the city title insurance in the sum of Five
Hundred ($500.00) Dollars, showing title in the city free and clear of all encumbrances, save
and except patent reservations, in and to Lots 4 and 5, Block 8 of the Original Plat of Las
Vegas Townsite, as shown by map thereof in the office of the County Recorder of Clark County,
Nevada, in Book 1 of Plats, Page 17.

The golden West Oil and Refining Company, a corporation, agrees that it will immediately take
all steps necessary to furnish the city with a clear title and title insurance to Lots 4 and 5,
Block 2, of the Original Plat of Las Vegas Townsite, as shown by may thereof in the office of
the County Recorder of Clark County, Nevada, in Book 1, Plats, page 17. And it further agrees
that the said title will be furnished the city within a reasonable time, and that the expenses
of the said title and escrow will be borne by the Golden West Oil and Refining Company.

Provided, however, should you be unable within the months from the date hereof to furnish
us title insurance to Lots Four (4) and Five (5) in Block Two (2) aforesaid, as herein
provided, then and in such event, on demand of the City, you will return the said Five Hundred
Dollars ($500.00), so being held by you, to the City of Las Vegas.

It is understood and agreed that time is of the essence of this agreement.

Dated this 30th day of June, 1936.

BY AUTHORITY OF THE BOARD OF CITY COMMISSIONERS

Mayor.

Attest:

City Clerk
