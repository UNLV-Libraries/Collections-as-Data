According to your Notice of Sale, dated May 24, 1950, which is
incorporated herein by reference, there will be sealed proposals received
up to the hour of 3.30 P.M., Monday, June 26, 1950, for the sale of
Seventy-One Thousand Six Hundred and Sixty-One Dollars and Fifty-One Cents
($71,661.51) City of Las Vegas Meadows Addition Sanitary Sewer Improvement
Bonds, Series of July 1, 1950, in the denomination of One Thousand ($1,000.00)
Dollars and one bond in the denomination of Six Hundred and Sixty One Dollars
and Fifty-One Cents ($661.51).

For all, but not less than all, of the legally issued bonds, as
described above, we bid you the par value thereof, or 100% of face value,
plus accrued interest to the date of delivery

at the rate of 4% (four per cent) per annum.

and
