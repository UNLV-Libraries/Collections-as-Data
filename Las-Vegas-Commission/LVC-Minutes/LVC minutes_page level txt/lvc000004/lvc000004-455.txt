TO: HELEN SCOTT, CITY CLERK
CITY OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, is hereby
called to be held in the Board of Commissioners Room in the City Clerk's Office, Thursday
the 8th day of January, 1942, at the hour of 10:30 A. M., on said day to consider the
following:

1. The request of Governor Carville that the Mayor and Chief of Police meet with
him in Carson City, Saturday, December 10th, 1942.

MAYOR OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, a special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City

office Thursday, the 8th day of January, 1942, at the hour of 10:30 A. M., on said day to

1. The request of Governor Carville that the Mayor and Chief of Police meet with
