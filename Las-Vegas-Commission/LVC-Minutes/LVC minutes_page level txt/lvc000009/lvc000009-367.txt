CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

Date:	December 29, 1954

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada is
hereby called to be held in the Council Chambers of the City Hall, on the 29th day of
December, 1954, at the hour of 7:30 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Bid Openings:

a)	Bid No. 54.40 - Spencer Street Storm Drain

b)	Bid No. 54.41 - Repair to water pressure system at Golf Course

2.	Request of Chambers of Commerce to send Chief Elmer Gates to New York City.

3.	PUBLIC WORKS:

a)	Final acceptance and release of bond on Acres Addition.

b)	Proposed intersection at South 3rd, Utah and 5th Streets.

4.	Report on Assessment District No. 100-25 - 5th Street Lighting.

5.	Gaming Applications

6.	Liquor Applications

7.	City Managers Reports:

a)	Award of Towing Contract

b)	Meritorious Increases

s/ C. D. Baker
Mayor
