The letter notifying the Board that they were not satisfied with the streets in Southridge
Tracts Nos. 1,2,3 and 4 received from the Sub-dividers, Howard and Hassett was read to

Commissioner Peccole moved the City Engineer be advised to make a complete survey and
report on this subdivision and that the bonds be held until we have an official release,
and Howard and Hassett be notified of this action#

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner

Bunker.

At this time Mr. George Rudiak, representing Dr. Levon Kassabian Gregior Kassabian,
protesting the request of the Power Company for a use permit to extend the San Francisco
Street Substation arrived. Mr. Rudiak urged the denial of the request.

Commissioner Peccole moved this use permit be allowed.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner

Bunker.

At this time Mr. Dick White appeared before the Board protesting the treatment he has
received in the installation of sewer in the Meadows Addition. Mr. White explained
to the Board that he had requested the installation of an additional sewer outlet. Mr.
White stated that he had only two outlets while the property across the street received
six.

Commissioner Whipple moved the City Manager check the assessment paid by Mr. White as
compared to the property owners across the street and based on this report take further
action and give Mr. White a letter.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner

Bunker.

At this time the request of W. Nickols, 210½ North 9th for permission to keep goats
on his property was present for consideration.

Commissioner Whipple moved this request of Mr. Nickols be denied.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner
Bunker.
