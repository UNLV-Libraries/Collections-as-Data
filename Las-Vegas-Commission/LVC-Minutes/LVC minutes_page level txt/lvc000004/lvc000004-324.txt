CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO---VIOLA BURNS, CITY CLERK

City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas Clark County, Nevada is

hereby called to be held in the Board of Commissioners Room in the City Clerk s office Thursday the
23rd day of January, 1941 at the hour of eleven o'clock A.M. on said day to consider and act upon the
following business:

1.	To sign a lease with the U.S Government for use of the Las Vegas Airport.

Dated this 23rd day of January, 1941.

John L. Russell

Mayor of the City of Las Vegas.

NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO— John L. Russell Mayor and to Commissioners

Joe S. Ronnow, C.V.T. Gilbert, Herb Krause and
Al Corradetti of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

of the City of Las Vegas a Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners is hereby called to be
held in the Board of Commissioners Room in the City Clerk's Office Thursday the 23rd day of Jan-
uary, 1941 at the hoar of eleven o'clock A.M. on said day to consider and act upon the following:

1. To sign a lease with the U.S. Government for use of the Las Vegas Airport.

You will govern yourselves accordingly.

Viola Burns
City Clerk
