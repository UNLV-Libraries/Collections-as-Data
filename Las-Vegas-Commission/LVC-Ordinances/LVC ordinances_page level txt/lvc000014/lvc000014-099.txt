ORDINANCE NO. 234

AN ORDINANCE PROVIDING FOR THE CLEANING OF LOTS WITHIN THE LIMITS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS,

AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR THE VIOLATION THEREOF, AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS
OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH, AND PARTICULARLY REPEALING ORDINANCE NO. 91 of the
CITY OF LAS VEGAS, APPROVED, MARCH 15, 1922.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, do ordains as follows:

Section 1. Every person or person owning a lot or lots	the limits of the City of

Las Vegas, Clark County, State of Nevada, are hereby required to clean the debris, rubbish
garbage, weeks over four inches in height, and brush from the same on or before the 30th
day of April of each year, commencing with the year 1938; and thereafter also to clean lot
or lots of debris, rubbish, garbage, weeks over four inchces in height, and brush, within
ten days after notice, either verbal or in writing, given to such person or persons by the
Commissioners of Streets and Public Property in the City of Las Vegas. Failure to comply
with any of the provisions of this section or with the requirements of such notice shall be
a misdemeanor and every person found guilty of violating any of the provisions of this
Section or any requirement of any such notice sall, upon conviction thereof, be fined not
less than Five Dollars ($5.00) nor more than Three Hundred Dollars ($300) or imprisonment
in the City Jail until such fine	is paid at the rate of	not exceeding one day	for	each

Two Dollars of such fine in case	such fine is not paid,	or by imprisonment in	the	City Jail

not to exceed three months or by both such fine and imprisonment.

PROVIDED, that when there shall exist debris, rubbish garbage, weeks over four inches
in height, or bursh upon a lot or lots owned by a non-resident of the City of Las Vegas
 contrary to the provisions of this ordinance, a written notice shall be posted by or at
the instance of such Commissioner of Streets and Public Property in a conspicuous place
on each lot in view of the public, notifying the owner or owners of said lot or lots to
clean the same within ten days form the date of posting of such notice; and in the event of
the failure of such owner or owners to clean such lot or lots within ten days after the
date of posting such notice as aforesaid, the cleaning of said lot or lots shall be done
on behalf of said City of Las Vegas by the Commissioner of Streets and Public Property
thereof and the cost thereof shall become a debt in favor of said City of Las Vegas against
the owner or owners of such lots	or lot so cleaned, and	the amount thereof may be	recovered

from the owner or owners of such	lot or lots in a civil	action to be brought	and	prosecuted

in any court of competent jurisdiction.

SECTION 2. Ordinance Number 91 of the City of Las Vegas entitled "AN ORDINANCE PROVIDING FOR
THE CLEANING OF LOTS WITHIN THE LIMITS OF CLARK'S LAS VEGAS TOWNSITE IN THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS
AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR THE VIOLATION THEREOF AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS
OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH," Approved arch 15, 1922 is hereby expressly repealed
and all other ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict with the Ordinances are here-
by repealed.

SECTION 3. The City Clerk is hereby authorized to have this Ordinance published in the
Las Vegas Review-Journal, a newspaper printed and published in the City of Las Vegas, Clark
County, Nevada, for a period of two weeks, that is to say, once each week for a period of
two weeks.

H.P. Marble

Mayor Pro Tem.

Attest:

Viola Burns
City Clerk

day of April, A.D. 1938, by the following vote: Commissioners Krause and Ronnow and His
