The Commission considered the request of Continental Air Lines that the City petition
the CAB for additional service into Las Vegas.

Commissioner Sharp moved that the City of Las Vegas file a petition to intervene
in the Service to Phoenix Case of the Continental Air Lines.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Bunker,Fountain, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes,
none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

Mrs. Jeanne Houssels appeared before the Commission concerning the application of
property owners to rezone Paradise Park from R-3 and R-4 to R-l. Mrs. Houssels re-
quested that the Building Department be directed to stop the issuance of building
permits in this area until the applications for rezoning can be considered.

Commissioner Bunker moved this matter be held in abeyance until the next regular
meeting.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Bunker,Fountain,Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes,
none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

At this time Mr. Mel Cooper and his attorney, Mr. Harry Claiborne appeared before
the Commission concerning the stop order placed on his apartments at 129-131 Balti-
more Avenue.

Mr. Cooper presented the plans for his construction as approved by the Planning Com-
mission, and stated that there were only eight units to be rented.

Discussion was held and Mr. Cooper was advised that this apartment house would be
checked when completed to see that there were only eight rentals.

Commissioner Sharp moved the Mayor and Clerk be authorized to sign the contract
with Motorola Communications and Electronics for the maintenance of radio units.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye;
noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

City Manager Kennedy requested authorization for payment of $1400 for Address-O-
Graph plates and printing for the sewer rental bills.

Commissioner Sharp moved this payment be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye;
noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

There being no further business to come before the Board, Commissioner Sharp moved
this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye;
noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.
