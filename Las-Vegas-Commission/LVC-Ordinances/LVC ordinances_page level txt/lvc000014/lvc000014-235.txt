ORDINANCE NO. 304

AN ORDINANCES OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS CREATING AND ESTABLISHING FIRE ZONES.

THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS DO ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

Fire District, and said Fire District shall be know and designated as Fires Zones, One (1),

Two (2) and Two Special (2s), and Three (3). The fire zones shall comprise and included such
territory or portions of the City of Las Vegas as outlined and described in Sections 3, 4, and
5 of this Ordinance.

altered, repaired, or converted shall be subject to the "Requirements Based on Location in

ments thereto. -
