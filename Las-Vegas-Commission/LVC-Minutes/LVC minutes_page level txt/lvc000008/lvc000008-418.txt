WHEREAS, C.C. BOYER is at the present time unable to attend to his duties
as the City Engineer of the City of Las Vegas, due to illness; and

WHEREAS, there are several final subdivision maps ready to be signed and
approved by the City of Las Vegas,

NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED;

That J.D. GOULETTE, Senior Assistant City Engineer of the City of Las Vegas,
and a Registered Professional Engineer of the State of Nevada, License No. 719,
be and he is hereby authorized and directed to execute in the place and stead of
the City Engineer the required certificate upon final subdivision maps during the
absence and illness of C.C. BOYER,

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that this authorization applies to subdivision maps

only.
