The Commission reconsidered the acceptance of Virginia Manor #2 and the re-
lease of bond. Mr. Giger, representing the Subdivider D. H. Hand was present
and advised the Board that Mr. Hand was willing to put up a cash bond to cover
the access street of Van Buren from "N" Street to Highland Drive, and re-
quested the release of the bond.

Commissioner Whipple moved the bond for Virginia Manor #2 subdivision be
released subject to the Subdivider furnishing a bond to cover the access
Street in an amount to be determined by the Engineering Department.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:
Commissioners Bunker and Fountain.
