ORDINANCE No. 335 An ordinance entitled, "AN ORDINANCE TO REPEAL SECTION 11 OF

ORDINANCE No. 325 OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS ENTITLED, ' AN ORDIN-
	ANCE TO PROHIBIT GAMING AND THE OPERATION OF SLOT MACHINES IN

THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS WITHOUT FIRST OBTAINING A LICENSE THERE-
FORE; REGULATING THE SAME: FIXING THE AMOUNT OF LICENSE; PROVIDING
THE PENALTY FOR VIOLATION THEREOF; AND REPEALING ORDINANCES NOS.

77, 82, 88, 103, 116, 165, 245, 271, 287 and 319 AND ALL ORDIN-
ANCES AND PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH AND OTHER
MATTERS PROPERLY RELATING THERETO'," having been read to the
Board by title at the July 7 meeting and referred to a committee
composed of Commissioners Baskin and Moore for recommendation
at this meeting, which said committee reported favorably on the
proposed ordinance.

Thereafter said ordinance was read in full to the Board and upon
motion of Commissioner Moore, seconded by Commissioner Baskin
and carried by the following vote: Commissioners Baskin, Clark,
Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. The ordin-
ance was adopted on its first reading.
