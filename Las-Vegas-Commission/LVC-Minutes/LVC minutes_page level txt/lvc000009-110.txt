The application of Gerald A. Nelson for variance to construct a duplex
within 15 feet of the front property line and within 7 feet of the side
property line facing Bonneville and within 10 feet of the garage, in
addition to a single family residence which exists on the property
located on the NE comer of Bonneville and 4th Street, as recommended
by the Board of Adjustments, provided that adequate off street parking
is provided, was considered at this time.

Thereafter Commissioner Sharp moved the recommendation of the Board of
Adjustments be accepted with the stipulation that adequate off street
parking be provided and the following resolution adopted:

BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
in a regular meeting on the 3th day of May, 1954, having considered the
application of Gerald A. Nelson for a variance to construct a duplex
within 13 feet of the front property line and within 7 feet of the side
property line facing Bonneville and within 10 feet of the garage, in
addition to a single family residence which exists on the property
located on the NE comer of Bonneville and 4th Street; and

The Board having considered the decision of the Board of Adjustment
recommending approval of this application provided that adequate off
street parking be approved, and

The applicant, Gerald A. Nelson, having provided adequate off street
parking at this time.

NOW, THEREFORE, it is the decision of the Board that the application
of Gerald A. Nelson, V-27-54 as approved by the Board of Adjustment, be
and the same is hereby granted, and the Inspection Departments are
authorized to issue the necessary permits.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.
