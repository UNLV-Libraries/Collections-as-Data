OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, CITY OF LAS VEGAS,

CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA,

DECEMBER 19th, 1932.

Minutes of a recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las
Vegas, held on the 19th day of December, 1932 at the hour of three o'clock P.M.

Roll call showed the following present, Commissioners Mundy, Hansell and Thomas, City
Clerk and acting City Attorney.

Absent Mayor Cragin and Commissioner German.

The contractors for the Federal Building now under construction in the City of Las Vegas
appeared before the Board of Commissioners and petitioned that body for permission to tie into
the sewer main on Stewart Street in front of the said building.

On motion of Commissioner Thomas seconded by Commissioner Hansell it was moved that
the contractors for the Federal Building be granted permission to tie into the sewer main on
Stewart Street in front of the said building and that the work must be done by a licensed plumb-
er of the City of Las Vegas, and that no fee be demanded of the Federal government for tieing
into the said sewer main or for inspections or fixtures fees.

Vote on said motion was as follows: Commissioners Mundy, Thomas and Hansell voting
aye. Noes none.

On motion of Commissioner Hansell seconded by Commissioner Thomas it was moved
that one slot machine license be granted to Robert Robinson. Application being in proper form
and money to cover same being on file with the Clerk.

Vote on said motion was as follows: Commissioners Mundy, Thomas and Hansell voting
aye. Noes none.
