Meeting called to order at the hour of 3:10 p.m. with the following

Commissioner Clark and City Manager T. E. Fennessy.

RESOLUTION	The following Resolution was presented to the Board for their consideration:

RE: Las Vegas

Housing	R E S O L U T I 0 N

Authority

WHEREAS:

The City Council of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada have appointed a
disposition committee of five representative persons to recommend the
ultimate disposition of the government housing project situated in the
corporate limits of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada and known as Nevada
26021; and

WHEREAS:

The disposition committee has reported and has recommended that the
Housing Authority of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada make application to 

the Federal Public Housing Authority for the acquisition of this project
for the exclusive use for rent of persons of low income; and

WHEREAS:

The Housing Authority of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada have passed
a Resolution making application to the Federal Public Housing Authority
for the acquisition of the Kelso Turner Housing Project, situated in
the City of Las Vegas, Nevada and known as Nevada 26021 for the ex-
clusive use for rental for persons of low income; and

WHEREAS:

The Housing Authority of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada did in such
Resolution and Application agree to:

(1)	to operate the project as a low rent housing project

(2)	not to sell or otherwise dispose of the project during its
economic life.

(3)	turn all net revenues over to the government for a fixed
period of years, which fixed period of years shall be
determined on the basis of the estimated life of the project
for decent, safe and sanitary low-rent purposes;

(4)	that all of the property and assets of the Housing Authority
of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada will be exempt from the State
and local taxation except for in lieu of payments established
by policy; and

WHEREAS:

It is necessary that the City Council of the city of Las Vegas, Nevada
confirm and approve the Resolution adopted by the Housing Authority of
the City of Las Vegas, Nevada;

NOW, THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED:

That the City Council of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada does here
and now confirm and approve of the Resolution adopted by the Housing
Authority of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada for the acquisition of
Nevada 26021 for the uses as aforesaid; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED:

That the City clerk of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada shall forward
to the Federal Public Housing Authority at San Francisco, California
three (3) certified copies of the extracts of Minutes showing this
Resolution and one copy to the Housing Authority of the City of Las Vegas
Nevada.

Thereafter Commissioner Moore moved the adoption of the foregoing
Resolution.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye.

Noes, None. Absent; Commissioner Clark.
