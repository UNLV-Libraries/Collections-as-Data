FIRE HYDRANTS	City Manager Kennedy requested approval of Purchase Requisition #4822 for 50 - A-24009 

Purchase Requisition	Improved Fire Hydrants, color yellow, three foot (3') bury, conforming to Las Vegas City

Specifications at an estimated cost of $130 each; total, $6,500, to the Mueller Company, 2801
Approved	E. 12th Street, Los Angeles 23, California.

Mr. Kennedy explained that the City's existing stock is being expended quite rapidly and that
this requisition will be required inasmuch as approximately 6 weeks is required for delivery.

Many existing approved subdivisions will require more hydrants than in stock at the present
time.

Commissioner Sharp moved that Purchase Requisition #4822 for 50-A-24009 Improved Fire Hydrants,
color yellow, at an estimated cost of $130 each; total, $6,500 to the Mueller Company, 2801 E.
12th Street, Los Angeles 23, California, be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners	

Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

TOWN TAVERN - LICENSE	City Manager Kennedy requested that the Liquor and Gaming Licenses of Earl Turman et al,

SUSPENSION	doing business as the Town Tavern, 600 Jackson Street be suspended based on police 

investigation and report.

Show Cause Order

Commissioner Whipple moved that the Liquor and Gaming Licenses of Earl Turman et al, doing
business as the Town Tavern, 600 Jackson Street, be suspended effective immediately and
