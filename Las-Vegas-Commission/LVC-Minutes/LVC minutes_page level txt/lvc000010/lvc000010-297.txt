Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Fountain.

City Manager Kennedy requested permission to secure bids for the purchase of cable and
boxes to be used in connection with the new fire alarm system.

.Commissioner Bunker moved the request of the City Manager be approved.

Bunker, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Fountain.
