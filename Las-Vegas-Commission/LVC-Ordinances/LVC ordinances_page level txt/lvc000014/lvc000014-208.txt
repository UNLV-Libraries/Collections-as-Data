ORDINANCE NUMBER 290

AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND SECTION 11 OF ORDINANCE NUMBER 250 OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS ENTITLED
"AN ORDINANCE REGULATING THE SALE OF MILK, CREAM AND OTHER DAIRY PRODUCTS IN THE CITY OF
LAS VEGAS: DEFINING STANDARD FOR MILK AND CREAM: PROVIDING FOR THE ISSUANCE OF PERMITS FUR
THE SALE OF MILK AND CREAM AND THE REVOCATION OF SUCH PERMITS: PROVIDING FOR THE CREATION
OF A MILK ORDINANCE COMMITTEE: PROVIDING FOR THE APPOINTMENT OF A DAIRY AND MILK INSPECTOR:
AUTHORIZING THE CITY COMMISSIONERS TO PROMULGATE RULES AND REGULATIONS FOR THE ENFORCEMENT
OF THIS ORDINANCE: PROVIDING A PENALTY FOR THE VIOLATION OF THIS ORDINANCE: REPEALING
ORDINANCE NUMBER 200 OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, ENTITLED 'AN ORDINANCE REGULATING THE SALE
OF MILK, CREAM, AND OTHER DAIRY PRODUCTS IN THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS: DEFINING STANDARD FOR
MILK AND CREAM: PROVIDING FOR THE ISSUANCE OF PERMITS FOR THE SALE OF MILK AND CREAM, AND
THE REVOCATION OF SUCH PERMITS: AUTHORIZING THE CITY COMMISSIONERS TO PROMULGATE RULES AND
REGULATIONS FOR THE ENFORCEMENT OF THIS ORDINANCE: PROVIDING A PENALTY FOR THE VIOLATION
THEREOF: AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH:' AND
REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT WITH THIS ORDINANCE.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

Section 1. Section 11 of Ordinance Number 250 is hereby amended to read as follows:

All expenses for examination and treatment of livestock shall be paid by the owners
or operators of the dairy herds of cows being responsible except such as that given during
regular routine inspection.
