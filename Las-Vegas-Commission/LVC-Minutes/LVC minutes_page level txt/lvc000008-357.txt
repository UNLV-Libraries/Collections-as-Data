CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

April 20, 1953

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, is hereby called
to be held in the Council Chambers at the City Hall, on the 20th day of April, 1953, at the hour
of 7:30 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Rezoning Public Hearing:	Proposed Heers Subdivision (3) (West of Twin Lakes),

and Louis J. Ortner (Meadows Addition) from R-3 to R-4.

2.	Public Hearing - appeal of variance of Dr. Martin W. Payne. His request to
have an orthopedic surgery office on the NW comer of 7th and Hoover was den-
ied by the Planning Commission, This Hearing tonight is on Dr, Payne’s ap-
peal of the Planning Commissions' denial,

3.	Opening of bids for coolers for the Police Department.

4. Assessment District. Assessment Roll for 100-20. Public Hearing.

5.	Slum Clearance Reports.

6.	Payroll Warrants,

_____s/ C.D. Baker
