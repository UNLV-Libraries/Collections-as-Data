A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners held this lbth day of June, 1949 was
called to order at the hour of 4:15 P.M. by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin with the following
members present:	Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple; City Manager J. M. Murphy,

City Attorney Howard W. Cannon and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger.

At this time His Honor read a letter under date of April 20, 1949 from the Nevada
Biltmore Hotel by B. Mahlon Brown, Secretary, heretofore received by t he Board of
Commissioners .

Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved that inasmuch as the Nevada Biltmore Hotel by
its secretary B. Mahlon Brown, had informed this Board that they had disposed of their
interest in said Nevada Biltmore Hotel Corporation, the Retail Tavern License No. 791,
Retail Cabaret License No. 792, and the Gaming License No. 1421 are hereby revoked.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

NEVADA BILTMORE HOTEL Commissioner Whipple moved that the application of S. D. Hunter, under date of April 22,
Gaming Application Denied l949 for a Retail Tavern and gaming license at the Nevada Biltmore Hotel, 600 North Main,

	be denied.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

There being no further business to come before this special meeting, Commissioner Bunker
moved that this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
