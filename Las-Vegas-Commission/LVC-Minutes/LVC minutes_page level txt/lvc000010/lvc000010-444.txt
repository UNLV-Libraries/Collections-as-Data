(CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING)

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada held
this l4th day of March, 1957 was called to order at the hour of 4:50 P.M. by His Honor
Mayor Baker with the following members present:

Mayor	C.D. Baker

Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

Commissioner	Harris P. Sharp

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

City Manager	A.H. Kennedy

Asst. City Attorney	Ralston O. Hawkins

City Clerk	Shirley Lodwick

Absent:	Commissioner	E.H. Fountain

City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

Commissioner Whipple moved that a telegram be sent to the Clark County Delegation at the
Legislature requesting their support of the Convention Hall legislation.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Fountain.

Commissioner Sharp moved the creation of the Department of Public Safety be approved, to
include the Police and Fire Departments, and that Elmer Gates be appointed as Acting
Director of Public Safety at the pleasure of the Board in addition to his duties as Fire
Chief at a salary of $900.00 per month.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner

Fountain.

There being no further business to came before the Board, Commissioner Sharp moved this
meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Fountain.
