His Honor asked if there were any objections. None were voiced.

Mr. Keefer appeared before the Board at this time on behalf of the petitioners,
requesting that this vacation be allowed.

Commissioner Jarrett moved that this request for vacation of Bonneville between 13th
and 14th Streets be continued until April 20th to obtain a report from the Traffic
and Parking Commission.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
