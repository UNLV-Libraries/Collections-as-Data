SEWER AGREEMENT
Strip Hotels

SIGN DOWN
11th & Franklin

Commissioner Sharp moved that the City Clerk be instructed to notify the various
hotels included in the sewer agreement to pay sewer rental directly to the Riviera
Hotel. The Riviera will be credited with such payments toward the $100,000.00
they have advanced.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

At this time a complaint regarding a traffic sign which was knocked down at the
corner of 11th and Franklin Streets was considered by the Board.

Commissioner Sharp moved that this traffic sign be replaced at the corner of 11th
and Franklin Streets.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

There being no further business to come before the Board of Commissioners, Commis-
sioner Whipple moved that the meeting be adjourned.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
