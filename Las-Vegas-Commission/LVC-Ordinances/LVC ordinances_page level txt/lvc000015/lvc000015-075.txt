less than thirty (30) days nor more than six (6) months, or by both such
fine and imprisonment. Any person whose operator's or chauffeur's license
has been revoked, as herein provided, who shall drive a motor vehicle
within the City of Las Vegas, while sale revocation is in effect shall be

by a fine of not more than five hundred dollars ($500.00), or imprison-
ment in the City Jail for not more than six (6) months, or by both such
fine and imprisonment.
