To: Howell C. Garrison, Mayor, and to Pat Clark, M. C. Tinch, Arthur F. Smith, Sr., and
A. P. Rubidoux, Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

YOU AND EACH OF YOU WILL PLEASE TAKE NOTICE: that pursuant to a call this day issued by
the Mayor of the City of Las Vegas, a special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the
City of Las Vegas, Nevada will be held in the Board of Commissioner's Room in the City Clerk's
Office Friday the 12th day of December, 1941 at the hour of 7:30 P.M on said day to con-
sider and act upon the following business:

1. To consider and act upon the request for approval of the Huntridge Subdivision.

You will govern yourselves accordingly.

City Clerk

Minutes of a special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,

Clark County, Nevada held on the 12th day of December, 1941, at the hour of 7:50 P.M.

Present: Mayor Garrison. Commissioners Tinch, Clark, Smith and Rubidoux. City
Clerk, Helen Scott. City Engineer, Harlan T. Brown.

Absent: Paul Ralli, City Attorney,

Commissioner Rubidoux moved that the plat for the subdivision "Huntridge" be approved,
and that the Mayor and city clerk be empowered to sign said plat. Motion seconded by
Commissioner Clark and carried by the following vote. Commissioners Smith, Tinch, Clark,
Rubidoux and His Honor voting aye. Noes, hone.

Commissioner Smith moved that notice of this Special Meeting nowin session be and
is hereby amended so as to take action conferring authority upon the Mayor and City Clerk
to sign request for excise tax exemption and the document captioned "PD-1 priority
rating application" be incorporated in the Minutes by reference. Motion seconded by
Commissioner Clark and carried by the following vote. Commissioners Tinch, Smith, Clark,
Rubidoux and His Honor voting aye. Noes, none.

Commissioner Smith moved that the Mayor and City Clerk be and they hereby are, author-
ized to sign requests for priority ratings or tax exemptions without further action of the
Board of City Commission rs for such material as may be deemed necessary and desired. Motion
seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following vote. Commissioners Smith,

Tinch, Clark, Rubidoux and His Honor voting aye. Noes, none.

Commissioner Clark moved that this meeting adjourn until December 16th, 1941 at the hour
of 7:50 P.M. Motion seconded by Commissioner Tinch and carried by the following vote. Com-
missioners Smith, Tinch, Clark, Rubidoux and His Honor voting aye. Noes, none.
