OFFICE OF THE CITY CLARK CITY OF LAS VEGAS
CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

MARCH 11th, 1935.

Minutes of a recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
held on the 11th day of March 1935 at the hour of three o'clock P.M.

The following answered roll call, Mayor Cragin, Commissioners Arnett, Marble, German
City Attorney Henderson and City Clerk.

Absent Commissioner Down.

At this time C.F. Reid appeared before the Board in regard to securing a gaming license for the
second quarter year for 224 No. 1st St. He was assured by the Board that they would approve the
granting of such a license at the start of the new quarter.

No further business appearing before the Board at this time the meeting recessed until the
15th day of March at the hour of three o'clock P.M.
