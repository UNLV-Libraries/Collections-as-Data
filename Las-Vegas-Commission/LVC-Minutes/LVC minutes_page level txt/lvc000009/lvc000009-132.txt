WHEREAS, the Board of City Commissioners believes that the citizens
of the City of Las Vegas will gladly take voluntary conservation measures
in the use of water so that thei neighbors in the City who are less
fortunate, may have sufficient water during the daytime to attend to the
common necessities of life.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the Board of Commissioners of
the City of Las Vegas that all residents of the City of Las Vegas are
asked to refrain from watering lawns, trees, gardens, and using water
for other agricultural purposes between the hours of 8:00 A.M. and
6:00 P.M. of each day from this date until June 1, 1954; and provided
further, that the alternate watering days heretofore provided by pro-
clamation shall apply during the permitted watering hours.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes,
none. Absent:	Commissioner Jarrett.

There being no further business to come before the Board at this time,
Commissioner Whipple moved this meeting adjourn.
