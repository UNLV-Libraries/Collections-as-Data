An ordinance entitled:	AN ORDINANCE CREATING SANITARY SEWER IMPROVEMENT ASSESS-

MENT DISTRICT NO. 200-13; PROVIDING PUBLIC IMPROVEMENTS BY INSTALLING A SANITARY
SEWER ALONG CERTAIN STREETS AND PORTIONS OF STREETS THEREIN; PROVIDING FOR SPECIAL
ASSESSMENTS ACCORDING TO BENEFITS TO DEFRAY THE STATED ENTIRE COST THEREOF; DIRECT-
ING THE PREPARATION OF AN ASSESSMENT ROLL AND THE REPORTING OF THE SAME TO THE
BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS; PROVIDING FOR FILING OF SAID ROLL WITH THE CITY CLERK;
PROVIDING A TIME AND PLACE TO REVIEW SAID ASSESSMENTS AND NOTIFICATION THEREOF;
PROVIDING FOR THE CORRECTION, AND/OR CONFIRMATION AND APPROVAL OF SAID ROLL THEREAT
OR THEREAFTER; PROVIDING FOR THE ENDORSEMENT ON SAID ROLL OF SAID CONFIRMATION AND
APPROVAL; PROVIDING FOR THE DELIVERY OF SAID APPROVED ROLL TO THE EX-OFFICIO CITY
ASSESSOR; PROVIDING THAT SAID ASSESSMENT SHALL CONSTITUTE A LIEN; PRESCRIBING THE
METHOD OF PAYING SAID ASSESSMENTS AND THE INTEREST THEREON; PROVIDING FOR THE
DISPOSITION OF SAID ASSESSMENTS; DIRECTING THE NOTIFICATION BY MAIL WEEN AND WHERE
SAID ASSESSMENT IS DUE AND PAYABLE; RATIFYING, APPROVING AND CONFIRMING ALL ACTION
HERETOFORE TAKEN TOWARD MAKING SAID IMPROVEMENT IN SAID DISTRICT, TOWARD US CREA-
TION AND TOWARD LEVYING AND EFFECTING SPECIAL ASSESSMENTS; AND PROVIDING OTHER
MATTERS RELATING THERETO, was read by title and referred to Commissioners Fountain
and Whipple.

Commissioner Fountain moved the City Manager be instructed to request the Southern
Nevada Telephone Company to appear at the Commission meeting of February 1, 1956
to report on the progress being made on the installation of telephones in new
subdivisions.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Bunker, Fountain, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:
Commissioner Sharp.

WATER SOFTENING EQUIPMENT; PROVIDING FOR THE ISSUANCE OF PERMITS; PROVIDING FOR

Memorial Hospital for care of the jail prisoners, retroactive to May, 1955.

missioners Bunker, Fountain, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Ab-
sent:	Commissioner Sharp.
