OF EVERY KIND OF LAWFUL BUSINESS TRANSACTED OR CARRIED ON WITHIN THE CORPORATE LIMITS OF THE CITY OF
US.VEGAS, STATE OF NEVADA: FIXING THE RATES OF LICENSE TAX AND REGUUTION; PROVIDING FOR THE COLLECTION
OF LICENSE FEES; PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO; PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR THE VIOLATION
OF THIS ORDINANCE; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH.

WHEREAS, the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas has been considering -the licensing
procedures and ordinances of the City with the purpose in mind of making changes as a convenience to
the citizens of the City of Las Vegas; and

WHEREAS, a postcard survey was made by the City of persons not already licensed on an annual
basis, and such survey is sufficiently favorable to warrant a change of all license fees to be on
a semi-annual basis.

NOW THEREFORE, THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF US VEGAS DOES ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

SECTION 1, Notwithstanding (the provisions of any other ordinance of the City of Las Vegas, alL
license fees heretofore paid on a quarterly basis required by any ordinance of the City of Las Vegas
shall be paid on a semi-annual basis as hereinafter provided.

SECTION 2. Any person licensed by the City of Las Vegas whose semi-annual license fee is
greater than $750.00 may pay such license fee in installments as follows:

50/£ on the due date, and the remaining $0% within ninety (90) days from the due date.

SECTION 3. Permanent licenses shall be issued by the Supervisor of Licensing and Revenue of
the City of Las Vegas, authorizing a person to conduct a lawful business within the City, regardless
of whether the fees to conduct said business are paid annually, semi-annually, or on any other basis.
Such permanent license shall be prominently displayed in the place of business for which it is issued
and shall be retained by the licensee as long as his license is in effect.

SECTION U. All businesses required to have a license under any of the ordinances of the City
of Las Vegas shall be notified before the due date, of each license fee, provided* however, that the
failure to notify any lawful business shall not be held to waive the payment of a business license,
and provided further that the actual receipt of such notice is in no case required.
