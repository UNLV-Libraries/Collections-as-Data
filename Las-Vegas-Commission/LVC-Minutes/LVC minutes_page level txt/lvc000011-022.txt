RESOLUTIONS ACCEPTING 	The Board considered a deed from Homer P. and Irene E. Carey for a 50 foot right-of-
R/W DEEDS	way for Washington Avenue vest of Pecos Drive.

Washington Avenue	On motion of Commissioner Fountain, duly seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried

unanimously, the following resolution is adopted and order made:

RESOLUTION OF ACCEPTANCE OF QUITCLAIM DEED

On motion of Commissioner Fountain, duly seconded by Commissioner Bunker, and
carried unanimously, the following Resolution is adopted, and order made:

BE IT RESOLVED, this 18 day of December, 1957; by the Board of Commissioners
of the City of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada, that the Deed, dated
the 9 day of December, 1957; executed by Homer P. Carey and Irene E. Carey to the
City of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada, and that the real property
described therein be, and is hereby accepted, for the purpose of street; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that a copy of this Resolution be attached to said
Deed, and that the same be recorded in the Office of the County Recorder of the
County of Clark, State of Nevada, and this resolution be filed in the records of
this Board.
