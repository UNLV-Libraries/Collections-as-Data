At this time the Board considered the request of the United States Government
for additional land in portions of Section 28 and Section 33, T19S, R62E, MDB&M.

Thereafter Commissioner Bunker moved this request be granted subject to ingress and
egress to property the City still retains in this area.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner

Whipple.

At this time the Board considered the petition of Christian V. Carstensen and others
to vacate a portion of North 9th Street extending west of Block 3, Lots 2-6,

Grandview Addition to the east line of Lot "I", Bucks Addition and the east line of
9th Street.

Thereafter Commissioner Bunker moved the public hearing on this petition for vacation
be set for July 21, 1954 at 7:30 P.M.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Jarrett, Bunker, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:

Commissioner Whipple.

It was reported that the President of the Union Pacific Railroad would be in Las
Vegas the end of June to discuss further the matter of the disposition of the Old
Ranch property.

Commissioner Sharp moved to hold approval on the tentative map of Dawson Park in
abeyance pending additional information on this matter.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:

Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, none
Absent:	Commissioner Whipple.

Commissioner Sharp moved the tentative map of the Magrini Subdivision be approved subject
to a 60 foot street being obtained by the Right Of Way Department.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner

Whipple.

Commissioner Jarrett moved the request of the Clark County Sheriffs Relief Assn for
permission to hold a Fireworks Display at Cashman Field on July 3, 1954, be
approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner

Whipple.

At this time Commissioner Bunker member of the committee for recommendation on the
proposed agriculture ordinance asked for additional time before reporting.

Commissioners Jarrett and Sharp members of the Committee on recommendation on the
proposed height regulation ordinance asked for additional time before reporting.

At this time Commissioner Jarrett member of the committee on recommendation on the
proposed setback ordinance asked for additional time before reporting.

At this time an ordinance entitled:	AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND SECTION IV OF ORDINANCE

NO. 378, REFERRED TO AS SECTION 10 CHAPTER 24, of THE LAS VEGAS CITY CODE, 1949,

BY AMENDING THE LAND USE PLAN MAP ADOPTED BY THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, AND CHANGING
THE ZONE DESIGNATION OF CERTAIN AREAS ON THE SAID MAP, was read by title to the Board
and referred to Commissioners Jarrett and Sharp for study and recommendation.

At this time the Commission considered leasing property at 4th and Ogden for a
parking lot.

Thereafter Commissioner Sharp moved that the City Engineer, City Attorney and Chief
of Police be authorized to negotiate with B. H. Johnson for the leasing of Lots 17,

18, 19 and 20, Block 23; CLVT, and report back to the Commission at the next meeting
with a form of contract.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner

Whipple.

At this time further discussion was held on the approval of the final map of the
Scotch "80" Addition.

Thereafter Commissioner Sharp moved the final resubdivision map be approved.
