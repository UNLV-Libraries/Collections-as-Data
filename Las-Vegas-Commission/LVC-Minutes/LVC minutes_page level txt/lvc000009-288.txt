POLICY ON PURCHASE
Procedure

Commissioner Sharp moved the City Engineer be authorized to proceed with a study of
the cost of a central heating system and refrigeration for the entire City Hall,
with a breakdown for specific sections.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:

Commissioners Bunker, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor Mayor Baker voting aye;
noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Jarrett.

Commissioner Bunker moved the City Manager be instructed to proceed immediately
to furnish sufficient help to the Municipal Court to bring the records up to
standard and the maintenance of the operation as recommended by the Grand Jury.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:

Commissioners Bunker, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Absent:	Commissioner Jarrett.

Mayor Baker stated that he felt the Department Heads should analyze the
competent employees they may lose because of insufficient salaries and make
recommendations to the City Manager.

Commissioner Whipple moved that the spending of money, outside of emergencies, not be
allowed without going to bid or the the Board, of purchases over $1,000.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:

Commissioners Bunker, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Absent:	Commissioner Jarrett.

There being no further business to come before the Board at this time, Commissioner
Whipple moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner

Jarrett.

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

Date:	September 9, 1954

TO: SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk
Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, is
hereby called to be held in the Council Chambers of the City Hall on the 9th day
of September, 1954, at the hour of 4:30 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Report of the City Engineer on Sewer Program.

a)	Award of Schedule D bid - sewer improvement.

b)	Award of bid for Pumping Plant.

2.	Dawson Park Tentative Map.

3.	Bids for football uniforms for Junior League
Football Team.

4.	Request of Charles J. Ketcham for extension of time
on Use Permit for shopping center on Bonanza Rd.

5. Award of the following bids:

a) 54.28 - Furniture & Supplies

b) 54.29 - Blueprint Machine

6. Protest of Use Permit of Rancho Drive Realty.

s/ C. D. BAKER
Mayor
