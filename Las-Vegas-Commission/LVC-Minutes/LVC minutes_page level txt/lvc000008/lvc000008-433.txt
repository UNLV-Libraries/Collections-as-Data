His Honor stated that this was the time set for the public hearing on the proposed increase
Of rates in garbage fees, and asked any interested parties to speak on the matter.

Various property owners entered into the discussion with members of the Board of Commissioners
and Mr. C. T. Hibbs, CPA who audited the accounts of the Southern Nevada Disposal Company,
Inc.
