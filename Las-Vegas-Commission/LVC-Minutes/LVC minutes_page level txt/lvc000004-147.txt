RESOLUTION

BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Clark County,

Nevada, that the above and foregoing NOTICE OF PROPOSED STREET IMPROVEMENT be given to the public
and that a copy of said notice be published for at least two weeks in the Las Vegas Evening
Review-Journal, a Daily newspaper printed and published in the City of Las Vegas, Clark County,
Nevada, and that copies of said notice be posted in at least three public places in each ward
in said City, and that copies of said notice be posted in at least three public places near the
site of said proposed work.
