Section 3. The officials now or hereafter charged by law with the duty of levying
taxes for the payment of said bonds and interest shall in the manner provided by law,
make ab annual levy of an additional tax upon the property, real and personal, within
the limits of the City, sufficient to meet the annual payments or principal and semi-
annual payments of interest on said bonds maturing as herein provided. The proceeds from
the taxes levied shall be kept by the County Treasurer, ex-officio City Treasurer, in a
special fund to be know as "Las Vegas Sewerage Fund," and the funds deposited therein
shall be used only for the payment of interest on said bonds, as the same becomes due
and payable, and in the payment of the principal of and retirement of said bonds as they
respectively become due and payable under the terms of this Ordinance.

Section 4. Be it further ordained that the Mayor and City Clerk are hereby 
authorized to execute said bonds for and on behalf of the City substantially in the manner and
form aforesaid, and to deliver the same to the purchaser or purchasers thereof, upon
payment of the purchase price thereof. The proceeds of the sale of said bonds shall be
placed in a special fund and shall be used exclusively for the purposes above specified in
such manner and to such extent as the Board of Commissioners may by ordinance, order or
resolution from time to time direct.
