OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, LAS VEGAS,

CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

JANUARY 20th, 1931.

Minutes of a recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas, held on the 20th day of January 1931 at the hour of three o'clock P.M.

Present Mayor Hesse, Commissioners Thomas, and Smith together with the City Attorney
and Cterk.

Absent Commissioners German, and Hansell.

Minutes of previous meetings read and approved as read.

The Board at this time discussed the business of drilling a well on the park site.

No action was taken on this matter at this time.

No further business appearing before the Board at this time the meeting recessed
until Wednesday the 21st day of January 1931 at the hour of three o'clock P.M.

OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, CITY OF LAS VEGAS,

CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

JANUARY 21st, 1931.

At a recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,
held on the 21st day of January 1931 at the hour of three o'clock P.M.

Present Mayor Hesse, Commissioners Smith, German, Thomas together with the City attor-
ney and Clerk.

Absent Commissioner Hansell.

Upon motion of Commissioner Smith, seconded by Commissioner German it was moved to
transfer $400.00 from the Publicity Fund to the General Fund. Said $400.00 had been borrowed
from the Publicity Fund from the General Fund several month previous.

Vote on said motion was as follows: Commissioners German, Thomas, and Smith, and His
Honor the Mayor voting Aye. Noes none.

Mr. J.P. King was present at the meeting and presented figures for the estimated cost
of drilling a well on the City parksite.

Mr. Stevens, City Attorney was directed to draw up a contract between the City of
Las Vegas, and Terry Diskin for the drilling of the well.

No further business appearing before the Board at this time the meeting recessed until
Tuesday the 27th day of January 1931 at the hour of three o'clock P.M.
