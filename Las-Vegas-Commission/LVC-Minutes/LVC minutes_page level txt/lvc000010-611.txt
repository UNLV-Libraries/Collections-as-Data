Thereafter Commissioner Bunker moved the following resolution be adopted:

RESOLUTION

WHEREAS, the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas has
held a public hearing considering the desirability of exchanging certain
real property with the Clark County School District; and

WHEREAS, notice of such hearing has been held in accordance with
Chapter 98, 1957 Laws of Nevada; and

WHEREAS, no objections to the proposed exchange have been entered
with the Board and the said exchange of property with the Clark County
School District will result in a better public use for the said land
than to have it remain in the possession of the City of Las Vegas,
