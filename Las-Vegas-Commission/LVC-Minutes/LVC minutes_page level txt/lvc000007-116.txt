WESTLEIGH TRACT	Mayor Cragin read the recommendation of the Regional Planning Commission

Tentative Map	concerning the approval of the tentative map of Westleigh Tract.

	Mr. Louis Weiner, Jr. appeared before the Board to protest the recent changing

of zone for this Westleigh Tract. Mr. Weiner stated that his clients object
on the grounds that the Commission is adopting plats and approving re-zoning have
abused the discretion of ordinances adopted by the City. Further, the procedure
set forth and adopted by ordinance has not been followed by the Planning Commission 
and City Commission.

Mr. Weiner also said that for the City Commission to adopty proposed tentative
plans would be to deprive the land owners of adjoining and adjacent property
of proper use of their land as set forth by the Land Use Ordinance at the time
same was adopted.

Thereafter, Commissioner Peccole moved the recommendation of the Regional
Planning Commission be accepted and also the improvements as specified and
agreed to be the Subdivider plus the installation of fire hydrants to be
spotted by the Fire Department, and paid for by the Subdivider.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole and Whipple voting aye; noes, none.
