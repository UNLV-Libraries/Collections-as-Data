Thereupon, it appearing to the Board that R. W. THOMAS has received a plurality of all
votes cast for the office of City Commissioner of the Second Class, it was THEREFORE
RESOLVED on motion of Commissioner Smith, seconded by Commissioner Mundy, that the said
R. W. Thomas be and he is hereby declared to be the duly elected City Commissioner of the
Second Class of the City of Las Vegas for the ensuing four years, and it was further
RESOLVED that the city clerk be and he is hereby directed to issue a certificate of
election to the said R. W. Thomas. Voting on said Resolution, Commissioners O. J. Smith,

W. B. Mundy, W. C. German, Roy Neagle and His Honor the Mayor, J. F. Hesse, Aye; Noes,

none.
