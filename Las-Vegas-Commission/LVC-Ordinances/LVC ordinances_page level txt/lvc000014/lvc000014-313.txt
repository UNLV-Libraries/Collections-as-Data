EMERGENCY ORDINANCE NO. 342

AN ORDINANCE	TO AMEND	THE TITLE OF AND	TO AMEND	SECTION 7 OF ORDINANCE	NO. 210 OF TEE CITY

OF LAS	VEGAS	ENTITLED	"AN ORDINANCE TO	FIX, IMPOSE AND	PROVIDE FOR THE	COLLECTION OF A

LICENSE TAX ON MOTOR PROPELLED VEHICLES OPERATED FOR HIRE, AND TO REGULATE THE OPERATION
AND RUNNING OF THE SAME, WITHIN THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; FIXING A PENALTY FOR THE VIOLATION
OF ANY OF THE PROVISIONS OF THIS ORDINANCE; AND REPEALING ORDINANCE NO. 100 AND ALL ORDINANCES
OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH;" AND REPEALING ORDINANCES NO. 266 AND 279 AND
ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH.

WHEREAS, an emergency exists in respect to traffic conditions in the City of Las
Vegas and it is necessary for the immediate preservation of the public health, safety and
welfare to provide further supervision of vehicles, as defined in the above-entitled
ordinance, in their operation upon the streets of said city, NOW, THEREFORE,

The	Board of	Commissioners of	the City	of Las	Vegas do ordain	as follows:

Section 1. The title of Ordinance No.	210 of	the City of Las	Vegas is hereby

amended to read as follows:

AN ORDINANCE TO FIX, IMPOSE AND PROVIDE FOR THE COLLECTION OF A LICENSE TAX ON
MOTOR PROPELLED VEHICLES OPERATED FOR HIRE, AND TO REGULATE THE OPERATION AND RUNNING OF
THE SAME, WITHIN THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF PARKING STANDS,

AND FIXING AND IMPOSING THE FEE TO BE COLLECTED FOR THE ISSUANCE OF PERMITS TO PARE THEREIN;

FIXING A PENALTY FOR THE VIOLATION OF ANY OF THE PROVISIONS OF THIS ORDINANCE; REPEALING
ORDINANCE NO. 100 AND ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH; AND OTHER
MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO.

Section 2. Section 7 of Ordinance No. 210 of the City of Las Vegas is hereby
amended to read as follows:

Section 7. CERTAIN ACTS UNLAWFUL - It shall be unlawful:

(a)	To operate a vehicle as herein defined, within the City of Las Vegas without
having first obtained a license, provided the security as herein provided, and paid the
prescribed tax therefor, as in this ordinance provided.

(b)	To fail to display the state, county or city license number of such vehicle.

(c)	To permit passengers to ride on the running boards or sit upon the doors

of such vehicle, and to permit more than one person to ride with the operator or chauffeur.

(d)	To operate any vehicle at a rate of speed in excess of that fixed by the laws
of the State of Nevada and the ordinances of the City of Las Vegas.

(e)	To fail to come to a full stop within twenty-five (25) feet of the nearest
rail of any steam railroad when approaching the same, and to proceed across said railroad
track without first determining that no engine, car or train is approaching in the direction
of said vehicle.

(f)	To alter the seating capacity of any vehicle so as to make the seating capacity
thereof greater than is provided for by the license under which it is operated, without the
consent of the Board of City Commissioners.

(g)	To fail, refuse or neglect to operate such vehicle in accordance with the de-
signated tariff of fares and during the hours set forth in the application made for such
vehicle license, except on Sundays and a reasonable time for going to and from meals, and

in case of accidents, break-downs or other casualties, or upon the surrender of said license.

(h) To park a vehicle, as herein defined, for the purpose of soaking or awaiting
employment, at any place upon the streets of the City of Las Vegas, other than at stands de-
signated therefor, as herein provided, and marked by appropriate signs, by the City Engineer,
who shall, subject to the provisions of Section XXIV of Ordinance No. 170 of the City of Las
