Las Vegas, Nevada
May 31, 1950 

A special meeting held this 31st day of May, 1950 was called to order at the
hour of 3:30 P.M. by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin with the following members
present: Commissioners Bunker, Peccole, Whipple; City Manager C. W. Shelley

City Attorney Howard W. Cannon and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger. Absent:
Commissioner Moore;

EL CENTRO TRACT #1           Commissioner Whipple moved the final map of El Centro Subdivision Tract No. 1
Final Map	be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same upon the

	recommendation of the Regional Planning Commission contained in their letter

	of May 31, 1950.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
Absent: Commissioner Moore.

BIDS - Assessment	This being the time set for the opening of bids on the installation of certain

Districts	improvements in Special Assessment Districts in the City, His Honor announced

that these bids would now be opened.

At this time Commissioner Moore arrived.

Mr. J. Hargrove, representing the State Contractors Board appeared at this
time and stated that according to State Law the City could not open bids from
concerns who do not have State Contractor's licenses.

City Attorney Howard W. Cannon told the Board that it was his opinion that a
person or firm was not a contractor just because he submits a bid, and advised
the Board to open any and all bids. Mr. Cannon stated that if a bid were awarded
to an unlicensed contractor, said bidder must have a state license before entering
into a contract with the City.
