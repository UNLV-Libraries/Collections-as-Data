NOTICE

NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that the Board of Commissioners of the
City of Las Vegas is in receipt of several certified copies of maps re-
zoning the following described property from "RE" Residence Estate to the "R1"

Single Family Zones, to-wit:

NW¼ of Section 29, Township 20 South,

Range 61E, MDB & M

That the Board of Commissioners of said City will hold a public
hearing on the rezoning of said properties at a meeting to be held on the
26th day of June, 1950, at 3:30 P.M. of that date, in the Council Chambers in
the City Hall, on Stewart Avenue, at. the corner of North 5th Street, in the
City of Las Vegas, Nevada, and after such public hearing at such meeting,
will consider for adoption the above mentioned changes as adopted by the
Regional Planning Commission.

s/ Shirley Ballinger_________

City Clerk
