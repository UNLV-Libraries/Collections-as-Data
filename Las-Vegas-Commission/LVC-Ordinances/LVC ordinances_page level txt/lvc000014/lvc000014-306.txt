ORDINANCE NO. 339

AN ORDINANCE DECLARING CERTAIN TERRITORY ADJOINING AND CONTIGUOUS TO THE CORPORATE
LIMITS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS TO BE ANNEXED TO THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; ORDERING A PLAT
SHOWING SAID TERRITORY TO BE RECORDED IN THE OFFICE OF THE COUNTY RECORDER OF THE COUNTY OF.
CLARK, STATE OF NEVADA; AND OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATING THERETO.

WHEREAS, all the property owners, inhabitants and freeholders of the territory
hereinafter described have duly petitioned the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las
Vegas to annex said territory to said City; NOW, THEREFORE,

THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS DO ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

Section 1. All that certain territory in the County of Clark, State of Nevada,
adjoining and contiguous to the corporate limits of the City of Las Vegas, and being the
South Half (S½) of the Southwest Quarter (SW¼) of Section 22, Township 20 South, Range 61
East, M. D. B. & M., together with the tenements, property and inhabitants thereof, is hereby
declared to be annexed to and be a part of the City of Las Vegas and subject to all the taxes
and laws thereof.

Section 2. The City Engineer of the City of Las Vegas is hereby ordered and directed
to prepare a plat showing the territory hereby annexed to the City of Las Vegas and to cause
said plat to be recorded in the Office of the County Recorder of the County of Clark, State
of Nevada.

Section 3. This Ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its publication,
as in the next section provided, and final passage.

Section 4. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas shall cause this Ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks
immediately following its first reading and adoption, in the Las Vegas Evening Review Journal,
a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
