Commissioner Moore moved the license of Rufus M. White, Log Cabin Bar, 122
North 1st, Retail Tavern be suspended and .the Chief of Police be instructed to
notify them of this fact. Further that Mr. White be given an opportunity at
  his convenience to show cause why this license should not be revoked.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

There being no further business to come before this special meeting, the meeting
adjourned.

Las Vegas, Nevada
January 6, 1950

A regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners held this 6th day of January, 1950
was called to order at the hour of 3:25 by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin with
the following members present: Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple;

City Attorney Howard W. Cannon, City Engineer W. C. Anderson and City Clerk Shirley
Ballinger. Absent:	City Manager George O. Treem.

Commissioner Moore moved the Minutes of December 22, 1949 be approved by reference and
the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Moore moved that Service & Material Warrants Nos. 1834, 1886 through 1892
inclusive in the total sum of $32,287.21 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk au-
thorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
