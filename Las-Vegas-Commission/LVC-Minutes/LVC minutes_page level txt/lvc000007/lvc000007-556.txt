A Petition, dated the 17th day of November, 1941, signed by three (3) free-
holders residing in the area affected, having been filed with the Clerk of this Board,
petitioning for the vacation of the alley in Block 20 of Ladd's Addition hereinafter
described, and said Petition having been by Order of this Board referred to the Plan-

said Planning Commission having filed its report, dated January 2, 1952, with this
Board approving and recommending said vacation;

And this Board, by an Order made at its regular meeting held on the 16th day of
January, 1952, set the 20th day of February, 1952, at the hour of 7:30 o'clock P. M. at
the Commissioner's Room of the Board of Commissioners, in the War Memorial Building,
located at the Northwest Corner of Fifth and Stewart Streets, in the City of Las Vegas,

Clark County, Nevada, as the time and place for a public hearing on said Petition and
recommendation, and ordered the City Clerk, to cause the aforesaid alley to be vacated
to be posted with a Notice setting forth the time and place of the public hearing
and the extent of the proposed vacation;

And it appearing from the Affidavit of Charles V, Cudney, filed with the Clerk of
this Board, that the Notice provided for in said Order, a copy of which Notice is
attached to said Affidavit, was posted on the 18th day of January, 1952, in the
manner prescribed by said Order;

And this being the time fixed for the hearing on said Petition for vacation and the
recommendation of the Planning Commission thereon, and this Board having heard evi-
dence in support of and there being no opposition to said Petition and recommendation;

And it appearing to the satisfaction of this Board that the alley proposed to be va-
cated is no longer required for the public use and convenience, that said vacation
will inure to the benefit of the City of Las Vegas, and that neither the public nor
any person will be materially injured thereby;

of Block 20, Ladd's Addition, recorded in Book 2 of Flats,
page 56, Clark County Nevada records,
