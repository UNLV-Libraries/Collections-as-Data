BEFORE THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS

CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

STATE OF NEVADA)

County of Clark)

JOHN L. RUSSELL, being first duly sworn, on oath, deposes and says:

That the above entitled matter has been set for trial April 28, 1941, at 10 A.M.

That since and including April 21, 1941, affiant has served on the federal jury at said Las Vegas,
wherefore, he has been unable to confer with his counsel and to make adequate preparations for said
trial.

That he advised the federal judge of said trial and requested to be excused from such jury service
which request was denied, except that the said judge stated he might be excused April 28th only, and
no longer, but that said trial cannot be concluded in one day.

Wherefore, affiant requests that said trial be continued until after said jury service with a
reasonable time thereafter to prepare for said trial.

John L Russell

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 28th day of April, 1941.

Albert A Hinman

Notary Public in and for said

Notary seal.	County and State.

After considering said request on motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commissioner Gilbert it
was moved and carried that in as much as Mayor Russell and his counsel have ask for a continuance
of the trial of the Mayor, because Mayor Russell has been called for Federal Jury duty and that Att.
Hinman stated he has not had sufficient time to contact witnesses he would like to call, and that
the Board is willing to grant continuance so as not to cause the Mayor any inconvenience that the
Board extend the time of the hearing until Thursday May 8th, at the hour of ten A.M.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, and Corradetti and Mayor Pro Tem Krause voting aye. Noes,
none. Mayor Russell not voting.

Mayor Russell and Counsel approved the date of hearing as of May 8th, 1941 at 10 A.M.

No further business appearing before the Board the meeting adjourned.
