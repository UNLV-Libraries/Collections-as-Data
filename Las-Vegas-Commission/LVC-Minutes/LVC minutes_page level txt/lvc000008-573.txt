AGREEMENT - STATE HIGHWAY DEPARTMENT Light Pole Bases on 5th Street	At this time an agreement with the State Highway Department for the installation of light pole bases on 5th Street in conjunction with the widening of 5th Street from Oakey Blvd. to the North City Limits, was considered.  Commissioner Whipple moved the aforementioned agreement known as the State Highway Department Agreement No. CL-03, for the installation of light pole bases on 5th, be approved and the Mayor and Clerk authorised to sign same.  Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote: Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
CITY PARK NAMED  Dutton Park	The request of the U-WAN-UN Study Club that the City Park located at Charleston Boulevard and Park Paseo be named Dutton Park in honor of Mrs. Mary Dutton who donated said land, was presented for consideration.  Thereafter Commissioner Jarrett Moved that this request be granted and said park area be named Dutton Park.  Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote: Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
ORDINANCE NO 58O Rezoning	At this time Commissioners Jarrett and Whipple reported favorable on a proposed ordinance to enact the rezoning of property generally located on the west side of North 5th, north of the Alpine Village from R1 to R-3 and Lots 6 through 10, Block 22 of resubdivision of Block 13 and portions of Blocks 21 and 22, Wardie Addition from R-3 To C-l.  There after said committee reported favorably on this ordinance entitled: AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND SECTION IV OF ORDINANCE NO. 378, REFERRED TO AS SECTION 10, CHAPTER 2k, OF THE LAS VEGAS CITY CODE, 1949, BY AMENDING THE LAND USE PLAN MAP ADOPTED BY THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, AND CHANGING THE ZONE DESIGNATION OF CERTAIN AREAS ON SAID MAP, which was then read in full to the Board.  Commissioner Whipple moved the above entitled Ordinance No. 580 be adopted.  Motion secionted by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote: Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none;
