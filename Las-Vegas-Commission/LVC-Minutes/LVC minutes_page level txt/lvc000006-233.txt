LIQUOR APPLICATION	Commissioner Baskin moved that the application of Dave Simon and

DAVE SIMON AND JOE	Joe Harrison at the Westside Liquor Store, 415 Jackson Street for

HARRISON	a sealed package liquor license be denied on the ground that there

	are no package liquor licenses available as authorized by a Resolution

heretofore adopted. Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and
carried by the following vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore,

Whipple and His Honor voting aye. Noes, none.

GAMING APPLICATION	Commissioner Clark moved that the gaming application of Dave Stearns

DAVE STEARNS	Santa Anita Turf Club and Bar, 113 Fremont, for one race horse book

	be denied on the ground that this license had been transferred twice

	before and that there are sufficient race books in the City of Las

Vegas at the present time. Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin
and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore

voting aye. Voting no:	Commissioner Whipple and Mayor Cragin.

There being no further business to come before the meeting at this
time, Commissioner Clark moved that this meeting adjourn until March
5, 1948 at the hour of 2:00 p.m. Motion seconded by Commissioner
Baskin and carried by the following vote: Commissioners Baskin,

Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye. Noes, none.

APPROVED:

Mayor
