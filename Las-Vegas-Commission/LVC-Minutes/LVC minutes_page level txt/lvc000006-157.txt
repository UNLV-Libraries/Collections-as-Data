6.	This Contract shall not be abrogated, changed or modified prior to

the expiration of forty-five (45) years from the date of the conveyance
of the Project to the Authority, and shall be binding upon the City and
the owner of the Project so long as the owner of the Project is the
Authority or some other public body or governmental agency authorized
by law to engage in the development or administration of low-rent
housing projects, and so long as the Project is used for the purpose
of providing housing for families of low income".

Section 4. This Resolution shall be in full force and effect from and
after its passage.
