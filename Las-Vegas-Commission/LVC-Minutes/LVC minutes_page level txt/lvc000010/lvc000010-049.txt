An ordinance entitled:	AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND SECTION 20 OF ORDINANCE NO. 6l0 BY

INCREASING THE NUMBER OF MEMBERS OF THE BOARD OF ELECTRICAL EXAMINERS TO SEVEN;

REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH; AND PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPER-
LY RELATED THERETO, was reported on favorably by Commissioners Fountain and Sharp
with the following amendments:

1.	That instead of two members to be selected from a list certified
by the Southern Nevada Power Company that there be one member
from this group, and one Electrical Engineer registered by the
State of Nevada.

2.	That instead of two journeyman electricians on this Board, that
there be two journeyman master electricians, or equivalent.

Commissioner Sharp moved the above entitled Ordinance No. 673 be adopted, as amended.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:	Com-

missioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye;
noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.
