OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, CITY OF LAS VEGAS,
CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

JANUARY 4th, 1933.

Minutes of a recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas held on the 4th day of January 1933 at the hour of seven thirty o'clock P.M.

Roll call showed the following present, Mayor Cragin, Commissioners Mundy, German, and
Thomas, City Attorney and Clerk, also Alfred Blackman, Auditor.

At this time the Board entered	into	a	discussion	of	ways	and	means	to	finance	the City

of Las Vegas for the present year. After	some	discussion	on	the	matter	it	was	decided	by	the

members of the Board present to reduce expenses in all departments to the lowest possible figure.

On motion of Commissioner Mundy, seconded by Commissioner Thomas the following Resolu-
tion was introduced a d passed:

Resolved, that the following schedule for salaries for the following officers be in
effect from and after Jan. 1st, 1933 until the further order of the Board of Commissioners:

J.M. Ullom, City Treasurer	$50.00 Per month

F.C. DeVinney City Assessor	$50.00	"	"

A.S. Henderson City Attorney	$200.00	"	"

and the salary of Viola Burns, City Clerk be reduced 10% per month placing said salary at $180.00
per month upon said City Clerk, volunteering and offering to take such reduction in salary, same
to be in effect until the City is financially able to warrant the salary being returned to $200.00
per month.

Vote on the Resolution was as follows: Commissioners Mundy, German, Thomas and His
Honor the Mayor voting aye. Noes none.

The Clerk was directed to notify the above officers in writing of the changes in effect.

0 motion of Commissioner Thomas, seconded by Mayor Cragin the following R solution
was introduced and passed:

Resolved, that commencing with the 1st day of January, 1933 that the salary payroll
of the Police and Street Departments be reduced 10% or more. This reduction to be made on the
pay roll in its entirety.

Vote on the Resolution was as follows: Commissioners Mundy, German and Thomas and His
Honor the Mayor voting aye. Noes none.
