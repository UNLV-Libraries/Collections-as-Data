At a recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas held
on the 1st day of March 1930 at the hour of eleven forty-five P.M.

Present Mayor Hesse, Commissioners German, Smith, and Thomas together with the City Attorney
and Clerk.

Absent Commissioner Hansell.

Minutes of meeting of February 26th, 28th, at the hour of three P.M. and February
28th at the hour of seven thirty P.M. read and approved as read.

Commissioner German presented to the Board the Budget for the year 1930.

A fund was hereby created to be known as the Library Fund.

Upon motion of Commissioner German, seconded by Commissioner Thomas it was moved that the
Budget for the year 1930 be adopted as presented to the Board, and a copy of said Budget be in-
cluded in the minutes of this meeting.

Vote on said motion was as follows: Commissioners German, Smith, and Thomas, and His Honor
the Mayor Hesse voting Aye. Noes None.
