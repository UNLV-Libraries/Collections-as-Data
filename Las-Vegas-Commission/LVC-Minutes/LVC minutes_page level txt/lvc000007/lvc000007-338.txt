FIRE WORKS ORDINANCE   Mr. Madison Graves, representing the American Legion appeared before the Board at this
time to request the passage of an ordinance allowing the sale of "safe and sane" fire-
	works in the City of Las Vegas.

Commissioner Peccole moved a committee be appointed to study this application of the
American Legion for "safe and sane" fireworks in the City of Las Vegas, to report on
March 14, and Mr. Graves prepare an ordinance and submit the same to the City Attorney.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting ayes; noes, none.

Mayor Cragin appointed Commissioners Peccole and Whipple and Rex Jarrett and M. B. Fleming
to serve on the above mentioned committee.
