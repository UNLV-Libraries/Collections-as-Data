OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, CITY OF
LAS VEGAS, CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.
NOVEMBER 28th, 1936.

Minutes of a recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las
Vegas held on the 28th day of November 1936 at the hour of three o'clock P.M.

Roll call showed the following present Mayor Cragin, Commissioners Arnett, Marble and Down,
City Attorney and Clerk.

Absent Commissioner German.

The question of the awarding the bids presented for materials to be used on the sewer lateral
system in the City of Las Vegas, now under construction was brought before the Board for consider-
ation.

After some discussion on the matter on motion of Commissioner Marble seconded by Commissioner
Arnett it was moved and carried that Commissioner Down handle awarding of all bids on the pur-
chasing of materials used in the installation of the sewer lateral system.

Vote was as follows: Commissioners Arnett, Marble and down and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes none.

At this time a number of beauty shoppe operators appeared before the Board requesting a
change in the ordinance governing beauty shops.

No decision was reached on the matter and the operators were ask to appear before the
Board at December 4th, 1934 with a outline of the proposed ordinance.

On motion of Commissioner Marble seconded by Commissioner Down the following slot machine
licenses were granted:

Outpost Trading Company	2	L.L. Arnett	4

Vegas Sweet Shoppe	1	Art Schreiber	2

Vote was as follows: Commissioners Arnett, Marble and Down and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes none.

On motion of Commissioner Down seconded by Commissioner Arnett it was moved and carried
that C.V.T. Gilbert be granted a "family liquor store" license $50.00, and Anna Lujan a retail
beverage license price $10.00.

Vote was as follows: Commissioner Arnett, Marble end Down and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes none.

Ted Homan appeared before that Beard at this time in regard to securing the use of the
City street graders for leveling off the site of the old airport. No definite decision was
given at this time.

No further business appearing before the Board at this time the meeting adjourned.
