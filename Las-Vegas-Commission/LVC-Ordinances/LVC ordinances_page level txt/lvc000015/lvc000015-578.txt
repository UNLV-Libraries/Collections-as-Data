SECTION 3. Section 26 of Chapter 16, Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 1949, is hereby amended
to read as follows:

No permit or license shall be issued to any person, social club or association of
persons of any kind whatsoever, unless the applicant shall have an established
place of business within a zone permitting such type of license under the Planning
and Zoning Ordinances of the City of Las Vegas and unless the applicant is a citizen
of the United States, and of good moral character. In the event the applicant shall
be a corporation or association, the managing agent thereof must be a citizen of the
United States and a person of good moral character.

SECTION 4. All sales and/or transfers of the stock of merchandise of any intoxicating
liquor license holder, shall be made in compliance with Chapter 98, Nevada Revised Statutes
(Bulk Sales Act).

SECTION 5. All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby

repealed.

SECTION 6. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
shall cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following
its adoption in the Las Vegas Sun, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
