Las Vegas, Nevada
August 16, 1949

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners held this 16th day of august, 1949, was
called to the order by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin at the hour of 3:45 P.M. with the
following members present: Commissioners Bunker, Peccole, Whipple; Acting City Manager
George 0. Treem and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger. Absent: Commissioner Moore and City
Attorney Howard W. Cannon.

SAVOY CLUB -	At this time His Honor read the application of Norman Khoury, Savoy Club, 114 Fremont,for

Transfer of Gaming	4 slot machines, 1 "21" game, 1 Crap table, and 1 Race Horse Book licenses for the third

License	quarter of 1949.

	Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved that Messrs. M. B. Hicks and Harry Badger by granted

permission to transfer their gaming license to Mr. Norman Khoury, and that issuance of the
foregoing gaming licenses to Mr. Khoury be conditioned on the report of the Police Depart-
ment.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Moore.

There being no further business to come before this meeting, the meeting adjourned.
