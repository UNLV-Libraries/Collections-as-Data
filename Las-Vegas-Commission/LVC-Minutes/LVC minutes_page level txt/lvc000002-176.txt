OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK OF TEE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA.

May 12th, 1925.

At a special meeting of the Board of City Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Clark
County, Nevada, held on the 12th day of May, A.D. 1925, at the hour of three o'clock P.M.,
said meeting being held with the consent of all members of the Board. Present His Honor
the Mayor, W.C. German, with Commissioners A.R. Anderson, W.H. Elwell and F.E. Matzdorf,
together with the City Attorney and City Clerk.

The Board proceeded at this time to make a canvass of the vote cast at the general
city election held on the 5th day of May, 1925, and such canvass resulted as follows:
