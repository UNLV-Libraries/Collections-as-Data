An ordinance entitled:	AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND CHAPTER 16, CODE OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA,

1949, AS AMENDED, BY PROHIBITING THE SALE OF INTOXICATING LIQUORS WITHIN FOUR HUNDRED
(400') FEET OF ANY CHURCH, Was read by title and referred to Commissioners Bunker
and Peccole for recommendation.

An ordinance entitled:	AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND ORDINANCE NO. 304 ENTITLED:	AN ORDINANCE

OF THE CITY OF LaS VEGAS CREATING AND ESTABLISHING FIRE ZONES,": BY ADDING CERTAIN DESCRIBED
AREAS TO FIRE ZONES TWO AND TWO SPECIAL (2's) BY PROVIDING FIRE ZONES FOR REZONED AREAS;

AND OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO; REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES
IN CONFLICT THEREWITH; was read by title and referred to Commissioners Bunker and Peccole
for recommendation.
