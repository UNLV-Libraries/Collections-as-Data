A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, held this
27th day of July, 1953 was called to order at the hour of 8:00 P.M. by His Honor Mayor C.D.

Baker with the following members present:

Mayor	C.D. Baker

Commissioner Wendell Bunker
Commissioner Rex A. Jarrett
Commissioner Harris P. Sharp
Commissioner Reed Whipple

City Attorney Howard W. Cannon
Acting City. Mgr. A.H. Kennedy
City Clerk Shirley Ballinger

Also present were the following members of the Clark County Board of Commissioners:

Commissioner George Albright
Commissioner Rodney Colton
Commissioner Harley Harmon

At this time discussion was had on the proposed agreement with the County for Sewer service
to the proposed Sherwood Park Subdivision. This subdivision is comprised of approximately
forty-three acres adjoining the south city limits, running along San Francisco Street. Mr.

A.W. Ham, Jr. representing the Subdividers of this property was present and entered into the
discussion.

At the hour of 9:35 P.M. the meeting recessed.

The meeting reconvened at the hour of 9:55 P.M. with all members present as of the opening session.
