(CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING)

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada,
held this 9th day of June, 1959, was called to order at the hour of 8:40 a.m. by
Mayor Pro Tem Whipple with the following members present:

Mayor Pro Tem Reed Whipple	City Manager A. H. Kennedy

Commissioner William H. Elwell	City Attorney Calvin Cory

Commissioner E. W. Fountain	Acting City Clerk R. F. Boos

Commissioner Harris P. Sharp

Absent:	Mayor C. D. Baker

ASSESSMENT DISTRICT               Commissioner Sharp moved that the following resolution to publish notice of sale
100-43	of bonds for Assessment District 100-43, Main Street Lighting; Bonanza to Owens,

be adopted and order made:

Main St. Lighting;

Bonanza to Owens	RESOLUTION

Resolution to Publish	WHEREAS, the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas have taken steps

Notice of Sale of	to create certain public improvements within the City of Las Vegas alias described

Bonds Adopted	in Ordinance No. 788; and

WHEREAS, the time has now arrived to authorize and direct the Acting City Clerk
to advertise for the sale of bonds all in accordance with the proceedings heretofore
taken to construct said improvements by special assessment;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las
Vegas at a special meeting thereof held on the 9th day of June, 1959, that the
Acting City Clerk and the Clerk of the Board of Commissioners be and he is hereby
directed to publish notice that the Board of Commissioners will receive bids for
bonds for Special Assessment District No. 100-43  in an amount equal to the unpaid
assessments after the completion of the cash payment period of four weeks.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Acting City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of
Commissioners be and he hereby is directed to publish notice of the receipt of bids
for said bonds by publication for five consecutive weeks, one week apart in the Las	

Vegas Review Journal, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the Acting City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of
Commissioners be directed to publish in the said notice that the bids for said
bonds will be received until 8:00 p.m. on Wednesday, the 15th day of July, 1959,
and that said bids will be opened at 8:00 p.m. or thereabouts on said day.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that said notice shall be in substantially the form
attached to this resolution and made a part hereof.	

PASSED, ADOPTED AND APPROVED THIS 9th day of June, 1959.

/s/ Reed Whipple___________________
