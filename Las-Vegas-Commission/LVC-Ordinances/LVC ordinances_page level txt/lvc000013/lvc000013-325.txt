ORDINANCE NO. 164.

An Ordinance to amend Section 7 of Ordinance No. 48 of the City of Las Vegas,
entitled "An Ordinance of the City of Las Vegas, licensing, for the purpose of regulation
 and revenue, every kind of lawful business hereinafter specified, transacted
or carried on within the corporate limits of the City of Lias Vegas, State of Nevada,
fixing the rates of license tax upon the same and providing for the collection of said
of said license tax, and a punishment for carrying or or conducting any such business
without a license, and repealing Ordinances Nos. 1, 2, 4, 13, 14 and 35," as amended by
Section 1 of Ordinance No. 81 of said City, by herein amending certain subdivisions
thereof and by adding certain other subdivisions, and to amend said Ordinance No. 48
by adding two new Sections to be known as Sections Nos. 10a and 10b respectively, and to
repeal all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict therewith.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do Ordain as Follows:

Section 1. The following subdivisions of said Section 7 of said Ordinance No. 48
as amended by said Ordinance No. 81, are hereby amended so that the same shall read
as follows:
