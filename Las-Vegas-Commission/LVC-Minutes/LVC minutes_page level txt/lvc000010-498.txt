A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,
Nevada held this 22nd day of May, 1957 was called to order at the hour of
8:30 P.M. by His Honor Mayor Baker with the following members present:

Mayor	C. D. Baker

Commissioner	E. W. Fountain

Commissioner	Harris P. Sharp

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

City Manager	A. H. Kennedy

Adm. Assistant	Robert F. Boos

City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

Planning Director	Franklin J. Bills

Field Engineer	Ken Hansen

City Clerk	Shirley Lodwick

Absent:	Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

This being the time heretofore set for the public hearing on the petition
of Burley M. Jones and other property owners, and the recommendation of
the Planning Commission for the vacation of "M" Street between Washington
and Adams, His Honor asked if there were any objections to the vacation
of this street.

No one present objected, and there were no protests filed with the Clerk
concerning the proposed vacation.

Planning Director Bills stated that the vacation of this street was one
of the conditions set forth by the Planning Commission when recommending
approval of the tentative map of Bonanza Terrace, the same having been con-
sidered and approved by the Board of Commissioners at a regular meeting held
April 17, 1957.

Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved that pursuant to the recommendation
of the Planning Commission, this petition for the vacation of "M" Street
located between Washington and Adams, be approved, and the following order
adopted:

ORDER OF VACATION

A Petition, dated the 11th day of April, 1957, signed by three (3)
freeholders residing in the area affected, having been filed with the
Clerk of this Board, petitioning for the vacation of a portion of "M"

Street hereinafter described, and said Petition having been by Order of
this Board, referred to the Planning Commission of the City of Las Vegas
for its recommendation in the premises, and said Planning Commission hav-
