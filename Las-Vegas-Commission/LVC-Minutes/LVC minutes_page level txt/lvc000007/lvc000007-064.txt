Las Vegas, Nevada
March 7, 1950

A regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners held this 7th day of March, 1950 was
called to order by His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple at the hour of 3:35 P.M. with the
following members present: Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole; City Attorney Howard

W. Cannon, Acting City Manager W. C. Anderson and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger. Absent:
Mayor Cragin.

PAYROLL WARRANTS	Commissioner Moore moved that Payroll Warrants Nos. 1111 to 1113 inclusive in the net

sum of $25,664.15 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes, none.

Absent: Mayor Cragin.

CRANER TRAILER COURT	At this time Mrs. S. W. Craner and Miss Helen Craner appeared before the Board relative

to the reported unsanitary condition of their trailer court located at 129 South 7th
Street.

 

His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple read the report of A. B. Angel, Plumbing Inspector.

Miss Helen Craner told the Board that they did not feel they could afford to make the
necessary changes to bring the court up to the City Code at this time.

Commissioner Peccole moved that Mrs. Craner be given thirty days to get her trailer
court in order to conform with the City Code, otherwise the license will be revoked.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes, none.

Absent: Mayor Cragin.
