OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, CITY OF LAS VEGAS,
CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

MARCH 5th, 1934.

Minutes of a regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas, held on the 5th day of March 1934, at the hour of three o'clock P.M.

Roll call showed the following present, Mayor Pro Tem German, Commissioners Arnett,
Marble and Down, City Attorney and Clerk

Mayor Cragin arriving late.

All claims allowed as per claims and warrant register on file in the office of the

Clerk.

A copy of the yearly report for the year 1933 was presented to the Board. Same having
been prepared by Alfred Blackman, auditor for the City.

The transfer of the beverage license from A.E. Stephenson at the De Lux Auto court to
Clare McInery was brought before the Board for consideration. City Attorney Henderson ruling
that the application for transfer was in proper form on motion of Commissioner Down seconded
by Commissioner Marble it was moved and carried that the license be transferred.

Vote on said motion was as follows: Commissioners Arnett, Marble and Down and Mayor Pro
Tem German voting aye. Noes none.

was brought before the Board. Same having been prepared by Alfred W. Blackman, auditor for
the City of Las Vegas under the direction of the various members of the Board of City Com-
missioners.

Arnett seconded by Commissioner Marble it was moved and seconded that the Budget for
