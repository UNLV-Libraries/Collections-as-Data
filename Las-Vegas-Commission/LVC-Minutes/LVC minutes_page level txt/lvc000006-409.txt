CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

This special meeting held this 18th day of April, 1949 was called to order at the hour of
3:40 P.M. by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin with the following members present:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark, Whipple; City Manager J. M. Murphy, City Attorney C. Norman Cornwall and City
Clerk Shirley Ballinger. Absent:	Commissioner Moore.

Checker Cab	Corp.	taxi license be granted.

 Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners
