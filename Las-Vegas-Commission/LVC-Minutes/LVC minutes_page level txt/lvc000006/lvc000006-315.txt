Las Vegas, Nevada
 September 13, 1948

A recessed regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners held this 13th day of
September, 1948 was called to order at the hour of 3:15 P.M. by His Honor Mayor Cragin
with the following members present:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, and Whipple. Absent:

Commissioner Clark. Also present:	City Manager J. M. Murphy, City Attorney C. Norman

Cornwall and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger.

Veterans of Foregin Wars for permission to sponsor Siebrands Combined Circus and
	Carnival for the week beginning September 22, 1948 be granted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:
Commissioner Clark.
