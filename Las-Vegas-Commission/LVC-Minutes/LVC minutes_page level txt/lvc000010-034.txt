Director of Planning Franklin Bills reported at this time on the proposed rezoning
of South 4th and South 6th Streets from Bridger to Charleston. Mr. Bills stated
that the Planning Commission made the following recommendation:

1.	An amendment to the City Zoning Ordinance authorizing the establishment
of an addition district classification to the designated P-1 or Parking
District. That the uses in this district be as follows:

a.	Any use permitted in an R-1, R-2 and R-3 District.

b.	The establishment of commercial or patron off-street
parking lots.

2.	The reclassification of the half block strip, 140 feet in depth from
Bridger to Charleston from the alley east of 5th Street to 6th Street
from R-1 and R-3 to P-1.

3.	The reclassification of the half block strip, l40 feet in depth, from
Clark to Hoover from the alley west of 5th Street to 4th Street, from

R-3 and R-4 to P-1.

4.	The reclassification of the half block strip, l40 feet in depth between
6th Street and the alley east of 6th Street, from Bonneville to Hoover,
from R-1 to R-3.

5.	The reclassification of the half block strip, l40 feet in depth, from
Bridger to Hoover from 4th Street to the alley west of 4th Street
from R-3 to R-4.
