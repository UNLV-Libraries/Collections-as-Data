__CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

To--- Viola Burns, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada.

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, is hereby called
to be held in the Board of Commissioners Room in the City Clerk's office, Thursday the 22nd day of
August, 1940 at the hour of seven thirty P.M. to consider and act upon the following business:

1.	To consider the sponsoring of a City Beautification Project under W.P.A. and to consider
said project with C.J. Makey, Local W.P.A. Director.

2.	To consider the application of Hilderbrands United Shows to operate in the City of Las Vegas
for one week about Sept 16th 1940.

Dated this 22nd day of August, 1940.

John L. Russell

Mayor of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

Dated: August 16th, 1940.

Vote on the adoption oof the above and foregoing budget was as follows: Commissioners Ronnow,
Krause and Corradetti voting aye. His Honor the Mayor voting no.

Mayor Russell stated to the Board that he would not sign the above form of budget, and that
he would request a mandamus, Mayor Russell further stated that he much regretted signing the ori-
ginal budget adopted the first of February that said budget was padded and false.

On motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commissioner Corradetti it was moved and carried
that the Clerk mail a copy of said budget to the Nevada Tax Commission and set forth in said
letter the fact that the Mayor refused to sign said budget but the same had been adopted by the
majority of the Board. Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Krause, and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor
voting aye. Noes, none. Absent Commissioner Gilbert.

Mr. Murray Wollman appeared before the Board at this time and ask that the City take
all necessary steps to enact safety regulations in regard to the installation of gas tanks and
gas connections within the City.

No further business appearing before the Board at this time on motion of Commissioner Krause
seconded by Commissioner Corradetti it was moved and carried that this meeting adjourn.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting aye. Noes

none.

There upon said meeting adjourned.
