A resolution providing for certain parking meters that have a minimum of five cents was
discussed.

Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved the following resolution be adopted:

WHEREAS, it is deemed advisable by the City Commission that meter heads be installed in
certain portions of the City of Las Vegas wherein a 5¢ coin shall be the minimum coin
received therein; and

WHEREAS, Ordinance No. 622 provides that the City Commission may designate that such meter
heads should be installed in certain areas having a receptacle for a five cent piece only;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
does hereby determine that parking meter heads shall be installed within the area hereinafter
set forth having thereon receptacles for a five cent minimum only and containing no
receptacles for the deposit of a one cent coin.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the minimum parking time shall be thirty minutes for each five
cent coin deposited in any parking meter on either side of the streets on the following
streets and parts of streets, namely:

On Fremont Street from Main to 8th Street; on Main Street,

First Street, Second Street, Third Street, Fourth Street,

Fifth Street, Sixth Street, Seventh Street, and Eighth Street
from Carson Avenue to Ogden Avenue.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
