ORDINANCE NO. 724

AN ORDINANCE OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA TO AMEND SECTION 2 OF ORDINANCE NO. 573, BY CHANGING
THE MINIMUM BUILDING LINE SETBACK AND FUTURE WIDTH LINE ON CHARLESTON BOULEVARD; PROVIDING OTHER
MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT
THEREWITH.

THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS DO ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

SECTION 1. Section 2 of Ordinance No. 573 is hereby amended to read as follows:

4.	Charleston Boulevard - Rancho Road:

(a)	From Fremont Street to Sixteenth Street, Ninety (90) feet from the center line on each
side thereof.

(b)	From Sixteenth Street to Tenth Street, fifty (50) feet from the center line on each
side thereof.

(c)	From Tenth Street to Main Street, ten (10) feet from the street line or fifty (50) feet
from the center line on each side thereof.

(d) From Main Street to the South quarter (S¼) corner of Section 33, Township 20 South,

Range 61 East, M.D.B.&M., ten (10) feet from the property line or fifty (50) feet from the center
line on each side thereof.

(e)	From the South quarter (S¼) corner of Section 33, Township 20 South, Range 61 East,
M.D.B.&M., to the West City Limits, as set forth in Sections 14, 15, 16, 17, 18, and 19, Chapter
24, Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 1949, as amended.

(f)	From Rose Street (Rancho Road Portion) to Bonanza Road, as set forth in Sections l4,

15,	16, 17, 18, and 19, Chapter 24, Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 1949, as amended.

SECTION 2. All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed,
and the Master Plan of Streets and Highways shall be deemed amended to conform with the provisions
of Section 1 of this Ordinance.

SECTION 3. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
shall cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following
its first reasing and adoption in the Las Vegas Review Journal, a daily newspaper published in the
City of Las Vegas.

ATTEST:	APPROVED:

/a/ SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk	/a/ REED WHIPPLE, Mayor Pro Tem

The above and foregoing ordinance was first proposed and read by title to the Board of
Commissioners on the 15th day of August, 1956, and referred to the following committee composed of
Commissioners Fountain and Whipple for recommendation; thereafter the said committee reported
favorably on said ordinance on the 5th day of September, 1956, which was the regular meeting held on
said day, that at said regular meeting held on said 5th day of September, 1956, the proposed ordinance
was read in full to the Board of Commissioners as first introduced and adopted by the following vote:

Voting "Aye":	Commissioners Fountain, Sharp and Mayor Pro Tem Whipple

Voting "Nay":	None	Absent:	Mayor Baker and Commissioner Bunker

ATTEST:	APPROVED:

/s/ SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk	/a/ REED WHIPPLE, Mayor Pro Tem
