and as amended by Chapter 338, Statues of Nevada 1955, at page 562, is hereby
amended to read as follows:

SECTION 10. Mayor, Commissioners, and Municipal Judge - Salary of.

From and after the first Monday of April, 1959, the elected city officials of the
City of Las Vegas shall receive the following salaries and compensations:	The

mayor of the City of Las Vegas shall receive remuneration for her services the sum
of $6000 per annum; each of the commissioners shall receive the sum of $4200 per
annum; the city attorney shall receive such sum as may be determined by the mayor
and board of commissioners which shall not be less than $9000 nor more than
$12,500 per annum; which shall be full compensation for all services rendered
said city. The municipal judge shall devote his full time to the duties of his
office and shall not engage in other business for compensation.

SECTION 2. This act shall become effective on April 1, 1959.
