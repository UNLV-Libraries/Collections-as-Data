A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners held this 28th day of February, 1950 was
called to order at the hour of 7:15 P.M. by His Honor E. W. Cragin with the following
members present: Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple. Absent: City Attorney

Howard W. Cannon, City Manager George O. Treem and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger.

CITY MANAGER	Commissioner Moore moved the resignation of George O. Treem as City Manager be accepted,

Resignation	effective March 1, 1950 and that he be given thirty days pay in lieu of sick leave and

 	vacation he might have accrued.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

ACTING CITY MANAGER Commissioner Moore moved that the City Engineer, W. C. Anderson be appointed Acting City
Appointment	Manager in connection with his other duties.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

PURCHASE OF CARS	Commissioner Moore moved that the City Manager be authorized to purchase from A. C. Grant,

two 8-cylinder Fords at the bid price of $1562.20 to be purchased if the money is available.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

.Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

TIRE BID AWARDED	Commissioner Moore moved the U. S. Tire Supply Co., the low bidder, be awarded the bid for tires

both truck and passenger car, for the year ending February 28, 1951.
 

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

PLUMBING INSPECTOR Commissioner Moore moved the salary of the Plumbing Inspector, Arthur Angel, be raised to
Salary	$350,00 per month effective March 1, 1950. 

  

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Commissioner Whipple passed
his vote.

There being no further business to come before this special meeting, the meeting adjourned.
