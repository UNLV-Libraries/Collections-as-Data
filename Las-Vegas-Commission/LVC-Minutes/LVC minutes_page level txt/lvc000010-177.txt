CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk	Date:	1-23-56

Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, is hereby called to be
held in the Council Chambers of the City Hall, on the 23rd day of January, 1956, at the hour of 4.00 P.M. to
consider the following matters:

1.	Elimination of curb parking on South 5th Street between Oakey Blvd, and San Francisco Avenue.

2.	Service & Material Warrant No. 5557 in the total amount of $6,946.25.

3.	Petition to CAB for additional air service by Continental Air Lines to Las Vegas.

4.	Ordinance to repeal Ordinance No. 675; Hypodermic Ordinance.

5.	Rezoning of Paradise Park.

6.	Apartment house on Baltimore Avenue.

/s/ REED WHIPPLE
Mayor Pro Tem
