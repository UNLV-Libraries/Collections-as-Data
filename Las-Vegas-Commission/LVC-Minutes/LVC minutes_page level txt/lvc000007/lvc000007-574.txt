The recommendation of the Regional Planning Commission on the application of F.E. 
Searway for variance to permit the construction of a store in an R-2 zone at the
southwest corner of H and McWilliams Streets was read to the Board.

Commissioner Whipple moved that a public hearing on the appeal of the decision
of the Planning Commission by Mr. Searway be held April 16, 1952 at 7:30 P.M.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote: Com-

missioners Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Jarrett moved the tentative map of Hardy and Huff Subdivision as
recommended by the Regional Planning Commission be approved with the condition
that a 60 foot street be put through the approximate center of the Subdivision, run-
ning east and west.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the foil owing vote: Com-

missioners Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

The investigation forms for gaming and liquor applicants as submitted by the Po-
lice Department, were approved with the following changes:

1)	All persons that have at least 2% interest in the business be
listed 

2)	Before the signature of the applicant a statement be inserted that a
any false information will subject the license to cancellation
without notice.

Commissioner Whipple moved the City Manager write a letter to the Valley Park
Water Assn, that no one is permitted to tie into the City Sewer that is not in the
City limits.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried.

There being no further business to come before the meeting at this time Commis-
sioner Whipple moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried.
