ORDINANCE NO. 606

AN ORDINANCE TO PROVIDE FOR THE REDUCTION OF THE BUILDING SITE AREA REQUIREMENTS IN THE MEADOWS ADDITION
TO THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS RELATED THERETO; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR
PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH.

WHEREAS, The Master Plan of Land Use, 1953 Revision, for the City of Las Vegas, has classified the
entire area designated on the Official Maps as the Meadows Addition, as an R-3 (Limited Multiple,)

R-4 (Apartment Residence), C-1 (Limited Commercial) and C-2 (General Commercial), Use Districts; and

WHEREAS, the lot size of the lots in said Addition contain only 6,000 square feet, necessitating
a variance in every case of application for multiple dwellings since the minimum building site area
requirement in an R-3 (Limited Multiple) or R-4 (Apartment Resident) z:one is 7,000 feet.

WHEREAS, in order to save time and expense in the future it will be in the public interest to
provide for the issuance of building permits without first applying for a variance in each case.

THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, DO ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

SECTION 1. From and after the passage of this ordinance, the minimum building site requirement
of 7000 square feet for the construction of multiple dwellings shall be reduced to 6,000 square
feet for those lots in the Meadow Addition classified as R-3 in the Master Plan of Land Use, 1953
Revision. In no case, however, shall a multiple dwelling or dwelling group occupy a site which
provides less than 1700 square feet of land per family or dwelling unit in said R-3 (Limited Multiple)
classification.

SECTION 2. From and after the passage of this ordinance, the minimum building site requirement
of 7,000 square feet for the construction of multiple dwellings shall be reduced to 6,000 square feet
for those lots in the Meadows Addition classified as R-4 in the Master Plan of Land Use, 1953 Revision.

In no case, however, shall a multiple dwelling or dwelling group occupy a site which provided less than
700 square feet of land per family or dwelling unit in said R-4 classification

SECTION 3. Upon passage of this ordinance, the Building Department shall issue the necessary
permits without requiring a variance, provided the plans of such construction are in accordance
with any other ordinances of the City.

SECTION 4. All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

SECTION 5. This ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its final reading and adoption
and final publication as in the next section provided.

SECTION 6. The City Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas shall cause this
ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following its first
reading and adoption in the Las Vegas Review Journal, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las
Vegas.
