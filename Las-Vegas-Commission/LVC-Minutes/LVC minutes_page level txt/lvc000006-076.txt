REPORT OF AUDIT
ACCOUNTS

For the year 1946	At this time the report of the Audit of accounts of the City of

	Las Vegas for the Calendar year 1946 as submitted by George H.

Wood, was presented to the Board and ordered filed.

CITY HOUSING AUTHORITY Commissioner Baskin moved that the City of Las Vegas withdraw

	from the Clark County Housing Authority and proceed to organize

a City Housing Authority, for the purpose of taking over Kelso
Turner project and the City Clerk is hereby directed to so notify
Langden W. Post, Director Region Six, Federal Public Housing
Authority, San Francisco, Calif. of said action by wire.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark and His Honor voting aye; noes,

none. Absent:	Commissioner Corradetti.

C.A.A. LEASE CONTRACT	At this time the City Manager presented the Lease Contract No.

C. 6 C. A. -1158	C.6 C.A.-1158 between the Department of Commerce, Civil Aero-

	nautics Administration and the City of Las Vegas for the operation

of a radio range control quarters and teletype station at Mc-
Carran Field which said lease expires on June 30, 1947. After
some discussion Mayor Cragin referred the lease to the Legal
Department for the reason the City has agreed to turn over the
Airport to the Army in the near future and requested an opinion
from the City Attorney's office as to whether or not said lease
can be terminated when and if the Airport is turned over to the
Army.
