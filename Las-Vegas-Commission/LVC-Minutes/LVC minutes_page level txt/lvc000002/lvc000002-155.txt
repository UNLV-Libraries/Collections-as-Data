	CALL FOR SPECIAL MEETING

	Las Vegas, Nevada, Jany. 28, 1925

To the Board of City Commissioners of the
City of Las Vegas and to Florence S. Doherty,

City Clerk.

I hereby call a special meeting of the Board of City Com-
missioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, to be held at the office
of the City Clerk in the County Court House in the City of Las Vegas,

Nevada, on Wednesday the 28th day of January, 1925, at the hour of
three thirty o'clock P.M. of said day, to consider and act upon the
resignation of A.W. Ham, as City Attorney of the City of Las Vegas
and to consider and act upon the appointment of a successor in the
event the resignation heretofore tendered is accepted. And the City
Clerk is hereby directed to give notice of said meeting in pursuance
with this call therefor.

_____W.C. German_________________

Mayor of the City of
Las Vegas, Nevada.

NOTICE OF SPECIAL MEETING OF THE
BOARD OF CITY COMMISSIONERS.

Las Vegas, Nevada. Jany, 28, 1925.

To the members of the Board of City Commissioners
of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

In pursuance of a call this day made in writing by the
Mayor of the City of Las Vegas for a special meeting of the
Board of City Commissioners, I hereby in pursuance of said call
give you and each of you notice that a special meeting of the
Board of City Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada,
will be held at the office of the City Clerk in the County
Court House in the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, on Wednesday, the
28th day of January, 1925, at the hour of 3:30 o'clock P.M. of
said day, to consider and act upon the resignation of A.W. Ham,
as City Attorney of the City of Las Vegas, and to consider and
act upon the appointment of a successor in the event the
resignation heretofore tendered is accepted.

Witness my hand and the seal of said City.

Florence S. Doherty__________________

City Clerk of the City of Las Vegas.

We hereby acknowledge due service of the above and foregoing
notice this 28th day of January, 1925.

(Signed) F.E. Matzdorf
A.R. Anderson
W.H. Elwell

City Commissioners
W.C. German

Mayor.

Thereupon the Clerk read the resignation of A.W. Ham, as City Attorney, as follows:

	Las Vegas, Nevada. Jan. 28, 1925.

To the Mayor and City Commissioners,

City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

Gentlemen:

I hereby resign as City attorney of the City of Las
Vegas, Nevada.

My private legal business is such that I am compelled
to devote all my time to same.

Respectfully

A.W. Ham

whereupon, on motion of Commissioner Matzdorf, seconded by Commissioner Anderson, it was
ordered that Mr. Ham's resignation be accepted, to take effect at once. The vote on said
motion was as follows: Commissioners Matzdorf, Elwell, Anderson and His Honor the Mayor,
W.C. German, Aye. Noes, none.

Thereupon the Mayor announced that nominations were in order for the appointment of
a city attorney, to fill the place of A.W. Ham, resigned, whereupon Commissioners Anderson
nominated F.A. Stevens. Commissioner Matzdorf seconded the nomination.
