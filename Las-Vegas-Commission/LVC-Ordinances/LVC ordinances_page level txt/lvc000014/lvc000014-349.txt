ORDINANCE No. 361

AN ORDINANCE DECLARING CERTAIN TERRITORY ADJOINING AND CONTIGUOUS TO THE CORPORATE LIMITS OF THE CITY OF LAS
VEGAS TO BE ANNEXED TO THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; ORDERING A PLAT SHOWING SAID TERRITORY TO BE RECORDED IN THE
OFFICE OF THE COUNTY RECORDER OF THE COUNTY OF CLARK, STATE OF NEVADA; AND OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATING
THERETO.

WHEREAS, Las Vegas Union School District Board of Education, a body politic, of the County of
Clark, State of Nevada, owner, with reversion to the City of Las Vegas, Clark County, Nevada, of the pro-
perty herein described, has duly petitioned the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas to annex
said territory to said City; NOW, THEREFORE,

THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS DO ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

Section 1. All that certain territory in the County of Clark, State of Nevada, adjoining and
contiguous to the corporate limits of the City of Las Vegas, and being bounded and described as follows:

From a point on the easterly boundary of Sunrise Park Tract No. 1 as recorded in Book
2 of Plats, Page 2 Clark County Records, said point lying N. 0°27'45" E. a distance of
750.00 feet from a certain concrete monument denoting the center line intersection of
Sunrise Street and Second Street in said subdivision; thence N. 89°32'30" W. a dis-
tance of 30.00 feet to the true point of beginning; thence N. 0°27'45" E. a distance
of 500.00 feet to a point; thence N. 89°32'00" W. a distance of 527.65 feet to a
point, thence S. 2°22'15" W. a distance of 500.34 feet to a point, thence S. 89°32'30"

E. a distance of 544.31 feet to the point of beginning, containing 6.1$ acres, more or
less,

together with the tenements, property and inhabitants thereof, is hereby declared to be annexed to and be
a part of the City of Las Vegas and subject to all the taxes and laws thereof.

Section 2. The City Engineer of the City of Las Vegas is hereby ordered and directed to prepare
a plat showing the territory hereby annexed to the City of Las Vegas and to cause said plat to be recorded
in the Office of the County Recorder of the County of Clark, State of Nevada.

Section 3. This ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its publication, as in the next
section provided, and final passage.

Section 4. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
shall cause this Ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following
its first reading and adoption, in the Las Vegas Evening Review Journal, a daily newspaper published
in the City of Las Vegas.

/s/ E. W. Cragin___________
