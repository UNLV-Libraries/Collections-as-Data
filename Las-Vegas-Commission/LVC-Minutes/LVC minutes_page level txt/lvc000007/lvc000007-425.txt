RESOLUTION - Assessment	C

District No. 200-5	Commissioner Bunker introduced and moved the adoption of the following

	resolution:

RESOLUTION

WHEREAS, that pertain portion of the City of Las Vegas, hereinafter mentioned
has been and is still without adequate sanitary sewer facilities, and

WHEREAS, numerous requests have been had for the installation of sanitary
sewers in the hereinafter mentioned portion of the City of Las Vegas, and

WHEREAS, the Board of Commissioners are desirous that the citizens of this
area be supplied with adequate sanitary sewer facilities, and

WHEREAS, it appears that the sanitary sewers should be installed in and along
the following described streets, and portions of streets and casements, to-wit;

In Ashby Avenue from 115.77 feet East of the center line of Cashman Drive to
Strong Drive; in the casement on the west side of Lots 3 and 16, Block 1,

385.00 feet North to existing sanitary sewer 6.00 feet North of the center
line of Charleston Boulevard; in Campbell Drive, 385.00 feet North to ex-
isting sanitary sewer 6.00 feet North of the center line of Charleston
Boulevard; in Strong Drive from Ashby Avenue 663.55 feet North to the ex-
isting sanitary sewer 6.00 feet North of the center line of Charleston
Boulevard; all of above described area lying wholly within the McNeil Tract
Subdivision.

NOW, THEREFOR, BE IT RESOLVED, by the Board of City Commissioners of the
City of Las Vegas, at a regular meeting thereof held on the 15th day of August, 1951,
that the City Engineer be and is hereby directed to prepare plats, diagrams and es-
timates of cost for the installation a sanitary sewer system in and along the following
described streets and portions of streets and casements, to-wit:

In Ashby Avenue from 115.77 feet East of the center line of Cashman Drive
to Strong Drive; in the casement on the west side of Lots 3 and 16, Block
1, 385.00 feet North to existing sanitary sewer 6.00 feet North of the
center line of Charleston Boulevard; in Campbell Drive, 385.00 feet North
to existing sanitary sewer 6.00 feet North of the center line of Charleston
Boulevard; in Cahlan Drive 385.00 feet North to existing sanitary sewer

6.00 feet North of the center line of Charleston Boulevard; in Strong Drive
from Ashby Avenue 663.55 feet North to the existing sanitary sewer 6.00

feet North of the center line of Charleston Boulevard; all of above described
area lying wholly within the McNeil Tract Subdivision.
