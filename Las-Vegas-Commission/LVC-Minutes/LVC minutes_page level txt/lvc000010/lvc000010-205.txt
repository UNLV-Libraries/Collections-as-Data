Commissioner Sharp moved the application of Tom Rolofson for license for one
additional slot machine at Hamburger Heaven, 1215 E Street for the first
quarter of 1956 be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Com-

missioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and Mayor Baker voting aye; noes,
none.

Commissioner Sharp moved the application of W.J. Moore for license for 150 ad-
ditional Bingo chairs for the first quarter of 1956 at the Showboat, 2800 Boulder
Highway be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Com-

missioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp,Whipple and Mayor Baker voting aye; noes, none.

Members of the Nevada Beer Wholesaler's Association were present and discussed
the increase in their license fee. They pointed out to the Commission that the
fee is exorbitant as compared with license fees paid in surrounding States.

The Commission agreed to take this matter under advisement.

There being no further business to come before the meeting, Commissioner Sharp
moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Com-

missioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and Mayor Baker voting aye; noes,
none.
