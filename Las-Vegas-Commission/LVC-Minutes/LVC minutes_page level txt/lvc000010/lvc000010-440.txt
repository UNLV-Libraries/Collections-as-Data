PROCLAMATION

WHEREAS; it appears that the Charter of the City of Las Vegas, as amended; provides
that a Primary Election be held on the first Tuesday after the first Monday in May,

1957; and that a General Election be held on the first Tuesday after the first Mon-
day in June; 1957 in the City of Las Vegas; for the nomination and election at large
by the qualified voters of the City of two Commissioners of the second and fourth
class; respectively; a City Attorney; and a Judge of the Municipal Court.

NOW; THEREFORE; IT IS HEREBY ORDERED by the Board of Commissioners of the City
of Las VegaS; that a Primary Election be held in the City of Las Vegas on May 7;

1957; at which election there shall be nominated at large by the qualified voters
of said City; two Commissioners; one of the second class and one of the fourth class;
a City Attorney; and a Judge of the Municipal Court; and

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED; that such General Election be held in the City of Las
Vegas on June 4; 1957; at which election there shall be elected at large by the quali-
fied voters of said City; two Commissioners; one of the second class and one of the
fourth class; who shall be elected and hold office for a period of four years and
until their successors are elected and qualified; a City Attorney; who shall be elected
and hold office for a period of four years and until his successor shall be elected
and qualified; and a Judge of the Municipal Court; who shall be elected and hold of-
fice for a period of four years and until his successor shall be elected and qualified;
and

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED; that the places in said City of holding said elections are
hereby determined as follows:
