ORDINANCE NO. 733

AN ORDINANCE AMENDING SECTION IV OF ORDINANCE 688 DEALING WITH THE SALE OF INTOXICATING LIQUORS BY
PROVIDING THE LOCATION WHERE TAVERNS MAY BE MAINTAINED; PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR THE VIOLATION OF
THIS ORDINANCE; AND PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELAXED THERETO AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES
OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

SECTION 1. Section 4, Chapter 16, Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 1949 as amended by Section
5 of Ordinance 688, is hereby amended to read as follows:

Taverns may be maintained only in that portion of the City of Las Vegas bounded by Main
Street on the west, Ogden Street on the north, 2nd Street on the east, Carson Street on the south;

5th Street between Oakey Boulevard and San Francisco Avenue; Jackson Street from 100 feet east of
"D" Street to 100 feet west of "F" Street and from Monroe to Van Buren on "D" Street; and on each
side of Charleston Boulevard, commencing at the intersection of East Charleston and Fremont Street
and extending westerly along Charleston Boulevard a distance of 103 feet.

SECTION 2. All ordinance or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

SECTION 3. Any person who violates any provision of this ordinance shall be guilty of a
misdemeanor and shall be punished by a fine of not more than $500.00 or six months in the City Jail
or both, in addition to any other penalties which may be imposed by any other ordinances of the
City of Las Vegas.

SECTION 4. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
shall cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately fol-
lowing its first reading and adoption in the Las Vegas Review Journal, a daily newspaper published
in the City of Las Vegas.
