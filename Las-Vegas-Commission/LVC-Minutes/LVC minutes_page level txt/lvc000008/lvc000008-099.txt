CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

June 27, 1952

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is hereby called to
be held in the Council Chambers at the City Hall, on the 27th day of June, 1952 at the hour of
7:30 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Liquor Applications for the 3rd Quarter of 1952.

2. Gaming Applications for the 3rd Quarter of 1952.

3. Public Hearing on Emergency Loan

4. Report of Chief of Police on Gaming and Liquor licenses.

5. Ordinance on Assessment District No. 100-16.

6.	Ordinance on Assessment District No. 100-17.

7.	Final Payments to J.M. Murphy Const. Co.

s/ C.D. Baker
Mayor
