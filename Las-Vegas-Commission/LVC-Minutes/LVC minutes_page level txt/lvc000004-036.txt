OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK,

CITY OF LAS VEGAS, CLARK
COUNTY, NEVADA
APRIL 21st, 1937.

Minutes of a recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las
Vegas, Nevada held on the 21st day of April, 1937 at the hour of two o'clock P.M.

Roll call showed the following present Mayor Arnett, Commissioners Krause, Marble and
Perry City Clerk and Deputy City Attorney Ham.

Absent Commissioner Down, and City Attorney Taylor.

A letter from Bebek and Brkich, Contractors was presented to the Board at this time
asking the City that the said Contractors be allowed to hire a Hunt Prossess Man at the
rate of 75¢ per hour.

On motion of Commissioner Marble seconded by Commissioner Krause it was moved and carried
that it be

Resolved, That pursuant to a request made in writing from Bebek and Brkich, contractors
employed by the City of Las Vegas on Las Vegas 1937 Street Improvement Project District No. 3
also known as PWA Docket No. 1021 Nevada for Hunt Process man wages at rate of 75¢ per hour
be granted subject to the approval of the P.W.A. for the State of Nevada.

Vote was Commissioners Krause and Marble and His Honor the Mayor voting aye. Noes
none. Absent Commissioners Down and Perry.

On motion of Commissioner Marble seconded by Commissioner Krause it was moved and carried
that C.D.Baker, City Building Inspector issue a permit to Mrs. E.V. Breckenridge for the moving
into the City of Las Vegas a house from Boulder City Nevada.

Vote was Commissioners Krause, Marble and Perry and His Honor the Mayor voting aye.

Noes, none.

On motion of Commissioner Marble seconded by Commissioner Krause it was moved and
carried that the City of Las Vegas approve of the combining of the gasoline contract of the
Richfield Oil Company and the Rio Grande Oil Company for sale of gasoline to the City of Las
Vegas for the year 1937.

Vote was Commissioners Krause, Marble and Perry and His Honor the Mayor voting aye.

Noes, none.

Mr. Blackman, appeared before the Board at this time in regard to the securing of
aid from he City of Las Vegas for the operation of a recreational programme for the children
of Las Vegas and stated that the sum of $4,000.00 would be needed to carry on this programme.
Nr. Blackman was informed that the Board would give his request serious consideration.

No further appearing before the Board the meeting recessed and adjourned until Tues-
day the 27th day of April, 1937 at the hour of one thirty o'clock P.M.

OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK CITY OF LAS VEGAS
CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

APRIL 24th, 1937.

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO— Viola Burns, City Clerk,

City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

A Special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is hereby called to
be held in the Board of Commissioners room in the City Clerk's office, Saturday the 2 4th day of
April, 1937 at the hour of two o'clock P.M. on said day to consider and act upon the following
business:

1. To consider and act upon Change Order No. 1, for the Las Vegas, 1937 Street Improvement
District No. 3.

Dated this 24th day of April, 1937.

L.L. Arnett,

Mayor of the City of Las Vegas.

TO— L.L. Arnett, Mayor and to Jas. H. Down,
