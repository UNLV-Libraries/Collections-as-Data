CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

LAS VEGAS, NEVADA

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is hereby
called to be held in the Commissioners Council Chamber in the War Memorial Building
on Friday, May 16, 1947 at the hour of 10:00 A.M., to act upon the following:

1.	Check Election returns.

Meeting called to order at the hour of 10:00 A.M. by Mayor E. W.
