Absent Commissioner Joe S. Ronnow.

T he business to be considered at this time, was the signing and acceptance of proposal as
stated by the Works Progress Administration covering the grant, by the Works Progress Administration
and the sponsorship by the City in acquiring and developing of the air port now used and operated
by the Western Air Express Corporation.

Mr. C.J.Mackey representing the local W.P.A. appeared before the board with the necessary papers
to be acted upon by the Board in accepting such grant from the W.P.A. in getting the project
going. After some discussion on the matter and going over the plans as proposed by the C.A.A.
it was decided to contact Mr. Ayers of the C.A.A. by phone in Los Angeles before taking final action in the
matter at this time. Thereupon motion was made by Commissioner Gilbert and Seconded by Commissioner
Corradetti to recess the meeting until one o'clock PM. at which time the discussion would be re-
sumed. Vote being Commissioners, Gilbert, Krause & Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor Voting
Aye, Noes None. The meeting recessed until one o'clock P.M.

At one o'clock P.M. the Mayor and Commissioners reassembled to continue the meeting as was re-
cessed at twelve o'clock P.M. All being present as heretofore mentioned.

Mr. Ayers of the Civil Aeronautics Authority having been contacted by phone regarding the
programme outlined by the Works Progress Administration and giving as his opinion that the
Board would be in order to execute and sign the proposal as submitted by the W.P.A. therefore
upon motion by Commissioner Gilbert and Seconded by Commissioner Corradetti it was moved and
carried that the Mayor and Clerk be authorized and empowered to sign the papers covering the
project being numbered 50043 a copy of which is on file with the City Clerk. Vote being
Commissioners, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting Aye, Noes None.

No further business appearing before the board at this time, on motion of Commissioner
Gilbert and Seconded by Commissioner Corradetti, it was moved and carried that this meeting
adjourn. Vote Commissioners, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor Voting
Aye, Noes, None.

Thereupon said meeting adjourned*
