CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING	October 10, 1949

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is hereby called to
	be held in the Council Chambers at the City Hall, on the 10th day of October, 1949, at the

hour of 4:00 P.M., to consider the following matters:

1.	Emergency Loan

2.	Service & Material Warrants

3.	Gaming applications - Renewals and additional on file

4.	Solicitors, Canvassers and Peddlers licenses - recommendation from Chief of Police.

/s/ E. W. Cragin

First Nat'l Bank	with the Public Employees Retirement Board to take over half of the Emergency Loan to

this money.
