FROM CARSON AVENUE TO A POINT DISTANT APPROXIMATELY 237 FEET
	NORTH OF THE CENTER LINE OF BONANZA ROAD, IN THE CITY OF LAS

VEGAS; ESTABLISHING THE OFFICIAL GRADES OF THE STREET AND CURBS
IN NORTH MAIN AND BONANZA ROAD, ACCORDING TO THE PLANS OF THE

RELATING THERETO; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF
ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH," having been read by title

Motion seconded by Commissioner Moore and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor

voting aye. Noes, none.
