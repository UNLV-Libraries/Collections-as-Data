On motion of Commissioner German, seconded by Commissioner
Mundy the following Preambles and Resolution were duly adopted:

WHEREAS, Clark Street Between Sixth and Seventh Streets, in
Wardie Addition to the City of Las Vegas, and the alley between
Sixth and Seventh Streets from Clark Street to Bonneville Street
in said Wardie Addition, have been and are becoming in an impass-
ible condition by reason of being flooded with water leaking from
the water main supplying water to said section, and

WHEREAS the Water main supplying said Wardie Addition to the
City of Las Vegas in the alley above mentioned is in such a con-
dition that a continuation of the present condition constitutes
and is now a nuisance and a menace to the residents of said section
of said City of Las Vegas, and

WHEREAS, said leaking water main has now caused considerable
damage to the said street and alley and if said condition is allowed
to continue will cause further damage to said street and alley, and
may cause damage to the property bordering oh same, and

WHEREAS demands have been made upon the has Vegas Land & Water
Company, the South Nevada Land & Development Company, and the
Miller-Buzick Corporation, each of which are corporations doing busi-
ness in the City of Las Vegas, State of Nevada, to repair the same,
and

WHEREAS, no consideration has been given by any of the above
corporations to the demands made by the City, NOW THEREFORE,

BE IT RESOLVED, that the City of Las Vegas, by and
through its proper department, repair or cause to
be repaired the said pipe line so that said condition
will be remedied, and for that purpose the said de-
partment is hereby authorized and directed to repair
the same;

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that as soon as said repairs
are made, the said department making said repair
or repairs render a statement to the Board of Comm-
issioners showing the actual necessary cost thereof,
and that immediately thereafter the City Attorney
bring suit against any and all parties interested
or who may be interested in the supplying of said
water and the maintenance of said water distributing
system, for the amount expended by the City;

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the City Attorney shall
also bring such proceedings as he may determine pro-
per to compel the parties owning or to own or having
charge of the water distributing system for Wardie
Addition to the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, to maintain
the same in such manner as will adequately serve the
people of said Addition with water and not suffer any
damage to be done to the streets and alleys of said
City.

Vote on said Resolution was as follows: Commissioners
Mundy, German and His Honor the Mayor voting aye.

Noes none.
