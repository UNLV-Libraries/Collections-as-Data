OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS,
CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

March 16th, 1929.

At a recessed regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,
Clark County, Nevada, held on the 16th day of March, 1929, at the hour of 7:30 P. M.

PRESENT: His Honor, the Mayor, J. F. Hesse, Commissioners W. B. Mundy, W. C. German,
together with the City Attorney and Clerk; also Engineer for the City.

ABSENT: Commissioners O. J. Smith and Roy Neagle.

Meeting called to order by the Mayor.

Upon motion of Commissioner Mundy, seconded by Commissioner German, the following
resolution was duly adopted: RESOLVED that the City of Las Vegas, acting through the
Board of City Commissioners of said City, does hereby authorize and direct the Mayor
in behalf of said City, to consent to the assignment by Roche-Axman Company of Los Angeles,
of the compensation due them under that certain contract entered into between said City
and the said Roche-Axman Company on the 13th day of March, 1929, to California Group
Corporation, subject, however, to the right of said city to withhold and deduct such
sums as it is provided in the documents forming said contract that said city may withhold
and / or deduct. Vote on said resolution was as follows: Commissioners W. B. Mundy, W. C.
German and His Honor, the Mayor, J. F. Hesse voting aye; noes; none.

No further business appearing before the Board at this time, the meeting was recessed
to Tuesday, March 19th, 1929 at the hour of three o'clock, P. M.

OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS,
CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

March 19th, 1929.

At a recessed regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,
Clark County, Nevada, held on the 19th day of March, 1929, at the hour of three o'clock, P. M.

PRESENT: His Honor, the Mayor, J. F. Hesse, Commissioners W. B. Mundy, W. C. German,
together with the City Clerk.

ABSENT:	Commissioners O. J. Smith, Roy Neagle and the City Attorney.

Meeting called to order by the Mayor.

Upon motion of Commissioner Mundy, seconded by Commissioner German, it was unanimously
RESOLVED, that the Mayor be and he is hereby authorized and directed to sign, and the Clerk
of this Board be and he is hereby authorized and directed to attest and cause to be
published at least once a week for four consecutive weeks prior to April 20, 1929, in the
Las Vegas "REVIEW", a daily newspaper of general circulation in the County of Clark,
printed and published at the City of Las Vegas, in said County of Clark, a notice of the
sale of Fifty of Las Vegas Special 1929 Pavement and Curb Improvement Bonds, such notice
to be in substantially the following form, to-wit:

NOTICE TO BOND BIDDERS.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Clark County, Nevada, will

receive bids until__________o'clock _________M. on the ______day of _________, 1929, for the

purchase of fifty (50) bonds of the Las Vegas Special 1929 Pavement and Curb Improvement
issue. Said bonds consist of forty (40) bonds in the denomination of One Thousand Dollars
($1000) each, of which four (4) bonds of the denomination of One Thousand dollars each
will be payable in ten annual installments, commencing on the first day of January, 1930,
and also consists of Ten bonds in the denomination of Three Hundred Seventy seven and
88/100 Dollars ($577.88) each, of which one (1) of the denomination of Three Hundred
Seventy seven and 88/100 Dollars will be payable each year commencing on the first day of
January, 1930. Said bonds will bear interest at a rate not exceeding seven per centum
per annum, payable semi-annually, and will bear date January 1, 1929.
