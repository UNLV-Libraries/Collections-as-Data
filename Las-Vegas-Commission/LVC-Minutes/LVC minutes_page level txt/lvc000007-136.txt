Las Vegas, Nevada
June 29, 1950

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is hereby
called to be held in the Council Chambers at the City Hall, on the 29th day of June. 1950,
at the hour of 3:30 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Approve tentative Assessment Rolls for Bond retirement on the following
special Assessment Districts:

#200-1 - Boulder Dam Homesite Addition Sewer

#200-2 - West Charleston Blvd. Sewer

#100-3 - 17th & Spencer Street, Street improvement.

#100-7 - South Main Street Lighting.

2.	Approve revised Assessment Roll for Bond retirement on Special Assessment
District #200-3 - South Meadows Addition Sewer.

3.	Report of Board of Civil Service Trustees

s/ E. W. Gragin____________

Mayor
