Commissioner Peccole moved that pursuant to the report of the City Manager that the
sewer lines were insufficient, the application of Wilbur Clark to connect to the
City Sewer be denied.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Moore and carried by the following votes Commissioners
Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

At this time the fare rates of the taxicab companies were submitted to the Board for
their consideration. Commissioner Whipple moved that these rates as submitted be
approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following votes Commissioners
Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Moore moved this meeting recess.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following votes Commissioners
Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

The meeting was called to order at the hour of 5s20 P.M. by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin
with all members present as of the opening session.

Commissioner Moore moved that R. F. Malburg, Chief of Police of the City of Las Vegas
be given a vote of confidence.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following votes Commissioners
Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Commissioners Bunker and Peccole
passed their votes.

There being no further business to come before the meeting at this time, Commissioner
Bunker moved this meeting recess until December 16, 1949 at the hour of 3:00 P.M.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following votes Commissioners
Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Las Vegas, Nevada
December 16, 1949

A recessed regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners held this 16th day of December,
1949 was called to order at the hour of 3:40 P.M. by His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
with the following members present* Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole; City
Manager George O. Treem, City Attorney Howard W. Cannon and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger,
Absent: Mayor Cragin.

Commissioner Moore moved that Payroll Warrants Nos. 1096 to 1098 inclusive in the net
sum of $27,842.l6 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes, none.

Absent: Mayor Cragin,

Commissioner Moore moved that Service & Material Warrants Nos. 1879 to 1881 inclusive
in the total amount of $11,772,36 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized
to sign same.
