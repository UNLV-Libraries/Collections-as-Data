AFFIDAVIT OF PUBLICATION.

STATE OF NEVADA ) ss
 County of Clark)

C. P. Squires, being first duly sworn, deposes and says: That he publisher of the Las

Vegas Age, a tri-weekly newspaper of general circulation, printed and published at
Las Vegas, in the County of Clark, State of Nevada, and that the Attached City 

Ordinance #151 was continuously published in said newspaper for a period of three issues
from January 7th to January 11th inclusive, being the issues of said newspaper for the

following dates, to-wit: January 7, 9, and 11, 1930.

That said newspaper was regularly issued and circulated on each of the dates above
named. That the charge for publishing the same was $489.60.

Signed C. P. Squires.

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 13th day of Jan. 1930.

Frank A. Stevens.

Notary Public in and for Clark County Nevada.

(Notarial Seal)
