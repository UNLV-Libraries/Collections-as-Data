An Ordinance entitled:	AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND CERTAIN SECTIONS OF CHAPTER 17, CODE OF THE

CITY OF LAS VEGAS, 1949, BY PROVIDING FOR THE POWERS AND DUTIES OF THE CITY CLERK AS TO
THE COLLECTION OF LICENSES; PROVIDING THE DUTY OF THE LICENSEE AS TO PAYMENTS; CHANGING THE
RATE OF LICENSE TAX FOR TRAILER PARKS AND COURTS, AND OTHER MATTERS PROPERTY RELATED THERETO,
having been referred to Commissioners Peccole and Whipple, was reported on favorably at this
time with the following revision.

It is recommended that quarterly license fees for Trailer Parks in the City of Las Vegas
be as follows:

a)	For one to 18 Trailer Spaces, #20.00

b)	for	19	to	30	Trailer	Spaces,	25.00

c)	For	31	to	50	Trailer	Spaces;	3 0.00

d)	For	51	to	 80	Trailer	Spaces,	35.00

e)	For	81	Trailer Spaces	and above 50.00

Thereafter the ordinance was read in full as recommended, and Commissioner Peccole moved the
