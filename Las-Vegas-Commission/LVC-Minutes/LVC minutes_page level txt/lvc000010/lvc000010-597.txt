WHEREAS, the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas and the
Clark County School District are both desirous of exchanging unused real
property belonging to both parties; and

WHEREAS, the properties to be exchanged have been appraised and
if such exchange is approved, Clark County School District will pay to
the City of Las Vegas the sum of $22,505.20 in cash, representing the
difference in the appraised value of the respective properties conveyed.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE
CITY OF LAS VEGAS that:

The Board hereby declares its intention to exchange the hereinafter
described property with the Clark County School District.
