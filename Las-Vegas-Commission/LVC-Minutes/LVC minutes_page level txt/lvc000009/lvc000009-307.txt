RESOLUTION

WHEREAS, the Mayor and Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas have learned with
deep sorrow of the death oh September 28, 1954, at Hawthorne, Nevada, of United States
Senator Pat McCarran; and

WHEREAS, Senator McCarran was from 1932 until his death one of the United States Senators
from the State of Nevada and has always rendered valuable aid and counsel to the people of
the State of Nevada and to the citizens of the City of Las Vegas; and

WHEREAS, during his long career he has served not only in the legislative branch of the
State of Nevada but also in the judicial branch, and during his entire career in public
life, he has endeared himself by words, actions, and deeds to the people of the State of
Nevada;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,
Nevada, that the condolences of the members of said Board are hereby noted over the passing
of an outstanding public figure and our sympathies are extended to his widow, her
children, and all other surviving relatives of the deceased; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that this Resolution be spread upon the minutes of the City of Las
Vegas, Nevada, and that a copy be delivered to Mrs. McCarran, and then when this body
adjourns at the completion of business today, it do so in memory of this distinguished
servant to the people of the State of Nevada.

Dated at Las Vegas, Nevada, this 29th day of September, 1954.

ATTEST:

s/ Shirley Ballinger	s/ C. D. Baker
