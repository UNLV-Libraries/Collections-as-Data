CODE OF STANDARD
SPECIFICATIONS

LEGISLATIVE COMMITTEE
Governmental Affairs

ORDINANCE NO. 618
Setback

CLAIM FOR REFUND
Sal Sagev Tavern

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye;
noes, none.

Commissioner Whipple moved that three copies of the Code of Standard
Specifications placed on file in the City Clerk's Office and she be authorized
to publish the Notice that said copies are on file.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote;
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes,
none.

The City Attorney reported that he had received a letter from the Legislative
Committee, Governmental. Affairs. The City Attorney requested the Commission to
inform him if they have any legislative amendments they wish to have enacted.

At this time Commissioners Jarrett and Whipple members of the committee for study
and recommendation on the proposed setback ordinance reported favorably.

An ordinance entitled:	AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND CHAPTER 24, Section 26, Code of Las

VEGAS, NEVADA, 1949, AS AMENDED BY ORDINANCE NO. 573 BY ADDING THE FOLLOWING
MINIMUM BUILDING LINE SETBACKS AND STREET WIDTH LINES FOR WESTERN STREET AND
INDUSTRIAL ROAD, 25TH STREET AND SAN FRANCISCO AVENUE AND DECATUR BOULEVARD TO
THOSE SETBACKS ALREADY ESTABLISHED IN ORDINANCE NO. 573; PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR
THE VIOLATION THEREOF: AND PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO was
read in full to the Board.

Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved the above entitled ordinance No. 618 be
adopted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

At this time the Board considered the claim for refund of J. Kell Houssels, Sal
Sagev Tavern, 1 Fremont Street for overpayment on two slot machines in the
amount of $80.00

Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved this claim for refund in the amount of $80.00
be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes,
none.

There being no further business to come before the Board at this time, Commissioner
Whipple moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes,
none.
