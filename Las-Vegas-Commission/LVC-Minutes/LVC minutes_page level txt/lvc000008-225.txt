An Ordinance entitled: AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND SECTION IV OF ORDINANCE NO. 378 ALSO REFERRED TO AS
SECTION 10, CHAPTER 24, OF THE LAS VEGAS CITY CODE, 1949, BY AMENDING THE LAND USE PLAN MAP ADOPTED
BY THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, AND CHANGING THE ZONE DESIGNATION OF CERTAIN AREAS ON THE SAID MAP: having
been reported on favorably by Commissioners Bunker and Peccole, was read in full to the Board.

Thereafter Commissioner Peccole moved the above entitled Ordinance No. 516 be adopted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett,
PECCOLE, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioners Bunker and Peccole reported favorably on the proposed ordinance to adopt the Building Code.

Thereafter Commissioner Bunker moved the City Clerk be ordered to publish the required notice that the
Building Code is on file and ready for adoption.

Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett and Peccole were appointed to meet with the Planning Commission and John
Lynott on the street right-of-way in Maryland Parkway Tract No. 2.

Commissioner Whipple moved that pursuant to the recommendation of the City Engineer that the San Fran-
cisco Street Sewer Project be accepted, that the request of Wells Cargo Inc. for final payment in the
total amount of $4,850.72 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign the warrant when
prepared.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett,
Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Peccole moved the recommendation of the Nevada Society of Professional Engineers, Southern
Nevada Chapter, that the following be appointed as members of the Engineering Board, be accepted:

HARLAN BROWN
GEORGE VON TOBEL
C.C. BOYER

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett,
Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioners Jarrett and Whipple, committee for recommendation on the proposed sign ordinance requested
additional time before reporting.

An Ordinance entitled: AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND CERTAIN SECTIONS OF CHAPTER 17, CODE OF THE CITY OF LAS
VEGAS, 1949, BY PROVIDING FOR THE POWERS AND DUTIES OF THE CITY CLERK aS TO THE COLLECTION OF LICENSES;
PROVIDING FOR THE DUTY OF THE LICENSEE AS TO PAYMENTS, CHANGING THE RAIL OF LICENSE TAX FOR TRAILER
PARKS AND COURTS, AND OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO, AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF
ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH; was read by title and referred to Commissioner Peccole and Whipple for
recommendation.

An Ordinance entitled: AN ORDINANCE TO REPEAL ORDINANCES NO. 331, AND 345, ALSO KNOWN AS CHAPTER 4, CODE
OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA, 1949 BY PROVIDING FOR A PUBLIC POUND, APPOINTING A POUND KEEPER AND PRESCRIBING
HIS POWERS AND DUTIES; PROVIDING FOR THE REGISTRATION OF DOGS AND OTHER ANIMALS; PROVIDING FOR THE

WITH; was read by title and referred to Commissioners Bunker and Jarrett for recommendation.

At this time Commissioner Peccole left the meeting.

Commissioner Whipple moved the following resolution commending the Junior Chamber of Commerce for the
Christmas Decorations on Fremont Street be adopted and made a portion of the Minutes of this Board.

WHEREAS, the junior Chamber of Commerce of the City of Las Vegas by hard work
and diligence, and with a deep sense of pride in their City was instrumental in decorating Fremont
Avenue from Main to 6th Streets in a true Christmas motif; and

WHEREAS, all the citizens of the City of Las Vegas appreciate the results achieved
by the efforts of the Junior Chamber of Commerce in beautifying their City during the Holiday Season;
and

WHEREAS, no one is more appreciative of these efforts than the Board of City Commissioners
of the City of Las Vegas,

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,
Nevada, at a regular meeting thereof held on the 17th day of December, 1952, that the said City by this
Resolution extends its compliments and appreciation to the Junior Chamber of Commerce of the City of Las
Vegas for the excellent Christmas Decorations placed on Fremont Street during the 1952 Holiday Season;
and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the City Clerk be and she hereby is directed to spread a
copy of this Resolution on the Minutes of the Books of said City; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the City Clerk be and she hereby is directed to send a
copy of this Resolution to the Junior Chamber of Commerce of the City of Las Vegas.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett,
Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Peccole.
