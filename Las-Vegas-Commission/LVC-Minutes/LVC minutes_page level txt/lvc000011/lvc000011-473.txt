BID NO. 59.35	The Commission considered the recommendation of the Public Works Department that

Traffic Signal Revision;	Bid No. 59.35 for the installation of the traffic signal revision at the intersection

5th and San Francisco	of 5th Street and San Francisco Avenue be awarded to the Nevada Electric Company,

317 Wall Street, in the amount of $3,864.00.

Award

Nevada Electric Company              Director of Public Works Sauer stated that this was the only bid received and that

the engineer's estimate for this installation was $2,775.63, and although the bid
received was higher it could still be considered within range. He further advised
that the City's proportionate share for 1/3 of this cost would be $1,288.00. This
would be in accordance with agreement between the City, County and State dated
February 16, 1959.

Commissioner Fountain moved that Bid No. 59.35 for the installation of the traffic
signal revision at the intersection of 5th Street and San Francisco Avenue be
awarded to the Nevada Electric Company, 317 Wall Street, in the amount of $3,864.00,
and the City's proportionate share of this cost to be $1,288.00.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Elwell and carried by the following vote:	

Commissioners Elwell, Fountain and His Honor Acting Mayor Pro Tem Sharp voting aye; noes,
none. Absent:	Mayor C. D. Baker and Mayor Pro Tem Reed Whipple.
