City Engineer Wade requested permission to spend $6,000 for processing of 10,000
yards of gravel for use by the Street Department. Mr. Wade advised the Commission
that the Clark County Road Department will perform this service at cost.

Commissioner Sharp moved that authorization be given to issue a purchase order not
to exceed 10,000 square yards of gravel at 60¢ per yard to the Clark County Road
Department for processing gravel for use by the Street Department.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Fountain, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner
Whipple.

City Engineer Wade presented the map showing the proposed location for the road for
the Rehabilitation Farm and Sewer Disposal plant.

Commissioner Fountain moved that the plot plan for the location of the road for the
Rehabilitation Farm and Sewer Plant be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Fountain, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner
Whipple.

City Engineer Wade requested a four weeks extension of time of equipment rental of a
Cat and Scraper to be used for road construction.

Commissioner Fountain moved that a purchase order be authorized to be issued to
Cashman for rental of a Cat and a Scraper for an additional four weeks from January
27, 1957 to be used for road construction.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Fountain, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner
Whipple.

Commissioner Bunker moved that the Public Works Department be instructed to prepare
plans and specifications and the City Clerk authorized to advertise for bids for
light globes.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Fountain, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner
Whipple.

On motion of Commissioner Bunker, duly seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried unani-
mously, the deed from Susanna J. Mankiewicz for Owens Avenue is accepted and the
City Clerk instructed to draw up the standard resolution accepting the deed which is
hereby adopted, and the City Clerk authorized to have the deed recorded:

WHEREAS, the City of Las Vegas has deemed it necessary to acquire certain property
hereinafter referred to for the purpose of the extension of Owens Avenue, and

WHEREAS, the City has purchased said property from the legal owner thereof,

NOW, THEREFORE, on motion of Commissioner Wendell Bunker duly seconded by
Commissioner Harris P. Sharp, and carried unanimously, the following resolution is
adopted, and order made:

BE IT RESOLVED, this 19th day of December, 1956, by the Board of Commissioners
of the City of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada, that the deed dated the
12th day of December, 1956, executed by Susanna J. Mankiewicz, Joseph S. Mankiewicz,

Mary Magdalene Mankiewicz unto the City of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada,
and that the real property described therein be, and is hereby accepted,

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that a copy of this resolution be attached to said deed,
and that the same be recorded in the office of the County Recorder of the County of
Clark, State of Nevada, and this resolution be filed in the records of this Board.
