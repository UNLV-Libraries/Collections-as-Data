By their as aforesaid, the City Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Clark County, Nevada
have unanimously sustained all of the charges filed against the accused , Mayor John L. Russell,
and accordingly, it is hereby

ORDERED AND ADJUDGED, by the said Board of City Commissioners of said City, that John L.
Russell, Mayor of said City, is guilty of the acts charged and specified in the written charges
filed herein, and that said acts constitute " incompetency, malconduct, malfeasance and nonf-
eanse in office", under Section 12, Chapter 11 of the Charter of the City of Las Vegas, Clark
county, Nevada, and therefore cause for the removal from his said office of Mayor, of the said
John L. Russell, under the provisions of said Section;

THAT the said John L. Russell be, and he is hereby removed from the Office of Mayor of
the City of Las Vegas, Clark County, Nevada; and that said Office be, and it is hereby declared
to be vacant from and after the 12th day of May, 1941; and it is further

ORDERED that this Judgment and Order be filed by the City Clerk of said City, and that the
substance thereof be entered in the Minutes and Records of the acts of the Board of City Comm-
issioners of said City, by said City Clerk.

Judgment and Order rendered and entered this 12th day of May, 1941.
