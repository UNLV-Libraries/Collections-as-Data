CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

Las Vegas, Nevada
May 5, 1952

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

	

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is 
hereby called to be held in the Mayor's Office at the City Hall, on the 5th day
of May, 1952, at the hour of 9:30 P.M. to consider the following matters;

1.	Payroll Warrants Nos. 1294 to 1298 in the net amount
of $30,399.64.

2.	Service & Material Warrants Nos. 2268 in the total amount of $9,525.36.

s/ C.D. Baker____________________

C.D. BAKER, Mayor

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas held this
5th day of May, 1952, was called to order at the hour of 10:00 A.M. with the fol-
lowing members present:

Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

City Manager	C.W. Shelley

PAYROLL WARRANTS	Commissioner Whipple moved that Payroll Warrants Nos. 1294 to 1298 inclusive in the

net amount of $30,399.64 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign
same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and Carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner

Peccole.

SERVICE & MATERIAL	Commissioner Whipple moved that Service and Material Warrant No. 2268 in the total

WARRANTS	amount of $9,525.36 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign

same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner
Peccole.

There being no further business to come before this special meeting, the meeting
adjourned.
