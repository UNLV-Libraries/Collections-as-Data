No. 331	PRESCRIBING THE POWERS AND DUTIES OF THE POUND MASTER; FIXING THE FEES

Public Pound	AND CHARGES TO BE COLLECTED BY HIM, AND DIRECTING THE DISPOSITION

	THEREOF; PROVIDING FOR THE REGISTRATION OF DOGS AND DOG KENNELS AND

FIXING THE FEES THEREFOR; PROVIDING FOR THE VACCINATION OF DOGS AND
CATS; PROVIDING FOR THE REDEMPTION, SALE OR OTHER DISPOSITION OF
BOGS AND OTHER ANIMALS IMPOUNDED; PROHIBITING CRUELTY TO ANIMALS;
PROVIDING A PENALTY FOR THE VIOLATION OF ANY OF THE PROVISIONS THERE-
OF; REPEALING ORDINANCES NO. 5, 12, 19, 51, 58, 63 AND 101, AND ALL
ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF ORDINANCES OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS IN CON-
FLICT THEREWITH, AND OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATING THERETO," having
heretofore been read to the Board by title was referred to a
Committee who held it up for further study and after revision is again
read by title and referred to Commissioners Baskin and Clark for report
at the next regular meeting.

At a recessed regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners held
