Meeting called to order by His Honor, Mayor Cragin, October 27, 1947 at the
hour of 4: p.m. with the following members present:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, and

Whipple. City Manager J. M. Murphy. City Clerk Helen Scott Reed. Absent:	Commissioner

Clark.

EMERGENCY LOAN	Verbal invitations to bid on an emergency loan having heretofore

BIDS	been made, and the city having been informed by the Bank of Nevada

	that they did not wish to submit a bid only one bid was received

	from the 1st National Bank of Nevada, Las Vegas Branch, which was

for a two and one-half year period at 3% interest per annum.

Thereafter, Commissioner Moore moved that the bid of the 1st National
Bank of Nevada, Las Vegas Branch be accepted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting

aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Clark.

Commissioner Baskin moved that the mayor and City Clerk be authorized
to sign the note in the sum of $80,000.00 at 3% interest per annum
for a two and one-half year period.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Moore and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting

aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Clark.

Commissioner Moore moved that the Minutes of October 27, 1947 being
a Special Meeting called at 10:00 a.m. be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioners Baskin and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting

aye; noes, none.
