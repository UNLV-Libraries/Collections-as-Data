BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that an ad valorem tax of $.04 on each one hundred dollars worth
of taxable property, situate, lying and being within the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, be levied,
Collected and paid for the year 1927, including the net proceeds of the mines, except such pro-
perty as is exempt from taxation, said tax to be known as and used as the Sewer Bond Interest
Fund.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that an ad valorem tax of $.15 on each one hundred dollars worth
of taxable property situate, lying and being within the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, be levied, Col-
lected and paid for the year 1927, including the net proceeds of the mines, except such property
as is exempt from taxation, said tax to be known as and used as the Sewer Bond Redemption Fund.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that an ad valorem tax of $.098 on each one hundred Dollars
worth of taxable property situate, lying and being within the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, be
levied, collected and paid for the year 1927, including the net proceeds of the mines, except
such property as is exempt from taxation, said tax to be known as and used as the Highway
Bond Interest Fund.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that an ad valorem tax of $.075 on each hundred dollars worth
of taxable property situate, lying and being within the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, be levied,
collected and paid for the year 1927, including the net proceeds of the mines for the year 1927,
said tax to be known as and used as the Highway Bond Redemption Fund.

The vote on said Resolution was as follows: Commissioners Mundy, Smith, Elwell and
His Honor the Mayor, J. F. Hesse, Aye. Noes, none.

No further business appearing it was ordered on motion duly made, seconded and
carried, that this Board adjourn until the 11th day of March, 1927, at the hour of three
o'clock p. m.
