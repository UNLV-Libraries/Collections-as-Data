Las Vegas, Nevada
August 15, 1949

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners held this 15th day of August, 1949,
was called to order at the hour 3:20 P.M. by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin with the
following members present: Commissioners Bunker, Peccole, Whipple; Acting City Mana-
ger George 0. Treem and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger. Absent: Commissioner Moore and
City Attorney Howard W. Cannon.

CHURCH TRACT	His Honor read a letter from the Regional Planning Commission dated August 12, 1949

Amended Map Approved	relative to the amended map of the Church Tract.

Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved that pursuant to the recommendation of the
	Regional Planning Commission the amended map of the Church Tract be approved and the

Mayor and City Clerk be authorized to sign same, subject to check by the City Engineer.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Moore.

APPOINTMENT OF CITY	Commissioner Bunker moved that upon the recommendation of Acting City Manager George

ENGINEER - Temporary	0. Treem that Fred Eaton, Engineering Department, be appointed Temporary City Engineer

with no increase in salary.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Moore.

There being no further business to come before the meeting at this time, the meeting
adjourned.
