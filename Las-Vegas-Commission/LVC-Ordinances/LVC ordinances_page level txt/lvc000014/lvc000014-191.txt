-AN ORDINANCE NO. 280

AN ORDINANCE VACATING THE SOUTHERLY SEVENTY-FIVE (75) FEET OF THE ALLEY RUNNING NORTHERLY
AND SOUTHERLY THROUGH BLOCK NUMBER FIFTEEN IN CLARK'S LAS VEGAS TOWNSITE IN THE CITY OF
LAS VEGAS, CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA, FOR POLICE DEPARTMENT PURPOSES.

The Board of commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

Section 1. That in order to promote the public welfare of said City of Las Vegas, it is here-
by ordained and directed that the southerly seventy-five (75) feet of the alley running norther-
ly and southerly through Block number fifteen (15) in Clark's Las Vegas Townsite in the City
of Las Vegas, as delineated on the official and record plat of the survey thereof on record 
in the office of the County Recorder of Clark County, Nevada, in Book I of Plats at Page 37
Clark County, Nevada Records, be and the same is hereby vacated for use by the Las Vegas Police
Department as a parking area, provided, however, that said southerly seventy-five feet of
said alley shall be closed and kept closed after the passage and going into effect of this
Ordinance, solely by and at the expense of the City of Las Vegas.

Section 2. This Ordinance shall take effect and be in force and effect from and after its
passage and publication for two weeks in the Las Vegas Review-Journal, a daily newspaper
published in the City of Las Vegas.

I hereby certify that the foregoing Ordinance No. 280 was read for the first time at a re-
gular meeting of the Board of Commissioners for the City of Las Vegas, held on the 16th of
November, 1942; that Notice of Intention to Vacate said alley was published in the Las Vegas
Review Journal on the 18th and 23rd days of November, 1942; that the foregoing Ordinance
was read for the second time on December 4, 1942, at a regular meeting of said Board of com-
missioners, and passed upon the following vote:

Voting Aye: Commissioners Clark, Rubidoux, Smith, Tinch, and His Honor Mayor Howell C,

Garrison.

Voting No: None
Absent : None
