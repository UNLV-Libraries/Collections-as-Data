The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas does ordain as follows:

Section 1. Section IV, Subparagraph L is hereby amended to read as follows:

Residence Requirements - An applicant for a position of any
kind in the competitive service must have been a resident
of the State of Nevada for a period of six (6) months or a
resident of Clark County for thirty (30) days prior to date
of application; provided that in an emergency so declared
by the Board of Commissioners, the residence requirements
may be dispensed with to secure qualified personnel.

Section 2. All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

Section 3. That by reason of the fact that certain departments of the City are unable to obtain
qualified personnel immediately, and since it is necessary to immediately employ qualified personnel for
the City of Las Vegas it is hereby declared that an. emergency exists, and that this ordinance is necessary
for the immediate preservation of the public peace, health and safety.

Section 4. This ordinance shall be in effect on and after its passage, adoption, and publication
thereof in the Las Vegas Sun, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas, for a period of once a
week for two consecutive weeks immediately following its first reading.
