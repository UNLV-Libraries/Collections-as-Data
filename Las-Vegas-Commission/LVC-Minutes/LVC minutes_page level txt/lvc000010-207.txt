Las Vegas, Nevada

March 7, 1956

A regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,
Nevada held this 7th day of March, 1956 was called to order at the hour
of 7.40 P.M. by His Honor Mayor Baker with the following members present:

Mayor	C.D.Baker

Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

Commissioner	E.W.Fountain

Commissioner	Harris P.Sharp

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

City Manager	A.H. Kennedy

City Attorney	Howard W.Cannon

City Engineer	George E.Wade

Planning Director	Franklin J. Bills

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Absent:	Asst. City Mgr.	R.W.Notti

This being the time set for the public hearing on the appeal of the granting
Of the Use Permit to Lloyd H. Skrenes to maintain an off-street parking lot
on Lots 1 and 2, Block 2, Maryland Tract No. 2, His Honor asked if there
was anyone present who wished to speak on this matter.

Mr. James C. Young representing Mrs. Olga Shields appeared before the Board.

Mr. Young stated that he was also appearing on behalf of Mrs. Jeanne Houssels
who represents the owners of Maryland Park Manor. Mr. Young urged the Com-
mission to deny this use permit.

Commissioner Bunker moved the appeal be denied.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye;
noes, none.

This being the time set for Paul Faulstich, masonry contractor to appear and
show cause why his license should not be revoked for faulty construction in
Charleston Heights Tract No. 3, Mr. John Yoxem was present representing Mr.
Faulstich.

Mr. Yoxem advised the Commission that his company would make all the necessary
repairs in order that the chimneys in this tract will come up to the Code.

He stated that the steel for this construction had been purchased and de-
livered to the job, but had not been put in place.

Thereafter the Paul Faulstich Company was granted 90 days to complete this
work.

Mr. Sam Lionel, representing the Burns Construction Company, general con-
tractors for Charleston Heights Tract No. 3 was present at this time.

The following property owners in Charleston Heights Tract No. 3 appeared
and objected to the construction of their homes:

Rex Breese	5310	Evergreen

Robert Baldwin	5313	Evergreen

George Berkhardt	5413	Evergreen

Mrs. O'Brien	5621	Herron

The Commission requested that the Building Department submit a report
every meeting as to the progress that Bums Construction Company is making
to correct the deficiencies in this tract.
