PROVIDING A NEW SCHEDULE OF FEES; TERM OF CERTIFICATES OF QUALIFICATION; PROVIDING FOR RENEWALS OF CERTIFI-

INANCE; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH:

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

SECTION 1. Section 1.13 of the Warm Air Heating Code, as adopted by Ordinance No. 607, is amended to
read as follows:

Schedule of Fees.	$ .50 

a.	For the installation or replacement of each warm air heating appliance or heat producing equipment.

b.	For each warm air outlet of a warm air heating system or unit or appliance	$ .25

c. For each gas piping 1 through 5 outlets	1.00

d. For each gas piping system of six (6) or more outlets, per outlet	.10

e.	For the installation of any unit, appliance or device supplied with water	.50

f.	Water distribution system or part	2.50

g.	Water pipe (per foot)	½ cent

h.	Minimum fee for any permit	2.50

SECTION 2. Section 2.10 of the Warm Air Heating Code, as adopted by Ordinance No. 607, is amended to
read as follows:

Sec. 2.10—Term of Certificates of Qualification

Every Warm Air Heating Certificate of Qualification shall remain in force and effect until its expira-
tion date, unless cancelled or revoked. Warm Air Heating Certificates of Qualification shall expire on
on December 31 of each year.

SECTION 3. Section 2.11 of the Warm Air Heating Code, as adopted by Ordinance No. 607, is amended to
read as follows:	

All Warm Air Certificates of qualification, except certificates which have been cancelled or revoked,
may be renewed from year to year upon request and payment of the required fee. If a renewal of a certificate
be requested and the required fee paid within thirty (30) days of expiration date of such certificate, the
renewal fee shall be $5.00 for a Warm Air Heating Contractor. $2.50 for a Warm Air Heating Installer. If
such renewal be requested and the required fee paid more than thirty (30) days but less than ninety (90)
days after the expiration date of such certificate, the renewal fee shall be doubled. No certificate may
be renewed more than ninety (90) days after the expiration date of such certificate, but in such event, a new
examination will be required.
