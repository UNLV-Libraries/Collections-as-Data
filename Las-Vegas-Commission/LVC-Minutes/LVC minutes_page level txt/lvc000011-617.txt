WHEREAS, the Board of City Commissioners desires to recognize the 
worthwhile efforts of the Service League;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the Board of City Commissioners at a
regular meeting held on the 18th day of November, 1959, that the following
described real property, encompassed within the Lions Club Park, is hereby
renamed

"SERVICE LEAGUE CENTER"

and set aside for the Service League to develop and maintain as a public
facility, retaining, however, the right and privilege of the public to at
all times use the said area for recreational purposes.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the description of said park area is as
follows:

A certain tract or parcel of land lying and being situate
in the City of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada,
and being a portion of the South One-Half (S½) of the
Northwest One-quarter (NW¼) of Section 26, Township 20
South, Range 61 East, M. D. B. & M., and being more
particularly described as follows, to-wit:

Beginning at point on the North right-of-way of Washington
Avenue said point being South 89° 49' 07" West distant
1035.74 feet and North 0° 00' 35" East distant 50 feet
from the center one-quarter of Section 26, Township 20 South,

Range 61 East, thence North 0° 00' 35" East a distance
of 700.08 feet to a point on the North line of that
certain parcel conveyed by the Las Vegas Land and Water
Company to the City of Las Vegas as described by Document
171912, Book 211, Official Records as Parcel 1, recorded
August 28, 1959 in the Office of the County Recorder,

Clark County, Nevada, thence South 89° 32' 47" West along
said North line a distance of 515-01 feet, thence South
0° 00' 35" West a distance of 400 feet, to a point on
North line of the area set aside as the Lion's Park
Little League Ball Park, thence North 89° 49' 07" East
a distance of 170 feet, thence South 45° 05' 09" East
along the Westerly line of said parcel a distance of
84.71 feet, thence South 0° 00' 35" West along the East
line of said Ball Park a distance of 237.63 feet to a
point on the North right-of-way line of Washington Avenue
aforementioned, thence North 89° 49' 07" East a distance

285 feet to the point of beginning, containing 6.173
acres more or less, save and except the North 40 feet
and East 60 feet thereof reserved for street purposes.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that this new designation shall be posted and
marked upon all of the maps of the City and shall be known by the name

"SERVICE LEAGUE CENTER"

hereafter.
