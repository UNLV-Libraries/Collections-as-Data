LIQUOR LICENSE

Andy's Liquor Store

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

At this time Commissioner Sharp moved the action taken by the Board in regard to
Any's Liquor Store be rescinded. Justification for this motion is that Mr. Bruner should
be repremanded and his license suspended for thirty days, but the Board has given other
Liquor licenses a second chance.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, Commissioner Whipple.

Commissioner Sharp then moved the license be suspended for thirty days as of December
16, 1953.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor voting aye; noes, Commissioners Bunker and Whipple.
