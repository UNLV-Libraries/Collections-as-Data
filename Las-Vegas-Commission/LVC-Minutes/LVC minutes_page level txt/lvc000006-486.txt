Las Vegas, Nevada
September 7, 1949

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Clark County, Nevada, met in regular
session pursuant to due legal notice and call, at the regular meeting place of said
Board in Las Vegas, Nevada, on Wednesday, the 7th day of September, 1949, at the hour
of 3:20 o'clock P.M. The following members of the Board of Commissioners were present:

Mayor:	E. W. Cragin

Commissioners:	Wendell Bunker

Robert T. Moore
William Peccole
Reed Whipple

Also Present:	City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

Acting City Manager George 0. Treem
City Clerk Shirley Ballinger

Absent:	None

The meeting was called to order by the Mayor. Thereupon, the following proceedings,
among others, were had and taken, to-wit:

PAYROLL WARRANTS	Commissioner Whipple moved that Payroll Warrants Nos. 1068, 1072 to 1074 inclusive in the

net sum of $26,424.39 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
