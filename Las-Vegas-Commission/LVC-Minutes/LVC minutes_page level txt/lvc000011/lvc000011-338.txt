February 23, 1959

City Manager

City Hall

Las Vegas, Nevada

Dear Sir:

In reference to application for a business license now in your office for
Belle Barron and James R. Rose to do business under the Name of "Celebrities
Clothing Exchange", it is desired that the writers take this opportunity to
elaborate on the type of business that we intend to conduct.

In our application we indicated that only BETTER USED CLOTHING would be
handled. Therefore we would like to CERTIFY that the following type of 
merchandise will be the only type bought, displayed or sold on this license when
issued:

1.	All	Clothing	that	is	worn	on	the	person	of	Women.

2.	All	Clothing	that	is	worn	on	the	person	of	Men.

3.	All	Clothing	that	is	worn	on	the	person	of	Children.

4.	All	type of babies wear including blankets.

No other merchandise of any description will be bought, displayed, sold, or
stored on these premises.

It is further agreed that the store will be maintained in a clean, bright, and
orderly manner, befitting the shopping area of its location.

Violation of any part of this letter, along with the standing regulation set
forth by the City of Las Vegas, will cause this license to be in violation and
subject to revocation by the City of Las Vegas.

/s/ Belle Barron_______________

/s/ James R. Rose______________
