N.B. This motion to be incorporated in above minutes:

On motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commissioner Gilbert the following Resolution
was introduced and passed:

WHEREAS the prospective income of the City of Las Vegas will be greatly decreased through
the lowering of the tax levy by the Board of Clark County Commissioners, and

WHEREAS, there seems to be only one method of balancing the City Budget, vis: through a
hearty cooperation of all city employees in a systematic and rigid reduction in expenditures,

THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED: that previous approval of a majority of the Board be required in
connection with all requisitions for supplies in excess of $25.00 and that all employees be urged
to avoid emergency purchases unless a real emergency exists,

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes, none. Absent non.
