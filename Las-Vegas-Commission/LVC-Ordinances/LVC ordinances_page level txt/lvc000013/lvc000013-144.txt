ORDINANCE NO. 96

	An Ordinance of the City of Las Vegas to compel the attendance at meetings of the Board of

City Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas by members thereof.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

Section 1. It shall be and is hereby declared to be the duty of each and every member of

the Board of City Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas to attend at least one regular meeting

of said Board each month, and also to attend each special meeting thereof upon the notice thereof
being given to him. Provided, however, that the absence of such member may be excused on account
of sickness of himself or of members of his immediate family residing in said City of Las Vegas,
which renders his absence reasonably necessary; and provided further, that such absence may be
excused where prior to such meeting leave for such absence may have been given him by said
Board of Commissioners or by the Mayor, or by the Mayor pro tem, while acting Mayor, and provided
further, no such absence shall extend over the period of sixty days.

Section 2. In the event of the absence of any member of said Board at any regular or
special meeting, except as provided for in Sect ion 1, the said Board of Commissioners in the
event a quorum of such Board is present, or the Mayor or Mayor Pro Tem while acting as Mayor
in the event a quorum of such Board is not present, may by order direct the City Marshal or
any City Policeman of the City of Las Vegas to forthwith apprehend such absent member and bring
him forthwith before the said Board, and such officer after bringing such member before the



Board shall compel his attendance during the entire session of such Board.

Section 3. It shall be the duty of such City Marshal or Policeman, as the case may be,
on receiving the order provided in Section 2, of this Ordinance to forthwith execute the same,
and after bringing such member before the said Board to compel his attendance during the
entire session of said Board.

Section 4. This Ordinance shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage
and adoption and publication for one week (one issue) in Las Vegas Review a weekly newspaper
published in said City of Las Vegas.

Passed and adopted on the second reading this 6th day of December A. D. 1922 upon the

following vote: Commissioners Arnold. Gorton. and Hodgens voting aye. Noes, none______________
