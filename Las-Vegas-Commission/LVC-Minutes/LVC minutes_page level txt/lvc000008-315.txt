Las Vegas, Nevada
April 1, 1953

A regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, called to
order at the hour of 7:30 P.M. by His Honor Mayor C.D. Baker with the following members
present:

Mayor	C.D. Baker

Commissioner	Wendell Danker

Commissioner	Rex A. Jarrett

Commissioner	William Peccole

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

City Manager	C.W. Shelley

Asst. City Mgr.	Dora Lord

City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Commissioner Bunker moved Service & Material Warrants Nos. 2396 to 2400 inclusive in the
total amount of $20,408.50 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Whipple moved the Minutes of March 18, and 25, 1953, be approved and the Mayor
and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Bunker moved Shirley Ballinger be appointed as City Clerk effective March 16,

1953, at a salary increase of $425.00 per month and the following resolution adopted:

WHEREAS, Assembly Bill No. 371 was signed by the Governor on March 16, 1953; and

WHEREAS, Section 4 of said Bill made no provision of salary of the City Clerk of
the City of Las Vegas from said date; and

WHEREAS, the office of the City Clerk is an electvie office whose term will expire
on June 1st, 1953; and

WHEREAS, it is necessary that a salary be fixed for a City Clerk for the City of Las
Vegas;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, By the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
as follows:

1.	That the salary of the City Clerk of the City of Las Vegas shall be in the sum of
$425.00 per month, beginning March 16, 1953.

2.	That the functions and duties of the City Clerk shall remain the same as provided
in the charger of the City of Las Vegas.

PASSED, ADOPTED AND APPROVED This 1st day of April, 1953.

s/ C.D. BAKER
Mayor

ATTEST:

s/ SHIRLEY BALLINGER
City Clerk

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Bunker moved City Manager, Chet Shelley be detained as City manager and his contract
renewed for one year at the salary of $1,000.00 per month.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting ayej noes, none.
