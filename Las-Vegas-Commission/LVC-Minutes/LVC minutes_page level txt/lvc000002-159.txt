irrevocably pledged.

issued for the purpose of constructing a highway through said City of Las Vegas to connect

which more than a majority of the qualified electors voted in favor of the issuance of said 
bonds, and proceeding of the Board of City Commissioners duly passed and adopted, and it
authorized by and issued in full conformity with the requirements of the Constitution and
laws of the State of Nevada, including Chapter 74 of the Session Laws of said State for the
year 1923, and all acts of the Legislature amendatory thereof, and supplemental thereto.

limitations.
