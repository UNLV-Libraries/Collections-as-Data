RESOLUTION -	Commissioner Bunker moved the following resolution be adopted:

Highway Markers

^	RESOLUTION

WHEREAS; the new Inter-State Highway System is being established with a new
numbering system; supplementing the present U. S. Highway numbering scheme with an
entirely new and separate numbering scheme; and

WHEREAS; the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas wishes to pro-
mote the best possible method of directing travelers to the places in Las Vegas
to which they desire to go; and

WHEREAS; it is the judgment of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las
Vegas that "City" or "alternate" routes through Las Vegas should be designated
with the same numerical designation as the Inter-State route.

NOW; THEREFORE; BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas at a regular meeting thereof held on the 20th day of November; 1957;
that the Board does respectfully request that all pertinent officials; both
State and Federal; select and designate with the same number as the Inter-State
Highway System; those alternate or service routes from the inter-changes into
the City of Las Vegas, in order to coordinate and synchronize with the Inter-
State route number.

PASSED, ADOPTED; and APPROVED this 20th day of November; 1957.

Motion seconded by Commissioner S harp and carried by the following vote:	

Commissioners Bunker; Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. 

Absent: Commissioner Fountain.
