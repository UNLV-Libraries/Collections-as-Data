Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved the recommendations of the Civil Service Board of Trustees

Director of Public Works gave a short report on the status of proposed assessment districts for

At this time Commissioner Bunker returned to the meeting.

Mr. Herb Jones appeared before the Board on behalf of Mr. J. C. Millian applicant for a Used
Car lot license at 1519 Fremont Street. Mr. Jones was advised to check with the various depart-
