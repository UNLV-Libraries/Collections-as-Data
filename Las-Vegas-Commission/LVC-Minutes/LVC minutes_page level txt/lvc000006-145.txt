CITY LICENSES	Commissioner Whipple moved that the City Clerk be instructed to notify

	the various inspectors in the City not to issue any permit to any

Company until their city license is paid. Motion seconded by Commis-
sioner Baskin and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners Baskin,

Moore, Whipple, and His Honor Mayor Pro-tem Clark noting aye. Noes,
none. Absent:	Mayor Cragin.

CEMETERY DEPARTMENT Commissioner Baskin moved that the purchase procedure be waived and

PURCHASE OF LAWN MOWER that the city purchase one Torrel Super Grass Cutting machine from
	Inland Service in the sum of $907.14 for the reason that it is the

only machine available. Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and
carried by the following vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple

and His Honor Mayor Pro-Tem Clark voting aye. Noes, none. Absent:

Mayor Cragin.

LEASE VETERANS'	Commissioner Whipple moved that the lease between the City of Las Vegas

COORDINATING COUNCIL and the Veterans' Coordinating Council for space in the War Memorial

Building be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk be authorized to
sign same. Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by
the following vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple and His Honor

Mayor Pro-Tem Clark voting aye. Noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Cragin.

CENTRAL SWIMMING	Commissioner Moore moved that the recommendation of Pat Diskin, Director

POOL - rates	of Municipal Pools, that the admission prices at the central swimming

pool be 35¢ for adults and 15¢ for students be approved. Motion seconded
by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Baskin, Moore, Whipple and His Honor Mayor Pro-tem Clark voting aye.

Noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Cragin.

GEORGE SHANNON SALARY Commissioner Moore moved that George Shannon be paid the difference

in his salary between the amount he receives from the Nevada Industrial
	Commission and his salary from the City up to and including August 31,

1947 and the Mayor and City Clerk be authorized to sign his check.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple and His honor Mayor Pro-

tem Clark voting aye. Noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Cragin.

ADJOURNMENT	There being no further business to come before the meeting at this time,

Commissioner Moore moved that this meeting adjourn until September 22nd,

1947 at the hour of 2:00 p.m. Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple
and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple

and His Honor Mayor Pro-tem Clark voting aye. Noes, none. Absent:

Mayor Cragin.
