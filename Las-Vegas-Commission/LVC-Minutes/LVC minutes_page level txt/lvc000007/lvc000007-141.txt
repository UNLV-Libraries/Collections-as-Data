Las Vegas, Nevada
June 30, 1950

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners held this 30th day of June, 1950
was called to order at the hour of 3:25 P.M. by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin with
the following members present: Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple; City

Manager C. W. Shelley and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger. Absent: City Attorney

Howard W. Cannon.

APPOINTMENT OF            His Honor stated that this meeting had been called for the purpose of considering
CHIEF OF POLICE	the appointment of a Chief of Police.

	After discussion was held by the Board on City Manager C. W. Shelley's recommendation

for Chief of Police it was agreed that it would be best to interview Mr. I. B. Maxwell,
Chief of Police of Montebello, California, before any action was taken by the Board.

Mr. Shelley stated that he could have Mr. Maxwell in Las Vegas whenever the Board would
like to meet with him.

It was agreed the Board would meet July 1, 1950 at 10:00 A.M.

There being no further business to come before this special meeting, Commissioner
Moore moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:

Commissioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
