Consideration was given to the claim of Anne N. Powell for damages caused to her property
from the drainage from the disposal plant. The Commission was advised that the original
claim of Mrs. Powell in the amount of $16,000.00 had been denied on October 3, 1956, and
this claim was for an additional $1,500.00 from the 15th of October, 1956 to March 19,

1957 when said claim was filed with the City Clerk.

Commissioner Sharp moved the claim of Anne N. Powell in the total amount of $17,500.00 be
denied.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner

Fountain.
