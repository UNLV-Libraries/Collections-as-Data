CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

March 31, 1954

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada is hereby
called to be held in the Council Chambers of the City Hall, on the 31st day of March, 1954, at
the hour of 4:00 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Assessment Districts:	a) No. 100-23; b) No. 100-25; c) No. 100-26; d) No. 100-27

2.	Hiring of Pershing, Bosworth, Dick and Dawson as bond attorneys for assessment districts.

3.	Mr. Vernon Welborn will be present regarding a canopy across the alley behind the Horseshoe Club.

4.	Civil Service recommendations on merit increases.

5. Request of U.S.N.R.T.C. for name of Las Vegas Naval unit in memory of a local veteran.

6.	Recommendations form City Manager on bids for Tires & Tubes and Batteries.

7.	City Managers Reports

8.	Gaming Applications.

9.	Liquor Application

______s/ C. D. Baker___________

 Mayor
