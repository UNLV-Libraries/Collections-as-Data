LAS VEGAS, NEVADA
September 22, 1947

At a regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners held the 22nd
day of September, 1947.Meeting called to order by His Honor Mayor E. W.

Cragin at the hour of 2:00 p.m. with the following members present:
Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple, Acting City Manager Geo. 0. Treem,

City Attorney, C. Norman Cornwall, and City Clerk, Helen Scott Reed.

Absent:	Commissioner Clark.

MINUTES	Commissioner Whipple moved that	the minutes of September 5, and	the

	Minutes of the special meetings	of September 11th and 19th be approved

by reference and the Mayor and City Clerk be authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple and His Honor Mayor Cragin

voting aye. Noes, none. Absent:	Comm. Clark.

PAYROLL CLAIMS	Commissioner Whipple moved that	the payroll claims Nos. 1268 to	1273 inc.,

	in the net sum of $22,085.61 be	approved and the Mayor and City	Clerk

authorized to sign same. Motion seconded by Commissioner Moore and
carried by the following vote; Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple
and His Honor voting aye. Noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Clark.

SERVICE AND	Commissioner Whipple moved that service and material warrants Nos. 1274

MATERIAL WARRANTS	in the sum of $11,369.45 be approved and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized

	to sign same. Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the

following vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting

aye. Noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Clark.

GARBAGE ORDINANCE Commissioner Baskin moved that the City Attorney be directed to make an

investigation of the present garbage ordinance and if found to be insufficient

and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Moore, Whipple

and His Honor voting aye. Noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Clark.
