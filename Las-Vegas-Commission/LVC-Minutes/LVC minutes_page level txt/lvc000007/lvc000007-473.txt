CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING	Las Vegas, Nevada

October 24, 1951

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk 

Las Vegas, Nevada

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas is hereby called to be held in the Council Chambers at the
City Hall, on the 24th day of October, 1951, at the hour of 4:30 P.M.
to consider the following matters:

1. Recommendations from the Regional Planning Commission
on rezoning - set date of public hearing.

2. Approval of Final Map on Oakridge Subdivision

  3. Resolution re: San Francisco Street, condemnation
proceedings.

4. Final Map - Resubdivision of Blocks 1 & 9, Meadows addn.

5. Gaming Application Ten Pin Tavern

s/ C. D. Baker_________________________

C. D. BAKER, Mayor

TO: C.D. BAKER, Mayor and to WENDELL BUNKER, REX A. JARRETT, WILLIAM
PECCOLE, REED WHIPPLE, Commissioners, and C. W. SHELLEY, City
Manager, HOWARD W. CANNON, City Attorney of the City of Las Vegas,

Nevada.

You and each of you will please take notice that pursuant to a call
this day issued by Mayor Baker, a Special Meeting of the Board of Commiss-
ioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, will be held in the Council
Chambers at the City Hall, 5th and Stewart, on the 24th day of October,

1951, at the hour of 4:30 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Recommendations from the Regional Planning Commission
on rezoning - set date of public hearing.

2.	Approval of Final Map on Oakridge Subdivision

3.	Resolution re: San Francisco Street, condemnation

proceedings.

4.	Final Map - Resubdivision of Blocks 1 & 9, Meadows Addn.

5. Gaming Application Ten Pin Tavern

Shirley Ballinger
