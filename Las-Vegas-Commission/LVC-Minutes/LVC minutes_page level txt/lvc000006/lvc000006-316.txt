OFFICE OF THE PLANNING COMMISSION
September 8, 1948

Honorable Mayor and Board of City Commissioners
War Memorial Building
Las Vegas, Nevada

Re:	Relocation of Sidewalk

Desert Plaza

Gentlemen:

At a regular meeting of the Joint Planning Commission held August 31, 1948, Mr. Harold
Stocker appeared and asked permission to relocate the sidewalk as approved on his
original plot plan for Desert Plaza. Mr. Stocker proposed the elimination of a
planting strip with the construction of a six (6) foot sidewalk directly in back of
the curb.

After a general discussion and consideration of all data, the Planning Commission
recommended that this proposed sidewalk be approved.

Yours very truly,

LAS VEGAS — CLARK COUNTY
