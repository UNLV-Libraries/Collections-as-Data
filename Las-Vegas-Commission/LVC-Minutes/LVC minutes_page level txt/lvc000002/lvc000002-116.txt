At a special called meeting of the Board of City Commissioners of the City of Las
Vegas, Nevada, held on the 31st day of January, A.D. 1924, at the hour of eight

Waiver of Notice and

ing of said meeting at the Commissioners' Room in the said City of
Las egas on this 31st day of January, 1924 at the hour of eight
o'clock P.M. on said date, and consent to the transaction of any
and all business of the City thereat.
