LAS VEGAS, NEVADA
JANUARY 7, 1948

At a regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners held this 7th day
of January, 1948. Meeting called to order by His Honor, Mayor E. W. Cragin
at the hour of 2:00 P.M. with the following members present:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and City Clerk, Helen Scott Reed. Absent:
City Manager J. M. Murphy and City Attorney, C. Norman Cornwall.

Mrs. T. L. Williams appeared before the Board at this time stating that
she was a member of the Business and Professional Women and that they
intended to make community safety a project for this year, and would
appreciate the cooperation of the Board. The Board was unanimously
in favor of the Project and pledged their assistance.
