At this time two requests of the City for easement for the installation of sewer pipe lines were
presented to the Board for consideration. One request for easement was made of the Union Pacific
Railroad Company to cress their right-of-way in order to install a sewer line to serve the proposed
Cadillac Arms tract. The other was a request of the City of the State Highway Department for permission
to cross the Boulder Highway so that s sewer line can be installed to serve the Eastwood Subdivision.
