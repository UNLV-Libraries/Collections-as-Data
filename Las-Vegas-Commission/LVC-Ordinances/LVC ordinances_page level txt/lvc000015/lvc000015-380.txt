ORDINANCE NO. 652

AN ORDINANCE TO PROVIDE FOR THE REDUCTION OF THE BUILDING SITE AREA REQUIREMENTS ON LOTS WITHIN THE CITY
ZONED R-2, R-3, AND R-4 BEFORE AUGUST 1, 1955; PROVIDING FOR THE ISSUANCE OF BUILDING PERMITS; PROVIDING
OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT
HEREWITH.

WHEREAS, there are many lots within the City of Las Vegas which have been zoned R-2, R-3, and R-4
although said lots contain less than 7000 square feet in area; and

WHEREAS, a lot size of less than 7000 square feet in area will not permit the construction of multiple
dwellings even though the zoning may so permit; and

WHEREAS, a variance from the strict application of the Zoning Ordinance is necessary in every case and
in order to save time and expense in the future it will be in the public interest to provide for the issu-
ance of building permits without first applying for a variance in each case.

NOW, THEREFORE, the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas does ordain as follows:

SECTION 1. From and after the passage of this ordinance, the minimum building site requirement of
7000square feet for the construction of multiple dwellings shill be reduced to 6000 square feet for those
lots zoned R-2, R-3; or R-4 by the Master Plan of Land Use, 1953 Revision, as amended. In no case, how-
ever, shall a multiple dwelling or dwelling group occupy a site which provides less than 3000 square feet
of land per family or dwelling unit in an R-2 classification; or less than 1750 square feet of land per
family or dwelling unit in an R-3 classification; or less than 700 square feet of land per family or dwell-
ing unit in an R-4 classification.

SECTION 2. Upon the passage of this ordinance, the Supervisor of Building and Safety shall issue the
necessary permits without requiring a variance, provided the plans of such construction are in accordance
with any other ordinances of the City.

SECTION 3. The provisions of this ordinance shall apply only to those areas zoned or rezoned R-2,

R-3, or on or before August 1, 1955.



Section 4. All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

and final publication as in the next section provided.
