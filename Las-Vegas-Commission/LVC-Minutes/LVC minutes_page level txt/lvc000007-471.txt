New-Club Savoy	Commissioner Peccole moved the application of Norman Khoury, Club

	Savoy, 116 Fremont for license for 54 slot machines for the fourth

quarter of 1951 be granted.

This motion died for lack of a second.

Commissioner Whipple moved the application of Norman Khoury, Club
Savoy, 116 Fremont for license for 54 slot machines for the fourth
quarter of 1951 be tabled.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following
vote: Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Whipple and His Honor voting

aye; noes, none. Commissioner Peccole passed his vote.

Additional	Commissioner Whipple moved the application of Robert Testolin and

James Fare, Cinnabar, 122 North 2nd Street for one Roulette for the
fourth quarter of 1951 be granted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following
vote: Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor

voting aye; noes, none.
