GAMING LICENSE	The meeting reconvened at the hour of 11:45 P.M. with all members present as of the opening

session with Commissioner Rex A. Jarrett being present also.

At this time the application of Dr. M. M. Bernstein, Eldorado Club, Inc. dba Horseshoe Club,

128 Fremont Street for a license for 72 slot machines, 3 Craps, 3 "21", 2 Roulette, 1 Race
Horse Book, 1 Race Horse Keno, 1 Poker, 1 Pangingue for the third quarter of 1951 was
presented to the Board for their consideration.

City Attorney Howard W. Cannon gave a brief history of Dr. Bernstein's interests in the
gaming industry since 1945.

At the hour of 11:59 P.M. Commissioner Peccole moved this meeting recess until 12:01 A. M.

August 8, 1951.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Bunker, Peccole, Jarrett and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner

Whipple.

Thereafter Commissioner Peccole moved the application for renewal of license for the third
quarter of 1951 of the Eldorado Club, Inc., dba Horseshoe Club for the above mentioned gaming
devices be granted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote: Commissioners Bunker,

Peccole, Jarrett and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Whipple.

Commissioner Peccole moved that Dr. Bernstein be instructed that the Commission expects him
to devote the majority of his time to the operation of this gaming business and that he under-
stands he is personally held responsible for the operation of the Club; further that he employ
local help as far as possible. Also to acquaint him with Section 9 of Chapter 13 of the Las
Vegas City Code as to the terms and conditions under which he accepts the license and under
which the Commission may revoke the same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Whipple.

Commissioner Bunker moved that the investigation of Dr. Bernstein made by the City Attorney
be compiled in proper form and made a part of the minutes of this special meeting.
