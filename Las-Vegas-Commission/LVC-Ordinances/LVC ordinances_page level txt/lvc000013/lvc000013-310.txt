	ORDINANCE NO. 175

An Ordinance to amend Ordinance No. 159 of he City of Las Vegas by adding thereto
a new section to be known as Section 12a, and repealing all ordinances and parts of ordinances
 in conflict therewith.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do Ordain as follows:

Section 1. Ordinance No. 159 of the City of Las Vegas, entitled: "An Ordinance

regulating the doing of plumbing work and providing for the examination of persons engaged
in the same, and creating an examining board, providing a penalty for the violation thereof,
 and repealing all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict therewith" is hereby
amended by adding thereto a new section to be known as Section 12a, as follows:

Section 12a. Before any person who has received a certificate of competency shall
engage in the business of plumbing, or doing plumbing work, including the laying of cast
iron sewers, such person shall pay to the City Clerk of said City an annual license fee,
as follows: 1. For every plumbing business where t he same is carried on at a fixed place

of business in the City of Las Vegas, in carrying on such business per annum, $100.00.

2. For every kind of plumbing business other than that described in Subdivision 1 of this 
Section, per annum, $200.00. Such payment shall constitute the fee for the current calendar 
year, or portion thereof, in which such license shall be issued. Any license issued
to any master plumber shall expire at the end of the calendar year; provided, that upon payment
 of an annual license fee of $100.00, where same is carried on in a fixed place of business,
 and $200.00 for every other kind of plumbing business, the particular license shall
be renewed yearly until revoked. The license above provided for shall be issued by the City
Clerk.

Every person, firm or corporation before obtaining a certificate as "Master Plumber",
shall deposit with the City Clerk, the sum of One Hundred Dollars ($100.00) in cash as a
guarantee for the payment of all fees or permits or inspections that may become due and he
owed by said certificate holder to the City of Las Vegas, and as a further guarantee that
all plumbing work done under the authority of said certificate will be done in compliance
with all the provisions and requirements of all the ordinances applicable thereto. Such
deposit shall at all times be maintained at One Hundred Dollars ($100.00), and in event
said depositor shall discontinue business said deposit shall be returned to such depositor,
less any amounts due for fees or under any of the other provisions of this ordinance.

Section 2. All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict with the provisions
of this Ordinance are hereby repealed.

Section 3. This Ordinance shall be in effect from and after its passage and adoption
 and publication for a period of one week (6 issues) in Las Vegas Age a daily newspaper 
published in the City of Las Vegas.

Passed and adopted this 20th day of August, 1931, by the following vote of the Board
of City Commissioners: Mundy, Hansell, and His Honor Mayor pro tem, W. C. German, voting
aye. Noes none. First reading July 6, 1931. Second and Final reading August 20, 1931.

Approved this 20th day of August, 1931.
