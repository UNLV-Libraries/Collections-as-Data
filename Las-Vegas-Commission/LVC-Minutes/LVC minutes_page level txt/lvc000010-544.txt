City Manager Kennedy continued that the City Engineering Department is working on
H Street improvement and is presently straightening out the right-of-way. he
stated that plans are being made for the improvement of Eastern Avenue between St.
Louis and San Francisco; Owens Avenue from Cherokee Lane to Highland Drive; Bruce
Street from Harris Avenue to Jansen Avenue; and Bonanza Road from 13th to approxi-
mately 15th Street and a further portion of Bonanza Road bordering the Greater Las
Vegas Addition.
