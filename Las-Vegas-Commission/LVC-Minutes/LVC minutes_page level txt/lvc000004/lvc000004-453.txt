electrical journeymen and the public utilities requesting them to appoint one man from each
group to act on the electrical examiner's board as provided for in Electrical Ordinance

Smith, Tinch, Clark, Rubidoux and His Honor voting aye. Noes, none.

Commissioner Rubidoux moved that bids be let on the new administration building at the
airport and that same be returned by February 4th, 1942. Motion seconded by Commissioner
Tinch and carried by the following vote. Commissioners Smith, Tinch, Clark, Rubidoux and
His Honor voting aye. Noes, none.

in back. Said bids to be returned February 4th, 1942. Motion seconded by Commissioner Tinch
and carried by the following vote. Commissioners Smith, Tinch, Clark, Rubidoux and His Honor
voting aye. Noes, none.
