Commissioner Sharp moved that a public hearing he held on February 16, 1955.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:

Commissioners Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Absent:	Commissioner Bunker.

At this time the Board considered the acceptance of an easement for Spencer Street
Storm Drain, executed by John H. Clark and Lois Clark, dated January 17, 1955.

On motion of Commissioner Jarrett, seconded by Commissioner Whipple, and duly carried,
the following resolution is adopted and order made:

BE IT RESOLVED, this 2nd day of February, 1955, by the Board of Commissioners of the
City of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada, that the right of way deed for
an easement, dated the 17th day of January, 1955, executed by John H. Clark and Lois
Clark, to the City of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada, and that the real
property described therein be, and is hereby accepted, for the purposes and intentions
as therein set forth; and,

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that a copy of this Resolution be attached to said deed, and
that the same be recorded in the office of the County Recorder of the County of Clark,
State of Nevada, and filed in the records of this Board.

At this time the Board considered the temporary crossing of effluent ditch.

Mr. George Wade, City Engineer, recommended this crossing subject to the securing of an
easement along 25th street.

Commissioner Sharp moved approval of this crossing subject to the above conditions.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner

Bunker.

 The Board took no action on this matter as the curb cut was less than 32 feet, and the
Director of Public Works can approve the application.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that a copy of this Resolution be attached to said deed, and
that the same be recorded in the office of the County Recorder of the County of Clark,
