ORDINANCE NO. 671

AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND CHAPTER 36, SECTION 58, CODE OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA, 1949, BY ADDING
A NEW SUBSECTION TO PREVENT VEHICLE PARKING ON SOUTH FIFTH STREET BETWEEN OAKEY BOULEVARD
AND SAN FRANCISCO STREET; PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR THE VIOLATION; REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES
OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH; AND PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELA-
TED THERETO.

WHEREAS, the volume of traffic on South Fifth Street between Oakey Boulevard and
San Francisco Street has the effect of congesting the streets and impeding the movement
of traffic especially whan parking on the street is permitted, and

WHEREAS, the practice of parking on said street tends to further impede traffic
and in addition, thereto constitutes a danger to the life, limb, and property of other
motorists, pedestrians and others, and;

WHEREAS, the Board of Commissioners of the County of Clark have prohibited parking
on Highway 91 south from San Francisco Street as a safety and traffic control measure;
and,

WHEREAS, it is the opinion of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
that the public interest will be best served by prohibiting parking on South Fifth Street
between Oakey Boulevard and San Francisco Street,

NOW, THEREFORE, the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as
follows:

SECTION 1. Chapter 36, Section 58, Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 1949, is hereby
amended by adding a new subsection to be designated (19) which shall read as follows:

19. On either side of South Fifth Street from Oakey Boulevard to San Francisco

Street.

SECTION 2. Any person violating the provisions of this ordinance shall be guilty
of a misdemeanor and upon conviction shall be punished by a fine of not less than $1.00
nor more than $500, and/or 6 months in the City Jail.
