EXTENSION OF SOUTH
17th Street



Commissioner Bunker moved that Mr. Richard Stadelman be given a building permit in the 1200
Block of South 17th Street providing he files the necessary bond to insure the installation
of sidewalks, curbs, gutters and street paving, together with the deed for the extension of
said street.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote; Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
