GENERAL ELECTION	Commissioner Bunker moved the following order of election be adopted:

	ORDER

It appearing that the Charter of the City of Las Vegas provides that a
General Election be held on the first Tuesday after the first Monday in
May, 1951, for the election at large by the qualified voters of the City,
of a Mayor, two commissioners of the first and third class, respectively,
a Judge of the Municipal Court, and a City Attorney, it is therefore,

ORDERED By the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas that such
General Election be held in the City of Las Vegas on May 8, 1951 at which
election there shall be elected at large by the qualified voters of the said

first and third class, respectively, who shall be elected and hold office for a
