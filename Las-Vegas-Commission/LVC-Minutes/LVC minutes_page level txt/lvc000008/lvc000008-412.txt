The application of E.L. Josephson and V.D. Eachus for a Use Permit to construct
and maintain a resort hotel of one hundred or more rooms to be located on the north-
east comer of Harrison and "I" Streets was considered at this time.

Thereafter Commissioner Bunker moved the application for Use Permit be approved
and the following resolution adopted:

RESOLUTION

WHEREAS, on the 6th day of May, 1953, E.L. Josephson and V.D. Eachus filed
an application with the Regional Planning Commission for a Use Permit to construct
and maintain a resort hotel of one hundred or more rooms to be located on the north-
east corner of Harrison and "I" Streets in the SW¼ SE¼ SE¼ Section 21, T 20 S,

R 61 E, MDB&M, which will include the operation of a cabaret and casino; and

WHEREAS, on May 14, 1953, the Regional Planning Commission voted to approve
the application subject to the condition that adequate off-street parking be pro-
vided; and

WHEREAS, such decision was filed with the Board of Commissioners on May 18,

1953, recommending that such Use Permit be approved.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, the recommendation of the Regional Planning
Commission that the application of E.L. Josephson and V.D. Eachus to construct and
maintain a resort hotel of one hundred or more rooms, to be located on the north-
east comer of Harrison and "I" Streets, be approved subject to the condition that
adequate off-street parking be provided, be and the same hereby is approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:	Com-
missioners Bunker, Jarrett and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Com-

missioners Peccole and Whipple.
