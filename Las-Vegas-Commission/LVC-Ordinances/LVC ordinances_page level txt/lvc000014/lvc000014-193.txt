STATE OF NEVADA) SS
COUNTY OF CLARK)

Dorothy D. Brimacombe, being duly sworn, deposes and says: That she is general manager
of the Las Vegas Age, a weekly newspaper of general circulation, printed and published
at Las Vegas, in the County of Clark, State of Nevada, and that the attached Ordinance
No. 281 was published in said newspaper for a period of two weeks from January 8, 1943
and January 15, 1943 inclusive, being the issues of said newspaper for the following
dates, to-wit: January 8, 1943 and January 15, 1943.

That said newspaper was regularly issued and circulated on each of the dates above named.

That the legal charge for publishing said legal notice was $23.70.

Signed: Dorothy D. Brimacombe

Subscribed and sworn to before me this 2nd. day of February, 1943.

C. D. Breeze

Notary Public in and for Clark County, Nevada.

(SEAL)

My commission expired June 9, 1943.

ORDINANCE NO. 282

AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND ORDINANCE NO. 201.

Entitled, "AN ORDINANCE REGULATING THE SLAUGHTERING OF ANIMALS AND SANITARY CONDITIONS OF
ALL PLACES DISPENSING FOOD AND REFRESHMENTS TO THE PUBLIC; PROVIDING FOR THE INSPECTION OF
MEATS AND MEAT PRODUCTS TO BE POSSESSED AND SOLD IN THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; PROHIBITING THE
SALE AND POSSESSION OF UNINSPECTED MEATS FOR HUMAN FOOD; AUTHORIZING THE APPOINTMENT OF MEAT
INSPECTORS; PROHIBITING THE SALE OF MEAT AND MEAT PRODUCTS BY UNLICENSED ESTABLISHMENTS AND
PROVIDING A PENALTY FOR THE VIOLATION THEREOF.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

That Section Twelve of Ordinance No. 201 is hereby amended by placing a period after the
word "dollars" in line 3 and deleting "and each retail establishment shall pay to the City
Clerk a monthly inspection fee of One ($1.00) Dollar." so that Section Twelve as amended
shall read "Each official slaughtering establishment designated by the Board of City Com-
missioners shall pay to the City Clerk an annual license fee of Twenty-five ($25.00) Dollars.

Proposed, read aloud in full, and adopted this 16th day of December 1942, by the following
vote:

Voting Aye: Commissioners Clark, Rubidoux, Smith, Tinch and His Honor.

Voting No:	None.

Absent:	None.

Published in the Las Vegas Evening Review-Journal, a daily newspaper published in the City
of Las Vegas, Nevada, once a week for two weeks, to-wit:

Read a second time and passed the 4th day of January, 1943, by the following vote:

Voting Aye: Commissioners Clark, Rubidoux, Smith, Tinch and Mayor Garrison.

Voting No:	None.

Absent:	None.

Approved this 4th day of January, 1943.

Howell C. Garrison_________

Mayor
