	resolution be forwarded to the Chairman and Members of the Clark County delegation

to the Assembly and B. Mahlon Brown, State Senator.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote: Commis-

the North City Limits; further we wish him to notify us when Mr. George Egan will
be in Las Vegas in order that property owners on 5th Street can be notified of a

Motion seconded by Commissioner Moore and carried by the following vote: Commis-
