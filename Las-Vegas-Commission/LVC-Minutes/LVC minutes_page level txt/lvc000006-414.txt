Las Vegas, Nevada
May 2, 1949

A recessed regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners held this 2nd day of May, 1949
was called to order at the hour of 3:30 P.M. by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin with the following
members present:	Commissioners Clark, Moore, Whipple; City Manager J. M. Murphy, City

Attorney C. Norman Cornwall, City Clerk Shirley Ballinger and City Comptroller Harold A. Laird.
Absent:	Commissioner Baskin.

TAXI APPLICATION	Commissioner Clark moved that the application of the Union Cab for two additional taxis be

Union Cab	granted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Baskin.

LIQUOR APPLICATION	Commissioner Clark moved that the application of James G. Leavitt, Nevada Sport service Inc.

Nevada Sport service	Inc. for a Retail Beverage license at Cashman Field during the 2nd quarter of 1949 be granted.



Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Baskin.

BUREAU OF COMMUNITY	His Honor read a telegram from Senator Pat McCarran relative to a meeting to be held in

FACILITIES - Meeting	Washington D. C. on May 5, 1949 with the Bureau of Community Facilities on Sewage Treatment

Plants. Thereafter Commissioner Clark moved that the City Manager be directed to attend the
	aforementioned meeting at the expense of the City.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Moore and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners Clark,

Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Baskin.
