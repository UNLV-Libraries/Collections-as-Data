Commissioner Bunker moved this application for variance be approved and the following resolu-

resolution

WHEREAS, on the 12th day of June, 1952, Merle Frehner filed an application with the
Regional Planning Commission for variance to permit the erection of a duplex unit in the re-
quired rear yard on property situated at 1142 South 3rd Street in an R-3 (Multiple Dwelling)

Use District; and

WHEREAS, on June 19, 1952, the Regional Planning Commission held a public hearing on
this application for variance, at which time no objections were raised and no communications pro-
testing the granting of said variance were received; and

WHEREAS, on June 19, 1952, the Regional Planning Commission approved the application
on the ground that several structures in the vicinity have been allowed to extend into the re-
quired rear yard and to restrict the applicant to the wording of the ordinance would be unfair
in view of these existing buildings; and

WHEREAS, such decision of the Regional Planning Commission was filed with the Board
of Commissioners on June 23, 1952, recommending that such variance be granted#

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, the recommendation of the Regional Planning Commission
that Merle Frehner be granted a variance to permit the erection of a duplex unit in the required
rear yard on property situated at 1142 South 3rd Street, be and the same hereby is approved.
