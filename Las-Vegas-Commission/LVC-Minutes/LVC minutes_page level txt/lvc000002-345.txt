OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS
CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

September 11th, 1928.

At a recessed regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners of the City
of Las Vegas, Clark County, Nevada, held on the 11th day of September, 1928, at the
hour of three o'clock P. M.

PRESENT: Hon. J. F. Hesse, Mayor, Commissioners LeRoy Neagle and O. J. Smith,
together with the City Attorney and Clerk.

ABSENT: Commissioners W. C. German and W. B. Mundy.

Meeting called to order by the Mayor.

Mr. C. C. Boyer, Division Highway Engineer of the State of Nevada, appeared
before the Board and presented a resolution and requested this Board to adopt the
same. Upon motion duly made, seconded and unanimously carried, said resolution
was duly passed and adopted, said resolution reading as follows:

"	RESOLUTION

WHEREAS, The Department of Highways, State of Nevada, proposes the re-
construction of Federal Aid Projects 87-A and 101-A, within the city
limits of Las Vegas, the work to consist of resurfacing the present
roadway and applying asphaltic fuel oil, mixed method, and

WHEREAS, The cost of such work has been estimated by the State Highway
Department to be Eight Thousand Two Hundred Ninety Six Dollars and
Twenty Six Cents ($8,296.26), of which the city's share will amount to

Three Thousand One Hundred Thirty Four Dollars and Eighty Seven Cents

($3,134.87).

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, That funds in the amount estimated herein

be immediately made available to the Department of Highways, State of

Nevada, for carrying on the proposed construction.

"

Thereupon, no further business appearing, it was ordered upon motion duly
made, seconded and carried, that this meeting be in recess until Thursday, the
13th day of September, 1928, at the hour of three o'clock, P. M.

No further business appearing, it was ordered upon motion duly carried, that
a recess be declared until Tuesday the 11th day of September, 1928, at the hour
of three o'clock, P. M.
