BUILDING APPLICATION
Jones, Wiener & Jones
230 South 5th

Las Vegas, Nevada
April 23, 1954.

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada
held this 23rd day of April, 1954, was called to order at the hour of 10:00
A.M. by His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple with the following members present:

Mayor Pro Tem	Reed Whipple

Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

Commissioner	Rex A. Jarrett

Commissioner	Harris P. Sharp

City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

Asst. City Mgr.	Robert W. Notti

City Engineer	George Wade

Deputy City Clerk	Grace Valenziano

Absent:	Mayor	C. D. Baker

City Manager	George Rounthwaite

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Commissioner Jarrett moved the applicant having otherwise adequately provided
for off-street parking and loading, the Building Department be authorized
to allow the construction to proceed at 230 South 5th Street, in accordance
with the Building Permit issued.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting
aye; noes, none. Commissioner Sharp passed his vote. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

Jones, Wiener and Jones agreed to furnish the lease for twenty-five years
on property in the immediate area for the purpose of off-street parking
and loading.

Commissioner Sharp moved that sub-paragraph (c) of Paragraph H of Section
12 of Chapter 24 concerning provision for storage or parking of automobiles
be deleted from the Code.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

There being no further business to come before the Board, the meeting
adjourned.
