#345

WHEREAS, on the 16th day of October, 1952, Mrs. Byron Thornton filed an application
with the Regional Plannihg Commission for a variance to build a duplex in addition to an
existing single family dwelling situated at 417 N. 8th Street between Mesquite Avenue and
Linden Avenue in a R-2 (Two Family Residence) Use District; and

WHeREAS, on October 23, 1952, the Regional Planning Commission held a Public Hearing
on this application for variance at which time no objections were raised and no communications
protesting the granting of said variance were raised; and

WHEREAS, on October 23, 1952 the Regional Planning Commissioners approved the
application as they felt that this use would not be detrimental to surrounding
propertys; and

WHEREAS, such decision was filed with the Board of Commissioners on November 5, 1952
recommending that such variance be granted.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, the recommendation of the Regional Planning Commission
that Mrs. Byron Thornton be granted a variance to build a duplex in addition to an existing
single family dwelling (Situated at 417 N. 8th St. between Mesquite Avenue and Linden Ave.
in a R-2 (Two Family Residence) Use District, be and the same hereby is approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote; Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
