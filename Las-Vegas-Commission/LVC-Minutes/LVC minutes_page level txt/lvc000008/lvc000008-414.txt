CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

May 25, 1953

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is
hereby called to be held in the Council Chambers at the City Hall, on the 25th
day of May, 1953, at the hour of 7:55 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Assessment District No. 100-21 - Construction Bid

2.	Sewer Improvement Contract with Perliter & Soring

3.	Golf Course Extension

4. Assessment Districts Nos. 100-19 and 100-20

s/ C.D. Baker
Mayor
