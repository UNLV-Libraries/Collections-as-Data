UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

LAS VEGAS 1937

IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT NO. 3 BOND

KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS:

That the City of Las Vegas, a Municipal Corporation, existing
under and by virtue of the laws of the State of Nevada, for value
received, acknowledges itself indebted to, and hereby binds itself
and promises to pay to the bearer,

ONE THOUSAND DOLLARS ($1,000.00)

in current legal tender of the United States of America, at the
office of the City Treasurer of said City, in the City of Las Vegas,

Clark County, Nevada, on the 1st day of January, 1938, with interest
thereon from date hereof at the rate of seven percent (7%) per annum
until paid; said interest payable annually on the 1st day of January
of each year hereafter, during the term of this bond, upon the pre-
sentation and surrender of the proper coupons as they severally be-
come due and payable, both principal and interest payable in current
legal tender of the United States of America at the office of the City
Treasurer in the City of Las Vegas, Clark County, State of Nevada.

This bond is one of a series of twenty bonds in denominations as
follows: Ten (10) bonds of the denomination of One Thousand Dollars
($1,000.00) each, payable in ten (10) annual, equal installments
commencing on the 1st day of January, 1938, and one (1) bond in the
sum of Six Hundred Fifty-four Dollars Fifty-five Cents ($654.55) pay-
able on the 1st day of January, 1938, and nine (9) bonds in the sum of
Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00) each, one of which is payable on the
1st day of January, 1939, and one additional bond payable on the 1st
day of January of each and every year thereafter until each and every
bond in the denomination of Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00) shall have
been paid.

All of which bonds are authorized by virtue of the provision of
that certain act of the Legislature of the State of Nevada entitled
"An Act to authorize municipalities to issue bonds for the purpose of
paying the cost of municipal improvements for which special assessments
are levied", approved March 13, 1909, as amended by an Act of the
Legislature of the State of Nevada, entitled, "An Act to amend Sections
one and two of an Act entitled, 'An Act to authorize municipalities to
issue bonds for the purpose of paying the cost of municipal improvements
for which special assessments are levied', approved March 13, 1909, and
other matters relating thereto.", approved March 13, 1913, and of City
Ordinance No. 221 approved January 4, 1937, City Ordinance No. 222
approved January 4, 1937, and City Ordinance No. 223 approved May 6,

1937, and of the proceedings of the Board of Commissioners of the City
of Las Vegas, State of Nevada had and taken under and by virtue of the
provisions of the said acts of the Legislature of the State of Nevada
and the said Ordinances and the City Charter of the City of Las Vegas.

It is hereby certified that all of the things and conditions re-
quired to be done precedent to and in the issuance of this bond have
been done, happened and performed as required by law and in the event
that the fund created by special assessment as in said Ordinances Nos.

221, 222, and 223 of the City of Las Vegas contained shall be insuf-
ficient to pay this bond in full as the same becomes due, together with
interest thereon, the deficiency shall be paid by the City of Las Vegas
out of its general fund and the City of Las Vegas thus binds itself.

This bond shall be known as "LAS VEGAS 1937 IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT
NO. 3 BOND" and shall be exempt from taxation by the City of Las Vegas.

of its board of City Commissioners caused its corporate seal to be here-
unto affixed and this bond to be signed by its Mayor and the City Clerk
and countersigned by its City Assessor and the coupons hereunto attached
to be authenticated by the signature of the City Assessor, in the City
of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada, this 1st day of January,

A. D. 1937.
