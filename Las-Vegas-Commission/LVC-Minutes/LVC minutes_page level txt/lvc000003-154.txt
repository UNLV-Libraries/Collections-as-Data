Present Mayor Hesse, Commissioners Thomas and Smith together with the City Clerk.

Absent Commissioners Hansell and German and City Attorney Stevens.

Due to the absence of Commissioner German, Finance Commissioner Mayor Hesse
appointed Commissioner Thomas to act as Finance Commissioner and approve balance of claims
against the City for the month of February.

Moneys received for the month of February as follows;

$878.90 City Licenses, fees, and sale of cemetery lots.

805.00	Municipal Court Fines.

1,683.90	Total.

Moneys proportioned as follows:

$1,000.00	Police and Fire Fund

145.00	Cemetery Fund

65.50	Water, Sewer and Light Fund.

473.40	General Fund.

1,683.90

On motion of Commissioner Thomas seconded by Commissioner Smith it was moved that
Cliff Whitmore be hired as a janitor for the Clerk's Office and Commissioners Room at $20.00
per month. Said amount to be paid out of the General Fund.

Vote on said motion was as follows: Commissioners Thomas and Smith and His Honor
the Mayor voting Aye. Noes none.

No further business appearing before the Board at this time the meeting recessed
until Tuesday the 10th day of March 1931 at the hour of three o'clock P.M.
