CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, CITY CLERK

Las Vegas, Nevada

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is hereby
called to be held in the Office of the Mayor at the War Memorial Building on the
26th of November, 1948 at the hour of 3:00 P.M. to consider the following matters:

	1. Hearing on the Land Use Ordinance

2. Approve Service & Material Warrant No. 1692 in the
amount of $250,000 to State of Nevada for Storm
Sewer Construction.
