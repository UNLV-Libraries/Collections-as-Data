Las Vegas, Nevada
July 7, 1953

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO: SHIRLEY BALLINGER
City Clerk

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las
Vegas is hereby called to be held in the Council Chambers at the City
Hall, on the 7th day of July, 1953, at the hour of 4:00 P.M. to consider
the following matters:

1.	Proposed agreement with Clark County on use of sewer plant.

2.	Sale of City "Strip" Property.

3.	Recommendations on following bids:

a.	Bid No. 53.23 - Rodding Machine for Sewer Plant

b.	Golf Course Extension

4. Set date for opening of construction bids for first portion
of sewer improvement district.

5.	Ordinance recommended by the Grand Jury.

6.	Right-of-way on North 5th Street - Mr. L. Chambers of the State
Highway Department will be present.

7.	Report on proposed motel and hotel rental ordinance from City
Attorney.

8.	Bids on Bonds for Assessment Dist. 100-11, & 100-18

9.	Property for State Building, deed.

s/ C.D. BAKER
Mayor
