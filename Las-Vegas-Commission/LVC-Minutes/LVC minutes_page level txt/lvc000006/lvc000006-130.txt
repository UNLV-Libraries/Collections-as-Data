BID #47.33 - Oil	This being the time heretofore set for the opening of bids for an oil

Distributor	distributor, said bid being as follows:

July 31, 1947

To be opened 2:00 P.M.
August 7, 1947

Item #1

Trailer type oil distributor approximately 600 gallon capacity com-
plete with all necessary attachments for road patching work, shoulder
repairs, sealcoating, and secondary construction. Single axle, dual
wheels, not less than 15 horse power motor connected to at least
100 gallon per minute pump. Must handle at least 12' length spray
bar with output of 50 to 100 gallon per minute. Burner must be
sufficient capacity to provide hot material quickly and steadily at
all times. Must be so equipped that all residue left in spray bar
and pipe lines can be pulled back into tank emptying all pockets where
material might collect and harden. Distributor should be equal in
all respects to Model SJ-E-9413 manufactured by Standard Steel Works,

North Kansas City, Mo. Bidders must submit complete specifications
and descriptive literature and quote price FOB Las Vegas, Nevada less
federal excise tax. Delivery date must be shown.

The following bids were received:

C. E. Roeder Equipment Co, Reno, Nevada	$2,016.00

Smith, Booth, Usher Co. Los Angeles, Cal.	1,900.00

Commissioner Whipple moved that in accordance with the City Manager's
recommendation that Bid #47.33 in the amount of $2016.00 be awarded
to the C. D. Roeder Equipment Company.

vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting
