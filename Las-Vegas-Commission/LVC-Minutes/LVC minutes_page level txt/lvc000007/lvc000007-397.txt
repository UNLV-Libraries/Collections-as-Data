Las Vegas, Nevada

called to be held in the Mayor’s Office at the City Hall, on the28th day of June, 1951,
at the hour of 7:30 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1. Gaming and Liquor Applications for the 3rd quarter of 1951

 	2. Paving of cross streets on Fremont

4. Service and Material Warrants
