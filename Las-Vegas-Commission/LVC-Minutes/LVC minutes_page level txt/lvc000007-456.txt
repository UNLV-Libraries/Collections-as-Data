WESTSIDE IMPROVEMENTS	Commissioner Bunker moved a special improvement district be created on

the Westside for the installation of sidewalks, curbs and gutters and
that the Engineering Department commence the necessary study and survey
so that the consents can be obtained to raise the assessed valuation
to the right amount.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following
vote: Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor

voting aye; nays, none.
