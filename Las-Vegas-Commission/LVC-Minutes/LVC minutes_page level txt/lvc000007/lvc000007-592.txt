Commissioner Peccole moved the following resolution be adopted:

RESOLUTION

WHEREAS, the City of North Las Vegas has Simon Perliter doing engineering
work on the sewer project to connect with Las Vegas City sewer and are desirous of
having him at the same time do the engineering on the settling tanks in connection
therewith to be used by North Las Vegas and the City of Las Vegas,

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Board of Commissioners of the City
of Las Vegas approve Simon Perliter for the engineering work and study in connection
with said settling tanks at no cost to the City of Las Vegas.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote: Commission-

ers Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes,
none. Absent: Mayor Baker.

Commissioner Bunker moved that the position of Special Inspector (Police Department)
