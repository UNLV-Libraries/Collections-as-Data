CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

Date:	May 23, 1955_______

TO: SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk
Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada is hereby
called to be held in the Mayor's Office of the City Hall on the 23rd day of May, 1955, at
the hour of 4:30 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Gaming Application

2.	Liquor Application

3.	Cigarette Application

s/ C. D. Baker____________
