Commissioner Fountain moved Vacation VAC-7-59 be approved:

ORDER OF VACATION

A Petition, dated the 8th day of October, 1959, signed by Elks
Lodge No. 1468, by George F. Maxwell, Secretary, and two (2) freeholders
residing in the area affected; having been filed with the Clerk of this
Board, petitioning for the vacation of the Easterly 5' of Lot 5 and the
Westerly 5' of Lot 6, Block 4 of Biltmore Addition Annex No. 3, herein-
after described; and said petition having been by order of this Board
referred to the City Planning Commission for its recommendation in the
premises, and said Planning Commission having filed its report, dated
October 29, 1959, with this Board approving and recommending such
vacation;

And this Board, by an order made at its regular meeting held on the
4th day of November, 1959, set the 16th day of December, 1959, at the
hour of 7:30 P.M., in the Council Chambers of the City Hall, 5th and
Stewart Streets, in the City of Las Vegas, Clark County, Nevada, as the
time and place for a public hearing on said petition and recommendation,
and ordered the City Clerk to cause the aforesaid utility easement
proposed to be vacated to be posted with a notice setting forth the
time and place of the public hearing and the extent of the proposed
vacation;

And it appearing from the affidavit of Fred H. Callihan filed with
the Clerk of this Board, that the notice provided for in said order, a
copy of which notice is attached to said affidavit, was posted on the
16th day of November, 1959, in the manner prescribed by said order;

And this being the time fixed for the hearing on said petition for
vacation and the recommendation of the City Planning Commission thereon,
and this Board having heard evidence in support of and there being no
opposition to said petition and recommendation;

And it appearing to the satisfaction of this Board that the
utility easement proposed to be vacated is no longer required for the
public use and convenience, and that said vacation will enure to the
benefit of the City of Las Vegas, and that neither the public nor any
person will be materially injured thereby.

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the following described utility easement
in the City of Las Vegas, being a portion of Biltmore Addition Annex No.
3 more particularly described as follows:

The Westerly 5' of Lot 6 and Easterly 5' of

Lot 5, Block 4 of Biltmore Addition Annex No.

3, as shown in Book 2 of Plats, Page 48 in the Office

of the County Recorder, Clark County, Nevada.

be and the same is hereby vacated.

DATED: December 16, 1959.
