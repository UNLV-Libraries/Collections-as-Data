Las Vegas, Nevada
April 4, 1955

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada held
 this 4th day of April, 1955 was called to order at the hour of 4:45 P.M. by His Honor
Mayor Baker with the following members present:

Mayor	C. D. Baker

Commissioner	Rex A. Jarrett

Commissioner	Harris P. Sharp

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

Assistant City Attorney	Ralston O. Hawkins

Assistant City Manager	Robert	W.	Notti

City Engineer	George	E.	Wade

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Absent:	Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

City Manager	George	H.	Rounthwaite

City Attorney	Howard	W.	Cannon

This being the time heretofore set for the opening of Bid No. 55.09 for the construction
of the Jefferson Avenue Pool Building, bids were received from the following firms:

FAZIO CONSTRUCTION	CO.	$51,844.00

SIERRA CONSTRUCTION CO.	48,946.00

LONGLEY CONSTRUCTION CO.	45,598.00

Commissioner Sharp moved that these bids be referred to the City Engineer for tabulation,
computation and recommendation back to the Board on April 6, 1955*

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Bunker.

At this time Director of Public Works George E. Wade reported that a telegram had been
received from the Tide Water Associated Oil Company requesting that Bid No. 55*02 awarded
to them on March 21, 1955 be reconsidered. Mr. Wade explained that the discount shown in
Column II of the bid form was for explanatory purposes only and had already been deducted
from the bid price. Mr. Wade further recommended that the request of Tide Water Associated
that they not be held to the bid be accepted, and the Commission consider awarding this
bid to the Texas Company who are actually the low bidder.
