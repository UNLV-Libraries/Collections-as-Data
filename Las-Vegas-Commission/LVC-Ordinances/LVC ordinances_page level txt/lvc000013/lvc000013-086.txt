or owning or occupying the premises wherein any dog makes its home to call upon and pay to the
City Clerk the license or tax hereinbefore provided for and fixed, and upon so paying said fee or tax
to the said official, the latter shall give to the person so paying the same a receipt therefor,

together with a metal tag bearing the legend: "Dog Tax for __ __ __ __ __ __ __ _ (the current year) Paid,

Las Vegas, Nevada" which tags shall be consecutively numbered and a record thereof kept by the
official distributing and issuing the same, such tag shall be firmly fixed to a leathern or metal

collar and constantly worn by the dog upon which the tax or license has been paid. The moneys so

received by the said Clerk shall be paid over to the City Treasurer at the end of every month.

Section 3. Any person who after the first day of January, 1915, shall own, keep, entertain,

harbor or feed any dog upon which the said license fee or tax has not been paid for the current year,

or who shall own or keep or occupy the premises whereon or wherein any dog upon which the said
license fee or tax has not been paid for the current year shall be maintained or kept, shall be	

deemed guilty of a misdemeanor and fined in any sum not less than one dollar and not exceeding the

sum of 20 dollars ($20.00) for each and every such offence.

Section 4. After the first day of January, 1915, it shall be the duty of the police and every
member of the police of said City to immediately shoot, kill or destroy any dog which may be owned
kept, harbored or maintained within the City of Las Vegas upon which the license or tax for the
current year has not been paid, or which does not wear a collar and tag advertising the fact of such
payment, and make a report thereof to the City Clerk.

Section 3. This ordinance shall take effect from and after its passage, approval and 
publication as by law provided.

Section 6. All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

I hereby certify that the foregoing ordinance was read for the first time at a regular meeting
of the City Board of Commissioners held on the 2nd day of September 1914, and read for the second time 
and passed at its meeting on the 7th day of October, 1914, by the following vote, to-wit:- Commissioners
Griffith, aye; Curtiss, aye; Smith, aye; Sullivan, aye; and his Honor the Mayor, aye. Noes, None.

	Approved this 7th, day of October, 1914.

Attest:- Harley A. Harmon, City Clerk.	W.B. Hawkins, Mayor.
