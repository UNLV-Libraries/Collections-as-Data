BE IT RESOLVED, this 6th day of November, 1957, by the Board
of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State
of Nevada, that the deed, dated the 24th day of October, 1957,
executed by Maybelle Davis to the City of Las Vegas, County of
Clark, State of Nevada, and that the real property described
therein be, and is hereby accepted, for the purposes of a
street; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that a copy of this Resolution be
attached to said deed, and that the same be recorded in the Office
of the County Recorder of the County of Clark, State of Nevada,
and this resolution be filed in the records of this Board.
