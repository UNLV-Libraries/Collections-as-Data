Las Vegas, Nevada
January 7, 1947

At a regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners held this
7th day of January 1947. Meeting called to order at the hour of
2:45 P.M. by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin with the following members
present:	Commissioners Clark, Corradetti; City Attorney, C. Norman

Cornwall; City Manager T. E. Fennessy; and City Clerk Helen Scott
Reed. Absent:	Commissioners Bates, and Baskin.

At this time Mr. F. C. Carpenter appeared before the Board and stated
that everything was ready to complete his building at 7th and Ogden
in compliance with the Building Code.

HEARING - Roscoe	At this time Mr. Roscoe Hilburn appeared before the Board and was

Hilburn,	admonished for his failure to comply with the provisions of the

Electrical Code.

Thereafter Commissioner Clark moved that on the recommendation of
Frank Partie, City Electrician, Mr. Hilburn be given another chance
and his license remain in good standing.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Corradetti and carried by the
following vote:	Commissioners Clark, Corradetti and His Honor

voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioners Baskin and Bates.

SERVICE & MATERIAL	Commissioner Clark moved that Service & Material Warrants Nos. 1019

WARRANTS		to l024 inclusive in the sum of $16,856.42 be approved and the Mayor

and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Corradetti and carried by the
following vote:	Commissioners Clark, Corradetti and His Honor voting

aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioners B skin and Bates.

POLICE STATION --	Commissioner Clark moved that the third periodical report on the

3rd Periodical 	Police Station be approved when the Comptroller and City Manager are

Report	satisfied with the supporting papers.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Corradetti and carried by the following
vote; Commissioner Clark, Corradetti and His Honor voting aye; noes,
none. Absent:	Commissioners Baskin and Bates.

SWIMMING POOL	Commissioner Clark moved that the payment of $17,190.00 on the first

periodical estimate on the Swimming Pool and the Mayor and City
	Clerk authorized to sign this warrant for same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Corradetti and carried by the
	following vote; Commissioners Clark, Corradetti and His Honor

voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioners Baskin and Bates.

AUDITOR'S REPORT 	The preliminary report of George H. Wood, dated December 19, 1946

was ordered filed.

CONVENTION 	the special committee of	the American Legion to consider current

problems of public utilities.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Corradetti and carried by the
following vote:	Commissioners Clark, Corradetti and His Honor

voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioners Baskin and Bates.
