ORDINANCE NO. 763

AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND SECTION 5, OF ORDINANCE NO. 718, CREATING A SOLICITATIONS REVIEW BOARD
OF THE CM OF LAS VEGAS, BY PROVIDING FOR SIX MEMBERS THEREOF, ONE OF WHOM SHALL BE A MEM-
BER OF THE YOUTH ADVISORY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY
RELATED THERETO; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH.

THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS DO ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

SECTION 1. Section 5 of Ordinance No. 718 which amended Section 5 of Ordinance No.

677, is hereby further amended to read as follows:

SOLICITATIONS REVIEW BOARD. A Solicitations Review Board, composed of six (6) mem-
bers, one of whom shall be a member of the Youth Advisory Council of the City of Las Vegas,
shall be appointed by the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, and such Board is
hereby created. The members of such Board shall hold office for a period of two years. Those
persons already members of said Board shall continue to serve out the term of their appoint-
ment. The person appointed by virtues of the provisions of this Ordinance shall have a two
year appointment. Appointments shall be made so that as near as possible the terms shall be
staggered. Vacancies on the said Board shall be filled by appointment made by the Board of
Commissioners.

(a)	The Supervisor of Licenses and Revenue shall submit an applications to the
Solicitations Review Board for its recommendations.

(b)	It shall be the duty of the Solicitations Review Board to meet regularly or on
call by the Supervisor of Licenses and Revenue, to review such applications and recommend to
the Board of Commissioners the issuance of a permit for each application concerning which it
has been found that the provisions of Section 4 of Ordinance 718 have been complied with.

(c)	The Solicitations Review Board shall recommend the denial of permits for all
other applications, or the withholding of permits until such time as the standards set forth in
Section 4 of Ordinance 718 shall be fully met.

SECTION 2. This Ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its publication as
in the next section provided, in final passage.

SECTION 3. The City Clerk and the Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City
of Las Vegas shall cause this Ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks
immediately following its first reading and adoption, in the Las Vegas Sun, a daily newspaper
published in the City of Las Vegas.
