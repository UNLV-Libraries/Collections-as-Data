CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO— VIOLA BURNS, CITY CLERK

LAS VEGAS, NEVADA.	

A Special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Clark County is
hereby called to be held in the Board of Commissioners Room in the City Clerks Office, Saturday
the 4th day of September, 1937 at the hour of nine thirty o'clock A.M. on said day to consider
and act upon the following:

1.	To act on the payment of city claims for the month of August, 1937

2. To act upon the application of Corey's Club for license for the operation of a "Twenty-
one" table, and the application of Tom Williams Jr. for a retail beverage license to be
used at the skating rink to be operated at 115 Fremont Street, 2nd floor.

3.	To act upon the application of property owners in Woodland Park for proper water service

for that area.

4.	An other matters that might legally be brought before the Board for consideration and
action.

Dated this 4th day of September, 1937

L.L. Arnett,

Mayor of the City of Las Vegas,

OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, CITY OF
LAS VEGAS, CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.
SEPTEMBER 4th, 1937

Minutes of a Special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas held on
the fourth day of September, 1937 at the hour of nine thirty o'clock A.M.

Present Mayor Arnett, Commissioners Krause, Marble and Ronnow, and City Clerk

Absent Commissioner Perry and City Attorney Austin.

All claims allowed as per "Claims & Warrant Register" on file in the office of the Clerk.

On motion of Commissioner Marble seconded by Commissioner Krause it was moved and carried
that license for a "Twenty-One" table be granted to Corey Club.

Vote Commissioners Krause, Marble and Ronnow and His Honor the Mayor voting aye. Noes, none.

Mr. Krause stated to the Board that he received on benefits from the Claim of the Southern
Nevada Disinfectant Company, said claim signed by Kermit Krause.

On motion of Commissioner Krause seconded by Commissioner Marble it was moved and carried
that a retail beverage license be granted to Tom Williams Jr. for operation at the skating rink
at 115 Fremont St.

Vote Commissioners Krause, Marble and Ronnow and His Honor the Mayor voting aye. Noes, none.

The following petition was presented to the Board at this time:

TO THE HONORABLE— THE MAYOR, AND THE BOARD OF CITY
COMMISSIONERS OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA.

WE, the undersigned residents of and owners of lots in Woodlawn Park Addition in the City
of Las Vegas, Nevada, do hereby petition you honorable body to furnish the undersigned with
water, controlled and regulated by the City of Las Vegas. We feel, that in order to develop and
make habitable, the said addition, and for proper protection against fire, and because condit-
ions are becoming unsanitary, it is necessary to have an adequate water supply. Therefore, we do
hereby request that the City of Las Vegas furnish the necessary water.
