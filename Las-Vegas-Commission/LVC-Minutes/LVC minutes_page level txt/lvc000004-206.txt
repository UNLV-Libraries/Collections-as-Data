OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK CITY OF
LAS VEGAS, CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.

JULY 5th, 1939

Minutes of a regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas held
on the 5th day of July, 1939 at the hour of seven o'clock P.M.

Present Mayor Russell, Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti, City Attorney
and Clerk.

Minutes of meeting of June 27th read and approved as read.

On motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commissioner Corradetti it was moved and car-
ried that demands and warrants Nos. 178 to 187 be allowed and that the Clerk and Mayor sign the
same.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes, none.

the Clerk presented to the Board the report of the Municipal Judge, A.G. Blad, for the
month of June, 1939.

On motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commission Gilbert it was moved and carried
that the report be accepted and filed.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes, none.

Bids for the purchase of lamps for the City for the year were opened at this time and
on motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commissioner Gilbert it was moved and carried
that the bids for lamps for the City be handed to LeRoy Whipple Purchasing Clerk of the City
for tabulation and that he report his findings to Commissioner Corradetti.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes, none.

On motion of Commissioner Gilbert seconded by Commissioner Corradetti it was moved and
carried that the bids for the purchase of supplies for the Recreation Department be rejected.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes, none.

On motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commissioner Krause it was moved and carried
that the Purchasing Clerk purchase supplies need for the Recreational Department upon the
approval of Commissioner Gilbert and Director Burger.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes, none.	

On motion of Commissioner Gilbert seconded by Commissioner Krause it was moved and carried
that the City purchase from Ed Von Tobel Lumber Co. sheet metal in the sum of $78.90 to be used
on the grand stand at the City Park.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes, none.

This being the time set for the opening of bids for the purchase of suitable supplies for
the installation of a checking system for the dispensing of gas from the City barns and no bids
being received it was on motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commissioner Gilbert moved
and carried that the Purchasing Clerk purchase the necessary supplies not to exceed the sum
of $50.00.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes, none.

At this time the Clerk read to the Board a letter from the Eagles Drum Corps asking that
the City from its publicity fund give towards to cost of the trip of the said Drum Corps to
the National Convention in Chicago next month.

On motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commissioner Gilbert it was moved and carried
that this letter be filed for consideration at the meeting to be held on the 17th day of July.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting aye.
Noes, none.

The application of Dr. E. Winter for City Health Officer and Harry Gravelle for position
with the Police Department were read, and on motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commis-
sioner Gilbert it was moved and carried that the Clerk file these letter of application for
future reference.

Vote Commissioners Gilbert, Ronnow, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes, none.

The Clerk presented proof from Patrick Cline asking that the cash bond held in the name
of the Acme Plumbing & Sheet Metal Company of which Patrick Cline was the owner, stating that
he desired that said bond transferred to the credit of H. Brockmiller, a master plumber of the
City of Las Vegas.

On motion of Commissioner Gilbert seconded by Commissioner Krause it was moved and carried
that this cash bond of $100.00 be transferred from the Acme Plumbing & Sheet Metal Co. Patrick
Cline owner, to H. Brockmiller.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes, none.

On motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commissioner Krause it was moved and carried
that all gaming and slot machines licenses applied for at this time be granted. This being all
renewals.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow, Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting
aye. Noes, none.
