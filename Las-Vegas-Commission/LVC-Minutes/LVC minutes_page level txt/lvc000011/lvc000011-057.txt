EFFLUENT DITCH	Mr. Kennedy presented a contract with Masako Miura for a portion of the NE¼ of

the SE¼ of Section 15, T21S, R62E for construction of the effluent ditch below
the new Sewage Disposal Plant. The City Manager stated that Mrs. Miura 
requests payment of $250.00 for a total of 1.31 acres. Mr. Kennedy advised the
Board that there was right-of-way to be acquired between the present property
held by the City for the effluent ditch and the property owned by Mrs. Miura
and therefore, requested that the contract be approved subject to clearance
of the right-of-way between Mrs. Miura's property and the property acquired
for the effluent ditch purposes.

Commissioner Sharp moved the contract with Mrs. Miura be approved subject to
check add clearance of the right-of-way between Mrs. Miura's property and
property presently owned by the City for effluent ditch purposes and that at
such time as the intervening right-of-way is cleared the Mayor and Clerk be
authorized to sign the contract.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	

Commissioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple 
voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.
