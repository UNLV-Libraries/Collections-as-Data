Las Vegas, Nevada
May 26, 1954

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,
Nevada, is hereby called to be held in the Council Chambers of the City Hall,
on the 26th day of May, 1954, at the hour of 3:30 P.M. to consider the follow-
ing matters:

1.	Auction Permit

2.	Assessment District No. 100-23

3.	Approval of purchase of land from UPRR - Front portion
of City Bams property.

4. Re-set public hearing on the vacation of Canosa Ave.

5. Berkely Square Subdivision - final map.

6. Request of City Manager for time off for Naval Training
from June 7 to June 18 inclusive.

s/ C. D. Baker____________

Mayor

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,

Nevada held this 26th day of May, 1954 was called to order at the hour of
3:30 P.M. by His Honor Mayor Baker with the following members present:

Mayor	C. D. Baker

Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

City Manager George Rounthwaite
Asst. City Mgr. Robert W. Notti
Asst. City Atty Ralston O. Hawkins
City Engineer	George Wade

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Absent:	Commissioner Harris P. Sharp

Commissioner	Rex A. Jarrett

City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

Also Present:	Mr. Dave Waddy of Silas Mason Engineering

At this time the application of E. B. Rosenstiel and Phillip P. Wilensky for
a permit to conduct an auction on June 3, 1954, to dispose of property form-
erly owned by Brandley and Simmons at 1023 South 1st Street, was considered
by the Board.

At this time Commissioner Jarrett arrived at the meeting.

Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved this auction permit be granted subject
to compliance with the ordinance governing auctions and auctioneers and to
clearance from the Police Department.

AUCTION PERMIT
