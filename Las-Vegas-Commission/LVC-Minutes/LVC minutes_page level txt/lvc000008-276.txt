BOND ISSUE	Commissioner Peccole moved that the City Attorney be instructed to prepare notices

Question on Ballot	for publication as soon as the law allows, informing the public that it is the

intention of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas to place on the
	ballot at the next General Election to be held May 5, 1953, the question as to whether

bonds should be issued in the sum of $900,000.00 to finance a sewer main outfall sewer
construction program; further that the City Clerk is hereby directed to have said
publications made in accordance with the law governing the same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote; Commissioners
Bunker, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner
Jarrett.
