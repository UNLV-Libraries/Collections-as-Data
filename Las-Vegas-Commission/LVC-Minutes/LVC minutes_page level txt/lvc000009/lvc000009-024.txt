

PROPOSED ORDINANCE
Warm Air Heating Code

BOARD OF APPEALS



BOARD OF ZONING ADJUSTMENT

At this time the proposed ordinance on the Warm Air Heating Code
entitled:	AN ORDINANCE PROVIDING FOR THE PROTECTION OF THE PUBLIC HEALTH

AND SAFETY, AND THE EXAMINATION, REGISTRATION, LICENSING OF PERSONS
ENGAGED IN THE BUSINESS OF WARM AIR HEATING, OR BALORING AT THE TRADE
AS A WARM AIR HEATING INSTALLER REQUIRING A PERMIT FOR THE INSTALLATION
OR ALTERATION OF WARM AIR HEATING APPLIANCES, AND WARM AIR HEATING
APPLIANCES, AND WARM AIR HEATING SYSTEMS; CREATING AN ADMINISTRATIVE
OFFICE AND A BOARD OF WARM AIR HEATING EXAMINERS, AND PRESCRIBING
THEIR DUTIES; DEFINING CERTAIN TERMS; ESTABLISHING MINIMUM REGULATIONS
FOR THE INSTALLATION, ALTERATION OR REPAIR OF WARM AIR HEATING
APPLIANCES, AND WARM AIR HEATING SYSTEMS AND THE INSPECTION THEREOF,
PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR ITS VIOLATION AND REPEALING CONFLICTING
ORDINANCES, was read by title to the Board and referred to
Commissioners Jarrett and Whipple for study and recommendation.

Commissioner Sharp moved to accept the resignation of John Replogle
from the Board of Appeals.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor

voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Jarrett moved to appoint Mr. Ewalt Anderson to serve
on the Board of Appeals as a structural engineer.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor

voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Bunker moved to appoint Mr. Robert Schmutzer to serve
on the Board of Zoning Adjustment.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting
aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Whipple moved that John Replogle and Ernie Schroer
be appointed to serve on the Board of Zoning Adjustment.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor

voting aye; noes, none.

There being no further business to come before this meeting, Commis-
sioner Whipple moved this meeting adjourn.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor

voting aye; noes, none.
