Mintues of a special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
held on the 15th day of January 1937 at the hour of ten o'clock A.M.

Roll call showed the following present, Mayor Arnett, Commissioners Down, Krause, Marble
and Perry City Attorney and Clerk.

On motion of Commissioner Down seconded by Commissioner Marble it was moved and carried
that the following notice be published:

NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN: That the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, proposes to make certain im-
provements, to-wit: Constructing oil bound gravel pavement and concrete curbs and gutters upon
certain street in the city, together with work incidental thereto, according to plans and
specifications on file in the office of the City Clerk. Sealed bids for the construction of the
said improvements are invited for said work and will be received at the office of the City
Clerk in the City Hall in the City of Las Vegas until 10:00 o'clock A.M. on the 30th day of
January, 1937. Instructions to bidders, plans and specifications for the said improvements can
be seen and examined at the office of the City Clerk in the City Hall in the City of Las Vegas,
and copies may be obtained by the payment of the sum of fifteen Dollars ($15.00) therefor.

The right is reserved to reject any and all bids and to waive any and all defects in any
of said bids.

BY ORDER OF THE BOARD OF CITY
COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS
DATED JANUARY 15th, 1937.

Viola Burns
City Clerk.

Vote was Commissioners Down, Krause, Marble and Perry and His Honor the Mayor voting aye.
Noes, none.

The Clerk was directed to have said notice published in the Las Vegas Age a weekly news-
paper printed and published in the City of Las Vegas for three issues, January 15th, 22nd,
and 29th, 1937.

No further business appearing before the Board at this time the meeting recessed and
adjourned.

OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, CITY OF
LAS VEGAS CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA.
JANUARY 15th, 1937.

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO— Viola Burns, City Clerk
Las Vegas, Nevada.

A Special meeting of the Board of City Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is hereby
called to be held in the Board of Commissioners room in the City Clerk's office on Friday
the 15th day of January, 1937 at the hour of seven thirty o'clock P.M. on said day to con-
sider and act upon the following matters:

1.	To open bids for the purchase of a motor grader for the City Street Department.

2.	Approve semi-monthly salary bills.

3.	To act upon all gaming and slot machine liquor and beverage applications on file with the
Clerk.

Dated this 15th day of January, 1937.

L.L. Arnett

Mayor of the City of Las Vegas.
