ORDINANCE NO. AA6

AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND SECTIONS 1 AND 2 OF ORDINANCE NO. 360 OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS ENTITLED: "AN ORDIN-
ANCE ESTABLISHING A REGIONAL PLANNING COMMISSION AND AUTHORIZING THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY
OF LAS VEGAS, CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA, TO COLLABORATE WITH THE GOVERNING BODIES OF INCORPORATED CITIES IN
CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA, AND THE BOARD OF COUNTY COMMISSIONERS OF CLARK COUNTY, NEVADA, FOR THE CREATION OF
A REGIONAL PLANNING COMMISSION." ALSO REFERRED TO AS CHAPTER 24, SECTIONS 1 AND 2 OF THE LAS VEGAS CITY
CODE, AND TO REPEAL SECTIONS 3 AND A OF ORDINANCE NO. 360 ALSO REFERRED TO AS CHAPTER 2A, SECTIONS 3 AND
4 OF THE LAS VEGAS CITY CODE.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

Section 1. Section 1 of Ordinance No. 360 and also referred to as Chapter 24, Section 1 of the
Las Vegas City Code is hereby amended to read as follows:

There is hereby created a Regional Planning Commission pursuant to Section A of Section
5063.03 NCL to consist of seven (7) members, three members of which shall be appointed
by the Mayor and Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, three members of which
shall be appointed by the Board of County Commissioners of the County of Clark, and one
member of which shall be appointed by the Mayor and Board of Commissioners of the City
of North Las Vegas.

Section 2. Section 2 of Ordinance No. 360 and also referred to as Chapter 24, Section 2 of the
Las Vegas City Code is hereby amended to read as follows:

The term of office of each member of said Regional Planning Commission shall be six years
or until his successor is appointed and qualifies except that the respective terms of the
members first so appointed shall be one for a one-year term; one for a two-year term; one
for a three-year term; one for a four-year term; one for a five-year term; and two for a
six-year term. All appointments to fill vacancies shall be for the unexpired term. All
vacancies occurring in the Regional Planning Commission shall be filled by appointment by
the governing body which first made the appointment.

Section 3. Section 3 of Ordinance No. 360 also referred to as Chapter 24, Section 3 of the Las
Vegas City Code is hereby repealed.

Section A. Section A of Ordinance No. 360 also referred to as Chapter 24, Section A of the Las
Vegas City Code is hereby repealed.

Section 5. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas shall
cause this Ordinance No. 446 to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following its
final reading and adoption, in the Las Vegas Review Journal, a daily newspaper published in the City of
Las Vegas.
