Las Vegas, Nevada
May 11, 1955

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada held
this 11th day of May, 1955 was called to order at the hour of 8:25 P.M. by His Honor
Mayor Baker with the following members present:

Mayor	C. D. Baker

Commissioner	Wendell Bunker

Commissioner	Rex A. Jarrett

Commissioner	Harris P. Sharp

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

City Manager	George H. Rounthwaite

Assistant City Manager	Robert W. Notti

City Engineer	George E. Wade

Planning Director	Franklin Bills

City Attorney	Howard W. Cannon

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

At this time the service and material warrants for payment to the members of the
election boards, guards of the precincts and precinct polling locations were
presented to the Board for their consideration.

Commissioner Bunker moved that Service and Material Warrants Nos. 2372 and 3054 and 3056
in the total amount of $6,192.00 be approved and the Mayor and Clerk authorized to sign
same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

City Manager George H. Rounthwaite recommended that the application of Western Cab
Company for two additional taxicabs for the second quarter of 1955 and two cab stands
located on the southeast comer of Jackson and E Streets (El Morocco Club) and 900 W.
Bonanza (Moulin Rouge Hotel).

Commissioner Bunker moved the recommendation of the City Manager be accepted and the
Western Enterprises Inc. dba Western Cab Company be granted license for two additional
taxicabs and two cab stands at the southeast comer of Jackson and E Streets (El Morocco
Club) and 900 W. Bonanza (Moulin Rouge).

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

City Attorney Howard W. Cannon requested that the Commission adopt an emergency ordinance
to correct the amended Assessment Roll No. 33-1955 on Assessment District No. 100-25.

Thereafter Commissioner Sharp introduced and moved the adoption of the following ordinance
entitled: AN EMERGENCY ORDINANCE CONCERNING SPECIAL ASSESSMENT DISTRICT NO. 100-25;
CORRECTING IN CERTAIN PARTICULARS THE CONFIRMED AMENDED ASSESSMENT ROLL FOR SAID DISTRICT;
RATIFYING, APPROVING, AND CONFIRMING ACTION TAKEN HERETOFORE IN THE PROCEEDINGS FOR
ASSESSMENT DISTRICT NO. 100-25; PRESCRIBING DETAILS IN CONNECTION THEREWITH AND OTHER
MATTERS RELATING THERETO; REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH; AND DECLARING
AN EMERGENCY.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

At this time the application of George Tsouras for transfer of the tavern liquor license
of the Katydid Bar, located at 113 North 1st Street, said license issued to George Norris,
to the Golden Slot Bar at 22 Fremont, was considered by the Board.

Commissioner Whipple moved this transfer of the tavern liquor license from George Norris
dba Katydid Bar to George Tsouras dba Golden Slot Bar, 22 Fremont Street, be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
