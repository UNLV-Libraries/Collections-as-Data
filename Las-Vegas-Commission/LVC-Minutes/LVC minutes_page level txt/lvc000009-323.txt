Commissioner Sharp moved that the Building Department be authorized to give the Las Vegas Gas
Company a building permit for a warehouse.

Seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners Bunker

Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Committee members Jarrett and Sharp reported that they had not finished going over this
proposed ordinance and requested more time.

Commissioners Jarrett and Whipple stated Mr. Long and Mr. Hatfield were trying to
consolidate the Sign and Billboard ordinances and requested this be held in abeyance

The Committee reported favorably on this ordinance and said proposed ordinance was
referred to the City Attorney for legal opinion

Commissioners Bunker and Sharp reported favorably on this proposed ordinance, entitled
"AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND SECTION IV OF ORDINANCE NO. 378, REFERRED TO AS SECTION 10,

CHAPTER .24, OF THE LAS VEGAS CITY CODE, 1949, BY AMENDING THE LAND USE PLAN MAP ADOPTED BY
THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, AND CHANGING THE ZONE DESIGNATION OF CERTAIN AREAS ON THE SAID MAP.

The City Clerk read the entire ordinance.

Thereafter Commissioner Bunker moved this ordinance be adopted and numbered 620.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Committee members Bunker and Sharp requested more time on this proposed ordinance.
