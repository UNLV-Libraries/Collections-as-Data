ORDINANCE No. 328 The Committee composed of Commissioners Corradetti and Baskin

reported favorably on proposed Ordinance No. 328 entitled "AN
ORDINANCE DECLARING THE DETERMINATION OF THE BOARD OF COMMISSION-
ERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS TO MAKE CERTAIN IMPROVEMENTS IN THE
CITY OF LAS VEGAS BY INSTALLING COMPLETE STREET LIGHTING UNITS
WITH PARK WAY CABLE AND STEEL STANDARDS ALONG CERTAIN STREETS IN
SAID CITY:	PROVIDING THAT THE COST AND EXTENT THEREOF SHALL BE

PAID ENTIRELY BY SPECIAL ASSESSMENT UPON ALL LOTS INCLUDED IN THE
SPECIAL ASSESSMENT DISTRICT HEREBY ESTABLISHED ACCORDING TO FRONTAGE:
EXCEPT WHEREBY THE CHARTER OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS CERTAIN PORTIONS
THEREOF ARE REQUIRED PAID FROM THE GENERAL FUND OF THE CITY OF LAS
VEGAS, AND OTHER MATTERS RELATING THERETO," which said ordinance was
read in its entirety for the first time.

After which Commissioner Baskin moved its approval and adoption.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following
voter Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Corradetti and His Honor voting
aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner Bates.

HEARING ON PRO- In the regular corse of business the Board called for objection to

CLAMATION—	the consideration and passage of an ordinance providing for a bond

Swimming Pool/Bath issue in the amount of $100,000.00 for the construction of bath-
Houses 	houses and facilities in connection with swimming pools, of which

notice was given by proclamation published in the Las Vegas Evening
Review Journal. No objection having been voiced in response to the
Mayor's call the question was tabled for further consideration at a
recessed meeting to be held on March 24, 1947 at the hour of 10 a.m.
