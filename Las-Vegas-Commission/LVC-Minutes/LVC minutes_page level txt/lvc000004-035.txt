On motion of Commissioner Marble seconded by Commissioner Down it was moved and carried that the action
taken by this Board on Saturday the 3rd day of April, 1937 in regard to placing the following question on
a ballot at the time of the General City Election to be held on the 4th day of May, 1937 be rescinded:

STREET IMPROVEMENT BOND
OFFICIAL BALLOT

GENERAL ELECTION OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS
ON TUESDAY THE 4TH DAY OF MAY, 1937.

Instructions to voters: If in favor of the bond the voter will p ace a "X" in the square opposite
the word "Yes". If opposed to the bond he will place a "X" in the square opposite the word "No".

Shall the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas borrow the sum of Sixteen Thousand
and Five Hundred Ninety-seven and 95/100 ($16,597.95) Dollars to cover certain unpaid assessments
heretofore levied and confirmed by the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas for the
purpose of paying for the work of making improvements in the City of Las Vegas by grading for and the
construction of oil bound gravel pavements on certain streets and portions of streets in the City
of Las Vegas and particularly described in what is called "Las Vegas 1937 Improvements District
No. 3", and to issue and sell the bonds of the City of Las Vegas therefor, said bonds to be known as
"Las Vegas 1937 Improvement District No. 3 Bonds", which said bonds shall bear interest at no
exceeding seven (7%) per cent per annum?

FOR STREET IMPROVEMENT BOND	YES

AGAINST STREET IMPROVEMENT BOND___________________NO_______

Vote on the rescinding of the above was Commissioners Down, Krause, Marble and Perry and His Honor
the Mayor voting aye. Noes none, absent none.

On motion of Commissioner Marble seconded by Commissioner Perry it was moved and carried that the
meeting adjourn.

Vote was Commissioners Down, Krause, Marble and Perry and His Honor the Mayor voting aye. Noes,
none. Absent none.

There upon the meeting adjourned.
