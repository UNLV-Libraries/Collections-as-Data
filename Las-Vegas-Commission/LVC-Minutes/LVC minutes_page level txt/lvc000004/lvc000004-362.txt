THERE UPON it appearing to the Board that A.F. Smith Sr. has received a plurality of all
votes cast for the office of Commissioner Second Class of the City of Las Vegas, it was therefore
Resolved by Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commissioner Corradetti that the said A.F. Smith Sr.
be end he is declared to be the duly elected Commissioner of the Second Class of the City
of Las Vegas, for the ensuing four years, and it was further Resolved that the City Clerk be
and she hereby is directed to issued a certificate of Election to the said A.F. Smith Sr.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting aye. Noes, none.

There upon it appearing to the Board that C.R. "Pat" Clark had receive a plurality of all
votes cast for the office of Commissioner of the Fourth Class and it was Therefore Resolved by
motion of Commissioner Ronnow seconded by Commissioner Corradetti that the said C.R. "Pat" Clark
be and he is hereby declared to be the duly elected Commissioner of the Fourth Class of the
City of Las Vegas for the ensuing four years and it was further Resolved that the City Clerk be
and she is hereby directed to issue a certificate of election to the said C.R. "Pat" Clark.

Vote Commissioners Ronnow and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting aye. Noes, none.
