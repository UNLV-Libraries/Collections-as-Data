SOLICITORS LICENSE            At this time Mr. C. H. Everest, 111 E. Lewis Street appeared before the Board relative
to the securing of a Solicitors license. Mr. Everest explained that he was a Spanish
	American War Veteran and would like to sell his product, but could not afford to pay the

license fee required by ordinance.

Mr. Everest was requested to present the proper affidavits to the Board stating why he
could not afford to pay the required fee, and the Board of Commissioners would act on
his appeal at that time.
