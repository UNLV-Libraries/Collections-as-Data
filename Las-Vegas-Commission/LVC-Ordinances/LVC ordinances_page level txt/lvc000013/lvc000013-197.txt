ORDINANCE No. 130

An Ordinance to amend Section 1 of Ordinance Ho. 27 of the City of Las Vegas,
entitled "An Ordinance fixing the fire limits of the City of Las Vegas, regulating building
construction and the keeping and storage of combustibles and explosives.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas Do Ordain as follows:

Section 1. Section 1 of said Ordinance No. 27 is hereby amended so that the
same shall read as follows:

"Sec. 1. That all that portion of the City of Las Vegas, embraced within the
limits of Blocks Nos. 1-2-3-14-15-16-18-19-30-31-34 and 35, all in Clark's Las Vegas
Townsite, Clark County, Nevada according to the official and recorded plat of the survey
thereof, shall be hereafter known as the fire limits of the City of Las Vegas."

Section 2. This Ordinance shall take effect and be in force from and after its
passage and adoption and publication for one week (three issues) in Las Vegas Age, a
tri-weekly newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.

Passed and adopted this 3rd day of May, 1928 upon the following vote: Commissioners,
 Smith, Mundy, German, and Neagle, and his Honor the Mayor voting aye. Noes, none.
First reading April 16, 1928. Second reading May 3, 1928.

Approved this 3rd day of May, 1928.

J. F. Hesse	

Mayor
