Minutes of a Special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas Held on
the 21st day of April, 1941 at the of two o'clock P.M.

Present Mayor Russell, Commissioners Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti City Attorney and Clerk.

Absent Commissioner Ronnow.

Commissioners Gilbert and Corradetti reported to the Board that they were not satisfied with
the preoared applications to the W.P.A. for aid in connection with the construction of an access
Road to the Las Vegas airport and aid in construction of sidewalks and ask that these matters
be not be passed upon at this time but that a meeting be called on April 23rd if said applications
were in order.

There upon on motion of Commissioner Corradetti seconded by Commissioner Krause it was moved
and carried that this meeting adjourn.

Vote Commissioner Gilbert, Krause and Corradetti and His Honor the Mayor voting aye. Noes, none.
