Section 5. All Ordinances or parts of Ordinances, in conflict with this Ordinance,
are hereby repealed.

Section 6. This Ordinance shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage

and adoption and publication for one week (two issues) in the Las Vegas Review, a semi-

weekly newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
