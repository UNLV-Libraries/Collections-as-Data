CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

This special meeting was called to order by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin
at the hour of 5:00 P.M. with the following members present:	Commissioners

Clark, Moore, Whipple; City Manager J. M. Murphy, City Attorney C. Norman
Cornall and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger. Absent:	Commissioner Baskin.

ORDINANCE NO. 354 At this time Ordinance No. 354 relating to the installation of Parking
Parking Meter Meters, was reported on favorably by Commissioners Clark and Whipple
1st Reading	with the following recommended changes:

	1. Section 6.	(a) Said parking meter shall be operated in said

parking meter zone every day between the hours of 9:00 A.M. and
6:00 o'clock P.M., 

2. Section 6. (b) Parking or standing a vehicle in a designated
space in a parking meter zone shall be lawful for twelve (12)
minutes or more with a maximum of sixty minutes, and upon
deposit of a one (l) one-cent coin of the United States of
America for twelve (12) minutes, or of a one (1) five-cent
coin of the United States of America for sixty (60) minutes

in all parking meter zones.

3.	Section 8.	(d)  Each such owner or operator may within

twenty-four (24) hours of the time when such notice was attached
to such vehicle, pay to the police Department of the City of
Las Vegas as a penalty for and in full satisfaction of such
violation, the sum of One Dollar ($1.00) for the first offense,

Two Dollars ($2.00) for the second offense, and Three Dollars
($3.00) for the third and each subsequent offense thereafter.

4.	Section 9. COLLECTIONS It shall be the duty of the Chief of
Police to designate some member or members of the Police
Department, who shall be bonded, 

Thereafter the foregoing ordinance was read in full to the Board
for the first time.

Commissioner Whipple moved that the foregoing Ordinance be approved
on its first reading with the above mentioned changes to be included.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, hone.
Absent:	Commissioner Baskin.
