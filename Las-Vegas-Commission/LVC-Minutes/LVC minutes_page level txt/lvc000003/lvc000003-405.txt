"Whereas, the City of Las Vegas heretofore and on the 14th, day
of January, 1935, entered into an agreement with Las Vegas Post Number
8, The American Legion, Department of Nevada, in substance and to the
effect that the City of Las Vegas upon the said American Legion Post
expending the sum of Five Thousand ($5000.00) Dollars or upward upon
the erection of a Memorial Building upon land then under lease from the
City to the said Post, that the City would thereupon lease to the said
Post for a period of fifty years lands upon which the said building was
situate, and,

WHEREAS, it appearing to the Board at this time that the said Post
has expended a sum far in excess of Five Thousand ($5000.00) Dollars
upon the construction of a building upon said land, and,

WHEREAS, it appearing to the Board that the letter and intent
of the said agreement should be complied with and good cause appearing therefore, it is,

	ORDERED AND DIRECTED that the Mayor of the City of Las Vegas execute
a lease for and on behalf of the said City with the Las Vegas Post Number
8, The American Legion, Department of Nevada, conveying the following
described premises."

Commencing at the northeast corner of Block Thirty-Three (33) of
Clark's Las Vegas Townsite as per map of said Townsite on file and of
record in the office of the County Recorder of Clark County, Nevada, thence
along the prolongation of the easterly line of said Block 33 N. 28˚13' E.
eighty (80) feet to the point of beginning; thence N. 61˚ 47' W. 275.0 feet;
thence No. 28˚13' E. 300.0 feet; thence No. 68°01'20" E. 117.15 feet; thence
S. 61˚47' E. 200.0 feet; thence S. 28˚13' W. 390.0 feet to the point of beginning
, comprising a net area of 2.385 acres.
by lease in form as the hereunto annexed copy for a period of fifty years at the annual
rental of One ($1.00) Dollar per year payable in advance, said term to commence the first
day of September, 1935."

NOW THEREFORE, the said lessor, for and in consideration of the rents covenants
and agreements hereinafter contained and reserved on the part and behalf of the Lessee, to
be paid, kept and performed, does by these presents grant, demise and let unto the said
Lessee all of that said real property described as follows;

Commencing at the Northeast corner of Block Thirty-three (33) of Clark's
Las Vegas Townsite as per map of said Townsite on file and of record in the
Office of the County Recorder of Clark County, Nevada, thence along the
prolongation of the easterly line of said Block 33 N. 28°13' E. eighty
(80) feet to the point of beginning; thence N. 61°47' W. 275.0 feet; thence
N. 28°13' E. 300.0 feet; thence N.68°01'20" E. 117.15 feet; thence S.61°47'

E. 200,0 feet; thence S. 28°13' W. 390.0 feet to the point of beginning,
comprising a net area of 2.385 acres.

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the said premises, together with the appurtenances, from the
date of the execution of this instrument, for the full term of fifty (50) years from the
first day of September, 1935, next ensuing; the lesse to pay therefor the sum of Fifty
($50.00) Dollars, said sum being payable one ($1.00) Dollar on execution of this instrument,
the receipt whereof is hereby acknowledged, and an additional one ($1.00) Dollar on or before
the first of September of each year hereafter until the full sum of Fifty ($50.00) Dollars
shall have been paid.

PROVIDED FURTHER, that this lease shall terminate, at the option of the Lessor,
upon the happening of any one or more of the following events:

(1)	Failure of the Lesse to construct a Memorial Building upon the said premises,
of a value in excess of Fifteen Thousand ($15,000.00) Dollars, on or before the first day
of January, 1936.

(2)	Upon dissolution of said Post of the American Legion, for any reason.

(3) At any such time as the membership of said Las Vegas Post Number 8 of the
American legion, Department of Nevada, shall not contain at least ten (10) members who are
veterans of the World War.

UPON THE TERMINATION OF THIS LEASE, each all and every part of the improvements
constructed upon the said premises during the life of this lease, shall revert, together
with the land, to the Lessor.

thereunto duly authorized and the said Las Vegas Post Number 8, The American Legion,

Department of Nevada, a corporation, Lessee, has caused this instrument to be executed by
the chairman of its Board of Trustees and its finance Officer thereunto duly authorized.
