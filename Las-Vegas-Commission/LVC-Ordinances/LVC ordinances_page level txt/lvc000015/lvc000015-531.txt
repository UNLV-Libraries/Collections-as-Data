ORDINANCE NO. 747

AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND CHAPTER 36, SECTION 67, CODE OF LAS VEGAS, 1949, BY PROVIDING FOR ALLEY PARKING
WITHIN THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS; REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH; PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR
THE VIOLATION THEREOF; AND PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

SECTION 1. Chapter 36, Section 67, Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 1949, is hereby amended to
read as follows:

SECTION 67. PARKING PROHIBITED AT ALL TIMES IN CERTAIN ALLEYS.

When signs are erected giving notice thereof, no person shall park a vehicle at any time in
the alley or portions thereof described in the following schedule:

1.	On both sides of the north-south alley located between Fremont Street and Ogden
Avenue, and between Main Street and First Street, from a point 30 feet south of the east-west
alley in said block, to a point 75 feet north.

2.	On the north side of the east-west alley located between Fremont and Ogden; commencing
at the north-south alley located between Main Street and First Street, to a point 30 feet to the
east.
