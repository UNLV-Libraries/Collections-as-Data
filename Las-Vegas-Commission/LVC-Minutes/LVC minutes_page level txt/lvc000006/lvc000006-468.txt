A Special Meeting held this 28th day of July, 1949 was called to order by His Honor
Mayor E. W. Cragin at the hour of 3:25 P.M. with the following members present: Commis-
sioners Bunker, Moore, Peccole; City Manager J. M. Murphy, City Attorney Howard W.

Cannon and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger. Absent: Commissioner Whipple.
