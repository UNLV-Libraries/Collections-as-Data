NOTICE OF SPECIAL	Upon motion by Commissioner Clark duly seconded by Commissioner

ASSESSMENT	Moore and carried by unanimous vote the following Notice of Hear-

ing of Special Assessment was ordered published in the Las Vegas
Review Journal for a period of fourteen consecutive days exclusive
of Sundays, and hearing set for the 25th day of July, 1947 at the
hour of 3:00 P.M.

SURRENDER OF LEASE	Commissioner Moore moved that the Mayor and City Clerk be authorized

Ralph Stoughton	to sign that certain document entitled "Surrender of Lease" dated

July 2, 1947; concerning right of way at or near the Nevada
Biltmore Hotel, the lessee being Ralph Stoughton.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the follow-
ing vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His

Honor voting aye. Noes, none.

JOINT PLANNING	Commissioner Baskin moved that the City clerk be directed to send

COMMISSION	a copy of the letter dated June 27, 1947 from the Board of county

Commissioners addressed to T. E. Fennessy, City Manager, relative
to the budget of the Joint Planning Commission to the Joint Plan-
ning Commission for their information.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor

voting a ye. Noes, none.

STORM SEWER-----	Commissioner Whipple moved that the purchasing procedure be waived

North 5th Street	in connection with any purchases for pipe on the North 5th Street

storm sewer installation, and that the city manager be directed
to proceed on said installation on forced account in the approx-
imate sum of $14,044.69.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the follow-
ing vote:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His

Honor voting aye. Noes, none.
