CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TOr SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk
Las Vegas, Nevada

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is hereby called to
be held in the Council Chambers at the City, on the 20th day of July, 1953 at the hour of 4:00
P.M. to consider the following matter:

1. Payroll Warrants Nos. 1466 thru 1471 inclusive in the net amount of $38,321.83.

by Mayor Baker, a special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas,
Nevada, will be held in the Council Chambers, at the City Hall, 5th & Stewart, on the 20th
day of July, 1953, at the hour of 4:00 P.M. to consider the following matter:

1. Payroll Warrants Nos. 1466 thru 1471 inclusive in the total amount of $38,321.83.
