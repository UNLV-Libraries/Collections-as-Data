CRESTWOOD SUBDIVISION Commissioner Moore moved that the final map of the Crestwood Homes
Final Map Approved Subdivision be approved subject to the Planning Commission recommendations
	as per their letter dated August 5, 1948:

Re: _SUBDIVISONS - Crestwood Homes
Subdivision (Schroeder Tract)
Final Map

Gentlemen:

Transmitted herewith is a final map, a portion of the
approved tentative map for the subdivision of Lot 1, Section 2,

T. 21 S., R. 61 E., M.D.B. & M.

On August 3, 1948 the Joint Planning Commission approved
this final map and transmits same. A new owner's statement has been
presented this commission but conditions as set forth on August 5,

1947 will be adhered to.

It is the intention of the subdivider to develop this
tract in stages, with other portions to follow consecutively.

It may be pointed out that the time limit as set forth
by law did not expire before presentation of this final map.

Respectfully yours,

LAS VEGAS -- CLARK COUNTY
JOINT PLANNING COMMISSION

R. E. Singrey /s
R. E. SINGREY,

Secretary

RES; dm
Enc.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following
vote:	Commissioners Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye,

noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Baskin.
