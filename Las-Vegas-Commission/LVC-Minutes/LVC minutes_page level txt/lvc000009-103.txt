City Manager, George Rounthwaite, advised the Board that the agreement with
the County for the furnishing of fire protection to the fringe area has not
been renewed this year. Discussion was held on this matter and the City
Manager was requested to ask the County Commissioners to be present on
May 5, 1954, at 7:00 P.M. to discuss the matter further.

The City Manager reported at this time that the installation of the water
lines in the recreation areas was progressing satisfactorily.

The Mayor Pro Tem announced that this was the time and place designated for
the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada to meet and
review the assessments and hear any objections to said assessments which
may be made on the proposed improvements in Assessment District No. 100-23,
as shown on Assessment Roll No. 29-1954.

The Mayor Pro Tem then asked if any written objections concerning said
assessment roll had been filed in the office of the City Clerk. The City
Clerk answered there had been no objections filed in her office prior to
said meeting.

The Mayor Pro Tem then publicly asked if anyone desired to make any further
objections, suggestions, comments, or raise any questions concerning said
special assessment roll. There were no objections, suggestions or comments
voiced.

Thereafter Commissioner Sharp moved that the hearing on this Assessment Roll
No. 29-1954 be recessed until May 5, 1954, and that the following resolution
be adopted:

WHEREAS, the City of Las Vegas, and the officers thereof, have taken
action to improve certain streets and parts of streets, therein, to be
known as Assessment District No. 100-23; and

WHEREAS, the said City has directed the preparation of an assessment
roll; and

WHEREAS, such assessment roll was filed on the 21st day of April, 1954,

and

WHEREAS, a public protest hearing was held on the 3rd day of May, 1954,

and

WHEREAS, the said roll needs to be corrected and revised before con-
firmation to correct typographical or clerical errors and to modify all
assessments if the construction price proves to be substantially different
than the estimated price.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Board of Commissioners of the
City of Las Vegas at a special meeting thereof held on the 3rd day of May
1954, that the Ex-Officio City Assessor be, and he is hereby directed to
revise the assessment roll heretofore filed to show the amount of assess-
ments in the total of $427,841.26.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that said revised roll shall not increase any
assessments heretofore shown on the original roll; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the revised roll shall be filed with the
City Clerk bn the $th day of May, 1954.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple
voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor Baker.

The Mayor Pro Tem announced that this was the time and place designated for
the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada to meet and
review the assessments and hear any objections to said assessment which
may be made on the proposed improvements in Assessment District No. 100-25,
as shown on Assessment Roll No. 30-1954.

The Mayor Pro Tem then asked if any written objections concerning said
assessment roll had been filed in the office of the City Clerk. The City
Clerk answered there had been no objections filed in her office prior to
said meeting.

The Mayor Pro Tem then publicly asked if anyone desired to make any further
objections, suggestions, comments, or raise any questions concerning said
special assessment roll. There were no objections, suggestions or comments
voiced.

Commissioner Sharp moved that the hearing on this Assessment Roll No. 30-
1954. be recessed until May 5, 1954, and the following resolution adopted:

WHEREAS, the City of Las Vegas, and the officers thereof, have taken
action to improve certain streets and parts of streets, therein, to be
known as Assessment District No. 100-2$; and

WHEREAS, the said City has directed the preparation of an assessment

WHEREAS, such assessment roll was filed on the 21st day of April, 1954,

and

WHEREAS, a public protest hearing was held on the 3rd day of May, 1954.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the Board of Commissioners of the
City of Las Vegas at a special meeting thereof held on the 3rd day of May,
