EMPLOYMENT OF ALIENS	At this time the matter of the employment of aliens in casinos was brought

before the Board. Discussion was held and it was decided that an amendment
to the ordinance be prepared to permit the employment of aliens in positions that
are associated with the gambling and liquor industries, in such positions as
cashiers, cocktail waitresses, and employees of like nature.
