A Special Meeting held this 12th day of July, 1949 was called to order at the hour of 3:45

Peccole, Whipple; City Manager J.M. Murphy and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger. Absent: Commis-
sioner Moore and City Attorney Howard W. Cannon.
