ORDINANCE NO. 507	Discussion was held with the Trailer Parks in the City in regard to the proposed amendment

to the Trailer Park Ordinance.



Thereafter an ORDINANCE Entitled: AN ORDINANCE OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, NEVADA, REGULATING
THE CONSTRUCTION USE AND OPERATION OF TRAILER PARKS, PROVISION PENALTIES FOR THE VIOLATION
THEREOF, AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCE IN CONFLICT WITH THIS ORDINANCE,
having been reported on favorably by Commissioners Peccole and Whipple was read in full to
the Board.

Commissioner Peccole moved the above entitled Ordinance No. 507 be adopted.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote; Commissioners
Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent: Commissioner
Bunker.

There being no further business to come before the Board at this time, the meeting adjourned.

Commissioner Whipple moved that Service & Material Warrant Nos. 2333 to 2337 and 2339 in the
total amount of $33,759.92 be approved and the Mayor and city Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker,
Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Whipple moved the minutes of October 15, 17, 20, 1952 be approved by reference and
the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

This being the time set for the Public Hearing on the vacation of South 3rd Street between
Wyoming & Oakey Blvd., His Honor asked if there were any protestants present. Mr. Dudsey
was present and requested the Board to hold this matter in abeyance because of the Fire
Chief’s objection.

Thereafter, Commissioner Bunker moved the recommendation of the Regional Planning Commission
that the vacation of South 3rd Street be approved be held in abeyance and the Engineering
Department submit a map of the area.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Peccole and carried by the following vote; Commissioners Bunker,
Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
