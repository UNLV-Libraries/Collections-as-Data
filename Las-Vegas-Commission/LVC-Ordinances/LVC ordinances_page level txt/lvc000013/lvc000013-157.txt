	ORDINANCE NO. 105		

			

	An Ordinance to amend Subdivision number 19 of section 2 of ordinance No. 81 of



     the City of Las Vegas, entitled "An Ordinance to amend Section Seven (7) of Ordinance No.



48 of the City of Las Vegas, entitled "An Ordinance of the City of Las Vegas, licensing, 

for the purpose of regulation and revenue, every kind of lawful business hereinafter 
specified, transacted or carried on within the corporation limits of the City of Las Vegas
State of Nevada, fixing the rates of license tax upon the same and providing for the
collection of said license tax, and a punishment for carrying on or conducting any such
business without a license, and Repealing Ordinances Nos. 1, 2, 4, 13, 14 and 35, approved
December 3, 1913, and amended Dec. 13, 1915 by ordinance No. 62 of the City of Las Vegas,
and further amended March 5, 1919 by Ordinance No. 74, and repealing all ordinances or
parts of Ordinances in conflict therewith."

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do Ordain as follows:-

of section 2

Section 1. Subdivision number 19 of said ordinance No. 81 is hereby amended
so that the same shall read as follows:

(19) For each traveling show--------------$10.00 per day;

provided, however, that when fifty (50) per cent, or more,
of the net proceeds from such performance is given in aid
of any local educational or charitable purpose or to the
local volunteer fire department, no license shall be 
required.

Section 2. This Ordinance shall take effect and be in force from and

after its passage and publication for one week (one issue) in the Las Vegas Review a

weekly newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas, Nevada.

Passed by the Board of City Commissioners this 21st day of April, 1924,

upon the following vote:	Commissioners Matzdorf, Anderson, Shield, Elwell, and His Honor

the Mayor, W. C. German, Voting aye, noes, none.

First reading April 1, 1924.

Second reading April 21, 1924.

ATTEST:	APPROVED THIS 21st day of April, 1924.

 FLORENCE S. DOHERTY	W. C. German._______
