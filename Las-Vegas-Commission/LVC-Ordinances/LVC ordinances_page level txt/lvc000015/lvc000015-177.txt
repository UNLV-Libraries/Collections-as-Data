ORDINANCE NO. 325

AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND ORDINANCE NO. 304 ENTITLED:	"AN ORDINANCE OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS CREATING AND

ESTABLISHING FIRE ZONES"; BY ADDING CERTAIN DESCRIBED AREAS TO FIRE ZONES TWO AND TWO SPECIAL (2s); BY PRO-
VIDING FIRE ZONES FOR: REZONED AREAS; AND OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO; REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR
PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Ve&as do ordain as follows:

SECTION 1. Section 4 of Ordinance No. 304 is hereby amended by adding the following described
areas to Fire Zone #2:

That area bounded by the east property line of North 5th Street, the west
line of the alley between 12th and 13th Streets, a line 100 feet north of
and parallel to the property line on the north side of Stewart Avenue and 
the center line of Fremont Avenue; all of Block 6, Ladd’s Addition; that
area bounded by the alley north of Fremont Avenue on the north and the
center line of Fremont Avenue on the south, and lying between the east
property line of North l5th Street and the west property line of North
Bruce Street; that portion of the south half of the southeast quarter of
the southeast quarter (SE¼) of Section 33, T20S, R61E, MDB&M, lying south
of Fremont Avenue, excluding therefrom the Lawrence Love Tract; Lots 11
to 17 in Block 8, Crestwood Addition; the east half (E½) of Block 22,

Wardie Addition; Lots 27 to 32 in	Block 24, Lots 11 to	32 in	Block 23,	all

of Blocks 26, 27 and 28,	Pioneer Heights Addition; the south half (S½)	of

plot known as Buol Land;	Lot 1 of	Beckley Subdivision;	Lots 7 and 29 in

Block 18; Lots 13 and 26	in Block 14 and all of Blocks	3 and	10, all

situate in the South Addition; Lots 17 to 20 in Block 12, Lots 29 to 32 in
Block 11, Lots 13 to 16 in Block 21, Lots 1 to 4 in Block 22, Lots 17 and
18 in Block 8, Lots 13 and 16 in Block 9, all situate in Clark's Las
Vegas Townsite; Lots 1 to 3 in Block 21, Lots 1 to 12 in Block 13, Lots
1 to 12 and 18 to 22 in Block 7, Lots 1 to 17 in Block 1, all situate in
Boulder Addition; all lots fronting on South "A" Street in Block 1, 2, 3, 4
and 5 of Boulder Addition; that area lying between the north line of
Bonanza Road and a line 130 feet north of and parallel thereto and ex-
tending west from the west line of "H” Street for a distance of 1800 feet.

SECTION 2. Section 4 of Ordinance No. 304 is hereby further amended to include the following
described areas in Fire Zone Two Special (2s):

All of that area lying between the center line of South "A" Street and the
Union Pacific Railroad right-of-way, from Charleston Blvd, to New York Avenue;
all of that area in Section 4, T 21 S, R 6l E, MDB&M, from the West Boundary
of the Meadows Addition to the Union Pacific Railroad right-of-way; all of
that area in the north half (N½) of the south half (S½) of the southeast
quarter (SE¼) of Section 28, T 20 S, R 61 E, MDB&M; all of that portion of
the northeast quarter (NE¼) Section 4, T 21 S, R 6l E, MDB&M, lying west of
the Union Pacific Railroad right-of-way not already zoned residential.

SECTION 3. Any areas hereafter rezoned by the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
to C-1, C-2, or C-S, shall immediately upon such rezoning be considered in Fire Zone No. 2.

SECTION 4. Any areas hereafter rezoned by the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
to M-1 or M-2 shall immediately upon such rezoning be considered in Fire Zone No. Two Special (2s).

SECTION 5. All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith, are hereby repealed.

SECTION 6. This ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its publication as in the next
section provided, and final passage.

SECTION 7. The City Clerk and the Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
shall cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following its
first reading and adoption, in the Review Journal, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
