Las Vegas, Nevada
October 18, 1954

( CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING)

A special meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, was
held this 18th day of October, 1954, and called to order at the hour of 4:30 P.M.
by His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple with the following members present:

Mayor Pro Tem	Reed Whipple

Commissioners	Wendell Bunker

Commissioner	Rex A.	Jarrett

Commissioner	Harris	P. Sharp

City Manager	George	Rounthwaite

Asst. City Manager	Robert	W. Notti

City Attorney	Howard	W. Cannon

City Engineer	George	E. Wade

City Clerk	Shirley Ballinger

Absent:	Mayor	C. D. Baker

City Manager George Rounthwaite reported on the proposal to install ducts for the fire
alarm system along with the Telephone Company. Mr. Rounthwaite explained that we
can use the ducts fran Oakey to Carson and Ogden to Bonanza. There was discussion
on putting in this system on Carson from Main to 14th Street and on Fremont Street at an
approximate cost of $14,976.00.

No action was taken other than to direct the City Manager to ascertain if funds are
available for this work.

10-18-54
