the City of Las Vegas and the duties heretofore performed by the Clark County Humane Society shall be per-
formed by a Poundmaster appointed by the City Manager.

SECTION 3. Section 3 of Ordinance No. 521 is hereby amended to read as follows:

The operation of an Animal Shelter which is hereby authorized and established in the City shall
