ADOPTION OF REVISED BUDGET FOR FISCAL YEAR 1959-60

City Manager Kennedy advised that as a result of a meeting held with representatives of the County Commissioners
and the School Board, it appeared that it may be necessary for the City of Las Vegas to reduce its proposed 1959*60
tax rate by 11₵ in order that the over-all tax rate come within the $5.00 statutory limit.

In view of the above, Mr. Kennedy presented a revised budget reducing estimated expenditures from $5,148,316.52 to
$5,117,157.16 and the proposed tax rate from $1.58 to $1.47.

Commissioner Fountain moved that the revised budget as presented by the City Manager be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners Fountain, Sharp and His

Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Mayor C. D. Baker.
