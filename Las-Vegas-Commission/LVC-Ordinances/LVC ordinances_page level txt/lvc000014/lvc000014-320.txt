Upon the failure to perform said condition, the Building Inspector shall not issue a
certificate of occupancy for such new construction, and said construction shall be forthwith
removed.

Section 2. All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby
repealed.

Section 3. This ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its publication,
as in the next section provided, and final passage.

Section 4. T he City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City
of Las Vegas shall cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive
weeks immediately following its first reading and adoption, in the Las Vegas Evening
Review journal, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
