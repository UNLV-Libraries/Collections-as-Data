ORDINANCE NO. 481

AN ORDINANCE PROHIBITING ANY PERSON, FIRM, ASSOCIATION OR CORPORATION OWNING, MANAGING, OR BEING IN CON-
TROL OF ANY AUDITORIUM,THEATRE, OR MOTION PICTURE THEATRE, FROM ALLOWING SMOKING WITHIN SUCH BUILDING; PRO-
HIBITING ANY PERSON FROM SMOKING WITHIN SAID BUILDING; PROVIDING A PENALTY FOR THE VIOLATION THEREOF AND
OTHER MATTERS RELATING THERETO.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas does ordain as follows:

Section 1. It shall, be unlawful for any person, firm, association, or corporation, managing,
controlling, or presenting shows of any kind in any theatre building, motion picture theatre, including
projection rooms, within the City, to allow, or permit, any person to smoke within the confines of said
building; except in rest rooms or in a lounge provided by the theatre, completely enclosed and separated
from the main seating room.

Section 2. It shall be unlawful for any person to smoke within any auditorium, theatre building,
or motion picture theatre, including projection rooms, within the City, except in rest rooms or in a lounge
provided, by the theatre, completely enclosed and separated from the main seating room. This section
shall not be deemed to make it unlawful for a performer to smoke upon the stage during and as a part of any
theatrical production.

Section 3. There shall be displayed in a conspicuous place in every theatre or motion picture
theatre in which smoking is forbidden or prohibited by the terms of this ordinance, a notice to the effect
that smoking is prohibited therein, with reference to this ordinance as carrying such prohibition.

Section 4. Any person violating the provisions of this ordinance shall be guilty of a misdemean-
or and shall be punished by a fine of not less than Ten Dollars ($10.00) nor more than Five Hundred Dollars
($500.00) and in addition thereto may be imprisoned in the City Jail for a period of not to exceed six (6)
months.

Section 5. All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

Section 6. This ordinance shall be in full force and effect upon its final reading and adoption,
and final publication as in the next section provided.

Section 7. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas shall
cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following its first
reading and adoption, in the Las Vegas Morning Sun, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
