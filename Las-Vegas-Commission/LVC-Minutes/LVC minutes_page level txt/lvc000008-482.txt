Mr. Clifford Jones was present to discuss the Sale of the City owned Strip
Property with the Board. Mr. Jones spoke in behalf of Mr. Paul B. Sogg.
The following proposal was made:

To purchase the property owned by the City of Las Vegas
located on U.S. Highway #91 South of Las Vegas, Nevada,
and commonly known as and referred to as the "City of
Las Vegas Strip Property", which property consists of 477
feet of Highway Frontage and comprises 44.71 acres, for
the sum of $255,000.00 Dollars, payable in cash as follows:

(a)	Twenty Five Thousand Five $25,000.00 Dollars payable this date, which
sum is to be placed in an escrow to be set up to cover this transaction,
said escrow to close within ninety days from the date of its opening.

(b)	Further sum of One Hundred Thousand Dollars to be paid into such escrow
within thirty days from the opening date thereof.

(c)	The balance of One Hundred Forty Five Thousand Five Hundred Dollars to
be paid into said escrow on or before ninety days from the opening of
same.
