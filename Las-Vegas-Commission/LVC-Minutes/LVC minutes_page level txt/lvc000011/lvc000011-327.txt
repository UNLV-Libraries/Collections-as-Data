A regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada,

His Honor Mayor Baker with the following members present:

PAYROLL WARRANTS	Commissioner Sharp moved that regular Payroll Warrant Nos. 32534 - 33063 in the net

amount of $80,642.29 and Overtime Payroll Warrant No. 33064 in the net amount of
$4.94 for the pay period from February 16 - February 28, 1959, he approved.

Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes, none.
