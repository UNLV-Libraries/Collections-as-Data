BE IT RESOLVED, this 20th day of February, 1957, by the Board of Commissioners
of the City of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada, that the deed, dated the
18th day of February, 1957 executed by George H. Cobb and Kathleen M. Cobb to the City
of Las Vegas, County of Clark, State of Nevada, and that the real property described
therein be, and is hereby accepted, for the purposes and intentions as therein set forth; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that a copy of this Resolution be attached to said
deed, and that the same be recorded in the office of the County Recorder of the County
of Clark, State of Nevada, and this resolution be filed in the records of this Board.

Commissioner Bunker moved the City Engineer be instructed to proceed as soon as possible
to open, grade and gravel 6th, 7th and Bracken Avenue between 6th & 7th Streets.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Sharp moved the request of Graydon Stark and Keith Johnson to transfer
their tavern liquor license for the Bank Club from 2300 East Charleston to 2400 East
Charleston be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Fountain and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Commissioner Fountain moved the application of Thelma H. Pike for a Retail Liquor and
Beer Bar license for the Sundown Liquor Store, 124½ North Main Street, formerly issued to
Cecil Murray, be approved and the Supervisor of License and Revenue instructed to issue
the new license.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Bunker and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
