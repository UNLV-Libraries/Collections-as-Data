ORDINANCE NO. 552

 AN ORDINANCE PROVIDING FOR A CITY CLERK; PROVIDING FOR THE APPOINTMENT OF A CITY CLER£; PRESCRIBING THE
DUTIES THEREFORE; PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED "THERETO; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS
OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH:

WHEREAS, the Office of City Clerk of the City of Las Vegas was removed from that of an elective
office by Chapter 105, Statutes of Nevada, 1953; and

WHEREAS, the term of office of the City Clerk of the City of Las Vegas expires on the 1st Monday
of June, 1953; and

WHEREAS, it is necessary that such a position be created within the municipal government of the
City of Las Vegas,

NOW, THEREFORE, THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS, DO ORDAIN:

SECTION 1. There shall be a City Clerk of the City of Las Vegas who shall be appointed by the
Mayor and Board of Commissioners, and shall receive such compensation as may be fixed by said Board.

SECTION 2. The City Clerk shall keep his office at the place of meeting of the Board of Commis-
sioners, or some place convenient thereto, as the Board may direct. He shall keep the corporate seal and all

papers and records of the City and keep a record of the proceedings of the Board, whose meetings it shall be
his duty to attend. He shall countersign all contracts made in behalf of the City and every such contract or
contracts to which the City is a party shall be void unless signed by him. He shall countersign all orders
upon the treasurer in pursuance of any order or resolution of the Board of City Commissioners. He shall
issue all licenses as granted by the Board of Commissioners or as directed by ordinance, and shall receive
and collect all license fees. He shall also receive and collect such other fees of any nature as the City
Commission may direct by ordinance. He shall be allowed such deputies, assistants or clerks as the Board

of City Commissioners may prescribe.	In addition to the duties prescribed herein, he shall perform all other

duties required by the provisions of the Charter of the City of Las Vegas or any ordinance promulgated
thereunder, and such other duties not inconsistent therewith as may be prescribed by the Board of City
Commissioners.

SECTION 3. The City Clerk shall, before entering upon the discharge of his duties, execute to the
City a good and sufficient bond for the faithful performance of his duties, which bond shall be in such sum
and conditioned in such manner, as required by the Board of Commissioners, and the sureties thereon shall be
 approved by the Board.

SECTION If for any reason whatsoever the acts and duties performed by the City Clerk pursuant
to the Charter or any Ordinances of the City between March 16, 1953, and the effective date of this ordinance
should be questioned in any way whether by legal action or otherwise, such acts and duties performed pursuant
to the Charter and all other ordinances are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved.

SECTION 5. All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

SECTION 6. The effective date of this Ordinance shall be June 1, 1953.

SECTION 7. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas shall
cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following its first
reading and adoption, in the Las Vegas Review Journal, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
