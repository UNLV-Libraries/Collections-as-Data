

NEVADA TAX COMMISSION	Commissioner Bunker moved that Commissioner Peccole, City Attorney Howard W. Cannon, City

MEETING	Manager.C.W. Shelley and Comptroller Harold A. Laird be authorized to attend the Nevada Tax

Commission meeting on May 5, 1952 in Carson City.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; Noes, none. Commissioner
Peccole passed his vote. Absent: Mayor Baker.

There being no further business to come before the meeting at this time Cornmissioner Bunker
moved this meeting recessed until April 29, 1952 at 4:00 P.M.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote: Commissioners

Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole and His Honor Mayor Pro Tem Whipple voting aye; noes, none.

Absent: Mayor Baker.

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, City Clerk

Las Vegas, Nevada

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas is hereby called
to be held in the Council Chambers at the City Ball, on the 23rd day of April, 1952, at the
hour of 7:30 P.M. to consider the following matters:

1.	Advance payment to J.M. Murphy Construction Co.
on Assessment District No. 100-6 in the total
amount of $15,000.00

2.	Contract with North Las Vegas for connection to
the Sewer Disposal Plant.
