Las Vegas, Nevada

MINUTES	April 1, 1959

A regular meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada,
held this 1st day of April, 1959, was called to order at the hour of 7:30 P.M. by
His Honor Mayor Baker with the following members present:

Mayor	C. D. Baker

Commissioner	E. W. Fountain

Commissioner	Harris P. Sharp

Commissioner	Reed Whipple

City Manager	A. H. Kennedy

Deputy City Attorney               Sidney R. Whitmore
Planning Director	Franklin J. Bills

Dir. Public Works	Richard P. Sauer

Acting City Clerk	Robert F. Boos

Absent:	City Attorney	Calvin Cory

PAYROLL WARRANTS	Commissioner Whipple moved that Regular Payroll Warrants Nos. 33601 - 34136 for the

pay period March 16 - March 31, 1959 in the net amount of $79,402.82; and Overtime
Payroll Warrant Nos. 34137 - 34145 in the net amount of $459.95 be approved.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Sharp and carried by the following vote: Commissioners
Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

TRANSFER OF CREDIT	Commissioner Fountain moved that the transfer of Credit Union Withholding in the amount

UNION WITHHOLDING	of $17,235.48 be approved.
