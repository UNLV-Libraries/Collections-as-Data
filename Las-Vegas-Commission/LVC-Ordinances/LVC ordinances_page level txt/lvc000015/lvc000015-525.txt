ORDINANCE NO. 741

AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND ORDINANCE 525 ENTITLED "AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND ORDINANCE 304 ENTITLED: "AN ORDINANCE
OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS CREATING AND ESTABLISHING FIRE ZONES"; BY ADDING CERTAIN DESCRIBED AREAS TO FIRE
ZONES TWO AND TWO SPECIAL (2s); BY PROVIDING FIRE ZONES FOR RE-ZONED AREAS; AND OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY
RELATED THERETO; REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH"; BY PLACING
AREAS WHICH ARE GRANTED USB PERMITS OR VARIANCES TO CONDUCT A PROFESSIONAL OR COMMERCIAL USE IN FIRE
ZONE NUMBER TWO OR FIRE ZONE NUMBER TWO SPECIAL AS THE CASE MAY BE; REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS
OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH; AND PROVIDING PENALTIES FOR THE VIOLATION OF THIS ORDINANCE

THE BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS OF THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS DO ORDAIN AS FOLLOWS:

SECTION 1. Section 3 of Ordinance 525 is hereby amended to read as follows:

Any areas hereafter re-zoned by the Board of Commissioners of the City
of Las Vegas to C-1, C-2 or C-S, shall immediately upon such re-zoning
be considered in Fire Zone No. 2. Any areas hereafter granted a use
permit or variance to conduct a professional or commercial use similar
to the uses permitted in C-1, C-2 or C-S Zones shall immediately upon
the granting of said use permit or variance be considered in Fire Zone
No. 2.

SECTION 2. Section 4 of Ordinance 525 is hereby amended to read as follows:

Any areas hereafter re-zoned by the Board of Commissioners of the City
of Las Vegas to M-1 or M-2 shall immediately upon such re-zoning be
considered in Fire Zone No. 2 Special (2s). Any areas hereafter issued
a use permit or a zone variance to conduct a business or commercial use
similar to those permitted in an M-1 or M-2 Zone shall immediately upon
the granting of such use permit or variance be considered in Fire Zone
No. 2 Special (2s).

SECTION 3. All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

SECTION 4. Any person maintaining property in violation of the provisions of this ordinance
shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and upon conviction thereof shall be punished by a fine of not more than
Five Hundred ($500.00) Dollars and six (6) months in the City Jail, or any combination thereof.

SECTION 5. The City Clerk and Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
shall cause this ordinance to be published once a week for two successive weeks immediately following
its first reading and adoption in the Las Vegas SUN, a daily newspaper published in the City of Las Vegas.
