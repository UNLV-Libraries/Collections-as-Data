BUENA VISTA-Final Map	Mr. Harold Shidler former instigator of Dewhurst Lawn Tract appeared before the	Board and

approved-Formerly	introduced Mr. Royce J. Peterson who is now the owner and has changed the name	to Buena

Dewhurst Lawn	Vista Tract. His Honor read the letter of recommendation dated March 30, 1949	from the

Planning Commission relative to the approval of the final map.

	Thereafter Commissioner Baskin moved that the final map of Buena Vista be approved subject

to the conditions set forth by the Planning Commission and that the Mayor and City Clerk
be authorized to sign the agreement when presented.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Moore and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

THE WESTERNER	At this time Ryland Taylor and V. Gray Gubler, Esq. representing Catherine and	John Horden,

Liquor & Gaming	The Westerner, 23 Fremont who have made application for a retail tavern liquor	license and

gaming licenses for the second quarter of 1949 appeared before the Board on behalf of their
	clients. After considerable discussion Commissioner Clark moved that these applications be

held in abeyance until discussion can be had on the question of gaming and liquor licenses
now being issued that are not in use.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Moore and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

RESUBDIVISION -	Mr. Kelly Howard appeared before	the Board on behalf of	his proposal for the re subdivision

Noblitt & Church Addition of Noblitt & Church Additions in	order that the streets	in those additions will meet the

proper alignment. His Honor read a letter of recommendation from the Planning Commission
dated March 30, 1949. Thereafter Commissioner Moore moved that the Board be in favor of this
	resubdivision.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Clark and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

AMENDMENT TO LAND USE	His Honor read a letter of recommendation from the Planning Commission dated March 30, 1949

ORDINANCE #378	relative to the amendment to Ordinance No. 378 so that	the depth of lots having a 6,000 sq.

	ft. area will be excluded. Since	a notice that a public	hearing will be held on this amend-
