ORDER OF VACATION

A Petition, dated the 23rd day of January, 1957, signed by five (5)
freeholders residing in the area affected, having been filed with the Clerk
of this Board, petitioning for the vacation of a portion of Hinkle Drive
hereinafter described, and said Petition having been by Order of this Board
referred to the Planning Commission of the City of Las Vegas for its recom-
mendation in the premises, and said Planning Commission having filed its report,
dated March 27, 1957, and this Board approving and recommending said vacation;

And this Board by an order made at its regular meeting held on the 3rd
day of April, 1957, set the 8th day of May, 1957, at the hour of 7:30 o'clock
P.M. at the Commissioner's Room of the Board of Commissioners, in the City
Hall, located at 400 Stewart Street, in the City of Las Vegas, Clark County,
Nevada, as the time and place for the Public Hearing on said Petition and
recommendation, and ordered the City Clerk to cause the aforesaid portion of
street proposed to be vacated to be posted with a Notice setting forth the
time and place of the Public Hearing and the extent of the proposed vacation;
