At this time the Board considered the request of the Engineering Depart-
ment for advertising of bids to construct an accessory building to the
 City Park.

Thereafter Commissioner Bunker moved this request for advertising of
bids be approved and said bids to be opened June 16, 1954.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:
Commissioners Bunker, Jarrett, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye;
noes, none.
