ORDINANCE NO. 573

AN ORDINANCE TO AMEND CHAPTER 24, SECTION 26, CODE OF IAS VEGAS, NEVADA, 1949, BY CHANGING THE MINIMUM BUILD-
ING LINE SETBACK AND FUTURE WIDTH LINE IN CERTAIN AREAS; PROVIDING OTHER MATTERS PROPERLY RELATED THERETO;

AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT THEREWITH.

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas do ordain as follows:

SECTION 1. Paragraph 2, Section 26, Chapter 24, Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 1949, Page 418, is
hereby amended to read as follows:

2. Fifth Street:

(a)	From the south city boundary to Charleston Boulevard, ten (10) feet from the street line or
fifty (50) feet from the center line on each side thereof.

(b)	From Charleston Boulevard to Garces Street, five (5) feet from the street line or forty five
(45) feet from the center line on each side thereof.

(c)	From Garces Street to Stewart Street, three (3) feet from the street line or forty-three (43)
feet from the center line on each side thereof.

(d)	From Stewart Street to the North City Boundary, ten (10) feet from the street line or fifty
(50) feet from the center line on each side thereof.

SECTION 2. Paragraph 4, Section 26, Chapter 24, Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 1949, Page 419, is
hereby amended to read as follows:

4.	Charleston Boulevard - Rancho Road:

(a)	From Fremont Street to Fifteenth Street, Ninety (90) feet from the center line on each side

thereof.

(b)	From Fifteenth Street to Tenth Street, fifty (50) feet from the center line on each side

thereof.

(c)	From Tenth Street to Main Street, ten (10) feet from the street line or fifty (50) feet from
the center line on each side thereof.

(d)	From Main Street to the South quarter (S¼) corner of Section 33, Township 20 South, Range 61
East, MD.B.&M., ten (10) feet from the property line or fifty feet from the center line on each Side thereof.

(e)	From the South quarter (S¼) corner of Section 33, Township 20 South, Range 61 East, M.D.B.&M.,
to the South City Limits, as set forth in Sections 14, 15, 16, 17, 18, and 19.

)f) From Rose Street (Rancho Road Portion) to Bonanza Road, as set forth in Sections 14, 15, 16,

17, 18, and 19, Chapter 24, Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 1949 as amended.

SECTION 3. Paragraph 5, Section 26, Chapter 24, Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 1949 Page 4l9, is
hereby amended to read as follows:

5.	Bonanza Road.

(a)	From the Westerly end of the grade separation at the Union Pacific Railroad Tracks to a point
seven hundred (700) feet east of the north and south centerline of Section 28, Township 20 South, Range 61
East, M.D.B.&M., sixty (60) feet from the center line on each side thereof.

(b) From a point seven hundred (700) feet east of the North and South centerline of Section 28,
Township 20 South, Range 61 East, M.D.B.&M., as set forth in Sections 14, 15, 16, 17, 18 and 19, Chapter 24,
Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 19h9, as amended.

SECTION I;. Paragraph 6, Section 26, Code of Las Vegas, Nevada, 19ll9, page ip.9, is hereby amended
to read as follows:

6.	Maryland Parkway (Twelfth Street):

(a)	From Charleston Boulevard to the south property line of the Kelso-Turner housing project, fifty
(50) feet from the present center line on each side thereof.

(b)	From the south line of Section 26, T20S, R61E, M.D.B.&M., to the north line of said Section
26, fifty (50) feet from the north-south one-quarter (¼) line on each side thereof.

SECTION 5. All ordinances or parts of ordinances, in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.
