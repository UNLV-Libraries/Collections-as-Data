Las Vegas, Nevada
April 23, 1948

The Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas, Nevada, met in
recessed regular session, pursuant to due legal notice and call, at
the regular meeting place of said Board in Las Vegas, Nevada, on the
	23rd day of April, 1948, at the hour of 4:00 o'clock, p.m.

The following members of the Board of Commissioners were present:

E. W. Cragin	Mayor

R. T. Baskin	Commissioner

Robert T. Moore	Commissioner

C.	R. Clark	Commissioner

Reed Whipple	Commissioner

Also present:	Helen Scott Reed	City Clerk

Absent:	None

The meeting was called to order by the Mayor. Thereupon the
following proceedings, among others, were had and taken, to-wit:

Commissioner Clark introduced in writing an Emergency Ordinance entitle
"An ordinance providing for the issuance and public sale by the City of
Las Vegas of its negotiable coupon bonds in the aggregate principal
amount of Three Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($350,000) to finance
the construction of a sewage disposal plant as part of and in connectio
with the sewerage system of said City, prescribing the terms thereof
and providing for the levy and collection of an annual tax therefor",
and moved its adoption. Commissioner Whipple seconded the motion.

THEREUPON, by unanimous consent of the entire Board of Commissioners,
the proposed Ordinance was read aloud in its entirety, and is as
follows:

AN ORDINANCE PROVIDING FOR THE ISSUANCE AND PUBLIC
SALE BY THE CITY OF LAS VEGAS OF ITS NEGOTIABLE
COUPON BONDS IN THE AGGREGATE PRINCIPAL AMOUNT OF
THREE HUNDRED FIFTY THOUSAND DOLLARS ($350,000) TO
FINANCE THE CONSTRUCTION OF A SEWAGE DISPOSAL PLANT
AS PART OF AND IN CONNECTION WITH THE SEWERAGE SYSTEM
OF SAID CITY; PRESCRIBING THE TERMS THEREOF AND PROVIDING
FOR THE LEVY AND COLLECTION OF AN ANNUAL TAX THEREFOR.
