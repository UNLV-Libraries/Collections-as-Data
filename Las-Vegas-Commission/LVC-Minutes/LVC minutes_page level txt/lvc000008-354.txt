Commissioner Whipple moved the resignation of Harold Case as Fire Chief be accepted
and a resolution passed commending Chief Case for his time on the Fire Department
and spread in the Minutes of this Board.

	RESOLUTION

WHEREAS, Harold Case, has so faithfully and untiringly served the City of Las
Vegas for the past 29 years as Chief of the Fire Department; and

WHEREAS, Harold Case has contributed much time and effort to the development
and improvement of the City of Las Vegas; and

WHEREAS, Harold Case ever mindful of the great progress and expansion of the
City of Las Vegas, through his efforts and diligence was instrumental in protect-
ing the City from disasters by means of fire which helped insure such progress
and expansion; and

WHEREAS, Harold Case by faithfully performing his duties, has assured the pro-
gress and growth of the City of Las Vegas, because of his efforts the protection
and welfare of its citizens has been improved.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, by the Board of Commissioners of the City of
Las Vegas, at a regular meeting thereof held on the 15th day of April, 1953, that
said City, by this Resolution, expresses its gratitude and appreciation to Harold
Case for his efforts in obtaining these results, and thereby insuring to the citi-
zens of Las Vegas, Nevada, the future growth and development of the said city; and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that the City Clerk be and she is hereby directed to
send a copy of this Resolution to the Honorable Harold Case.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Jarrett and carried by the following vote:	Com-

missioners Bunker, Jarrett, Peccole, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
Absent: none.
