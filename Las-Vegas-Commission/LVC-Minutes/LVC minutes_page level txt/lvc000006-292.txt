TO:	SHIRLEY BALLINGER, CITY CLERK

Las Vegas, Nevada

A Special Meeting of the Board of Commissioners of the City of Las Vegas
is hereby called to be held in the Office of the Mayor at the War
Memorial building on 23rd day of July, 1948, at the hour of 3 P.M. to
consider the following matters:

1.	Proposed Ordinances - 2nd Reading of Nos. 354 and 355,
and report on Ord. Nos. 356 and 359.

2.	Tabulation and award of Bid #48.11

3.	Award of Bid #48.10

4.	Cancellation and termination of Lease - M.E. Leavitt & Wes Neeley

5.	Gaming applications

6.	Purchase of accounting machine & addressing machine.

CALL OF SPECIAL MEETING

This special meeting was called to order by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin at the hour
of 3:00 P.M. with the following members present:	Commissioners Clark, Moore,

Whipple; City Manager J. M. Murphy, and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger; Absent:
Commissioner Basking and City Attorney C. Norman Cornwall.

PROPOSED CURFEW The committee composed of Commissioners Clark and Moore asked for further time
ORDINANCE	to report on the Curfew Ordinance. They also asked that a copy of said proposed

	Ordinance be referred to Judges Henderson and McNamee before being adopted.

ORDINANCE NO. 354 At this time Ordinance No. 354 relating to the installation of Parking Meters,

2nd reading	was read in full to the Board for a second time.

Thereafter Commissioner Whipple moved that the foregoing Ordinance be adopted
on its second reading.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Moore and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none. Absent:	Commissioner Baskin

At this time City Attorney C. Norman Cornwall arrived.

ORDINANCE NO. 355 At this time Ordinance No. 355 prescribing the fees for driveway permits was
2nd reading	read in full to the Board for the second time.
