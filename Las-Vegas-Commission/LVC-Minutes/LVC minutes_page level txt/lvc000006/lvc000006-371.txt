RESIGNATION OF	Commissioner Baskin moved that the resignation of A. G. Blad, Municipal Judge, effective

MUNICIPAL JUDGE	January 1, 1949 be accepted. Further that Judge Blad be given a vote of thanks for cooper-

ation and service.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

APPOINTMENT OF	Commissioner Clark moved that Walter J. Richards be appointed Municipal Judge to fill the

MUNICIPAL JUDGE	unexpired term of A. G. Blad, effective January 1, 1949.

	Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

REVERSION TO STANDARD Commissioner Clark moved that Daylight Savings Time in the City of Las Vegas be abandoned

and that the City revert to Pacific Standard Time on and after 1:00 A. M. Daylight Savings
Time, January 1, 1949.

	Motion seconded by Commissioner Baskin and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

There being no further business to come before the meeting at this time Commissioner Baskin
moved that this meeting adjourn until January 7, 1949 at the hour of 2:00 P.M.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.

Las Vegas, Nevada
January 7, 1949

A regular meeting of the Board of City Commissioners held this 7th day of January, 1949
was called to order at the hour of 3:15 P.M. by His Honor Mayor E. W. Cragin with the
following members present:	Commissioners Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple; City Manager

J. M. Murphy, City Attorney C. Norman Cornwall, and City Clerk Shirley Ballinger.

Commissioner Clark moved that the minutes of December 22 and 29, 1948 be approved by re-
ference and the Mayor and City Clerk authorized to sign same.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commissioners

Baskin, Clark, Moore, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
