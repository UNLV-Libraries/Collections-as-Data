City Attorney Cannon advised the Commission that a bus belonging to Vegas Transit Lines
had run into a fire truck at 5th and Stewart on March 12, 1954. He stated that demand
had been made on Vegas Transit and they had requested that the City hold off a settle-
ment until they had settled the personal injuries involved. Mr. Cannon stated that no
settlement has been made and that the statute of limitations is about to run out. He
requested that he be given authorization to file suit against Vegas Transit Lines to
recover the amount of the loss which is approximately $900.00.

Commissioner Bunker moved the City Attorney be authorized to file suit against Vegas
Transit Lines to recover the damages to the fire truck.

Motion seconded by Commissioner Whipple and carried by the following vote:	Commis-

sioners Bunker, Fountain, Sharp, Whipple and His Honor voting aye; noes, none.
